Historical Context

The World Behind the Text — Politics, Culture, Daily Life

You cannot understand what was written without understanding the world it was written in. Who was in power? What did people eat? How did they worship? What were they afraid of? What did they hope for?

Each book page gives you the historical, cultural, political, and economic backdrop — so when you read the text in the Interlinear, you see it through the eyes of the people who first heard it.
