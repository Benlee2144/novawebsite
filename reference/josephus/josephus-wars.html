Josephus: Wars of the Jews

Flavius Josephus (c. 37–100 AD) — Translated by William Whiston

Josephus was a first-century Jewish historian who lived through the destruction of the Temple in 70 AD. His writings provide invaluable historical context for understanding the world of the Bible — the politics, customs, conflicts, and daily life that shaped the events recorded in Scripture.
