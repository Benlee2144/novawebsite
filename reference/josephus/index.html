Josephus — Complete Works

Flavius Josephus (37–100 AD) was a first-century Jewish historian whose writings provide invaluable context for understanding the world of the New Testament. A Pharisee, priest, and eyewitness to the destruction of Jerusalem in 70 AD.

Antiquities of the Jews

A history of the Jewish people from creation to the reign of Nero, written around 93–94 AD. Twenty books covering from Adam to the outbreak of the Jewish revolt.
