The Septuagint

The Greek Old Testament — The Bible the Apostles Read

This is the Greek translation of the Hebrew Scriptures, produced by Jewish scholars in Alexandria around 250 BC. When Matthew, Paul, Luke, and the author of Hebrews quote the Old Testament, they almost always quote this version — not the Hebrew directly. — Swete’s Edition
