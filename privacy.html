Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

The Short Version

We respect your privacy. We don’t sell your data. We don’t track you across the internet. We use minimal, privacy-respecting analytics to understand which studies are helpful so we can create more of them.

What We Collect

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how people use this site. This collects:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • General geographic region (country/city level)
  • Device type and browser
  • Referral source (how you found us)

This data is aggregated and anonymized. We use it to understand which studies are most helpful and to improve the site.

What We Don’t Collect

  • We don’t collect your name, email, or personal information (unless you voluntarily provide it)
  • We don’t use advertising trackers
  • We don’t sell or share data with third parties for marketing
  • We don’t use cookies for tracking (only essential site functionality)

Third-Party Services

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical information (IP addresses, browser type) as part of their hosting service. See GitHub’s privacy statement for details.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Use an ad blocker or privacy extension to opt out of analytics
  • Browse this site freely without providing any personal information
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Changes

If we update this policy, we’ll update the date at the top. No sneaky changes.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us at the contact information on our About page.
