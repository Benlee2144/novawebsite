ἀναχωρέω

anachōréō — G402

Transliteration: anachōréō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G402

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and G5562 (χωρέω);

Definition

to retire

KJV Translation

depart, give place, go (turn) aside, withdraw self
