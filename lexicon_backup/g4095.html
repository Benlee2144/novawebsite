πίνω

pínō — G4095

Transliteration: pínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4095

Derivation: a prolonged form of ; which (together with another form) ; occurs only as an alternate in certain tenses;

Definition

to imbibe (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

drink
