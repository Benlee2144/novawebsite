ὠδίν

ōdín — G5604

Transliteration: ōdín

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5604

Derivation: akin to G3601 (ὀδύνη);

Definition

a pang or throe, especially of childbirth

KJV Translation

pain, sorrow, travail

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

Included with: ὠδίς
