ʻêts — H6086

Transliteration: ʻêts

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6086

Derivation: from H6095 (עָצָה);

Definition

a tree (from its firmness); hence, wood (plural sticks)

KJV Translation

[phrase] carpenter, gallows, helve, [phrase] pine, plank, staff, stalk, stick, stock, timber, tree, wood.
