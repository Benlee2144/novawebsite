σφάζω

spházō — G4969

Transliteration: spházō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4969

Derivation: a primary verb;

Definition

to butcher (especially an animal for food or in sacrifice) or (generally) to slaughter, or (specially), to maim (violently)

KJV Translation

kill, slay, wound
