ὑπολείπω

hypoleípō — G5275

Transliteration: hypoleípō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5275

Derivation: from G5295 (ὑποτρέχω) and G3007 (λείπω);

Definition

to leave under (behind), i.e. (passively) to remain (survive)

KJV Translation

be left
