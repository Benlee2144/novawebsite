κακόω

kakóō — G2559

Transliteration: kakóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2559

Derivation: from G2556 (κακός);

Definition

to injure; figuratively, to exasperate

KJV Translation

make evil affected, entreat evil, harm, hurt, vex
