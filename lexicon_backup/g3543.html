νομίζω

nomízō — G3543

Transliteration: nomízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3543

Derivation: from G3551 (νόμος);

Definition

properly, to do by law (usage), i.e. to accustom (passively, be usual); by extension, to deem or regard

KJV Translation

suppose, thing, be wont
