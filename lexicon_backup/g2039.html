ἐργασία

ergasía — G2039

Transliteration: ergasía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2039

Derivation: from G2040 (ἐργάτης);

Definition

occupation; by implication, profit, pains

KJV Translation

craft, diligence, gain, work
