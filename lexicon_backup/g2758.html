Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2758

κενόω

kenóō — G2758

Transliteration: kenóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2758

Derivation: from G2756 (κενός);

Definition

to make empty, i.e. (figuratively) to abase, neutralize, falsify

KJV Translation

make (of none effect, of no reputation, void), be in vain
