καταχράομαι

katachráomai — G2710

Transliteration: katachráomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2710

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G5530 (χράομαι);

Definition

to overuse, i.e. misuse

KJV Translation

abuse
