שָׁנֶה

shâneh — H8141

Transliteration: shâneh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H8141

Derivation: (in plural or (feminine) שָׁנָה; from H8138 (שָׁנָה);

Definition

a year (as a revolution of time)

KJV Translation

[phrase] whole age, [idiom] long, [phrase] old, year([idiom] -ly).
