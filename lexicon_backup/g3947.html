παροξύνω

paroxýnō — G3947

Transliteration: paroxýnō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3947

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and a derivative of G3691 (ὀξύς);

Definition

to sharpen alongside, i.e. (figuratively) to exasperate

KJV Translation

easily provoke, stir
