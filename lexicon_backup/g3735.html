ὄρος

óros — G3735

Transliteration: óros

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3735

Derivation: probably from an obsolete (to rise or \

Definition

perhaps akin to G142 (αἴρω); compare G3733 (ὄρνις)); a mountain (as lifting itself above the plain)

KJV Translation

hill, mount(-ain)
