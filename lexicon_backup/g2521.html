κάθημαι

káthēmai — G2521

Transliteration: káthēmai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2521

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά);

Definition

and (to sit; akin to the base of G1476 (ἑδραῖος)); to sit down; figuratively, to remain, reside

KJV Translation

dwell, sit (by, down)
