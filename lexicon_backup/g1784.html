ἔντιμος

éntimos — G1784

Transliteration: éntimos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1784

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and G5092 (τιμή);

Definition

valued (figuratively)

KJV Translation

dear, more honourable, precious, in reputation

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
