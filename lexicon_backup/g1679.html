ἐλπίζω

elpízō — G1679

Transliteration: elpízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1679

Derivation: from G1680 (ἐλπίς);

Definition

to expect or confide

KJV Translation

(have, thing) hope(-d) (for), trust
