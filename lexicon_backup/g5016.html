ταραχή

tarachḗ — G5016

Transliteration: tarachḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5016

Derivation: feminine from G5015 (ταράσσω);

Definition

disturbance, i.e. (of water) roiling, or (of a mob) sedition

KJV Translation

trouble(-ing)
