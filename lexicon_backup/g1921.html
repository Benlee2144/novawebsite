ἐπιγινώσκω

epiginṓskō — G1921

Transliteration: epiginṓskō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1921

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G1097 (γινώσκω);

Definition

to know upon some mark, i.e. recognize; by implication, to become fully acquainted with, to acknowledge

KJV Translation

(ac-, have, take)know(-ledge, well), perceive
