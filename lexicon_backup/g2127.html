εὐλογέω

eulogéō — G2127

Transliteration: eulogéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2127

Derivation: from a compound of G2095 (εὖ) and G3056 (λόγος);

Definition

to speak well of, i.e. (religiously) to bless (thank or invoke a benediction upon, prosper)

KJV Translation

bless, praise
