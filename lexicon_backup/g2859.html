κόλπος

kólpos — G2859

Transliteration: kólpos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2859

Derivation: apparently a primary word;

Definition

the bosom; by analogy, a bay

KJV Translation

bosom, creek

__1. prop., the bosom: Jhn.13:23; fig., of close association, ἐν (εἰς τὸν) τοῖς κ. Ἀβραάμ, Luk.16:22-23 (cf. \2); ὁ ὢν εἰς τὸν κ. τ. πατρός, Jhn.1:18
