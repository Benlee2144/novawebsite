καθαίρω

kathaírō — G2508

Transliteration: kathaírō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2508

Derivation: from G2513 (καθαρός);

Definition

to cleanse, i.e. (specially) to prune; figuratively, to expiate

KJV Translation

purge
