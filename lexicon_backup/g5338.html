φέγγος

phéngos — G5338

Transliteration: phéngos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5338

Derivation: probably akin to the base of G5457 (φῶς) (compare G5350 (φθέγγομαι));

Definition

brilliancy

KJV Translation

light
