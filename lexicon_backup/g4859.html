σύμφωνος

sýmphōnos — G4859

Transliteration: sýmphōnos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4859

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G5456 (φωνή);

Definition

sounding together (alike), i.e. (figuratively) accordant (neuter as noun, agreement)

KJV Translation

consent
