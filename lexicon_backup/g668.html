ἀποφεύγω

apopheúgō — G668

Transliteration: apopheúgō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G668

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G5343 (φεύγω);

Definition

(figuratively) to escape

KJV Translation

escape
