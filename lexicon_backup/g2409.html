ἱερεύς

hiereús — G2409

Transliteration: hiereús

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2409

Derivation: from G2413 (ἱερός);

Definition

a priest (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

(high) priest
