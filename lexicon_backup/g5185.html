τυφλός

typhlós — G5185

Transliteration: typhlós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5185

Derivation: from, G5187 (τυφόω);

Definition

opaque (as if smoky), i.e. (by analogy) blind (physically or mentally)

KJV Translation

blind
