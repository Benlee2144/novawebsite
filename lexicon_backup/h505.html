אֶלֶף

ʼeleph — H505

Transliteration: ʼeleph

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H505

Derivation: prop, the same as H504 (אֶלֶף);

Definition

hence (the ox's head being the first letter of the alphabet, and this eventually used as a numeral) a thousand

KJV Translation

thousand.
