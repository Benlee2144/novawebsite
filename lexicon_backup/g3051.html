λόγιον

lógion — G3051

Transliteration: lógion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3051

Derivation: neuter of G3052 (λόγιος);

Definition

an utterance (of God)

KJV Translation

oracle

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

λόγ-ιον, τό,
oracle, especially one preserved from antiquity, [Refs 5th c.BC+]: more frequently in plural, oracles, [Refs 5th c.BC+] (the former being prose, the latter verse, accusative to [Refs 1st c.AD+]
__2 τὰ λ. Κυρίου the sayings of the Lord, [LXX+NT]
__II τὸ λ. τῶν κρίσεων the oracular breastplate worn by the Jewish High-Priest, [LXX+1st c.AD+]
