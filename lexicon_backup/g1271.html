διάνοια

diánoia — G1271

Transliteration: diánoia

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1271

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G3563 (νοῦς);

Definition

deep thought, properly, the faculty (mind or its disposition), by implication, its exercise

KJV Translation

imagination, mind, understanding
