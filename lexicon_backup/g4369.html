προστίθημι

prostíthēmi — G4369

Transliteration: prostíthēmi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4369

Derivation: from G4314 (πρός) and G5087 (τίθημι);

Definition

to place additionally, i.e. lay beside, annex, repeat

KJV Translation

add, again, give more, increase, lay unto, proceed further, speak to any more
