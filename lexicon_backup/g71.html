ἄγω

ágō — G71

Transliteration: ágō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G71

Derivation: a primary verb;

Definition

properly, to lead; by implication, to bring, drive, (reflexively) go, (specially) pass (time), or (figuratively) induce

KJV Translation

be, bring (forth), carry, (let) go, keep, lead away, be open

[in LXX for בּוא hi., לקח, נהג, etc. ;]
__1. to lead, bring, carry: with ace, before ἐπί, εἰς, ἕως, πρός and simple dative; metaphorically, to lead, guide, impel: Jhn.10:16 Rom.2:4, Heb.2:10, 2Ti.3:6, al.
