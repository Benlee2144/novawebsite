Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4049

περισπάω

perispáō — G4049

Transliteration: perispáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4049

Derivation: from G4012 (περί) and G4685 (σπάω);

Definition

to drag all around, i.e. (figuratively) to distract (with care)

KJV Translation

cumber
