Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4900

συνελαύνω

synelaúnō — G4900

Transliteration: synelaúnō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4900

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G1643 (ἐλαύνω);

Definition

to drive together, i.e. (figuratively) exhort (to reconciliation)

KJV Translation

+ set at one again
