Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1163

δεῖ

deî — G1163

Transliteration: deî

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1163

Derivation: 3rd person singular active present of G1210 (δέω);

Definition

also deon deh-on'; neuter active participle of the same; both used impersonally; it is (was, etc.) necessary (as binding)

KJV Translation

behoved, be meet, must (needs), (be) need(-ful), ought, should
