Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2041

ἔργον

érgon — G2041

Transliteration: érgon

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2041

Derivation: from a primary (but obsolete) (to work);

Definition

toil (as an effort or occupation); by implication, an act

KJV Translation

deed, doing, labour, work
