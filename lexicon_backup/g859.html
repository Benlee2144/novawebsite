ἄφεσις

áphesis — G859

Transliteration: áphesis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G859

Derivation: from G863 (ἀφίημι);

Definition

freedom; (figuratively) pardon

KJV Translation

deliverance, forgiveness, liberty, remission
