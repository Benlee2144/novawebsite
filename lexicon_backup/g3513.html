νή

nḗ — G3513

Transliteration: nḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3513

Derivation: probably an intensive form of G3483 (ναί);

Definition

a particle of attestation (accompanied by the object invoked or appealed to in confirmation); as sure as

KJV Translation

I protest by

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

νή,
