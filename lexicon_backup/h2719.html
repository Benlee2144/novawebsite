חֶרֶב

chereb — H2719

Transliteration: chereb

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2719

Derivation: from H2717 (חָרַב);

Definition

drought; also a cutting instrument (from its destructive effect), as a knife, sword, or other sharp implement

KJV Translation

axe, dagger, knife, mattock, sword, tool.
