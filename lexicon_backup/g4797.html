Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4797

συγχέω

synchéō — G4797

Transliteration: synchéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4797

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and (to pour) or its alternate;

Definition

to commingle promiscuously, i.e. (figuratively) to throw (an assembly) into disorder, to perplex (the mind)

KJV Translation

confound, confuse, stir up, be in an uproar
