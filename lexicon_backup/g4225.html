Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4225

πού

poú — G4225

Transliteration: poú

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4225

Derivation: genitive case of an indefinite pronoun (some) otherwise obsolete (compare G4214 (πόσος));

Definition

as adverb of place, somewhere, i.e. nearly

KJV Translation

about, a certain place

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

που,

(perhaps ποι) τῆς χώρας some part of the country, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II without reference to Place, in some degree, καί πού τι [Refs 5th c.BC+]: frequently to qualify an expression, perhaps, I suppose, [Refs 8th c.BC+]; added to introductory Particles, οὕτω π...
