ὅπως

hópōs — G3704

Transliteration: hópōs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3704

Derivation: from G3739 (ὅς) and G4459 (πῶς);

Definition

what(-ever) how, i.e. in the manner that (as adverb or conjunction of coincidence, intentional or actual)

KJV Translation

because, how, (so) that, to, when
