συναίρω

synaírō — G4868

Transliteration: synaírō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4868

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G142 (αἴρω);

Definition

to make up together, i.e. (figuratively) to compute (an account)

KJV Translation

reckon, take
