προσαγορεύω

prosagoreúō — G4316

Transliteration: prosagoreúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4316

Derivation: from G4314 (πρός) and a derivative of G58 (ἀγορά) (mean to harangue);

Definition

to address, i.e. salute by name

KJV Translation

call
