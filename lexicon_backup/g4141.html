Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4141

πλήσσω

plḗssō — G4141

Transliteration: plḗssō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4141

Derivation: apparently another form of G4111 (πλάσσω) (through the idea of flattening out);

Definition

to pound, i.e. (figuratively) to inflict with (calamity)

KJV Translation

smite
