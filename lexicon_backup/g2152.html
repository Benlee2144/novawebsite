εὐσεβής

eusebḗs — G2152

Transliteration: eusebḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2152

Derivation: from G2095 (εὖ) and G4576 (σέβομαι);

Definition

well-reverent, i.e. pious

KJV Translation

devout, godly

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
