ἀπωθέομαι

apōthéomai — G683

Transliteration: apōthéomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G683

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and the middle voice of or (to shove);

Definition

to push off, figuratively, to reject

KJV Translation

cast away, put away (from), thrust away (from)
