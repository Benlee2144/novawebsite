μιμνήσκω

mimnḗskō — G3403

Transliteration: mimnḗskō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3403

Derivation: a prolonged form of G3415 (μνάομαι) (from which some of the tenses are borrowed);

Definition

to remind, i.e. (middle voice) to recall to mind

KJV Translation

be mindful, remember
