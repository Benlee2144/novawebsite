Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G5214

ὑμνέω

hymnéō — G5214

Transliteration: hymnéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5214

Derivation: from G5215 (ὕμνος);

Definition

to hymn, i.e. sing a religious ode; by implication, to celebrate (God) in song

KJV Translation

sing a hymn (praise unto)
