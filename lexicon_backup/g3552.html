νοσέω

noséō — G3552

Transliteration: noséō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3552

Derivation: from G3554 (νόσος);

Definition

to be sick, i.e. (by implication, of a diseased appetite) to hanker after (figuratively, to harp upon)

KJV Translation

dote
