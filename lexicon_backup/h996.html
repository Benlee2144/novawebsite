Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H996

בֵּין

bêyn — H996

Transliteration: bêyn

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H996

Derivation: (sometimes in the plural masculine or feminine); properly, the constructive form of an otherwise unused noun from H995 (בִּין); a distinction; but used only as a preposition,

Definition

between (repeated before each noun, often with other particles); also as a conjunction, either...or

KJV Translation

among, asunder, at, between (-twixt...and), [phrase] from (the widest), [idiom] in, out of, whether (it be...or), within.
