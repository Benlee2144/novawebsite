ἐκδέχομαι

ekdéchomai — G1551

Transliteration: ekdéchomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1551

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G1209 (δέχομαι);

Definition

to accept from some source, i.e. (by implication) to await

KJV Translation

expect, look (tarry) for, wait (for)
