αἰώνιος

aiṓnios — G166

Transliteration: aiṓnios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G166

Derivation: from G165 (αἰών);

Definition

perpetual (also used of past time, or past and future as well)

KJV Translation

eternal, for ever, everlasting, world (began)

⚠ Our Analysis Differs

Αιώνιος means “age-lasting” or “pertaining to an age” — not “eternal.” This single mistranslation built the entire doctrine of eternal conscious torment. See our study.
