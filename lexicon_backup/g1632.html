ἐκχέω

ekchéō — G1632

Transliteration: ekchéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1632

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and (to pour);

Definition

to pour forth; figuratively, to bestow

KJV Translation

gush (pour) out, run greedily (out), shed (abroad, forth), spill
