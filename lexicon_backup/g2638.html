Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2638

καταλαμβάνω

katalambánō — G2638

Transliteration: katalambánō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2638

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G2983 (λαμβάνω);

Definition

to take eagerly, i.e. seize, possess, etc. (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

apprehend, attain, come upon, comprehend, find, obtain, perceive, (over-)take
