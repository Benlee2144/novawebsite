אַל

ʼal — H408

Transliteration: ʼal

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H408

Derivation: a negative particle (akin to H3808 (לֹא));

Definition

not (the qualified negation, used as a deprecative); once (Job 24:25) as a noun, nothing

KJV Translation

nay, neither, [phrase] never, no, nor, not, nothing (worth), rather than.
