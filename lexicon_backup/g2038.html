ἐργάζομαι

ergázomai — G2038

Transliteration: ergázomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2038

Derivation: middle voice from G2041 (ἔργον);

Definition

to toil (as a task, occupation, etc.), (by implication) effect, be engaged in or with, etc.

KJV Translation

commit, do, labor for, minister about, trade (by), work
