ἀρεστός

arestós — G701

Transliteration: arestós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G701

Derivation: from G700 (ἀρέσκω);

Definition

agreeable; by implication, fit

KJV Translation

(things that) please(-ing), reason

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀρεστός [ᾰ], ή, όν,
acceptable, pleasing, [Refs 7th c.BC+]; οἴνου παρέχειν ἀρεστόν a sufficient quantity, [Refs]; satisfactory, [Refs 2nd c.BC+]; of persons, acceptable, approved, τινί[Refs 5th c.BC+]adverb, ἑωυτῷ ἀρεστῶς quite to his own satisfaction, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
