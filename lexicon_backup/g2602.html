καταβολή

katabolḗ — G2602

Transliteration: katabolḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2602

Derivation: from G2598 (καταβάλλω);

Definition

a deposition, i.e. founding; figuratively, conception

KJV Translation

conceive, foundation
