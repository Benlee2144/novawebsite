Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H3680

כָּסָה

kâçâh — H3680

Transliteration: kâçâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3680

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

properly, to plump, i.e. fill up hollows; by implication, to cover (for clothing or secrecy)

KJV Translation

clad self, close, clothe, conceal, cover (self), (flee to) hide, overwhelm. Compare H3780 (כָּשָׂה).
