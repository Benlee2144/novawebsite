συνίημι

syníēmi — G4920

Transliteration: syníēmi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4920

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and (to send);

Definition

to put together, i.e. (mentally) to comprehend; by implication, to act piously

KJV Translation

consider, understand, be wise
