ἀποπνίγω

apopnígō — G638

Transliteration: apopnígō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G638

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G4155 (πνίγω);

Definition

to stifle (by drowning or overgrowth)

KJV Translation

choke
