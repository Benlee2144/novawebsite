ἡττάω

hēttáō — G2274

Transliteration: hēttáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2274

Derivation: from the same as G2276 (ἥττον);

Definition

to make worse, i.e. vanquish (literally or figuratively); by implication, to rate lower

KJV Translation

be inferior, overcome
