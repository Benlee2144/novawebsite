προτρέπομαι

protrépomai — G4389

Transliteration: protrépomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4389

Derivation: middle voice from G4253 (πρό) and the base of G5157 (τροπή);

Definition

to turn forward for oneself, i.e. encourage

KJV Translation

exhort
