ἐάω

eáō — G1439

Transliteration: eáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1439

Derivation: of uncertain affinity;

Definition

to let be, i.e. permit or leave alone

KJV Translation

commit, leave, let (alone), suffer
