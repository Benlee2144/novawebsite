Ἰουδαῖος

Ioudaîos — G2453

Transliteration: Ioudaîos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2453

Derivation: from G2448 (Ἰουδά) (in the sense of G2455 (Ἰούδας) as a country);

Definition

Judæan, i.e. belonging to Jehudah

KJV Translation

Jew(-ess), of Judæa

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

Ἰουδαῖος, ὁ,
a Jewish man: Ἰουδαία, a Jewish woman; ἡ Ἰουδαία (i.e. γῆ) “Judaea”: —Ἰουδα-ϊκός, ή, όν, “Jewish”, [LXX] adverb -κῶς [NT+1st c.AD+]
