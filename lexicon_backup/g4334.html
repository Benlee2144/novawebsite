προσέρχομαι

prosérchomai — G4334

Transliteration: prosérchomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4334

Derivation: from G4314 (πρός) and G2064 (ἔρχομαι) (including its alternate);

Definition

to approach, i.e. (literally) come near, visit, or (figuratively) worship, assent to

KJV Translation

(as soon as he) come (unto), come thereunto, consent, draw near, go (near, to, unto)
