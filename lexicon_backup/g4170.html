Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4170

πολεμέω

poleméō — G4170

Transliteration: poleméō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4170

Derivation: from G4171 (πόλεμος);

Definition

to be (engaged) in warfare, i.e. to battle (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

fight, (make) war
