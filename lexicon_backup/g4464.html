ῥάβδος

rhábdos — G4464

Transliteration: rhábdos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4464

Derivation: from the base of G4474 (ῥαπίζω);

Definition

a stick or wand (as a cudgel, a cane or a baton of royalty)

KJV Translation

rod, sceptre, staff
