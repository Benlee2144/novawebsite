ἀπαιτέω

apaitéō — G523

Transliteration: apaitéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G523

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G154 (αἰτέω);

Definition

to demand back

KJV Translation

ask again, require
