אֵם

ʼêm — H517

Transliteration: ʼêm

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H517

Derivation: a primitive word;

Definition

a mother (as the bond of the family); in a wide sense (both literally and figuratively (like father))

KJV Translation

dam, mother, [idiom] parting.
