Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4717

σταυρόω

stauróō — G4717

Transliteration: stauróō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4717

Derivation: from G4716 (σταυρός);

Definition

to impale on the cross; figuratively, to extinguish (subdue) passion or selfishness

KJV Translation

crucify
