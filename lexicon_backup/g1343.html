δικαιοσύνη

dikaiosýnē — G1343

Transliteration: dikaiosýnē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1343

Derivation: from G1342 (δίκαιος);

Definition

equity (of character or act); specially (Christian) justification

KJV Translation

righteousness

__2 fulfilment of the Law, [LXX+NT]

__IV [Refs 4th c.BC+] name for four, [Refs]
