ἀνταποδίδωμι

antapodídōmi — G467

Transliteration: antapodídōmi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G467

Derivation: from G473 (ἀντί) and G591 (ἀποδίδωμι);

Definition

to requite (good or evil)

KJV Translation

recompense, render, repay

ἀντ-απο-δίδωμι
(ἀντί ἀποδίδωμι), [in LXX for שָׁלַם pi., גָּמַל, שׁוּב hi., etc. ;]
