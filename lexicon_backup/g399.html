ἀναφέρω

anaphérō — G399

Transliteration: anaphérō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G399

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and G5342 (φέρω);

Definition

to take up (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

bear, bring (carry, lead) up, offer (up)
