ἀπιστία

apistía — G570

Transliteration: apistía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G570

Derivation: from G571 (ἄπιστος);

Definition

faithlessness, i.e. (negatively) disbelief (lack of Christian faith), or (positively) unfaithfulness (disobedience)

KJV Translation

unbelief

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

__2 of things, τὰ εἰρημένα ἐς ἀ. πολλὴν ἀπῖκται[Refs 5th c.BC+]; πολλὰς ἀπιστίας ἔχει it admits of many doubts, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ἀτοπία καὶ ἀπιστία incredibility, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II want of faith, faithlessness, θνήσκει δὲ πίστις βλαστάνει δ᾽ ἀ.[Refs 5th c.BC+]; treachery, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
