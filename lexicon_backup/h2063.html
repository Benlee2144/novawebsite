זֹאת

zôʼth — H2063

Transliteration: zôʼth

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2063

Derivation: irregular feminine of H2089 (זֶה);

Definition

this (often used adverb)

KJV Translation

hereby (-in, -with), it, likewise, the one (other, same), she, so (much), such (deed), that, therefore, these, this (thing), thus.
