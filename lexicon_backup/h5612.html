סֵפֶר

çêpher — H5612

Transliteration: çêpher

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5612

Derivation: or (feminine) סִפְרָה; (Psalm 56:8 (H9 (אֲבֵדָה))), from H5608 (סָפַר);

Definition

properly, writing (the art or a document); by implication, a book

KJV Translation

bill, book, evidence, [idiom] learn(-ed) (-ing), letter, register, scroll.
