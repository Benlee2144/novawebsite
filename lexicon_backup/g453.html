ἀνόητος

anóētos — G453

Transliteration: anóētos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G453

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G3539 (νοιέω);

Definition

unintelligent; by implication, sensual

KJV Translation

fool(-ish), unwise
