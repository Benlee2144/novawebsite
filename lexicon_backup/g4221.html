ποτήριον

potḗrion — G4221

Transliteration: potḗrion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4221

Derivation: neuter of a derivative of the alternate of G4095 (πίνω);

Definition

a drinking-vessel; by extension, the contents thereof, i.e. a cupful (draught); figuratively, a lot or fate

KJV Translation

cup
