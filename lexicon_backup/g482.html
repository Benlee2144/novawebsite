ἀντιλαμβάνομαι

antilambánomai — G482

Transliteration: antilambánomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G482

Derivation: from G473 (ἀντί) and the middle voice of G2983 (λαμβάνω);

Definition

to take hold of in turn, i.e. succor; also to participate

KJV Translation

help, partaker, support
