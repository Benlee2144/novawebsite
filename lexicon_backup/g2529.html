καθοράω

kathoráō — G2529

Transliteration: kathoráō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2529

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G3708 (ὁράω);

Definition

to behold fully, i.e. (figuratively) distinctly apprehend

KJV Translation

clearly see
