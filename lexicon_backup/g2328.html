θερμαίνω

thermaínō — G2328

Transliteration: thermaínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2328

Derivation: from G2329 (θέρμη);

Definition

to heat (oneself)

KJV Translation

(be) warm(-ed, self)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

__2 metaphorically, θ. φιλότατι νόον [Refs 5th c.BC+]; πολλὰ θερμαίνοι φρενί is probably falsa lectio for{π. θ. φρένα}, [Refs 4th c.BC+] —passive, κεναῖσιν ἐλπίσιν θερμαίνεται glows with hope, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; χαρᾷ θ. καρδίαν have one's heart warm with joy, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
