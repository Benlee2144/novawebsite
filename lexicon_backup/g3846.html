παραβάλλω

parabállō — G3846

Transliteration: parabállō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3846

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and G906 (βάλλω);

Definition

to throw alongside, i.e. (reflexively) to reach a place, or (figuratively) to liken

KJV Translation

arrive, compare
