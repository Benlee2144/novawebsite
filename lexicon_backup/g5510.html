χιών

chiṓn — G5510

Transliteration: chiṓn

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5510

Derivation: perhaps akin to the base of G5490 (χάσμα) (G5465 (χαλάω)) or G5494 (χειμών) (as descending or empty);

Definition

snow

KJV Translation

snow

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

χιών, όνος, ἡ:
(see. entry near the end):— snow, in [Refs 8th c.BC+] mostly of fallen snow, [Refs 8th c.BC+]; ἐπὶ χιόνι πεσούσῃ [Refs 5th c.BC+]; also of falling snow, ὥς τε νιφάδες χιόνος πίπτωσι θαμειαί thick fall the snow-flakes, [Refs 8th c.BC+]
__II snow-water, 'ice-coldwater, Θρῄκην χιόνι.. κατάρρυτον [Refs 5th c.BC+]; used to cool wine, εἰ χιών ἐστ᾽ ὠνία [Refs 5th c.BC+]: rare in plural, [Refs 4th c.BC+]. [ῐ by nature, ῑ Epic dialect for the sake of meter] (Cf. Sanskrit himás 'cold, winter', Latin hiems, Avest. zyam- 'winter', etc.)
