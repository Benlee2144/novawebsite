σφραγίς

sphragís — G4973

Transliteration: sphragís

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4973

Derivation: probably strengthened from G5420 (φράσσω);

Definition

a signet (as fencing in or protecting from misappropriation); by implication, the stamp impressed (as a mark of privacy, or genuineness), literally or figuratively

KJV Translation

seal
