ἐντρέπω

entrépō — G1788

Transliteration: entrépō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1788

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and the base of G5157 (τροπή);

Definition

to invert, i.e. (figuratively and reflexively) in a good sense, to respect; or in a bad one, to confound

KJV Translation

regard, (give) reference, shame
