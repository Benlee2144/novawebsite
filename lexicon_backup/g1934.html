ἐπιζητέω

epizētéō — G1934

Transliteration: epizētéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1934

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G2212 (ζητέω);

Definition

to search (inquire) for; intensively, to demand, to crave

KJV Translation

desire, enquire, seek (after, for)
