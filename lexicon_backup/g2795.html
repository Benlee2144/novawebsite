κινέω

kinéō — G2795

Transliteration: kinéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2795

Derivation: from (poetic for , to go);

Definition

to stir (transitively), literally or figuratively

KJV Translation

(re-)move(-r), way
