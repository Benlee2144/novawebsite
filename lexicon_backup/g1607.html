ἐκπορεύομαι

ekporeúomai — G1607

Transliteration: ekporeúomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1607

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G4198 (πορεύομαι);

Definition

to depart, be discharged, proceed, project

KJV Translation

come (forth, out of), depart, go (forth, out), issue, proceed (out of)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐκπορ-εύω,
make to go out, fetch out, [Refs 5th c.BC+]:—middle, with future middle [Refs 5th c.BC+] and aorist passive, go out or forth, march out, X.[same place], etc.; ἐπὶ λείαν [Refs 4th c.BC+]; εἰς στρατείαν ἐ. to march out to a place.., [Refs 2nd c.BC+]; but ἐκ τοῦ χάρακος [LXX+5th c.BC+]
