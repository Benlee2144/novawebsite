λοιποῦ

loipoû — G3064

Transliteration: loipoû

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3064

Derivation: genitive case singular of the same as G3062 (λοιποί);

Definition

remaining time

KJV Translation

from henceforth
