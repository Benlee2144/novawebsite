Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H3671

כָּנָף

kânâph — H3671

Transliteration: kânâph

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3671

Derivation: from H3670 (כָּנַף);

Definition

an edge or extremity; specifically (of a bird or army) a wing, (of a garment or bedclothing) a flap, (of the earth) a quarter, (of a building) a pinnacle

KJV Translation

[phrase] bird, border, corner, end, feather(-ed), [idiom] flying, [phrase] (one an-) other, overspreading, [idiom] quarters, skirt, [idiom] sort, uttermost part, wing(-ed).
