θαυμάσιος

thaumásios — G2297

Transliteration: thaumásios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2297

Derivation: from G2295 (θαῦμα);

Definition

wondrous, i.e. (neuter as noun) a miracle

KJV Translation

wonderful thing
