ἀγορά

agorá — G58

Transliteration: agorá

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G58

Derivation: from (to gather;

Definition

probably akin to G1453 (ἐγείρω)); properly, the town-square (as a place of public resort); by implication, a market or thoroughfare

KJV Translation

market(-place), street
