πηγή

pēgḗ — G4077

Transliteration: pēgḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4077

Derivation: probably from G4078 (πήγνυμι) (through the idea of gushing plumply);

Definition

a fount (literally or figuratively), i.e. source or supply (of water, blood, enjoyment) (not necessarily the original spring)

KJV Translation

fountain, well
