ὑπαντάω

hypantáō — G5221

Transliteration: hypantáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5221

Derivation: from G5259 (ὑπό) and a derivative of G473 (ἀντί);

Definition

to go opposite (meet) under (quietly), i.e. to encounter, fall in with

KJV Translation

(go to) meet
