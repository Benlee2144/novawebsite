ἐπίγνωσις

epígnōsis — G1922

Transliteration: epígnōsis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1922

Derivation: from G1921 (ἐπιγινώσκω);

Definition

recognition, i.e. (by implication) full discernment, acknowledgement

KJV Translation

(ac-)knowledge(-ing, - ment)
