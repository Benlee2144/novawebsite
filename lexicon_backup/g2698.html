κατατίθημι

katatíthēmi — G2698

Transliteration: katatíthēmi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2698

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G5087 (τίθημι);

Definition

to place down, i.e. deposit (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

do, lay, shew
