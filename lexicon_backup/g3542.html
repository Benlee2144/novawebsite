Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3542

νομή

nomḗ — G3542

Transliteration: nomḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3542

Derivation: feminine from the same as G3551 (νόμος);

Definition

pasture, i.e. (the act) feeding (figuratively, spreading of a gangrene), or (the food) pasturage

KJV Translation

X eat, pasture
