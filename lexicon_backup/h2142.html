Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H2142

זָכַר

zâkar — H2142

Transliteration: zâkar

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2142

Derivation: a primitive root; also as denominative from H2145 (זָכָר)

Definition

properly, to mark (so as to be recognized), i.e. to remember; by implication, to mention; to be male

KJV Translation

[idiom] burn (incense), [idiom] earnestly, be male, (make) mention (of), be mindful, recount, record(-er), remember, make to be remembered, bring (call, come, keep, put) to (in) remembrance, [idiom] still, think on, [idiom] well.
