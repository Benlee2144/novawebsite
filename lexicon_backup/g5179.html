τύπος

týpos — G5179

Transliteration: týpos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5179

Derivation: from G5180 (τύπτω);

Definition

a die (as struck), i.e. (by implication) a stamp or scar; by analogy, a shape, i.e. a statue, (figuratively) style or resemblance; specially, a sampler (\

KJV Translation

en-(ex-)ample, fashion, figure, form, manner, pattern, print
