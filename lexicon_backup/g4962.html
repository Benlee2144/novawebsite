συστρέφω

systréphō — G4962

Transliteration: systréphō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4962

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G4762 (στρέφω);

Definition

to twist together, i.e. collect (a bundle, a crowd)

KJV Translation

gather
