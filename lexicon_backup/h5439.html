סָבִיב

çâbîyb — H5439

Transliteration: çâbîyb

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5439

Derivation: or (feminine) סְבִיבָה; from H5437 (סָבַב);

Definition

(as noun) a circle, neighbour, or environs; but chiefly (as adverb, with or without preposition) around

KJV Translation

(place, round) about, circuit, compass, on every side.
