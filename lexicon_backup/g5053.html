τελευτάω

teleutáō — G5053

Transliteration: teleutáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5053

Derivation: from a presumed derivative of G5055 (τελέω);

Definition

to finish life (by implication, of G979 (βίος)), i.e. expire (demise)

KJV Translation

be dead, decease, die
