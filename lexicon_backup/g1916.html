ἐπιβοάω

epiboáō — G1916

Transliteration: epiboáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1916

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G994 (βοάω);

Definition

to exclaim against

KJV Translation

cry

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

ἐπι-βοάω, -ῶ
to cry out: Act.25:24 (Rec., for βοάω, which see).†
(AS)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

__2. utter or sing aloud over, [Refs 6th c.BC+]; shriek out besides, [Refs 5th c.BC+].
