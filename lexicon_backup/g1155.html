δανείζω

daneízō — G1155

Transliteration: daneízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1155

Derivation: from G1156 (δάνειον);

Definition

to loan on interest; reflexively, to borrow

KJV Translation

borrow, lend
