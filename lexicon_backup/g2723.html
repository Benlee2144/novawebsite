κατηγορέω

katēgoréō — G2723

Transliteration: katēgoréō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2723

Derivation: from G2725 (κατήγορος);

Definition

to be a plaintiff, i.e. to charge with some offence

KJV Translation

accuse, object

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
