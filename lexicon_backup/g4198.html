πορεύομαι

poreúomai — G4198

Transliteration: poreúomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4198

Derivation: middle voice from a derivative of the same as G3984 (πεῖρα);

Definition

to traverse, i.e. travel (literally or figuratively; especially to remove (figuratively, die), live, etc.);

KJV Translation

--depart, go (away, forth, one's way, up), (make a, take a) journey, walk
