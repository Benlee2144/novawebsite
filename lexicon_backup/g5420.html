φράσσω

phrássō — G5420

Transliteration: phrássō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5420

Derivation: apparently a strengthening form of the base of G5424 (φρήν);

Definition

to fence or inclose, i.e. (specially), to block up (figuratively, to silence)

KJV Translation

stop
