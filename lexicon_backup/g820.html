ἄτιμος

átimos — G820

Transliteration: átimos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G820

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and G5092 (τιμή);

Definition

(negatively) unhonoured or (positively) dishonoured

KJV Translation

despised, without honour, less honourable (comparative degree)
