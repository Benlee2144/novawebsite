καλύπτω

kalýptō — G2572

Transliteration: kalýptō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2572

Derivation: akin to G2813 (κλέπτω) and G2928 (κρύπτω);

Definition

to cover up (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

cover, hide
