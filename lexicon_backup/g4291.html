Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4291

προΐστημι

proḯstēmi — G4291

Transliteration: proḯstēmi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4291

Derivation: from G4253 (πρό) and G2476 (ἵστημι);

Definition

to stand before, i.e. (in rank) to preside, or (by implication) to practise

KJV Translation

maintain, be over, rule
