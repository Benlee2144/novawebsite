Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2901

κραταιόω

krataióō — G2901

Transliteration: krataióō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2901

Derivation: from G2900 (κραταιός);

Definition

to empower, i.e. (passively) increase in vigor

KJV Translation

be strengthened, be (wax) strong
