διαμάχομαι

diamáchomai — G1264

Transliteration: diamáchomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1264

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G3164 (μάχομαι);

Definition

to fight fiercely (in altercation)

KJV Translation

strive

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

διαμᾰχ-ομαι,

__2 exert oneself, strive earnestly, περὶ τούτου ὅπως.. [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__3 in argument, contend or maintain that.., with accusative et infinitive, [Refs]: with a negative, δ. τι μὴ εἶναι [Refs 5th c.BC+]; also δ. τι carry a point, [Refs]
