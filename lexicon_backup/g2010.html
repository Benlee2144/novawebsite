ἐπιτρέπω

epitrépō — G2010

Transliteration: epitrépō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2010

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and the base of G5157 (τροπή);

Definition

to turn over (transfer), i.e. allow

KJV Translation

give leave (liberty, license), let, permit, suffer
