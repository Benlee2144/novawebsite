δόκιμος

dókimos — G1384

Transliteration: dókimos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1384

Derivation: from G1380 (δοκέω);

Definition

properly, acceptable (current after assayal), i.e. approved

KJV Translation

approved, tried
