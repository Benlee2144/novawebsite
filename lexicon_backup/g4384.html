Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4384

προτάσσω

protássō — G4384

Transliteration: protássō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4384

Derivation: from G4253 (πρό) and G5021 (τάσσω);

Definition

to pre-arrange, i.e. prescribe

KJV Translation

before appoint

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

προ-τάσσω
[in LXX: \2 R (Α, προστ-) * ;]
__1. to place in front.
__2. to arrange beforehand: Act.17:26, Rec. (for προστ., Edd.).†
(AS)
