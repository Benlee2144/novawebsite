ἀσκέω

askéō — G778

Transliteration: askéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G778

Derivation: probably from the same as G4632 (σκεῦος);

Definition

to elaborate, i.e. (figuratively) train (by implication, strive)

KJV Translation

exercise
