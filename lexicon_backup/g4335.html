προσευχή

proseuchḗ — G4335

Transliteration: proseuchḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4335

Derivation: from G4336 (προσεύχομαι);

Definition

prayer (worship); by implication, an oratory (chapel)

KJV Translation

X pray earnestly, prayer

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

προσευχή, ἡ,
prayer, οἶκος προσευχῆς, of the Temple, [LXX+3rd c.AD+]
__II place of prayer, sanctuary, chapel, [Refs]; especially among the Jews. synagogue, [NT+3rd c.BC+]
