Πέτρος

Pétros — G4074

Transliteration: Pétros

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4074

Derivation: apparently a primary word;

Definition

a (piece of) rock (larger than G3037 (λίθος)); as a name, Petrus, an apostle

KJV Translation

Peter, rock

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

πέτρος, ὁ
