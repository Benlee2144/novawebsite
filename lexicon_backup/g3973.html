παύω

paúō — G3973

Transliteration: paúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3973

Derivation: a primary verb (\

Definition

to stop (transitively or intransitively), i.e. restrain, quit, desist, come to an end

KJV Translation

cease, leave, refrain
