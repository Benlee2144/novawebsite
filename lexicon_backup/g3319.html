μέσος

mésos — G3319

Transliteration: mésos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3319

Derivation: from G3326 (μετά);

Definition

middle (as an adjective or (neuter) noun)

KJV Translation

among, X before them, between, + forth, mid(-day, -night), midst, way
