ἐπιμέλεια

epiméleia — G1958

Transliteration: epiméleia

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1958

Derivation: from G1959 (ἐπιμελέομαι);

Definition

carefulness, i.e. kind attention (hospitality)
