ἀναμάρτητος

anamártētos — G361

Transliteration: anamártētos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G361

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G264 (ἁμαρτάνω);

Definition

sinless

KJV Translation

that is without sin
