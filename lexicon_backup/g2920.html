κρίσις

krísis — G2920

Transliteration: krísis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2920

Definition

decision (subjectively or objectively, for or against); by extension, a tribunal; by implication, justice (especially, divine law)

KJV Translation

accusation, condemnation, damnation, judgment
