πιστός

pistós — G4103

Transliteration: pistós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4103

Derivation: from G3982 (πείθω);

Definition

objectively, trustworthy; subjectively, trustful

KJV Translation

believe(-ing, -r), faithful(-ly), sure, true
