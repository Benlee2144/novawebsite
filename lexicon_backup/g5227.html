ὑπεναντίος

hypenantíos — G5227

Transliteration: hypenantíos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5227

Derivation: from G5259 (ὑπό) and G1727 (ἐναντίος);

Definition

under (covertly) contrary to, i.e. opposed or (as noun) an opponent

KJV Translation

adversary, against
