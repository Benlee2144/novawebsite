Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H4720

מִקְדָּשׁ

miqdâsh — H4720

Transliteration: miqdâsh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4720

Derivation: or מִקְּדָשׁ; (Exodus 15:17), from H6942 (קָדַשׁ);

Definition

a consecrated thing or place, especially, a palace, sanctuary (whether of Jehovah or of idols) or asylum

KJV Translation

chapel, hallowed part, holy place, sanctuary.
