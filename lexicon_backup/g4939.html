Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4939

σύντροφος

sýntrophos — G4939

Transliteration: sýntrophos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4939

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G5162 (τροφός) (in a passive sense);

Definition

a fellow-nursling, i.e. comrade

KJV Translation

brought up with
