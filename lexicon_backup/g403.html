ἀνάψυξις

anápsyxis — G403

Transliteration: anápsyxis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G403

Derivation: from G404 (ἀναψύχω);

Definition

properly, a recovery of breath, i.e. (figuratively) revival

KJV Translation
