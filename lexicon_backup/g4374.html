προσφέρω

prosphérō — G4374

Transliteration: prosphérō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4374

Derivation: from G4314 (πρός) and G5342 (φέρω) (including its alternate);

Definition

to bear towards, i.e. lead to, tender (especially to God), treat

KJV Translation

bring (to, unto), deal with, do, offer (unto, up), present unto, put to
