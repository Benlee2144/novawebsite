σπείρω

speírō — G4687

Transliteration: speírō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4687

Derivation: probably strengthened from G4685 (σπάω) (through the idea of extending);

Definition

to scatter, i.e. sow (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

sow(- er), receive seed
