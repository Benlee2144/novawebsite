στεφανόω

stephanóō — G4737

Transliteration: stephanóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4737

Derivation: from G4735 (στέφανος);

Definition

to adorn with an honorary wreath (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

crown
