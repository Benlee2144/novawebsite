διάβολος

diábolos — G1228

Transliteration: diábolos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1228

Derivation: from G1225 (διαβάλλω);

Definition

a traducer; specially, Satan (compare H07854)

KJV Translation

false accuser, devil, slanderer

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

διάβολ-ος, ον,
slanderous, backbiting, γραῦς [Refs 4th c.BC+]: superlative -ώτατος[Refs 5th c.BC+]; διάβολόν τι, aliquid invidiae, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II substantive, slanderer, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; enemy, [LXX]: hence, = Sâtân,[Refs]; the Devil, [NT]
__III adverb -λως injuriously, invidiously, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
