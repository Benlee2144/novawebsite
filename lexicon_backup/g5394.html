φλογίζω

phlogízō — G5394

Transliteration: phlogízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5394

Derivation: from G5395 (φλόξ);

Definition

to cause a blaze, i.e. ignite (figuratively, to inflame with passion)

KJV Translation

set on fire
