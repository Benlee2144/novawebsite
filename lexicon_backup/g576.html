ἀποβαίνω

apobaínō — G576

Transliteration: apobaínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G576

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and the base of G939 (βάσις);

Definition

literally, to disembark; figuratively, to eventuate

KJV Translation

become, go out, turn
