κρεῖσσον

kreîsson — G2908

Transliteration: kreîsson

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2908

Derivation: neuter of an alternate form of G2909 (κρείττων);

Definition

(as noun) better, i.e. greater advantage

KJV Translation

better
