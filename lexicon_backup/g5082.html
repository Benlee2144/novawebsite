τηλικοῦτος

tēlikoûtos — G5082

Transliteration: tēlikoûtos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5082

Derivation: from a compound of G3588 (ὁ) with G2245 (ἡλίκος) and G3778 (οὗτος);

Definition

such as this, i.e. (in (figurative) magnitude) so vast

KJV Translation

so great, so mighty
