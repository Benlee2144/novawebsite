אַךְ

ʼak — H389

Transliteration: ʼak

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H389

Derivation: akin to H403 (אָכֵן);

Definition

a particle of affirmation, surely; hence (by limitation) only

KJV Translation

also, in any wise, at least, but, certainly, even, howbeit, nevertheless, notwithstanding, only, save, surely, of a surety, truly, verily, [phrase] wherefore, yet (but).
