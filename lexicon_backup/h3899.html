לֶחֶם

lechem — H3899

Transliteration: lechem

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3899

Derivation: from H3898 (לָחַם); See also H1036 (בֵּית לְעַפְרָה)

Definition

food (for man or beast), especially bread, or grain (for making it)

KJV Translation

(shew-) bread, [idiom] eat, food, fruit, loaf, meat, victuals.
