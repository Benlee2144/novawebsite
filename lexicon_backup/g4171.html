πόλεμος

pólemos — G4171

Transliteration: pólemos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4171

Derivation: from (to bustle);

Definition

warfare (literally or figuratively; a single encounter or a series)

KJV Translation

battle, fight, war
