πρόθεσις

próthesis — G4286

Transliteration: próthesis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4286

Derivation: from G4388 (προτίθεμαι);

Definition

a setting forth, i.e. (figuratively) proposal (intention); specially, the show-bread (in the Temple) as exposed before God

KJV Translation

purpose, shew(-bread)
