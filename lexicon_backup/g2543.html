καίτοι

kaítoi — G2543

Transliteration: kaítoi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2543

Derivation: from G2532 (καί) and G5104 (τοί);

Definition

and yet, i.e. nevertheless

KJV Translation

although

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

καίτοι
(= καί τοι, and so also written in cl.)
concessive particle, [in LXX: \2 * ;]
and yet, although: Act.14:17; with ptcp., Heb.4:3.†
(AS)
