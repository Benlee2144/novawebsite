κεφαλή

kephalḗ — G2776

Transliteration: kephalḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2776

Derivation: from the primary (in the sense of seizing);

Definition

the head (as the part most readily taken hold of), literally or figuratively

KJV Translation

head
