βούλομαι

boúlomai — G1014

Transliteration: boúlomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1014

Derivation: middle voice of a primary verb;

Definition

to \

KJV Translation

be disposed, minded, intend, list, (be, of own) will (-ing)
