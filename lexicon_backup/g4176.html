πολιτεύομαι

politeúomai — G4176

Transliteration: politeúomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4176

Derivation: middle voice of a derivative of G4177 (πολίτης);

Definition

to behave as a citizen (figuratively)

KJV Translation

let conversation be, live
