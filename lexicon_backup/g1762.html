ἔνι

éni — G1762

Transliteration: éni

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1762

Derivation: contraction for the third person singular present indicative of G1751 (ἔνειμι);

Definition

impersonally, there is in or among

KJV Translation

be, (there) is

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἔνειμι

__b with dative plural, to be among, [NT+6th c.BC+]
__c with adverb loci, οἴκοι ἔνεστι γόος [Refs 8th c.BC+]; ἔνεστιν αὐτόθι is in this very place, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 absolutely, to be present in a place, οἶνος ἐνέην [Refs 8th c.BC+]; οὐδ᾽ ἄνδρες νηῶν ἔνι τέκτονες[Refs 4th c.BC+]; σίτου οὐκ ἐνόντος as there was no corn there, [
