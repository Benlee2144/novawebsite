Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4243

πρεσβεύω

presbeúō — G4243

Transliteration: presbeúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4243

Derivation: from the base of G4245 (πρεσβύτερος);

Definition

to be a senior, i.e. (by implication) act as a representative (figuratively, preacher)

KJV Translation

be an ambassador
