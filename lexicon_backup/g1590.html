ἐκλύω

eklýō — G1590

Transliteration: eklýō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1590

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G3089 (λύω);

Definition

to relax (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

faint
