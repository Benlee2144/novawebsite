ἐμβάλλω

embállō — G1685

Transliteration: embállō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1685

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and G906 (βάλλω);

Definition

to throw on, i.e. (figuratively) subject to (eternal punishment)

KJV Translation

cast into
