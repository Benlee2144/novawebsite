μερίς

merís — G3310

Transliteration: merís

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3310

Derivation: feminine of G3313 (μέρος);

Definition

a portion, i.e. province, share or (abstractly) participation

KJV Translation

part (X -akers)
