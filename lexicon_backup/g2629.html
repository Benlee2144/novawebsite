κατακόπτω

katakóptō — G2629

Transliteration: katakóptō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2629

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G2875 (κόπτω);

Definition

to chop down, i.e. mangle

KJV Translation

cut
