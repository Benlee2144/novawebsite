Παῦλος

Paûlos — G3972

Transliteration: Paûlos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3972

Derivation: of Latin origin;

Definition

(little; but remotely from a derivative of G3973 (παύω), meaning the same); Paulus, the name of a Roman and of an apostle

KJV Translation

Paul, Paulus
