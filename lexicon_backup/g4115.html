πλατύνω

platýnō — G4115

Transliteration: platýnō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4115

Derivation: from G4116 (πλατύς);

Definition

to widen (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

make broad, enlarge

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

__3 pronounce broadly, τὴν φωνήν [Refs 1st c.AD+]
__4 amplify, τὸν λόγον [Refs 1st c.BC+]:—passive, διήγησις πλατύνεται τρόποις [Refs]: absolutely, use amplification, [Refs 1st c.BC+]
