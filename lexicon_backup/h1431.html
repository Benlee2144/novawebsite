Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H1431

גָּדַל

gâdal — H1431

Transliteration: gâdal

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1431

Derivation: a primitive root; properly, to twist (compare H1434 (גְּדִל)), i.e.

Definition

to be (causatively make) large (in various senses, as in body, mind, estate or honor, also in pride)

KJV Translation

advance, boast, bring up, exceed, excellent, be(-come, do, give, make, wax), great(-er, come to... estate, [phrase] things), grow(up), increase, lift up, magnify(-ifical), be much set by, nourish (up), pass, promote, proudly (spoken), tower.
