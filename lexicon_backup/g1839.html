ἐξίστημι

exístēmi — G1839

Transliteration: exístēmi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1839

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G2476 (ἵστημι);

Definition

to put (stand) out of wits, i.e. astound, or (reflexively) become astounded, insane

KJV Translation

amaze, be (make) astonished, be beside self (selves), bewitch, wonder
