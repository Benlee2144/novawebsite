ζημία

zēmía — G2209

Transliteration: zēmía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2209

Derivation: probably akin to the base of G1150 (δαμάζω) (through the idea of violence);

Definition

detriment

KJV Translation

damage, loss
