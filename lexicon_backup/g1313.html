διάφορος

diáphoros — G1313

Transliteration: diáphoros

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1313

Derivation: from G1308 (διαφέρω);

Definition

varying; also surpassing

KJV Translation

differing, divers, more excellent
