ὁμολογία

homología — G3671

Transliteration: homología

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3671

Derivation: from the same as G3670 (ὁμολογέω);

Definition

acknowledgment

KJV Translation

con- (pro-)fession, professed

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ὁμολογ-ία,
Ionic dialect -ιη, ἡ, agreement, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; αἱ τῶν ὀνομάτων ὁ. verbal consistency, [Refs]
__2 assent, admission, concession, τῶν ἐπικαλουμένων [Refs 5th c.BC+]; κατὰ τὴν ἐμὴν ὁ. by my admission, [Refs]; ἐξ ὁ. διαλέγεσθαι argue from premises agreed upon or granted, [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__3 agreement, compact, συνθήκη καὶ ὁ. [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__3.b especially in war, terms of peace, truce, or surrender, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ὁμολογίῃ ἐχρήσαντο, of the conquered, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ἐς ὁμολογίην προκαλέεσθαι, of the conquerors, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__3.c in Law, contract, agreement, συγγραφὴ καὶ ὁ. [Refs 3rd c.BC+]; ὁ. τινὸς πρός τινα [Refs 1st c.AD+]
__4 vow, [LXX]
__5 in Stoic Philos., conformity with nature, summum.. bonum, cum positum sit in eo quod ὁμολογίαν Stoici, nos appellemus convenientiam, [Refs 1st c.BC+]
