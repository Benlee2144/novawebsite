μετεωρίζω

meteōrízō — G3349

Transliteration: meteōrízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3349

Derivation: from a compound of G3326 (μετά) and a collateral form of G142 (αἴρω) or perhaps rather G109 (ἀήρ) (compare \

Definition

to raise in mid-air, i.e. (figuratively) suspend (passively, fluctuate or be anxious)

KJV Translation

be of doubtful mind
