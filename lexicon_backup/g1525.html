εἰσέρχομαι

eisérchomai — G1525

Transliteration: eisérchomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1525

Derivation: from G1519 (εἰς) and G2064 (ἔρχομαι);

Definition

to enter (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

X arise, come (in, into), enter in(-to), go in (through)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
