ἀποδέχομαι

apodéchomai — G588

Transliteration: apodéchomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G588

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G1209 (δέχομαι);

Definition

to take fully, i.e. welcome (persons), approve (things)

KJV Translation

accept, receive (gladly)
