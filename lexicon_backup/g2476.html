ἵστημι

hístēmi — G2476

Transliteration: hístēmi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2476

Derivation: a prolonged form of a primary (of the same meaning, and used for it in certain tenses);

Definition

to stand (transitively or intransitively), used in various applications (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

abide, appoint, bring, continue, covenant, establish, hold up, lay, present, set (up), stanch, stand (by, forth, still, up)
