ἤπιος

ḗpios — G2261

Transliteration: ḗpios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2261

Derivation: probably from G2031 (ἔπος);

Definition

properly, affable, i.e. mild or kind

KJV Translation

gentle
