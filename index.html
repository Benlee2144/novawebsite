Go Deeper

Most English translations compress thousands of years of meaning into a handful of familiar words. The Living Word takes you back to the source—the original Hebrew, Aramaic, and Greek texts—to uncover what Scripture actually says, word by word.

Every study includes the original language text with transliteration, Strong’s concordance numbers, historical context showing how meaning has shifted over centuries, cross-referenced verses, and deep exposition that treats the Bible as the living document it is.
