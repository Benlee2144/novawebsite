προσκόπτω

proskóptō — G4350

Transliteration: proskóptō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4350

Derivation: from G4314 (πρός) and G2875 (κόπτω);

Definition

to strike at, i.e. surge against (as water); specially, to stub on, i.e. trip up (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

beat upon, dash, stumble (at)
