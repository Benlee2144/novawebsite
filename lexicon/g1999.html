ἐπισύστασις

episýstasis — G1999

Transliteration: episýstasis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1999

Derivation: from the middle voice of a compound of G1909 (ἐπί) and G4921 (συνιστάω);

Definition

a conspiracy, i.e. concourse (riotous or friendly)

KJV Translation

that which cometh upon, + raising up
