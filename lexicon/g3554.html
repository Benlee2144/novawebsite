νόσος

nósos — G3554

Transliteration: nósos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3554

Derivation: of uncertain affinity;

Definition

a malady (rarely figuratively, of moral disability)

KJV Translation

disease, infirmity, sickness
