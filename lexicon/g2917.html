κρίμα

kríma — G2917

Transliteration: kríma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2917

Derivation: from G2919 (κρίνω);

Definition

a decision (the function or the effect, for or against (\

KJV Translation

avenge, condemned, condemnation, damnation, + go to law, judgment
