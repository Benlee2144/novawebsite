Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4046

περιποιέομαι

peripoiéomai — G4046

Transliteration: peripoiéomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4046

Derivation: middle voice from G4012 (περί) and G4160 (ποιέω);

Definition

to make around oneself, i.e. acquire (buy)

KJV Translation

purchase
