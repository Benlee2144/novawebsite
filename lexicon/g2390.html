ἰάομαι

iáomai — G2390

Transliteration: iáomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2390

Derivation: middle voice of apparently a primary verb;

Definition

to cure (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

heal, make whole
