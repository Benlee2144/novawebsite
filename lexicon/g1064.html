γαστήρ

gastḗr — G1064

Transliteration: gastḗr

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1064

Derivation: of uncertain derivation;

Definition

the stomach; by analogy, the matrix; figuratively, a gourmand

KJV Translation

belly, + with child, womb
