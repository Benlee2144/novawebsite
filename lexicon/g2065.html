ἐρωτάω

erōtáō — G2065

Transliteration: erōtáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2065

Derivation: apparently from G2046 (ἐρέω) (compare G2045 (ἐρευνάω));

Definition

to interrogate; by implication, to request

KJV Translation

ask, beseech, desire, intreat, pray
