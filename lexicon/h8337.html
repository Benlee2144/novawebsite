שֵׁשׁ

shêsh — H8337

Transliteration: shêsh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H8337

Derivation: masculine שִׁשָּׁה; a primitive number; compare H7797 (שׂוּשׂ)

Definition

six (as an overplus beyond five or the fingers of the hand); as ord. sixth

KJV Translation

six(-teen, -teenth), sixth.
