Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G830

αὐθαίρετος

authaíretos — G830

Transliteration: authaíretos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G830

Derivation: from G846 (αὐτός) and the same as G140 (αἱρετίζω);

Definition

self-chosen, i.e. (by implication) voluntary

KJV Translation

of own accord, willing of self
