Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H2821

חָשַׁךְ

châshak — H2821

Transliteration: châshak

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2821

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to be dark (as withholding light); transitively, to darken

KJV Translation

be black, be (make) dark, darken, cause darkness, be dim, hide.

Appears In (12 verses)

Amos 5:8Ecclesiastes 12:2Isaiah 13:10Isaiah 5:30Jeremiah 13:16Job 18:6Job 3:9Job 38:2Lamentations 4:8Lamentations 5:17Psalms 139:12Psalms 69:23

1) to be or become dark, grow dim, be darkened, be black, be hidden
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to be or grow dark
1a2) to have a dark colour
1a3) to grow dim
1b) (Hiphil)
1b1) to make dark, cause to be dark
1b2) to hide, conceal
