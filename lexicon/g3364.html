Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3364

οὐ μή

ou mḗ — G3364

Transliteration: ou mḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3364

Derivation: i.e. G3756 (οὐ) and G3361 (μή);

Definition

a double negative strengthening the denial; not at all

KJV Translation

any more, at all, by any (no) means, neither, never, no (at all), in no case (wise), nor ever, not (at all, in any wise)

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

never, not ever, a double negative strengthening the denial; not at all, surely not
