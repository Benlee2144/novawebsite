Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G158

αἴτιον

aítion — G158

Transliteration: aítion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G158

Derivation: neuter of G159 (αἴτιος);

Definition

a reason or crime (like G156 (αἰτία))

KJV Translation

cause, fault

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

αἴτιον, -ου, τό, see: αἴτιος.
αἴτιος, -α, ὁ
(αἰτία), [in LXX: 1Ki.22:22 (סבב) Dan LXX Bel 41, TH ib. 42, Su 53, \2, \2 * ;]
__1. causative of, responsible for; as subst., ὁ αἰ., the cause, author: Heb.5:9; τὸ αἰ., the cause, Act.19:40.
__2. blameworthy, culpable; as subst., ὁ αἰ., the culprit, the accused (Lat. reus); τὸ αἰ. (= αἰτία, 3), the crime, Luk.23:4,14,22.
† (AS)
