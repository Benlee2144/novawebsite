Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4689

σπένδω

spéndō — G4689

Transliteration: spéndō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4689

Derivation: apparently a primary verb;

Definition

to pour out as a libation, i.e. (figuratively) to devote (one's life or blood, as a sacrifice) (\

KJV Translation

(be ready to) be offered

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

σπένδω
[in LXX chiefly for נָסַךְ hi. ;]
to pour out as a drink-offering, make a libation; pass., figuratively, σπένδομαι, I am poured out or offered as a libation (in the shedding of my life-blood): Php.2:17 (see Lft., in l), 2Ti.4:6 (for exx. from π., see Milligan, Selections, 114 f.; MM, xxiii).†
(AS)
