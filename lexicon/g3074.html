λύκος

lýkos — G3074

Transliteration: lýkos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3074

Derivation: perhaps akin to the base of G3022 (λευκός) (from the whitish hair);

Definition

a wolf

KJV Translation

wolf

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
