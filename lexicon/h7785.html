שׁוֹק

shôwq — H7785

Transliteration: shôwq

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7785

Derivation: from H7783 (שׁוּק);

Definition

the (lower) leg (as a runner)

KJV Translation

hip, leg, shoulder, thigh.

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

1) leg, thigh
1a) of man; specifically the lower leg (calf) as opposed to the thigh
1b) of sacrificial animal; specifically the thigh, upper leg, hind leg, as the portion eaten
Aramaic equivalent: shaq (שָׁק "leg" H8243)
