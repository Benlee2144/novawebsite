תְּנוּפָה

tᵉnûwphâh — H8573

Transliteration: tᵉnûwphâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H8573

Derivation: from H5130 (נוּף);

Definition

a brandishing (in threat); by implication, tumult; specifically, the official undulation of sacrificial offerings

KJV Translation

offering, shaking, wave (offering).
