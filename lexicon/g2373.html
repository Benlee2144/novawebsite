Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2373

θυμόω

thymóō — G2373

Transliteration: thymóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2373

Derivation: from G2372 (θυμός);

Definition

to put in a passion, i.e. enrage

KJV Translation

be wroth
