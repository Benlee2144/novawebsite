בָּלַע

bâlaʻ — H1104

Transliteration: bâlaʻ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1104

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to make away with (specifically by swallowing); generally, to destroy

KJV Translation

cover, destroy, devour, eat up, be at end, spend up, swallow down (up).
