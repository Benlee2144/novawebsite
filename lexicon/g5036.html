ταχύς

tachýs — G5036

Transliteration: tachýs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5036

Derivation: of uncertain affinity;

Definition

fleet, i.e. (figuratively) prompt or ready

KJV Translation

swift
