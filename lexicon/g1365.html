Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1365

διστάζω

distázō — G1365

Transliteration: distázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1365

Derivation: from G1364 (δίς);

Definition

properly, to duplicate, i.e. (mentally) to waver (in opinion)

KJV Translation

doubt
