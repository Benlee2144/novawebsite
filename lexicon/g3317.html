μεσονύκτιον

mesonýktion — G3317

Transliteration: mesonýktion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3317

Derivation: neuter of compound of G3319 (μέσος) and G3571 (νύξ);

Definition

midnight (especially as a watch)

KJV Translation

midnight

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

μεσο-νύκτιος, ον,
of or at midnight, ἔκλειψις [Refs 5th c.BC+]l.7(6).5; μ. ὠλλύμαν [Refs 5th c.BC+]: neuter as adverb, [Refs 3rd c.BC+]: as substantive μεσο-νύκτιον, τό, [NT+5th c.BC+]at midnight, [NT]; μεσονύκτου seems to be falsa lectio in [Refs 4th c.BC+]:—the spelling μεσᾰνύκτιον is variant in [NT+3rd c.AD+]
