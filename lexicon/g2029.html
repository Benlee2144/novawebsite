ἐποπτεύω

epopteúō — G2029

Transliteration: epopteúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2029

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and a derivative of G3700 (ὀπτάνομαι);

Definition

to inspect, i.e. watch

KJV Translation

behold

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐποπτ-εύω,
(ἐπόπτης) overlook, watch, of an overseer, ἔργα τ᾽ ἐποπτεύεσκε [Refs 8th c.BC+]; ὁ πάντ᾽ ἐ. τάδε ἥλιος[Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 visit, punish, κότῳ [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__3 absolutely, keep watch, οἱ περὶ τοὺς νόμους ἐποπτεύοντες [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II become an ἐπόπτης, be admitted to the highest gkade at the mysteries, [Refs 1st c.AD+]: with accusative, view as an ἐπόπτης, [Refs 5th c.BC+]: proverbial, of attaining to the highest earthly happiness, ἐποπτεύειν δοκῶ [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__III study, meditate on, with dative, καθαρῇσιν ἐ. μελέτῃσιν [Refs 5th c.BC+]
