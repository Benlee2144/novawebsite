Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1553

ἐκδημέω

ekdēméō — G1553

Transliteration: ekdēméō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1553

Derivation: from a compound of G1537 (ἐκ) and G1218 (δῆμος);

Definition

to emigrate, i.e. (figuratively) vacate or quit

KJV Translation

be absent

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐκδημ-έω,
to be abroad, to be on one's travels, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; to be in exile, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II with accusative, travel through, δύσιν καὶ ἀνατολήν [Refs]
