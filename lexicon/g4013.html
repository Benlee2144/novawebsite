περιάγω

periágō — G4013

Transliteration: periágō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4013

Derivation: from G4012 (περί) and G71 (ἄγω);

Definition

to take around (as a companion); reflexively, to walk around

KJV Translation

compass, go (round) about, lead about
