πρόφασις

próphasis — G4392

Transliteration: próphasis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4392

Derivation: from a compound of G4253 (πρό) and G5316 (φαίνω);

Definition

an outward showing, i.e. pretext

KJV Translation

cloke, colour, pretence, show

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

πρόφᾰσ-ις, εως, ἡ,
