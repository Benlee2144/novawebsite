ἁρμός

harmós — G719

Transliteration: harmós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G719

Derivation: from the same as G716 (ἅρμα);

Definition

an articulation (of the body)

KJV Translation

joint

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἁρμός, ὁ,
(ἀραρίσκω) joint, in masonry, [Refs 3rd c.BC+]; ὁ προσιών, ἀπιὼν ἁ., front and back faces of blocks, [Refs], compare 22.463.40: plural, fastenings of a door, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ἁ. χώματος λιθοσπαδής a fissure in the tomb made by tearing away the stones at their joining, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; chink in the fitting of a door, ἁρμῷ τὴν ὄψιν προσβαλεῖν[Refs 1st c.BC+]
__2 bolt, peg, ἁ. ἐν ξύλῳ παγείς[Refs 5th c.BC+]
__3 shoulder-joint, [Refs]
