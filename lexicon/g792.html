ἀστήρ

astḗr — G792

Transliteration: astḗr

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G792

Derivation: probably from the base of G4766 (στρώννυμι);

Definition

a star (as strown over the sky), literally or figuratively

KJV Translation

star

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
