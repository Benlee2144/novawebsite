Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H3730

כַּפְתֹּר

kaphtôr — H3730

Transliteration: kaphtôr

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3730

Derivation: or (Amos 9:1) כַּפְתּוֹר; probably from an unused root meaning to encircle;

Definition

a chaplet; but used only in an architectonic sense, i.e. the capital of acolumn, or a wreath-like button or disk on the candelabrum

KJV Translation

knop, (upper) lintel.
