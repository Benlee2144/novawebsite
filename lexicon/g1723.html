ἐναγκαλίζομαι

enankalízomai — G1723

Transliteration: enankalízomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1723

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and a derivative of G43 (ἀγκάλη);

Definition

to take in one's arms, i.e. embrace

KJV Translation

take up in arms
