συνήδομαι

synḗdomai — G4913

Transliteration: synḗdomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4913

Derivation: middle voice from G4862 (σύν) and the base of G2237 (ἡδονή);

Definition

to rejoice in with oneself, i.e. feel satisfaction concerning

KJV Translation

delight
