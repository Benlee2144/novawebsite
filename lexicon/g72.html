ἀγωγή

agōgḗ — G72

Transliteration: agōgḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G72

Derivation: reduplicated from G71 (ἄγω);

Definition

a bringing up, i.e. mode of living

KJV Translation

manner of life
