Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2360

θροέω

throéō — G2360

Transliteration: throéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2360

Derivation: from to wail;

Definition

to clamor, i.e. (by implication) to frighten

KJV Translation

trouble
