זַבְדִּיאֵל

Zabdîyʼêl — H2068

Transliteration: Zabdîyʼêl

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2068

Derivation: from H2065 (זֶבֶד) and H410 (אֵל);

Definition

gift of God; zabdiel, the name of two Israelites

KJV Translation

Zabdiel.

Appears In (2 verses)

1 Chronicles 27:2Nehemiah 11:14

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at Neh.11.14;
son of: Haggedolim (H1419B)
§ Zabdiel = "God is my endowment"
1) father of Jashobeam, the chief of David's guard
2) a priest in Nehemiah's time
