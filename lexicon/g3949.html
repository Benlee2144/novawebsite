παροργίζω

parorgízō — G3949

Transliteration: parorgízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3949

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and G3710 (ὀργίζω);

Definition

to anger alongside, i.e. enrage

KJV Translation

anger, provoke to wrath
