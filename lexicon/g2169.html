εὐχαριστία

eucharistía — G2169

Transliteration: eucharistía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2169

Derivation: from G2170 (εὐχάριστος);

Definition

gratitude; actively, grateful language (to God, as an act of worship)

KJV Translation

thankfulness, (giving of) thanks(-giving)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
