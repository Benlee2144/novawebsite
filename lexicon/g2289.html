Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2289

θανατόω

thanatóō — G2289

Transliteration: thanatóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2289

Derivation: from G2288 (θάνατος) to kill (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

become dead, (cause to be) put to death, kill, mortify
