Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2419

Ἱερουσαλήμ

Hierousalḗm — G2419

Transliteration: Hierousalḗm

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2419

Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H03389);

Definition

Hierusalem (i.e. Jerushalem), the capitol of Palestine

KJV Translation

Jerusalem
