εὔχρηστος

eúchrēstos — G2173

Transliteration: eúchrēstos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2173

Derivation: from G2095 (εὖ) and G5543 (χρηστός);

Definition

easily used, i.e. useful

KJV Translation

profitable, meet for use

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

εὔχρηστ-ος, ον,
