θυγάτηρ

thygátēr — G2364

Transliteration: thygátēr

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2364

Derivation: apparently a primary word (compare \

Definition

a female child, or (by Hebraism) descendant (or inhabitant)

KJV Translation

daughter
