διασκορπίζω

diaskorpízō — G1287

Transliteration: diaskorpízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1287

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G4650 (σκορπίζω);

Definition

to dissipate, i.e. (genitive case) to rout or separate; specially, to winnow; figuratively, to squander

KJV Translation

disperse, scatter (abroad), strew, waste
