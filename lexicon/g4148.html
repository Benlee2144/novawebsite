πλουτίζω

ploutízō — G4148

Transliteration: ploutízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4148

Derivation: from G4149 (πλοῦτος);

Definition

to make wealthy (figuratively)

KJV Translation

en- (make) rich
