Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4815

συλλαμβάνω

syllambánō — G4815

Transliteration: syllambánō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4815

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G2983 (λαμβάνω);

Definition

to clasp, i.e. seize (arrest, capture); specially, to conceive (literally or figuratively); by implication, to aid

KJV Translation

catch, conceive, help, take
