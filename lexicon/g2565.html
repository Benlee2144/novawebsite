καλλιέλαιος

kalliélaios — G2565

Transliteration: kalliélaios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2565

Derivation: from the base of G2566 (καλλίον) and G1636 (ἐλαία);

Definition

a cultivated olive tree, i.e. a domesticated or improved one

KJV Translation

good olive tree

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

καλλῐ-έλαιος, ἡ,
garden olive, opposed to ἀγριέλαιος, [NT]:—feminine καλλῐ-ελαία, ἡ, [Refs 3rd c.AD+]: as adjective, κ. ἐλαία [Refs 3rd c.BC+]
