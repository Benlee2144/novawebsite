πραγματεύομαι

pragmateúomai — G4231

Transliteration: pragmateúomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4231

Derivation: from G4229 (πρᾶγμα);

Definition

to busy oneself with, i.e. to trade

KJV Translation

occupy
