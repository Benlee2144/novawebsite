Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1811

ἐξακολουθέω

exakolouthéō — G1811

Transliteration: exakolouthéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1811

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G190 (ἀκολουθέω);

Definition

to follow out, i.e. (figuratively) to imitate, obey, yield to

KJV Translation

follow
