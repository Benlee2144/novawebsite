νεωκόρος

neōkóros — G3511

Transliteration: neōkóros

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3511

Derivation: from a form of G3485 (ναός) and (to sweep);

Definition

a temple-servant, i.e. (by implication) a votary

KJV Translation

worshipper
