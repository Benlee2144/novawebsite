יָשׁוּב

Yâshûwb — H3437

Transliteration: Yâshûwb

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3437

Derivation: or יָשִׁיב; from H7725 (שׁוּב); he will return;

Definition

Jashub, the name of two Israelites

KJV Translation

Jashub.

Appears In (2 verses)

1 Chronicles 7:1Ezra 10:29

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

A man of the tribe of Issachar living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, first mentioned at Gen.46.13;
son of: Issachar (H3485);
brother of: Tola (H8439), Puah (H6312) and Shimron (H8110B); also called Yob (KJV: Job) at Gen.46.13;
§ Jashub = "he will return"
1) the 3rd son of Issachar and founder of the family of Jashubites
2) one of the sons of Bani who had to put away a foreign wife in the time of Ezra
