תָּבוּן

tâbûwn — H8394

Transliteration: tâbûwn

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H8394

Derivation: and (feminine) תְּבוּנָה; or תּוֹבֻנָה; from H995 (בִּין);

Definition

intelligence; by implication, an argument; by extension, caprice

KJV Translation

discretion, reason, skilfulness, understanding, wisdom.
