ἀετός

aetós — G105

Transliteration: aetós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G105

Derivation: from the same as G109 (ἀήρ);

Definition

an eagle (from its wind-like flight)

KJV Translation

eagle

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀετός,
