ἀδελφή

adelphḗ — G79

Transliteration: adelphḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G79

Derivation: feminine of G80 (ἀδελφός);

Definition

a sister (naturally or ecclesiastically)

KJV Translation

sister

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

ἀδελφή, -ῆς, ἡ
(ἀδελφός), [in LXX for אָהוֹת ;]
a sister: Mat.19:29, al.; metaphorically (MM, VGT, see word), of a member of the Christian community: Rom.16:1, 1Co.7:15 Jas.21:15, al.
(AS)

__2 kinswoman, [LXX]
__3 term of endearment, [LXX]; applied to a wife, [Refs 3rd c.BC+]:—sister (as a fellow Christian), [NT]
