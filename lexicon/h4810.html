מְרִי בַעַל

Mᵉrîy Baʻal — H4810

Transliteration: Mᵉrîy Baʻal

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4810

Derivation: from H4805 (מְרִי) and H1168 (בַּעַל); rebellion of (i.e. against) Baal;

Definition

Meri-Baal, an epithet of Gideon

KJV Translation

Meri-baal. Compare H4807 (מְרִיב בַּעַל).

Appears In (1 verse)
