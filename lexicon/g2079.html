ἐσχάτως

eschátōs — G2079

Transliteration: eschátōs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2079

Derivation: adverb from G2078 (ἔσχατος);

Definition

finally, i.e. (with G2192 (ἔχω)) at the extremity of life

KJV Translation

point of death

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

ἐσχάτως
adv.,
extremely, utterly; ἐ. ἔχειν (= Lat. in extremis esse), only in late writers (cf. ἐν ἐσχάτοις εἶναι, FlJ, Ant., ix, 8, 6), to be at the point of death: Mrk.5:28.†
(AS)
