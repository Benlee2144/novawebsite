ὁποῖος

hopoîos — G3697

Transliteration: hopoîos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3697

Derivation: from G3739 (ὅς) and G4169 (ποῖος);

Definition

of what kind that, i.e. how (as) great (excellent) (specially, as an indefinite correlative to the definite antecedent G5108 (τοιοῦτος) of quality)

KJV Translation

what manner (sort) of, such as whatsoever
