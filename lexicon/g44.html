ἄγκιστρον

ánkistron — G44

Transliteration: ánkistron

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G44

Derivation: from the same as G43 (ἀγκάλη);

Definition

a hook (as bent)

KJV Translation

hook

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἄγκιστρον, τό,
(ἄγκος) fish-hook, [Refs 8th c.BC+]; hook of a spindle, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; surgical instrument, [Refs 3rd c.AD+]; generally, hook, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]

Matthew 17:27
