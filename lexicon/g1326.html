διεγείρω

diegeírō — G1326

Transliteration: diegeírō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1326

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G1453 (ἐγείρω);

Definition

to wake fully; i.e. arouse (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

arise, awake, raise, stir up
