προσφορά

prosphorá — G4376

Transliteration: prosphorá

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4376

Derivation: from G4374 (προσφέρω);

Definition

presentation; concretely, an oblation (bloodless) or sacrifice

KJV Translation

offering (up)
