πόρνη

pórnē — G4204

Transliteration: pórnē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4204

Derivation: feminine of G4205 (πόρνος);

Definition

a strumpet; figuratively, an idolater

KJV Translation

harlot, whore

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

πόρν-η, ἡ,
harlot, prostitute, [Refs 7th c.BC+] because Greek prostitutes were commonly bought slaves.)
