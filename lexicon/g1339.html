διΐστημι

diḯstēmi — G1339

Transliteration: diḯstēmi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1339

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G2476 (ἵστημι);

Definition

to stand apart, i.e. (reflexively) to remove, intervene

KJV Translation

go further, be parted, after the space of
