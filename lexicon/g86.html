ᾅδης

háidēs — G86

Transliteration: háidēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G86

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as negative particle) and G1492 (εἴδω);

Definition

properly, unseen, i.e. \

KJV Translation

grave, hell

⚠ Our Analysis Differs

Hades is the Greek equivalent of Hebrew Sheol — the unseen realm of the dead, not a place of eternal punishment. Translating it as “hell” imports theology that the original word does not carry. See our study.
