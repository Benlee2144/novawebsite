ἀπέχομαι

apéchomai — G567

Transliteration: apéchomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G567

Derivation: middle voice (reflexively) of G568 (ἀπέχω);

Definition

to hold oneself off, i.e. refrain

KJV Translation

abstain

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

refrain, receive/be far, keep away from
