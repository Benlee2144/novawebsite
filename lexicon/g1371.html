Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1371

διχοτομέω

dichotoméō — G1371

Transliteration: dichotoméō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1371

Derivation: from a compound of a derivative of G1364 (δίς) and a derivative of (to cut);

Definition

to bisect, i.e. (by extension) to flog severely

KJV Translation

cut asunder (in sunder)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
