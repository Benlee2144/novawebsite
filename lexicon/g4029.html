περίκειμαι

períkeimai — G4029

Transliteration: períkeimai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4029

Derivation: from G4012 (περί) and G2749 (κεῖμαι);

Definition

to lie all around, i.e. inclose, encircle, hamper (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

be bound (compassed) with, hang about
