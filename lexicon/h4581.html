מָעוֹז

mâʻôwz — H4581

Transliteration: mâʻôwz

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4581

Derivation: (also מָעוּז ); or מָעֹז (also מָעֻז; from H5810 (עָזַז);

Definition

a fortified place; figuratively, a defence

KJV Translation

force, fort(-ress), rock, strength(-en), ([idiom] most) strong (hold).
