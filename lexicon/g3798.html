ὄψιος

ópsios — G3798

Transliteration: ópsios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3798

Derivation: from G3796 (ὀψέ);

Definition

late; feminine (as noun) afternoon (early eve) or nightfall (later eve)

KJV Translation

even(-ing, (-tide))

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ὀψία,
Ionic dialect -ιη (i.e. ὥρα), ἡ, the latter part of day, evening, opposed to ὄρθρος, frequently joined with δείλη (which see), μέχρι δείλης ὀψίης [Refs 5th c.BC+]; δείλης ὀ. late in the evening, [Refs 4th c.BC+]; ὀψίας alone, [Refs 2nd c.AD+] —Cf. ὄψιος.
