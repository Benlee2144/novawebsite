σεμνότης

semnótēs — G4587

Transliteration: semnótēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4587

Derivation: from G4586 (σεμνός);

Definition

venerableness, i.e. probity

KJV Translation

gravity, honesty

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

σεμνό-της, ητος, ἡ,
solemnity, dignity, σεμνότητ᾽ ἔχει σκότος [Refs 5th c.BC+]; [τῆς λέξεως] [Refs 4th c.BC+]; ἡ τοῦ τόπου σ. [Refs]; also of persons, seriousness, dignity, ἐπὶ τῆς σ. αὐθάδεις ὑπολαμβάνεσθαι [NT+5th c.BC+]
__II of a girl, reserve or shyness, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
