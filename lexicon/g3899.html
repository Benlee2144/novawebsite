Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3899

παραπορεύομαι

paraporeúomai — G3899

Transliteration: paraporeúomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3899

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and G4198 (πορεύομαι);

Definition

to travel near

KJV Translation

go, pass (by)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

παραπορεύομαι,
with future middle and aorist passive, go beside or alongside, [Refs 4th c.BC+]; of παιδαγωγοί, [Refs 1st c.BC+] accompanied the meal, [Refs 3rd c.BC+]
__II go past, pass by, [Refs 3rd c.BC+]; τὸν χάρακα [NT+2nd c.BC+]; of stars, pass through the zodiac, [Refs]
