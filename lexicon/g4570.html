σβέννυμι

sbénnymi — G4570

Transliteration: sbénnymi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4570

Derivation: a prolonged form of an apparently primary verb;

Definition

to extinguish (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

go out, quench
