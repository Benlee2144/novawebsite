גִּבּוֹר

gibbôwr — H1368

Transliteration: gibbôwr

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1368

Derivation: or גִּבֹּר; (shortened) intensive from the same as H1397 (גֶּבֶר);

Definition

powerful; by implication, warrior, tyrant

KJV Translation

champion, chief, [idiom] excel, giant, man, mighty (man, one), strong (man), valiant man.
