ὠτίον

ōtíon — G5621

Transliteration: ōtíon

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5621

Derivation: diminutive of G3775 (οὖς);

Definition

an earlet, i.e. one of the ears, or perhaps the lobe of the ear

KJV Translation

ear
