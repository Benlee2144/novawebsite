ἐξέλκω

exélkō — G1828

Transliteration: exélkō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1828

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G1670 (ἑλκύω);

Definition

to drag forth, i.e. (figuratively) to entice (to sin)

KJV Translation

draw away
