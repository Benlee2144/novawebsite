σκοτόω

skotóō — G4656

Transliteration: skotóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4656

Derivation: from G4655 (σκότος);

Definition

to obscure or blind (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

be full of darkness
