ἀπομάσσομαι

apomássomai — G631

Transliteration: apomássomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G631

Derivation: middle voice from G575 (ἀπό) and (to squeeze, knead, smear);

Definition

to scrape away

KJV Translation

wipe off
