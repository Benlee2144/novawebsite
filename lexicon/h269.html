אָחוֹת

ʼâchôwth — H269

Transliteration: ʼâchôwth

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H269

Derivation: irregular feminine of H251 (אָח);

Definition

a sister (used very widely (like brother), literally and figuratively)

KJV Translation

(an-) other, sister, together.
