Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H269

אָחוֹת

ʼâchôwth — H269

Transliteration: ʼâchôwth

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H269

Derivation: irregular feminine of H251 (אָח);

Definition

a sister (used very widely (like brother), literally and figuratively)

KJV Translation

(an-) other, sister, together.

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

1) sister

1c) relative
1c1) (metaph) of Israel's and Judah's relationship
1d) beloved
1d1) bride
1e) (fig.) of intimate connection
1f) another
