אוּלַי

ʼûwlay — H194

Transliteration: ʼûwlay

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H194

Derivation: or (shortened) אֻלַי; from H176 (אוֹ);

Definition

if not; hence perhaps

KJV Translation

if so be, may be, peradventure, unless.
