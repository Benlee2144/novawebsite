bûwç — H947

Transliteration: bûwç

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H947

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to trample (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

loath, tread (down, under (foot)), be polluted.
