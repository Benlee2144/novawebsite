ὑπηρέτης

hypērétēs — G5257

Transliteration: hypērétēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5257

Derivation: from G5259 (ὑπό) and a derivative of (to row);

Definition

an under-oarsman, i.e. (generally) subordinate (assistant, sexton, constable)

KJV Translation

minister, officer, servant

(ὑπό + ἐρέτης, a rower) [in LXX: Pro.14:35 (עֶבֶד), \2, al. ;]
