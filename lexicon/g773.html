Ἀσία

Asía — G773

Transliteration: Asía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G773

Derivation: of uncertain derivation;

Definition

Asia, i.e. Asia Minor, or (usually) only its western shore

KJV Translation

Asia
