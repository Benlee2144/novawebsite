Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3867

παραινέω

parainéō — G3867

Transliteration: parainéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3867

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and G134 (αἰνέω);

Definition

to mispraise, i.e. recommend or advise (a different course)

KJV Translation

admonish, exhort

παρ-αινέω, -ω
[in LXX: 2Ma.7:25-26, 3Ma.5:17 3Mac 7:12 * ;]
to exhort, advise: with accusative of person(s) and inf. (see Bl., § 72, 5; M, Pr., 205), Act.27:22; absol., before λέγων, Act.27:9.†
