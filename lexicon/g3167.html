μεγαλεῖος

megaleîos — G3167

Transliteration: megaleîos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3167

Derivation: from G3173 (μέγας);

Definition

magnificent, i.e. (neuter, plural as noun) a conspicuous favor, or (subjectively) perfection

KJV Translation

great things, wonderful works
