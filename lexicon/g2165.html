εὐφραίνω

euphraínō — G2165

Transliteration: euphraínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2165

Derivation: from G2095 (εὖ) and G5424 (φρήν);

Definition

to put (middle voice or passively, be) in a good frame of mind, i.e. rejoice

KJV Translation

fare, make glad, be (make) merry, rejoice
