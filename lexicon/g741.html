ἀρτύω

artýō — G741

Transliteration: artýō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G741

Derivation: from a presumed derivative of G142 (αἴρω);

Definition

to prepare, i.e. spice (with stimulating condiments)

KJV Translation

season
