Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4468

ῥᾳδιουργία

rhaidiourgía — G4468

Transliteration: rhaidiourgía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4468

Derivation: from the same as G4467 (ῥᾳδιούργημα);

Definition

recklessness, i.e. (by extension) malignity

KJV Translation

mischief

__II knavery, [Refs 3rd c.BC+]; δόλος καὶ ῥ. [NT]; of historians, [Refs 2nd c.BC+]; fraud, [Refs 1st c.AD+]
