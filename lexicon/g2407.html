Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2407

ἱερατεύω

hierateúō — G2407

Transliteration: hierateúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2407

Derivation: prolongation from G2409 (ἱερεύς);

Definition

to be a priest, i.e. perform his functions

KJV Translation

execute the priest's office

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἱερᾱτ-εύω,
Ionic dialect ἱερητ- [Refs] (later also in Northern Greece, Boeotian dialect, [Refs 6th c.BC+]; Lesb. ἰρητεύω [Refs]:—to be priest or priestess, θεῶν [Refs 2nd c.BC+]; τοῦ Διὸς τοῦ Σωτῆρος [NT+4th c.BC+]:—also ἱερᾱτ-εύομαι, [Refs]
__II passive, to be made holy, Zos.Alch.p.108B.

Appears In (1 verse)

Luke 1:8
