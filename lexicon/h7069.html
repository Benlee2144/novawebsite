Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H7069

קָנָה

qânâh — H7069

Transliteration: qânâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7069

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to erect, i.e. create; by extension, to procure, especially by purchase (causatively, sell); by implication to own

KJV Translation

attain, buy(-er), teach to keep cattle, get, provoke to jealousy, possess(-or), purchase, recover, redeem, [idiom] surely, [idiom] verily.
