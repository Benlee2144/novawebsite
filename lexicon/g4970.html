σφόδρα

sphódra — G4970

Transliteration: sphódra

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4970

Derivation: neuter plural of (violent;

Definition

of uncertain derivation) as adverb; vehemently, i.e. in a high degree, much

KJV Translation

exceeding(-ly), greatly, sore, very
