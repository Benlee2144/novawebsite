ἐπισκοπέω

episkopéō — G1983

Transliteration: episkopéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1983

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G4648 (σκοπέω);

Definition

to oversee; by implication, to beware

KJV Translation

look diligently, take the oversight

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

ἐπι-σκοπέω, -ῶ
[in LXX for פָּקַד ni., etc. ;]
__1. to look upon, observe, examine: before μή, Heb.12:16.
__2. As ἐπισκέπτομαι in LXX, NT, to visit, care for: 1Pe.5:2 (R, txt.; WH cm.; Cremer, 527).†
(AS)
