כֹּה

kôh — H3541

Transliteration: kôh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3541

Derivation: from the prefix k and H1931 (הוּא);

Definition

properly, like this, i.e. by implication, (of manner) thus (or so); also (of place) here (or hither); or (of time) now

KJV Translation

also, here, + hitherto, like, on the other side, so (and much), such, on that manner, (on) this (manner, side, way, way and that way), + mean while, yonder.

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

1) thus, here, in this manner
1a) thus, so
1b) here, here and there
1c) until now, until now...until then, meanwhile
Aramaic equivalent: kah (כָּה "thus" H3542)
