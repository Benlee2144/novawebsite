μετοικίζω

metoikízō — G3351

Transliteration: metoikízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3351

Derivation: from the same as G3350 (μετοικεσία);

Definition

to transfer as a settler or captive, i.e colonize or exile

KJV Translation

carry away, remove into
