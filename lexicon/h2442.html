Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H2442

חָכָה

châkâh — H2442

Transliteration: châkâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2442

Derivation: a primitive root (apparently akin to H2707 (חָקָה) through the idea of piercing);

Definition

properly, to adhere to; hence, to await

KJV Translation

long, tarry, wait.

Appears In (7 verses)

2 Kings 9:3Habakkuk 2:3Isaiah 30:18Job 32:4Psalms 106:13Psalms 33:20Zephaniah 3:8
