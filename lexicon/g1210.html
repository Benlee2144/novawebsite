Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1210

δέω

déō — G1210

Transliteration: déō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1210

Derivation: a primary verb;

Definition

to bind (in various applications, literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

bind, be in bonds, knit, tie, wind
