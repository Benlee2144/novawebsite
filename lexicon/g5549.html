Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G5549

χρονίζω

chronízō — G5549

Transliteration: chronízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5549

Derivation: from G5550 (χρόνος);

Definition

to take time, i.e. linger

KJV Translation

delay, tarry

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

χρονίζω
(χρόνος), [in LXX chiefly for אָחַר pi. ;]
to spend or take time, to tarry, linger, delay: Mat.24:48 25:5, Heb.10:37; before ἐν, with dative loc., Luk.1:21; with inf., Luk.12:45.†
(AS)
