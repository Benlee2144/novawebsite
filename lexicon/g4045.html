περιπίπτω

peripíptō — G4045

Transliteration: peripíptō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4045

Derivation: from G4012 (περί) and G4098 (πίπτω);

Definition

to fall into something that is all around, i.e. light among or upon, be surrounded with

KJV Translation

fall among (into)
