ἐνέδρα

enédra — G1747

Transliteration: enédra

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1747

Derivation: feminine from G1722 (ἐν) and the base of G1476 (ἑδραῖος);

Definition

an ambuscade, i.e. (figuratively) murderous purpose

KJV Translation

lay wait

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
