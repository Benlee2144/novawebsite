καταλείπω

kataleípō — G2641

Transliteration: kataleípō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2641

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G3007 (λείπω);

Definition

to leave down, i.e. behind; by implication, to abandon, have remaining

KJV Translation

forsake, leave, reserve

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
