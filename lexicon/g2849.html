κολάζω

kolázō — G2849

Transliteration: kolázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2849

Derivation: from (dwarf);

Definition

properly, to curtail, i.e. (figuratively) to chastise (or reserve for infliction)

KJV Translation

punish
