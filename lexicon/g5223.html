ὕπαρξις

hýparxis — G5223

Transliteration: hýparxis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5223

Derivation: from G5225 (ὑπάρχω);

Definition

existency or proprietorship, i.e. (concretely) property, wealth

KJV Translation

goods, substance

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
