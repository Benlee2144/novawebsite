Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1018

βραβεύω

brabeúō — G1018

Transliteration: brabeúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1018

Derivation: from the same as G1017 (βραβεῖον);

Definition

to arbitrate, i.e. (genitive case) to govern (figuratively, prevail)

KJV Translation

rule

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

__II with accusative, arbitrate, decide on, τὰ τῶν ἄλλων δίκαια [Refs 4th c.BC+]; δικαστήριον[LXX+1st c.AD+]:—passive, βραβευθήσεται τὰ τοῦ πολέμου τῷ νενικηκότι [Refs 5th c.BC+]
