כָּכָה

kâkâh — H3602

Transliteration: kâkâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3602

Derivation: from H3541 (כֹּה);

Definition

just so, referring to the previous or following context

KJV Translation

after that (this) manner, this matter, (even) so, in such a case, thus.
