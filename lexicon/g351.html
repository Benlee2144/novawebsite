ἀνάκρισις

anákrisis — G351

Transliteration: anákrisis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G351

Derivation: from G350 (ἀνακρίνω);

Definition

a (judicial) investigation

KJV Translation

examination
