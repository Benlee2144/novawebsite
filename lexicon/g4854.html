σύμφυτος

sýmphytos — G4854

Transliteration: sýmphytos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4854

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and a derivative of G5453 (φύω);

Definition

grown along with (connate), i.e. (figuratively) closely united to

KJV Translation

planted together
