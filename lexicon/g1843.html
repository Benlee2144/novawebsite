ἐξομολογέω

exomologéō — G1843

Transliteration: exomologéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1843

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G3670 (ὁμολογέω);

Definition

to acknowledge or (by implication, of assent) agree fully

KJV Translation

confess, profess, promise

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐξομολογ-έομαι,
confess, τὰς ἁμαρτίας [LXX+NT+1st c.AD+]; admit, acknowledge, μυθογραφίαν [NT+1st c.BC+]; especially in legal formulae, ἐ. εἰληφέναι [Refs 1st c.BC+]; acknowledge, υἱόν [Refs 3rd c.AD+]: absolutely, acknowledge a liability, [Refs 3rd c.BC+]
__2 make grateful acknowledgements, give thanks, sing praises, [LXX+1st c.AD+]
__II later in active, agree, consent, [NT]passive, ἐξωμολογημέναι ἀποδείξεις agreed, admitted proofs, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
