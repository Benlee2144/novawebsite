מְרֵמוֹת

Mᵉrêmôwth — H4822

Transliteration: Mᵉrêmôwth

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4822

Derivation: plural from H7311 (רוּם); heights;

Definition

Meremoth, the name of two Israelites

KJV Translation

Meremoth.

Appears In (6 verses)

Ezra 10:36Ezra 8:33Nehemiah 10:5Nehemiah 12:3Nehemiah 3:21Nehemiah 3:4

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at Ezr.10.36
§ Meremoth = "elevations"
1) a priest, son of Uriah of the family of Koz active in rebuilding the wall of Jerusalem and in the 7th course of temple service in the time of Ezra and Nehemiah
2) a priest in the time of Zerubbabel
3) a returning exile of the family of Bani who had a foreign wife in the time of Ezra
