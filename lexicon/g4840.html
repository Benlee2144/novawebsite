Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4840

συμπάρειμι

sympáreimi — G4840

Transliteration: sympáreimi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4840

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G3918 (πάρειμι);

Definition

to be at hand together, i.e. now present

KJV Translation

be here present with

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

συμπάρ-ειμι,
(εἰμί sum) to be present also or at the same time, [Refs 5th c.BC+], etc.; of a desire, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]; of planets, occupy a position together, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
__2 stand by, come to help, τινι [Refs 5th c.BC+]; of an advocate, [Refs 4th c.BC+]; act as one's representative, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
