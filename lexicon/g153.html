Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G153

αἰσχύνομαι

aischýnomai — G153

Transliteration: aischýnomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G153

Derivation: from (disfigurement, i.e. disgrace);

Definition

to feel shame (for oneself)

KJV Translation

be ashamed

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
