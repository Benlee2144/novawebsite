ἀφορμή

aphormḗ — G874

Transliteration: aphormḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G874

Derivation: from a compound of G575 (ἀπό) and G3729 (ὁρμάω);

Definition

a starting-point, i.e. (figuratively) an opportunity

KJV Translation

occasion
