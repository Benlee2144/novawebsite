φυτεύω

phyteúō — G5452

Transliteration: phyteúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5452

Derivation: from a derivative of G5453 (φύω);

Definition

to set out in the earth, i.e. implant; figuratively, to instil doctrine

KJV Translation

plant
