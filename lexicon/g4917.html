συνθλάω

synthláō — G4917

Transliteration: synthláō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4917

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and thlao (to crush);

Definition

to dash together, i.e. shatter

KJV Translation

break
