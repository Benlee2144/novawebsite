νήφω

nḗphō — G3525

Transliteration: nḗphō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3525

Derivation: of uncertain affinity;

Definition

to abstain from wine (keep sober), i.e. (figuratively) be discreet

KJV Translation

be sober, watch
