Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2684

κατασκοπέω

kataskopéō — G2684

Transliteration: kataskopéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2684

Derivation: from G2685 (κατάσκοπος);

Definition

to be a sentinel, i.e. to inspect insidiously

KJV Translation

spy out

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

κατασκοπ-έω,
