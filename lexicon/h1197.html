Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H1197

בָּעַר

bâʻar — H1197

Transliteration: bâʻar

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1197

Derivation: a primitive root; also as denominative from H1198 (בַּעַר)

Definition

to kindle, i.e. consume (by fire or by eating); to be(-come) brutish

KJV Translation

be brutish, bring (put, take) away, burn, (cause to) eat (up), feed, heat, kindle, set (on fire), waste.
