καταμανθάνω

katamanthánō — G2648

Transliteration: katamanthánō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2648

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G3129 (μανθάνω);

Definition

to learn thoroughly, i.e. (by implication) to note carefully

KJV Translation

consider
