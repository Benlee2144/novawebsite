טַבַּעַת

ṭabbaʻath — H2885

Transliteration: ṭabbaʻath

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2885

Derivation: from H2883 (טָבַע);

Definition

properly, a seal (as sunk into the wax), i.e. signet (for sealing); hence (generally) a ring of any kind

KJV Translation

ring.
