πικρία

pikría — G4088

Transliteration: pikría

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4088

Derivation: from G4089 (πικρός);

Definition

acridity (especially poison), literally or figuratively

KJV Translation

bitterness

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

__2 of temper, τὴν ἀπὸ τῆς ψυχῆς π. [Refs 4th c.BC+]; ἡ ἐπὶ τοῖς γεγονόσι π. [Refs 2nd c.BC+]; ἡ ἐν τοῖς λόγοις π. [Refs 1st c.BC+]
