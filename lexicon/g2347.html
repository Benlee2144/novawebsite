θλῖψις

thlîpsis — G2347

Transliteration: thlîpsis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2347

Derivation: from G2346 (θλίβω);

Definition

pressure (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

afflicted(-tion), anguish, burdened, persecution, tribulation, trouble

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

__2 crushing, castration, πώλων [Refs]
__3 metaphorically, oppression, affliction, [LXX+1st c.BC+]
