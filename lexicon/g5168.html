τρυμαλιά

trymaliá — G5168

Transliteration: trymaliá

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5168

Derivation: from a derivative of (to wear away;

Definition

akin to the base of G5134 (τραῦμα), G5147 (τρίβος) and G5176 (τρώγω)); an orifice, i.e. needle's eye

KJV Translation

eye
