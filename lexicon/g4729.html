Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4729

στενοχωρέω

stenochōréō — G4729

Transliteration: stenochōréō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4729

Derivation: from the same as G4730 (στενοχωρία);

Definition

to hem in closely, i.e. (figuratively) cramp

KJV Translation

distress, straiten
