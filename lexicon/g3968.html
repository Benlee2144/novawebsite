Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3968

πατρίς

patrís — G3968

Transliteration: patrís

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3968

Derivation: from G3902 (παράσημος);

Definition

a father-land, i.e. native town; (figuratively) heavenly home

KJV Translation

(own) country

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

πατρίς, ίδος,
poetry feminine of πάτριος, of one's fathers, πατρὶς αἶα, ἄρουρα, one's fatherland, country, [Refs 8th c.BC+]; γῆ π. [Refs 5th c.BC+]; π. πόλις the city of one's sires, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II substantive, ={πάτρα}[Refs 8th c.BC+]; κοινὴ π., i. e. the nether world, [Refs 1st c.AD+], ubi bene, ibi patria, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; simply, native town or village, [NT+5th c.BC+]
