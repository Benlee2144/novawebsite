ζηλόω

zēlóō — G2206

Transliteration: zēlóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2206

Derivation: from G2205 (ζῆλος);

Definition

to have warmth of feeling for or against

KJV Translation

affect, covet (earnestly), (have) desire, (move with) envy, be jealous over, (be) zealous(-ly affect)
