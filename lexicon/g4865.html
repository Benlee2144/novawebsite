Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4865

συναγωνίζομαι

synagōnízomai — G4865

Transliteration: synagōnízomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4865

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G75 (ἀγωνίζομαι);

Definition

to struggle in company with, i.e. (figuratively) to be a partner (assistant)

KJV Translation

strive together with
