κοινόω

koinóō — G2840

Transliteration: koinóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2840

Derivation: from G2839 (κοινός);

Definition

to make (or consider) profane (ceremonially)

KJV Translation

call common, defile, pollute, unclean

κοινόω, -ῶ
[in LXX: \2 א R* ;]
__1. in cl., to make common.
__2. In LXX, l.with, and NT (as κοινός, 2), to make ceremonially unclean, to profane (= cl., βεληλόω): Mat.15:11, 18 15:20, Mrk.7:15, 18 7:20, 23, Act.21:28; pass., Heb.9:13.
__3. to count unclean (cf. δικαιόω): Act.10:15 11:9.†
