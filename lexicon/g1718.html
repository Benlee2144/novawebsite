ἐμφανίζω

emphanízō — G1718

Transliteration: emphanízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1718

Derivation: from G1717 (ἐμφανής);

Definition

to exhibit (in person) or disclose (by words)

KJV Translation

appear, declare (plainly), inform, (will) manifest, shew, signify
