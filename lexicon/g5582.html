ψεῦσμα

pseûsma — G5582

Transliteration: pseûsma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5582

Derivation: from G5574 (ψεύδομαι);

Definition

a fabrication, i.e. falsehood

KJV Translation

lie
