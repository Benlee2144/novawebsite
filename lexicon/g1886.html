ἔπαυλις

épaulis — G1886

Transliteration: épaulis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1886

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and an equivalent of G833 (αὐλή);

Definition

a hut over the head, i.e. a dwelling

KJV Translation

habitation

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἔπαυλ-ις, εως, ἡ,
steading, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 farm-building, country house, [Refs 1st c.BC+]
__3 in military language, quarters, ἔ. ποιεῖσθαι encamp, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__4 unwalled village, [LXX]
