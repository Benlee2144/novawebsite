ἀναπαύω

anapaúō — G373

Transliteration: anapaúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G373

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and G3973 (παύω);

Definition

(reflexively) to repose (literally or figuratively (be exempt), remain); by implication, to refresh

KJV Translation

take ease, refresh, (give, take) rest
