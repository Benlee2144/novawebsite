δεκατέσσαρες

dekatéssares — G1180

Transliteration: dekatéssares

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1180

Derivation: from G1176 (δέκα) and G5064 (τέσσαρες);

Definition

ten and four, i.e. fourteen

KJV Translation

fourteen
