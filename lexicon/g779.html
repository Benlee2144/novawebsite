ἀσκός

askós — G779

Transliteration: askós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G779

Derivation: from the same as G778 (ἀσκέω);

Definition

a leathern (or skin) bag used as a bottle

KJV Translation

bottle
