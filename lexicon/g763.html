ἀσέβεια

asébeia — G763

Transliteration: asébeia

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G763

Derivation: from G765 (ἀσεβής);

Definition

impiety, i.e. (by implication) wickedness

KJV Translation

ungodly(-liness)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀσέβ-εια, ἡ,
ungodliness, impiety, opposed to ἀδικία, διὰ τὴν ἐκείνων περὶ μὲν θεοὺς ἀ. περὶ δὲ ἀνθρώπους ἀδικίαν[LXX+5th c.BC+]: at Rome, disloyalty to the Emperor (as θεός), [Refs 2nd c.AD+]; of Christianity, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
