ἀναλογίζομαι

analogízomai — G357

Transliteration: analogízomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G357

Derivation: middle voice from G356 (ἀναλογία);

Definition

to estimate, i.e. (figuratively) contemplate

KJV Translation

consider

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
