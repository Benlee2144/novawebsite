ἐνοχλέω

enochléō — G1776

Transliteration: enochléō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1776

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and G3791 (ὀχλέω);

Definition

to crowd in, i.e. (figuratively) to annoy

KJV Translation

trouble
