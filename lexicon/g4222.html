ποτίζω

potízō — G4222

Transliteration: potízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4222

Derivation: from a derivative of the alternate of G4095 (πίνω);

Definition

to furnish drink, irrigate

KJV Translation

give (make) to drink, feed, water
