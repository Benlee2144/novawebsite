ἔξω

éxō — G1854

Transliteration: éxō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1854

Derivation: adverb from G1537 (ἐκ);

Definition

out(-side, of doors), literally or figuratively

KJV Translation

away, forth, (with-)out (of, -ward), strange
