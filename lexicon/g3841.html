παντοκράτωρ

pantokrátōr — G3841

Transliteration: pantokrátōr

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3841

Derivation: from G3956 (πᾶς) and G2904 (κράτος);

Definition

the all-ruling, i.e. God (as absolute and universal sovereign)

KJV Translation

Almighty, Omnipotent
