θυσιαστήριον

thysiastḗrion — G2379

Transliteration: thysiastḗrion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2379

Derivation: from a derivative of G2378 (θυσία);

Definition

a place of sacrifice, i.e. an altar (special or genitive case, literal or figurative)

KJV Translation

altar
