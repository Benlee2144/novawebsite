πλέω

pléō — G4126

Transliteration: pléō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4126

Derivation: probably a form of G4150 (πλύνω) (through the idea of plunging through the water);

Definition

to pass in a vessel

KJV Translation

sail
