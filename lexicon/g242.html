ἅλλομαι

hállomai — G242

Transliteration: hállomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G242

Derivation: middle voice of apparently a primary verb;

Definition

to jump; figuratively, to gush

KJV Translation

leap, spring up
