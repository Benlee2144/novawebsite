ἀργός

argós — G692

Transliteration: argós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G692

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and G2041 (ἔργον);

Definition

inactive, i.e. unemployed; (by implication) lazy, useless

KJV Translation

barren, idle, slow
