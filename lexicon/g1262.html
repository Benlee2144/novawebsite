διαλύω

dialýō — G1262

Transliteration: dialýō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1262

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G3089 (λύω);

Definition

to dissolve utterly

KJV Translation

scatter
