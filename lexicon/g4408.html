πρῶρα

prōra — G4408

Transliteration: prōra

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4408

Derivation: feminine of a presumed derivative of G4253 (πρό) as noun;

Definition

the prow, i.e. forward part of a vessel

KJV Translation

forepart(-ship)
