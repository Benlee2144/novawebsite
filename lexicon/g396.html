ἀνατρέπω

anatrépō — G396

Transliteration: anatrépō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G396

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and the base of G5157 (τροπή);

Definition

to overturn (figuratively)

KJV Translation

overthrow, subvert
