σχῆμα

schēma — G4976

Transliteration: schēma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4976

Derivation: from the alternate of G2192 (ἔχω);

Definition

a figure (as a mode or circumstance), i.e. (by implication) external condition

KJV Translation

fashion
