ἡμιθανής

hēmithanḗs — G2253

Transliteration: hēmithanḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2253

Derivation: from a presumed compound of the base of G2255 (ἥμισυ) and G2348 (θνήσκω);

Definition

half dead, i.e. entirely exhausted
