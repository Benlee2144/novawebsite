βρύω

brýō — G1032

Transliteration: brýō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1032

Derivation: a primary verb;

Definition

to swell out, i.e. (by implication) to gush

KJV Translation

send forth

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

βρύω, poët., late prose and vernac.,
to be full to bursting;
__1. of the earth producing vegetation.
__2. Of plants putting forth buds.
__3. Of springs, to gush with water: Jas.3:11.
† (AS)
