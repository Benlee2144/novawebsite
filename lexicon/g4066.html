περίχωρος

períchōros — G4066

Transliteration: períchōros

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4066

Derivation: from G4012 (περί) and G5561 (χώρα);

Definition

around the region, i.e. circumjacent (as noun, with G1093 (γῆ) implied vicinity)

KJV Translation

country (round) about, region (that lieth) round about

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

περίχωρ-ος, ον,
round about a place: οἱ π. the people about, [Refs 4th c.BC+]; ἡ π. (i.e. γῆ) the country round about, [LXX+NT]; τὰ π. [Refs 4th c.AD+]
