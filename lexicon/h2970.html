Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H2970

יַאֲזַנְיָה

Yaʼăzanyâh — H2970

Transliteration: Yaʼăzanyâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2970

Derivation: or יַאֲזַנְיָהוּ; from H238 (אָזַן) and H3050 (יָהּ); heard of Jah;

Definition

Jaazanjah, the name of four Israelites

KJV Translation

Jaazaniah. Compare H3153 (יְזַנְיָה).

Appears In (2 verses)

Ezekiel 11:1Jeremiah 35:3

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at 2Ki.25.23;
son of: Hoshaiah (H1955H); also called Jaazaniah at 2Ki.25.23; Azariah at Jer.43.2;
Another spelling of ye.zan.yah (יְזַנְיָה "Jezaniah" H3153)
§ Jaazaniah = "Jehovah hears"
1) a Judean, son of the Maachathite, a captain of the Judean forces who visited Gedaliah at Mizpah after Gedaliah had been appointed governor of Judah by Nebuchadnezzar
2) son of Shaphan, an elder of Israel in the days of Ezekiel
3) son of Azur, one of the princes of the people against whom Ezekiel was directed to prophesy
4) a Rechabite, son of Jeremiah the prophet
