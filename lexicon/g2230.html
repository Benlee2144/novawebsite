ἡγεμονεύω

hēgemoneúō — G2230

Transliteration: hēgemoneúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2230

Derivation: from G2232 (ἡγεμών);

Definition

to act as ruler

KJV Translation

be governor
