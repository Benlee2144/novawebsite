πόρνος

pórnos — G4205

Transliteration: pórnos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4205

Derivation: from (to sell;

Definition

akin to the base of G4097 (πιπράσκω)); a (male) prostitute (as venal), i.e. (by analogy) a debauchee (libertine)

KJV Translation

fornicator, whoremonger
