ὀρθῶς

orthōs — G3723

Transliteration: orthōs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3723

Derivation: adverb from G3717 (ὀρθός);

Definition

in a straight manner, i.e. (figuratively) correctly (also morally)

KJV Translation

plain, right(-ly)
