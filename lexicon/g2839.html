κοινός

koinós — G2839

Transliteration: koinós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2839

Derivation: probably from G4862 (σύν);

Definition

common, i.e. (literally) shared by all or several, or (ceremonially) profane

KJV Translation

common, defiled, unclean, unholy
