κατανεύω

kataneúō — G2656

Transliteration: kataneúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2656

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G3506 (νεύω);

Definition

to nod down (towards), i.e. (by analogy) to make signs to

KJV Translation

beckon
