βάρβαρος

bárbaros — G915

Transliteration: bárbaros

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G915

Derivation: of uncertain derivation;

Definition

a foreigner (i.e. non-Greek)

KJV Translation

barbarian(-rous)
