εὐθυδρομέω

euthydroméō — G2113

Transliteration: euthydroméō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2113

Derivation: from G2117 (εὐθύς) and G1408 (δρόμος);

Definition

to lay a straight course, i.e. sail direct

KJV Translation

(come) with a straight course
