מַסֵּכָה

maççêkâh — H4541

Transliteration: maççêkâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4541

Derivation: from H5258 (נָסַךְ);

Definition

properly, a pouring over, i.e. fusion of metal (especially a cast image); by implication, a libation, i.e. league; concretely a coverlet (as if poured out)

KJV Translation

covering, molten (image), vail.
