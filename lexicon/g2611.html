καταδέω

katadéō — G2611

Transliteration: katadéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2611

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G1210 (δέω);

Definition

to tie down, i.e. bandage (a wound)

KJV Translation

bind up
