ἐπικαθίζω

epikathízō — G1940

Transliteration: epikathízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1940

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G2523 (καθίζω);

Definition

to seat upon

KJV Translation

set on

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐπικαθ-ίζω,
set upon, τινὰ ἐπί τι 5th c.BC: Hippocrates Medicus “Art.” 78: absolutely, (i.e. κλίμακι)[Refs]; τινὰ ἐν ἅρματι Vetus Testamentum Graece redditum 4 Ki..10.16; ἐν ὅπλοις ἀφανῶς ἐ. τὸ στρατιωτικόν 1st c.AD: Josephus Historicus “Antiquitates Judaicae” 18.3.1:—middle, φυλακὴν ἐπεκαθίσαντο had a guard set, [Refs]
__II. Intr., sit upon, πειθώ τις ἐπεκάθιζεν ἐπὶ τοῖς χείλεσι 5th c.BC: Eupolis Comicus 94.5; τοῖς καρχησίοις ἐ. light upon, [Refs 1st c.AD+]
__II.2. sit down against, besiege, πόλει 2nd c.BC: Polybius Historicus 4.61.6.

Matthew 21:7
