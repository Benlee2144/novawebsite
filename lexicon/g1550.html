ἐκδαπανάω

ekdapanáō — G1550

Transliteration: ekdapanáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1550

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G1159 (δαπανάω);

Definition

to expend (wholly), i.e. (figuratively) exhaust

KJV Translation

spend

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐκδᾰπᾰνάω,
exhaust, χορηγίας [Refs 2nd c.BC+]; τὸ αἷμα, τὸ ὑγρόν, [Refs 1st c.AD+]:—passive, ἐκδεδαπανῆσθαι ὑπὲρ τῶν ψυχῶν ὑμῶν [NT]

Appears In (1 verse)

2 Corinthians 12:15

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

εκ-δαπανάω, -ῶ
strengthened form of δαπανάω, to spend wholly; pass., with reflexive force, to spend oneself wholly: before ὑπέρ, 2Co.12:15.†
