ἀσφαλίζω

asphalízō — G805

Transliteration: asphalízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G805

Derivation: from G804 (ἀσφαλής);

Definition

to render secure

KJV Translation

make fast (sure)
