ἤρεμος

ḗremos — G2263

Transliteration: ḗremos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2263

Derivation: perhaps by transposition from G2048 (ἔρημος) (through the idea of stillness);

Definition

tranquil

KJV Translation

quiet

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἤρεμ-ος, ον,
later form for ἠρεμαῖος, quiet, ἤ. καὶ ἡσύχιος βίος [NT+5th c.BC+]; -ώτερος ἐπισπασμός gentler traction, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
__2 τὸ ἤ. smoothness, of pigments, [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__3 adverb -μως quietly, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]. (Cf. Lithuanian rìmti 'grow still', Gothic rimis (= ἡσυχία), Sanskrit ramṇāti 'set at rest'.)

1 Timothy 2:2
