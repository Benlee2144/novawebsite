γεννητός

gennētós — G1084

Transliteration: gennētós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1084

Derivation: from G1080 (γεννάω);

Definition

born

KJV Translation

they that are born

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

γενν-ητός, ή, όν,
begotten, υἱὸς γ., opposed to ποιητός, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; mortal, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]; γεννητοὶ γυναικῶν born of women, [NT]
__II generable, opposed to φθαρτός, [Refs 4th c.BC+]; ὑλὴ γ. matter for generation, [Refs] adverb -τῶς by means of generation, [Refs 4th c.AD+]

Appears In (2 verses)

Luke 7:28Matthew 11:11
