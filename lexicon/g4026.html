περιΐστημι

periḯstēmi — G4026

Transliteration: periḯstēmi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4026

Derivation: from G4012 (περί) and G2476 (ἵστημι);

Definition

to stand all around, i.e. (near) to be a bystander, or (aloof) to keep away from

KJV Translation

avoid, shun, stand by (round about)
