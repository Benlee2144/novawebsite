Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H8610

תָּפַשׂ

tâphas — H8610

Transliteration: tâphas

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H8610

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to manipulate, i.e. seize; chiefly to capture, wield, specifically, to overlay; figuratively, to use unwarrantably

KJV Translation

catch, handle, (lay, take) hold (on, over), stop, [idiom] surely, surprise, take.
