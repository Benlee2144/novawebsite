γλυκύς

glykýs — G1099

Transliteration: glykýs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1099

Derivation: of uncertain affinity;

Definition

sweet (i.e. not bitter nor salt)

KJV Translation

sweet, fresh
