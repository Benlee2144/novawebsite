ἐπικαλέομαι

epikaléomai — G1941

Transliteration: epikaléomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1941

Derivation: middle voice from G1909 (ἐπί) and G2564 (καλέω);

Definition

to entitle; by implication, to invoke (for aid, worship, testimony, decision, etc.)

KJV Translation

appeal (unto), call (on, upon), surname
