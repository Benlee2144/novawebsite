ἀνορθόω

anorthóō — G461

Transliteration: anorthóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G461

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and a derivative of the base of G3717 (ὀρθός);

Definition

to straighten up

KJV Translation

lift (set) up, make straight

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀνορθόω,
