ἐκβολή

ekbolḗ — G1546

Transliteration: ekbolḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1546

Derivation: from G1544 (ἐκβάλλω);

Definition

ejection, i.e. (specially) a throwing overboard of the cargo

KJV Translation

+ lighten the ship
