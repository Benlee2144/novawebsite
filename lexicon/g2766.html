κέραμος

kéramos — G2766

Transliteration: kéramos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2766

Derivation: probably from the base of G2767 (κεράννυμι) (through the idea of mixing clay and water);

Definition

earthenware, i.e. a tile (by analogy, a thin roof or awning)

KJV Translation

tiling
