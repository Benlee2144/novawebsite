ζεστός

zestós — G2200

Transliteration: zestós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2200

Derivation: from G2204 (ζέω);

Definition

boiled, i.e. (by implication) calid (figuratively, fervent)

KJV Translation

hot
