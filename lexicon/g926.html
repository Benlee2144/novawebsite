βαρύς

barýs — G926

Transliteration: barýs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G926

Derivation: from the same as G922 (βάρος);

Definition

weighty, i.e. (fig) burdensome, grave

KJV Translation

grievous, heavy, weightier

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
