נָקַם

nâqam — H5358

Transliteration: nâqam

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5358

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to grudge, i.e. avenge or punish

KJV Translation

avenge(-r, self), punish, revenge (self), [idiom] surely, take vengeance.
