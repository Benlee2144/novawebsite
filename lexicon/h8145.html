שֵׁנִי

shênîy — H8145

Transliteration: shênîy

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H8145

Derivation: from H8138 (שָׁנָה);

Definition

properly, double, i.e. second; also adverbially, again

KJV Translation

again, either (of them), (an-) other, second (time).
