πάροινος

pároinos — G3943

Transliteration: pároinos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3943

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and G3631 (οἶνος);

Definition

staying near wine, i.e. tippling (a toper)

KJV Translation

given to wine
