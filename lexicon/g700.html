ἀρέσκω

aréskō — G700

Transliteration: aréskō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G700

Derivation: probably from G142 (αἴρω) (through the idea of exciting emotion);

Definition

to be agreeable (or by implication, to seek to be so)

KJV Translation

please
