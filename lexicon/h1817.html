דֶּלֶת

deleth — H1817

Transliteration: deleth

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1817

Derivation: from H1802 (דָּלָה);

Definition

something swinging, i.e. the valve of adoor

KJV Translation

door (two-leaved), gate, leaf, lid. (Psalm 141:3).
