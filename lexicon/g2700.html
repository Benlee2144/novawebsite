κατατοξεύω

katatoxeúō — G2700

Transliteration: katatoxeúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2700

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and a derivative of G5115 (τόξον);

Definition

to shoot down with an arrow or other missile

KJV Translation

thrust through
