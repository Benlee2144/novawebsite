גְּבוּל

gᵉbûwl — H1366

Transliteration: gᵉbûwl

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1366

Derivation: or גְּבֻל; (shortened) from H1379 (גָּבַל);

Definition

properly, a cord (as twisted), i.e. (by implication) a boundary; by extension the territory inclosed

KJV Translation

border, bound, coast, [idiom] great, landmark, limit, quarter, space.
