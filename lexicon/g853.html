ἀφανίζω

aphanízō — G853

Transliteration: aphanízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G853

Derivation: from G852 (ἀφανής);

Definition

to render unapparent, i.e. (actively) consume (becloud), or (passively) disappear (be destroyed)

KJV Translation

corrupt, disfigure, perish, vanish away
