Chizqîyâh — H2396

Transliteration: Chizqîyâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2396

Derivation: or חִזְקִיָּהוּ; also יְחִזְקִיָּה; or יְחִזְקִיָּהוּ; from H2388 (חָזַק) and H3050 (יָהּ); strengthened of Jah;

Definition

Chizkijah, a king of Judah, also the name of two other Israelites

KJV Translation

Hezekiah, Hizkiah, Hizkijah. Compare H3169 (יְחִזְקִיָּה).

A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at Zep.1.1;
father of: Amariah (H0568O)
§ Hezekiah or Hizkiah or Hizkijah = "Jehovah is my strength"
1) 12th king of Judah, son of Ahaz and Abijah; a good king in that he served Jehovah and did away with idolatrous practices
2) great-great-grandfather of Zephaniah the prophet
3) son of Neariah, a descendant of David
4) head of a family of returning exiles in the time of Nehemiah
