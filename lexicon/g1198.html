δέσμιος

désmios — G1198

Transliteration: désmios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1198

Derivation: from G1199 (δεσμόν);

Definition

a captive (as bound)

KJV Translation

in bonds, prisoner

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

δέσμ-ιος, ον,
binding: metaphorically, binding as with a spell, enchaining, with genitive, ὕμνος ἐξ Ἐρινύων δ. φρενῶν [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__II passive, bound, captive, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; on leash, [κύων] probably in [Refs]; δ. φυγών, ={ἐκ δεσμῶν}, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
