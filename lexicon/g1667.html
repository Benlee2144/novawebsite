ἑλίσσω

helíssō — G1667

Transliteration: helíssō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1667

Derivation: a form of G1507 (εἱλίσσω);

Definition

to coil or wrap

KJV Translation

fold up
