σωφρονισμός

sōphronismós — G4995

Transliteration: sōphronismós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4995

Derivation: from G4994 (σωφρονίζω);

Definition

discipline, i.e. self-control

KJV Translation

sound mind

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

σωφρον-ισμός, ὁ,
teaching of morality or moderation, [Refs 1st c.BC+]; self-control, [NT]

Appears In (1 verse)

2 Timothy 1:7
