ἐξαιρέω

exairéō — G1807

Transliteration: exairéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1807

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G138 (αἱρέομαι);

Definition

actively, to tear out; middle voice, to select; figuratively, to release

KJV Translation

deliver, pluck out, rescue
