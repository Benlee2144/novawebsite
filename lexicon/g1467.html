ἐγκρατεύομαι

enkrateúomai — G1467

Transliteration: enkrateúomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1467

Derivation: middle voice from G1468 (ἐγκρατής);

Definition

to exercise self-restraint (in diet and chastity)

KJV Translation

can(-not) contain, be temperate

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
