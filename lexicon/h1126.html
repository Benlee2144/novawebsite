בֶּן־אוֹנִי

Ben-ʼÔwnîy — H1126

Transliteration: Ben-ʼÔwnîy

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1126

Derivation: from H1121 (בֵּן) and H205 (אָוֶן); son of my sorrow;

Definition

Ben-Oni, the original name of Benjamin

KJV Translation

Ben-oni.

Appears In (1 verse)

Genesis 35:18

brother of: Joseph (H3130);
half-brother of: Reuben (H7205), Simeon (H8095), Levi (H3878), Judah (H3063), Dan (H1835H), Naphtali (H5321), Gad (H1410), Asher (H0836), Issachar (H3485), Zebulun (H2074) and Dinah (H1783);
