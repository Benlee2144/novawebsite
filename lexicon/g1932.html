ἐπιείκεια

epieíkeia — G1932

Transliteration: epieíkeia

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1932

Derivation: from G1933 (ἐπιεικής);

Definition

suitableness, i.e. (by implication) equity, mildness

KJV Translation

clemency, gentleness

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐπιείκεια, ἡ,
reasonableness, [Refs 5th c.BC+].
__2. equity, opposed to strict law, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__3. of persons, reasonableness, fairness, [NT+5th c.BC+]; also, goodness, virtuousness, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II. personified, [Refs 1st c.AD+]
