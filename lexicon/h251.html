Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H251

אָח

ʼâch — H251

Transliteration: ʼâch

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H251

Derivation: a primitive word;

Definition

a brother (used in the widest sense of literal relationship and metaphorical affinity or resemblance (like father))

KJV Translation

another, brother(-ly); kindred, like, other. Compare also the proper names beginning with 'Ah-' or 'Ahi-'.

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

: compatriot
1) brother
1a) brother of same parents
1b) half-brother (same father)
1c) relative, kinship, same tribe
1d) each to the other (reciprocal relationship)
1e) (fig.) of resemblance
