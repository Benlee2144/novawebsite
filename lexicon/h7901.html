שָׁכַב

shâkab — H7901

Transliteration: shâkab

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7901

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to lie down (for rest, sexual connection, decease or any other purpose)

KJV Translation

[idiom] at all, cast down, (lover-)lay (self) (down), (make to) lie (down, down to sleep, still with), lodge, ravish, take rest, sleep, stay.
