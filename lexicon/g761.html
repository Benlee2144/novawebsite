ἀσάλευτος

asáleutos — G761

Transliteration: asáleutos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G761

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G4531 (σαλεύω);

Definition

unshaken, i.e. (by implication) immovable (figuratively)

KJV Translation

which cannot be moved, unmovable
