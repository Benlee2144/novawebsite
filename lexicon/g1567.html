ἐκζητέω

ekzētéō — G1567

Transliteration: ekzētéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1567

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G2212 (ζητέω);

Definition

to search out, i.e. (figuratively)investigate, crave, demand, (by Hebraism) worship

KJV Translation

en- (re-)quire, seek after (carefully, diligently)
