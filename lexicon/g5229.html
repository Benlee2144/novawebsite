ὑπεραίρομαι

hyperaíromai — G5229

Transliteration: hyperaíromai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5229

Derivation: middle voice from G5228 (ὑπέρ) and G142 (αἴρω);

Definition

to raise oneself over, i.e. (figuratively) to become haughty

KJV Translation

exalt self, be exalted above measure
