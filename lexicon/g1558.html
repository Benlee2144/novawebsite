ἔκδικος

ékdikos — G1558

Transliteration: ékdikos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1558

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G1349 (δίκη);

Definition

carrying justice out, i.e. a punisher

KJV Translation

a (re-)venger

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

ἔκδικος, -ον
(δίκη), [in LXX: Wis.12:12, Sir.30:6, 4Ma.15:29 * ;]
__1. without law, unjust.
__2. exacting penalty from; as subst., an avenger: Rom.13:4; before περί, 1Th.4:6 (in π., a legal representative; Milligan, Th., l.with).†
