προκαλέομαι

prokaléomai — G4292

Transliteration: prokaléomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4292

Derivation: middle voice from G4253 (πρό) and G2564 (καλέω);

Definition

to call forth to oneself (challenge), i.e. (by implication) to irritate

KJV Translation

provoke
