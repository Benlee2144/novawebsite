ἐκκακέω

ekkakéō — G1573

Transliteration: ekkakéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1573

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G2556 (κακός);

Definition

to be (bad or) weak, i.e. (by implication) to fail (in heart)

KJV Translation

faint, be weary

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐκκᾰκέω,
to be faint-hearted, lose heart, grow weary, variant for{ἐγκ-}, [NT]
