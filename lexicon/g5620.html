ὥστε

hṓste — G5620

Transliteration: hṓste

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5620

Derivation: from G5613 (ὡς) and G5037 (τέ);

Definition

so too, i.e. thus therefore (in various relations of consecution, as follow)

KJV Translation

(insomuch) as, so that (then), (insomuch) that, therefore, to, wherefore
