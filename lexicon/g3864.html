παραθαλάσσιος

parathalássios — G3864

Transliteration: parathalássios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3864

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and G2281 (θάλασσα);

Definition

along the sea, i.e. maritime (lacustrine)

KJV Translation

upon the sea coast
