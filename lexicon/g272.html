ἀμελέω

ameléō — G272

Transliteration: ameléō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G272

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and G3199 (μέλω);

Definition

to be careless of

KJV Translation

make light of, neglect, be negligent, no regard
