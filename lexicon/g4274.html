πρόδρομος

pródromos — G4274

Transliteration: pródromos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4274

Derivation: from the alternate of G4390 (προτρέχω);

Definition

a runner ahead, i.e. scout (figuratively, precursor)

KJV Translation

forerunner

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

πρό-δρομος, -ον
(προτρέχω) [in LXX: Num.13:21 (20), Isa.28:4 (בִּכּוּר), Wis.12:8 * ;]
running forward, going in advance. As subst., ὁ π., an advance guard, forerunner: Heb.6:20.†
(AS)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

πρόδρομ-ος, ον,
running forward with headlong speed, π. ἦλθον [Refs 5th c.BC+]; μόλε π. variant in [Refs 5th c.BC+]

__3 metaphorically, precursor, ἀστέρα.. ἀελίου π.[Refs 5th c.BC+]
__4 π. (i.e. οἶνος), see at {πρότροπος}.
__II as substantive, πρόδρομοι, οἱ,
__II.1 see above [Refs]
