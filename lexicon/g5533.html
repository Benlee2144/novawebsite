χρεωφειλέτης

chreōpheilétēs — G5533

Transliteration: chreōpheilétēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5533

Derivation: from a derivative of G5531 (χράω) and G3781 (ὀφειλέτης);

Definition

a loan-ower, i.e. indebted person

KJV Translation

debtor
