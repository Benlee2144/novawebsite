Pronunciation:

Strong’s: G1540

Definition

KJV Translation

hundred

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἑκᾰτόν,
[Refs 4th c.AD+] ἑκοτόν [Refs 4th c.BC+], οἱ, αἱ, τά, indeclinable:— a hundred, [Refs 8th c.BC+]: in compounds frequently loosely for very many. (Dissim. from sém [kcirc]ṃtóm, compare εἷς and Latin centum, Lithuanian ši[mtilde]tas, etc.)
