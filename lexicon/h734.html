אֹרַח

ʼôrach — H734

Transliteration: ʼôrach

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H734

Derivation: from H732 (אָרַח);

Definition

a well-trodden road (literally or figuratively); also a caravan

KJV Translation

manner, path, race, rank, traveller, troop, (by-, high-) way.
