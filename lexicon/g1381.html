Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1381

δοκιμάζω

dokimázō — G1381

Transliteration: dokimázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1381

Derivation: from G1384 (δόκιμος);

Definition

to test (literally or figuratively); by implication, to approve

KJV Translation

allow, discern, examine, X like, (ap-)prove, try
