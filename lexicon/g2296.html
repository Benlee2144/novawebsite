Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2296

θαυμάζω

thaumázō — G2296

Transliteration: thaumázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2296

Derivation: from G2295 (θαῦμα);

Definition

to wonder; by implication, to admire

KJV Translation

admire, have in admiration, marvel, wonder
