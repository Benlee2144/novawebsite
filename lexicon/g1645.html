ἐλαφρός

elaphrós — G1645

Transliteration: elaphrós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1645

Derivation: probably akin to G1643 (ἐλαύνω) and the base of G1640 (ἐλάσσων);

Definition

light, i.e. easy

KJV Translation

light

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
