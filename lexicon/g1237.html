διαδέχομαι

diadéchomai — G1237

Transliteration: diadéchomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1237

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G1209 (δέχομαι);

Definition

to receive in turn, i.e. (figuratively) succeed to

KJV Translation

come after
