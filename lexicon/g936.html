βασιλεύω

basileúō — G936

Transliteration: basileúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G936

Derivation: from G935 (βασιλεύς);

Definition

to rule (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

king, reign

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
