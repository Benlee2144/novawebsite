Συροφοίνισσα

Syrophoínissa — G4949

Transliteration: Syrophoínissa

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4949

Derivation: feminine of a compound of G4948 (Σύρος) and the same as G5403 (Φοινίκη);

Definition

a Syro-phœnician woman, i.e. a female native of Phœnicia in Syria

KJV Translation

Syrophenician
