נָגַע

nâgaʻ — H5060

Transliteration: nâgaʻ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5060

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

properly, to touch, i.e. lay the hand upon (for any purpose; euphemistically, to lie with a woman); by implication, to reach (figuratively, to arrive, acquire); violently, to strike (punish, defeat, destroy, etc.)

KJV Translation

beat, ([idiom] be able to) bring (down), cast, come (nigh), draw near (nigh), get up, happen, join, near, plague, reach (up), smite, strike, touch.
