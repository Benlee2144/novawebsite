מִשְׁכָּב

mishkâb — H4904

Transliteration: mishkâb

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4904

Derivation: from H7901 (שָׁכַב);

Definition

a bed (figuratively, a bier); abstractly, sleep; by euphemism, carnal intercourse

KJV Translation

bed(-chamber), couch, lieth (lying) with.
