περιέρχομαι

periérchomai — G4022

Transliteration: periérchomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4022

Derivation: from G4012 (περί) and G2064 (ἔρχομαι) (including its alternate);

Definition

to come all around, i.e. stroll, vacillate, veer

KJV Translation

fetch a compass, vagabond, wandering about
