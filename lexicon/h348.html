Transliteration: ʼÎyzebel

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H348

Derivation: from H336 (אִי)lemma אי missing vowel, corrected to אִי and H2083 (זְבֻל);

Definition

Izebel, the wife of king Ahab

KJV Translation

Jezebel.
