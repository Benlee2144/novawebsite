Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H3665

כָּנַע

kânaʻ — H3665

Transliteration: kânaʻ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3665

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

properly, to bend the knee; hence, to humiliate, vanquish

KJV Translation

bring down (low), into subjection, under, humble (self), subdue.
