οὐκοῦν

oukoûn — G3766

Transliteration: oukoûn

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3766

Derivation: from G3756 (οὐ) and G3767 (οὖν);

Definition

is it not therefore that, i.e. (affirmatively) hence or so

KJV Translation

then

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
