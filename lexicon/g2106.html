εὐδοκέω

eudokéō — G2106

Transliteration: eudokéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2106

Derivation: from G2095 (εὖ) and G1380 (δοκέω);

Definition

to think well of, i.e. approve (an act); specially, to approbate (a person or thing)

KJV Translation

think good, (be well) please(-d), be the good (have, take) pleasure, be willing
