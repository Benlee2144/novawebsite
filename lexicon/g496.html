ἀντιπίπτω

antipíptō — G496

Transliteration: antipíptō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G496

Derivation: from G473 (ἀντί) and G4098 (πίπτω) (including its alternate);

Definition

to oppose

KJV Translation

resist
