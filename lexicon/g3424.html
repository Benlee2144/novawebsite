μογιλάλος

mogilálos — G3424

Transliteration: mogilálos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3424

Derivation: from G3425 (μόγις) and G2980 (λαλέω);

Definition

hardly talking, i.e. dumb (tongue-tied)

KJV Translation

having an impediment in his speech

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

μογῐλάλος [ᾰ], ον,
(μόγις, λαλέω) having an impediment in one's speech, [LXX+NT+2nd c.AD+]
