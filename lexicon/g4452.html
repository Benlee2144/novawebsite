-πω

-pō — G4452

Transliteration: -pō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4452

Derivation: another form of the base of G4458 (-πώς);

Definition

an enclitic particle of indefiniteness; yet, even; used only in the comparative. See G3369 (μηδέπω), G3380 (μήπω), G3764 (οὐδέπω), G3768 (οὔπω), G4455 (πώποτε).
