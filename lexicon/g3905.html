παρατείνω

parateínō — G3905

Transliteration: parateínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3905

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and teino (to stretch);

Definition

to extend along, i.e. prolong (in point of time)
