ὑποφέρω

hypophérō — G5297

Transliteration: hypophérō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5297

Derivation: from G5259 (ὑπό) and G5342 (φέρω);

Definition

to bear from underneath, i.e. (figuratively) to undergo hardship

KJV Translation

bear, endure
