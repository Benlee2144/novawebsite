Ἑβραϊστί

Hebraïstí — G1447

Transliteration: Hebraïstí

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1447

Derivation: adverb from G1446 (Ἑβραΐς);

Definition

Hebraistically or in the Jewish (Chaldee) language

KJV Translation
