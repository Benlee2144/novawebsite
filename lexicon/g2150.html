Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2150

εὐσέβεια

eusébeia — G2150

Transliteration: eusébeia

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2150

Derivation: from G2152 (εὐσεβής);

Definition

piety; specially, the gospel scheme

KJV Translation

godliness, holiness

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

εὐσέβεια, ἡ
(compare εὐσεβία), reverence towards the gods or parents, piety or filial respect, εὐ. εἰς θεοὺς καὶ γονέας [Refs 5th c.BC+]; εὐ. Ζηνός towards him, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; εὐ. πρός, περὶ τοὺς θεούς, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ἡ πρὸς τὸ θεῖον εὐ. [Refs 1st c.BC+]; τὴν εὐ. τῶν πραχθέντων [Refs 5th c.BC+]: plural, acts of piety, [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__2 loyalty, ἡ ὑμετέρα πρός με εὐ. [Refs]; ἡ εἴς με εὔνοια καὶ εὐ. [Refs]
__3 = Latin [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
__4 credit or character for piety, εὐσέβειαν οἴσῃ [Refs 5th c.BC+]
