συστέλλω

systéllō — G4958

Transliteration: systéllō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4958

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G4724 (στέλλω);

Definition

to send (draw) together, i.e. enwrap (enshroud a corpse for burial), contract (an interval)

KJV Translation

short, wind up
