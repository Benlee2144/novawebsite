תַּחְפַּנְחֵס

Tachpanchêç — H8471

Transliteration: Tachpanchêç

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H8471

Derivation: or תְּחַפְנְחֵס; (Ezekiel 30:18), or תַּחְפְּנֵס; (Jeremiah 2:16), of Egyptian derivation;

Definition

Tachpanches, Techaphneches or Tachpenes, a place in Egypt

KJV Translation

Tahapanes, Tahpanhes, Tehaphnehes.

Appears In (1 verse)

Jeremiah 43:7

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)


§ Tahpanhes or Tahapanes or Tehaphnehes = "thou will fill hands with pity"
a city in Egypt; modern 'Tel Defenneh' or 'Tel Defneh' located approx 18 miles (29 km) east southeast from Tanis
