πλεονεκτέω

pleonektéō — G4122

Transliteration: pleonektéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4122

Derivation: from G4123 (πλεονέκτης);

Definition

to be covetous, i.e. (by implication) to over-reach

KJV Translation

get an advantage, defraud, make a gain
