ἐκδύω

ekdýō — G1562

Transliteration: ekdýō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1562

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and the base of G1416 (δύνω);

Definition

to cause to sink out of, i.e. (specially as of clothing) to divest

KJV Translation

strip, take off from, unclothe
