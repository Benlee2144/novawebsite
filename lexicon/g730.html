ἄῤῥην

árrhēn — G730

Transliteration: árrhēn

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G730

Derivation: probably from G142 (αἴρω);

Definition

male (as stronger for lifting)

KJV Translation

male, man
