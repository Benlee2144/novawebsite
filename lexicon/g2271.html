ἡσυχία

hēsychía — G2271

Transliteration: hēsychía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2271

Derivation: feminine of G2272 (ἡσύχιος);

Definition

(as noun) stillness, i.e. desistance from bustle or language

KJV Translation

quietness, silence
