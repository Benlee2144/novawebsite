Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H5774

עוּף

ʻûwph — H5774

Transliteration: ʻûwph

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5774

Derivation: a primitive root; to cover (with wings or obscurity); hence (as denominative from H5775 (עוֹף))

Definition

to fly; also (by implication of dimness) to faint (from the darkness of swooning)

KJV Translation

brandish, be (wax) faint, flee away, fly (away), [idiom] set, shine forth, weary.
