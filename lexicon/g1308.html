διαφέρω

diaphérō — G1308

Transliteration: diaphérō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1308

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G5342 (φέρω);

Definition

to bear through, i.e. (literally) transport; usually to bear apart, i.e. (objectively) to toss about (figuratively, report); subjectively, to \

KJV Translation

be better, carry, differ from, drive up and down, be (more) excellent, make matter, publish, be of more value
