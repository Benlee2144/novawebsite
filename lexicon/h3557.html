Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H3557

כּוּל

kûwl — H3557

Transliteration: kûwl

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3557

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

properly, to keep in; hence, to measure; figuratively, to maintain (in various senses)

KJV Translation

(be able to, can) abide, bear, comprehend, contain, feed, forbearing, guide, hold(-ing in), nourish(-er), be present, make provision, receive, sustain, provide sustenance (victuals).
