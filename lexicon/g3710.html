Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3710

ὀργίζω

orgízō — G3710

Transliteration: orgízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3710

Derivation: from G3709 (ὀργή);

Definition

to provoke or enrage, i.e. (passively) become exasperated

KJV Translation

be angry (wroth)
