Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G5277

ὑπολιμπάνω

hypolimpánō — G5277

Transliteration: hypolimpánō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5277

Derivation: a prolonged form for G5275 (ὑπολείπω);

Definition

to leave behind, i.e. bequeath

KJV Translation

leave

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ὑπολιμπάνω,
collateral form of ὑπολείπω, leave behind, [NT+4th c.AD+]
__2 middle, leave over, μὴ ὑπολιμπάνεσθε leave no arrears (uncollected), [Refs 3rd c.BC+]; reserve, κερμάτιον εἰς τοὺς προστάντας τῆς σωτηρίας ἡμων [Refs 3rd c.BC+]
__II intransitive, fail, τὰ νάματα ὑ. [Refs 1st c.BC+]
