ἀκρίβεια

akríbeia — G195

Transliteration: akríbeia

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G195

Derivation: from the same as G196 (ἀκριβέστατος);

Definition

exactness

KJV Translation

perfect manner

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀκρῑβ-εια, ἡ,
exactness, precision, [Refs 5th c.BC+] — frequently with Preps. in adverb sense, δι᾽ ἀκριβείας with minuteness or precision, [Refs 5th c.BC+] —ἡ ἀ. τοῦ ναυτικοῦ its efficiency, rigid discipline, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ἀ. νόμων strictness, severity, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; περὶ τὸ διάφορον strictness in money matters [Refs 2nd c.BC+]: plural, niceties, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 parsimony, frugality, [Refs 1st c.AD+]; ὕδωρ δι᾽ ἀκριβείας ἐστί τινι is scarce, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
