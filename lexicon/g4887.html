Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4887

συνδέω

syndéō — G4887

Transliteration: syndéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4887

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G1210 (δέω);

Definition

to bind with, i.e. (passively) be a fellow-prisoner (figuratively)

KJV Translation

be bound with
