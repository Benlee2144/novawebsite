רָמַת לֶחִי

Râmath Lechîy — H7437

Transliteration: Râmath Lechîy

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7437

Derivation: from H7413 (רָמָה) and H3895 (לְחִי); height of a jaw-bone;

Definition

Ramath-Lechi, a place in Palestine

KJV Translation

Ramath-lehi.

Appears In (1 verse)

Judges 15:17

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)


§ Ramath-lehi = "height of a jawbone"
a place by the rock Elam in northern Judah near the border of the Philistines; named by Samson after his slaughter of the 1000 Philistines with the jawbone of an ass
