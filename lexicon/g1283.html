διαρπάζω

diarpázō — G1283

Transliteration: diarpázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1283

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G726 (ἁρπάζω);

Definition

to seize asunder, i.e. plunder

KJV Translation

spoil
