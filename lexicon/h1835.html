Dân — H1835

Transliteration: Dân

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1835

Derivation: from H1777 (דִּין); judge;

Definition

Dan, one of the sons of Jacob; also the tribe descended from him, and its territory; likewise a place in Palestine colonized by them
