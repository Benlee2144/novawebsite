πιπράσκω

pipráskō — G4097

Transliteration: pipráskō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4097

Derivation: contracted from (to traverse;

Definition

from the base of G4008 (πέραν)); to traffic (by travelling), i.e. dispose of as merchandise or into slavery (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

sell
