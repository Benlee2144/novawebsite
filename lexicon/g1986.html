ἐπισπάομαι

epispáomai — G1986

Transliteration: epispáomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1986

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G4685 (σπάω);

Definition

to draw over, i.e. (with G203 (ἀκροβυστία) implied) efface the mark of circumcision (by recovering with the foreskin)

KJV Translation

become uncircumcised
