πιάζω

piázō — G4084

Transliteration: piázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4084

Derivation: probably another form of G971 (βιάζω);

Definition

to squeeze, i.e. seize (gently by the hand (press), or officially (arrest), or in hunting (capture))

KJV Translation

apprehend, catch, lay hand on, take
