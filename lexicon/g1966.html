ἐπιοῦσα

epioûsa — G1966

Transliteration: epioûsa

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1966

Derivation: feminine singular participle of a comparative of G1909 (ἐπί) and (to go);

Definition

supervening, i.e. (G2250 (ἡμέρα) or G3571 (νύξ) being expressed or implied) the ensuing day or night

KJV Translation

following, next
