Ἰησοῦς

Iēsoûs — G2424

Transliteration: Iēsoûs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2424

Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H03091);

Definition

Jesus (i.e. Jehoshua), the name of our Lord and two (three) other Israelites

KJV Translation
