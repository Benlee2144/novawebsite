εἰσκαλέω

eiskaléō — G1528

Transliteration: eiskaléō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1528

Derivation: from G1519 (εἰς) and G2564 (καλέω);

Definition

to invite in

KJV Translation

call in
