σωτηρία

sōtēría — G4991

Transliteration: sōtēría

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4991

Derivation: feminine of a derivative of G4990 (σωτήρ) as (properly, abstract) noun;

Definition

rescue or safety (physically or morally)

KJV Translation

deliver, health, salvation, save, saving
