בְּהֵמָה

bᵉhêmâh — H929

Transliteration: bᵉhêmâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H929

Derivation: from an unused root (probably meaning to be mute);

Definition

properly, a dumb beast; especially any large quadruped or animal (often collective)

KJV Translation

beast, cattle.
