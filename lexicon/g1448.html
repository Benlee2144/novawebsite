Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1448

ἐγγίζω

engízō — G1448

Transliteration: engízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1448

Derivation: from G1451 (ἐγγύς);

Definition

to make near, i.e. (reflexively) approach

KJV Translation

approach, be at hand, come (draw) near, be (come, draw) nigh

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐγγίζω,
aorist ἤγγισα [Refs 4th c.BC+]: perfect ἤγγικα [LXX+NT]: (ἐγγύς):—bring near, bring up to, τῇ γῇ τὰς ναῦς [LXX+2nd c.BC+]
__II mostly intransitive, approach, [LXX+4th c.BC+]; εἰς θάνατον[LXX]; ἕως[LXX+NT]; to be imminent, ἤγγικεν ἡ παρουσία τοῦ Κυρίου [NT]: also, with genitive, approximate to, [Refs 1st c.BC+]
__II.2 to be next of kin, [LXX]
__III with infinitive, to be on the point of doing, ναοῦ -οντος συμπεσεῖν [Refs]
