Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G5535

χρῄζω

chrḗizō — G5535

Transliteration: chrḗizō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5535

Derivation: from G5532 (χρεία);

Definition

to make (i.e. have) necessity, i.e. be in want of

KJV Translation

(have) need
