Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4760

στρατόπεδον

stratópedon — G4760

Transliteration: stratópedon

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4760

Derivation: from the base of G4756 (στρατιά) and the same as G3977 (πεδινός);

Definition

a camping-ground, i.e. (by implication) a body of troops

KJV Translation

army

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

στρᾰτόπεδ-ον, τό,
