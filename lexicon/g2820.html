κληρόω

klēróō — G2820

Transliteration: klēróō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2820

Derivation: from G2819 (κλῆρος);

Definition

to allot, i.e. (figuratively) to assign (a privilege)

KJV Translation

obtain an inheritance
