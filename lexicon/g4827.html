συμμαθητής

symmathētḗs — G4827

Transliteration: symmathētḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4827

Derivation: from a compound of G4862 (σύν) and G3129 (μανθάνω);

Definition

a co-learner (of Christianity)

KJV Translation

fellow disciple
