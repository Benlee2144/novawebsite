ἐπερώτημα

eperṓtēma — G1906

Transliteration: eperṓtēma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1906

Derivation: from G1905 (ἐπερωτάω);

Definition

an inquiry

KJV Translation

answer

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐπερώτ-ημα,
Ionic dialect ἐπειρ-, ατος, τό, question, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 answer to inquiry put to higher authority: hence, sanction, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
__3 = Latin stipulatio, PCair. Preis.1.16 [Refs 2nd c.AD+]: hence probably, pledge, [Refs].

Appears In (1 verse)

1 Peter 3:21
