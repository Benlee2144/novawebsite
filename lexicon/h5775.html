עוֹף

ʻôwph — H5775

Transliteration: ʻôwph

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5775

Derivation: from H5774 (עוּף);

Definition

a bird (as covered with feathers, or rather as covering with wings), often collectively

KJV Translation

bird, that flieth, flying, fowl.
