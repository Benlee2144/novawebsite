πατάσσω

patássō — G3960

Transliteration: patássō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3960

Derivation: probably prolongation from G3817 (παίω);

Definition

to knock (gently or with a weapon or fatally)

KJV Translation

smite, strike
