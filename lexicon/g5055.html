τελέω

teléō — G5055

Transliteration: teléō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5055

Derivation: from G5056 (τέλος);

Definition

to end, i.e. complete, execute, conclude, discharge (a debt)

KJV Translation

accomplish, make an end, expire, fill up, finish, go over, pay, perform
