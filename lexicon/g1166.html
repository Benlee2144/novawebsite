δεικνύω

deiknýō — G1166

Transliteration: deiknýō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1166

Derivation: a prolonged form of an obsolete primary of the same meaning;

Definition

to show (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

shew
