Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H1755

דּוֹר

dôwr — H1755

Transliteration: dôwr

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1755

Derivation: or (shortened) דֹּר; from H1752 (דּוּר);

Definition

properly, a revolution of time, i.e. an age or generation; also a dwelling

KJV Translation

age, [idiom] evermore, generation, (n-) ever, posterity.
