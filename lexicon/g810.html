ἀσωτία

asōtía — G810

Transliteration: asōtía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G810

Derivation: from a compound of G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G4982 (σώζω);

Definition

properly, unsavedness, i.e. (by implication) profligacy

KJV Translation

excess, riot
