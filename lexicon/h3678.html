כִּסֵּא

kiççêʼ — H3678

Transliteration: kiççêʼ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3678

Derivation: or כִּסֵּה; from H3680 (כָּסָה);

Definition

properly, covered, i.e. a throne (as canopied)

KJV Translation
