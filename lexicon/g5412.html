φορτίζω

phortízō — G5412

Transliteration: phortízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5412

Derivation: from G5414 (φόρτος);

Definition

to load up (properly, as a vessel or animal), i.e. (figuratively) to overburden with ceremony (or spiritual anxiety)

KJV Translation

lade, by heavy laden
