ἐπιβιβάζω

epibibázō — G1913

Transliteration: epibibázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1913

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and a reduplicated derivative of the base of G939 (βάσις) (compare G307 (ἀναβιβάζω));

Definition

to cause to mount (an animal)

KJV Translation

set on
