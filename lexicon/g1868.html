ἔπαινος

épainos — G1868

Transliteration: épainos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1868

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and the base of G134 (αἰνέω);

Definition

laudation; concretely, a commendable thing

KJV Translation

praise

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἔπαιν-ος, ὁ,
approval, praise, commendation, [Refs 5th c.BC+]: frequently in Trag. and Attic dialect, ἐπαίνου τυχεῖν ἔκτινος [Refs 5th c.BC+]; κλεινὴ καὶ ἔπαινον ἔχουσα meriting praise,[Refs 5th c.BC+]: plural, praises, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 complimentary address, panegyric (but distinguished from ἐγκώμιον, as the general from the particular, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
