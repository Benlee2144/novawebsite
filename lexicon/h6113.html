Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H6113

עָצָר

ʻâtsâr — H6113

Transliteration: ʻâtsâr

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6113

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to inclose; by analogy, to hold back; also to maintain, rule, assemble

KJV Translation

[idiom] be able, close up, detain, fast, keep (self close, still), prevail, recover, refrain, [idiom] reign, restrain, retain, shut (up), slack, stay, stop, withhold (self).
