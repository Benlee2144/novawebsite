Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2455

Ἰούδας

Ioúdas — G2455

Transliteration: Ioúdas

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2455

Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H03063);

Definition

Judas (i.e. Jehudah), the name of ten Israelites; also of the posterity of one of them and its region

KJV Translation

Juda(-h, -s); Jude

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
