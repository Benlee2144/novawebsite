μετακινέω

metakinéō — G3334

Transliteration: metakinéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3334

Derivation: from G3326 (μετά) and G2795 (κινέω);

Definition

to stir to a place elsewhere, i.e. remove (figuratively)

KJV Translation

move away
