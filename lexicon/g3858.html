παραδέχομαι

paradéchomai — G3858

Transliteration: paradéchomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3858

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and G1209 (δέχομαι);

Definition

to accept near, i.e. admit or (by implication) delight in

KJV Translation

receive
