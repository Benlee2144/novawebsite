περίεργος

períergos — G4021

Transliteration: períergos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4021

Derivation: from G4012 (περί) and G2041 (ἔργον);

Definition

working all around, i.e. officious (meddlesome, neuter plural magic)

KJV Translation

busybody, curious arts
