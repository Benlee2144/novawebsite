ἐκδιώκω

ekdiṓkō — G1559

Transliteration: ekdiṓkō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1559

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G1377 (διώκω);

Definition

to pursue out, i.e. expel or persecute implacably

KJV Translation

persecute
