προαύλιον

proaúlion — G4259

Transliteration: proaúlion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4259

Derivation: neuter of a presumed compound of G4253 (πρό) and G833 (αὐλή);

Definition

a forecourt, i.e. vestibule (alley-way)

KJV Translation

porch

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

προαύλ-ιον, τό,
(αὐλός) prelude on the flute, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II αὐλή) place before a court, vestibule, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
__II.2 space before a cattle-pen, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]

Mark 14:68
