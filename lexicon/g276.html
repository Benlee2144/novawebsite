ἀμετάθετος

ametáthetos — G276

Transliteration: ametáthetos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G276

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G3346 (μετατίθημι);

Definition

unchangeable, or (neuter as abstract) unchangeability

KJV Translation

immutable(-ility)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
