עָלַם

ʻâlam — H5956

Transliteration: ʻâlam

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5956

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to veil from sight, i.e. conceal (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

[idiom] any ways, blind, dissembler, hide (self), secret (thing).
