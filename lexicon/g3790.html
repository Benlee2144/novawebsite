ὀφρῦς

ophrŷs — G3790

Transliteration: ophrŷs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3790

Derivation: perhaps from G3700 (ὀπτάνομαι) (through the idea of the shading or proximity to the organ of vision);

Definition

the eye-\

KJV Translation

brow
