ἀκριβέστερον

akribésteron — G197

Transliteration: akribésteron

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G197

Derivation: neuter of the comparative of the same as G196 (ἀκριβέστατος);

Definition

(adverbially) more exactly

KJV Translation

more perfect(-ly)
