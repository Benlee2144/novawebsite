אָהַב

ʼâhab — H157

Transliteration: ʼâhab

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H157

Derivation: or אָהֵב ; a primitive root;

Definition

to have affection for (sexually or otherwise)

KJV Translation

(be-) love(-d, -ly, -r), like, friend.
