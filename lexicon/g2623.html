κατακλείω

katakleíō — G2623

Transliteration: katakleíō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2623

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G2808 (κλείω);

Definition

to shut down (in a dungeon), i.e. incarcerate

KJV Translation

shut up
