συναρπάζω

synarpázō — G4884

Transliteration: synarpázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4884

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G726 (ἁρπάζω);

Definition

to snatch together, i.e. seize

KJV Translation

catch
