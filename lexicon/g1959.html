ἐπιμελέομαι

epimeléomai — G1959

Transliteration: epimeléomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1959

Derivation: middle voice from G1909 (ἐπί) and the same as G3199 (μέλω);

Definition

to care for (physically or otherwise)

KJV Translation

take care of
