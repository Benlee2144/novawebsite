ἀκούω

akoúō — G191

Transliteration: akoúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G191

Derivation: a primary verb;

Definition

to hear (in various senses)

KJV Translation

give (in the) audience (of), come (to the ears), (shall) hear(-er, -ken), be noised, be reported, understand
