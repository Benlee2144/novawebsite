מָכוֹן

mâkôwn — H4349

Transliteration: mâkôwn

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4349

Derivation: from H3559 (כּוּן);

Definition

properly, a fixture, i.e. a basis; generally a place, especially as an abode

KJV Translation

foundation, habitation, (dwelling-, settled) place.
