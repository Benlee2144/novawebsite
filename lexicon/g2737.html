κατώτερος

katṓteros — G2737

Transliteration: katṓteros

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2737

Derivation: comparative from G2736 (κάτω);

Definition

inferior (locally, of Hades)

KJV Translation

lower
