חָמַם

châmam — H2552

Transliteration: châmam

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2552

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to be hot (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

enflame self, get (have) heat, be (wax) hot, (be, wax) warm (self, at).
