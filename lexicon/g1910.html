ἐπιβαίνω

epibaínō — G1910

Transliteration: epibaínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1910

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and the base of G939 (βάσις);

Definition

to walk upon, i.e. mount, ascend, embark, arrive

KJV Translation

come (into), enter into, go abroad, sit upon, take ship
