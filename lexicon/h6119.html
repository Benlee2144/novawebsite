Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H6119

עָקֵב

ʻâqêb — H6119

Transliteration: ʻâqêb

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6119

Derivation: or (feminine) עִקְּבָה; from H6117 (עָקַב);

Definition

a heel (as protuberant); hence, a track; figuratively, the rear (of an army)

KJV Translation

heel, (horse-) hoof, last, lier in wait (by mistake for H6120 (עָקֵב)), (foot-) step.
