περιεργάζομαι

periergázomai — G4020

Transliteration: periergázomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4020

Derivation: from G4012 (περί) and G2038 (ἐργάζομαι);

Definition

to work all around, i.e. bustle about (meddle)

KJV Translation

be a busybody

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
