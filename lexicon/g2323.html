θεραπεύω

therapeúō — G2323

Transliteration: therapeúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2323

Derivation: from the same as G2324 (θεράπων);

Definition

to wait upon menially, i.e. (figuratively) to adore (God), or (specially) to relieve (of disease)

KJV Translation

cure, heal, worship
