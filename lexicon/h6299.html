פָּדָה

pâdâh — H6299

Transliteration: pâdâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6299

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to sever, i.e. ransom; gener. to release, preserve

KJV Translation

[idiom] at all, deliver, [idiom] by any means, ransom, (that are to be, let be) redeem(-ed), rescue, [idiom] surely.
