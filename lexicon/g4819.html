Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4819

συμβαίνω

symbaínō — G4819

Transliteration: symbaínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4819

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and the base of G939 (βάσις);

Definition

to walk (figuratively, transpire) together, i.e. concur (take place)

KJV Translation

be(-fall), happen (unto)
