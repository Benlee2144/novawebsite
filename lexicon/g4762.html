στρέφω

stréphō — G4762

Transliteration: stréphō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4762

Derivation: strengthened from the base of G5157 (τροπή);

Definition

to twist, i.e. turn quite around or reverse (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

convert, turn (again, back again, self, self about)
