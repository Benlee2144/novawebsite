Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4650

σκορπίζω

skorpízō — G4650

Transliteration: skorpízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4650

Derivation: apparently from the same as G4651 (σκορπίος) (through the idea of penetrating);

Definition

to dissipate, i.e. (figuratively) put to flight, waste, be liberal

KJV Translation

disperse abroad, scatter (abroad)

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

σκορπ-ίζω,
