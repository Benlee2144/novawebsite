Κεδρών

Kedrṓn — G2748

Transliteration: Kedrṓn

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2748

Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H06939);

Definition

Cedron (i.e. Kidron), a brook near Jerusalem
