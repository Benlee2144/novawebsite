Transliteration: sâṭân

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7854

Derivation: from H7853 (שָׂטַן);

Definition

an opponent; especially (with the article prefixed) Satan, the arch-enemy of good

KJV Translation
