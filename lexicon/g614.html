ἀπόκρυφος

apókryphos — G614

Transliteration: apókryphos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G614

Derivation: from G613 (ἀποκρύπτω);

Definition

secret; by implication, treasured

KJV Translation

hid, kept secret
