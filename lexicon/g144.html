αἴσθησις

aísthēsis — G144

Transliteration: aísthēsis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G144

Derivation: from G143 (αἰσθάνομαι);

Definition

perception, i.e. (figuratively) discernment

KJV Translation

judgment
