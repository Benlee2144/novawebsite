עֵז

ʻêz — H5795

Transliteration: ʻêz

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5795

Derivation: from H5810 (עָזַז);

Definition

a she-goat (as strong), but masculine in plural (which also is used elliptically for goat's hair)

KJV Translation

(she) goat, kid.
