מָחָר

mâchâr — H4279

Transliteration: mâchâr

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4279

Derivation: probably from H309 (אָחַר);

Definition

properly, deferred, i.e. the morrow; usually (adverbially) tomorrow; indefinitely, hereafter

KJV Translation

time to come, tomorrow.
