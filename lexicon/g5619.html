ὡσπερεί

hōspereí — G5619

Transliteration: hōspereí

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5619

Derivation: from G5618 (ὥσπερ) and G1487 (εἰ);

Definition

just as if, i.e. as it were

KJV Translation

as

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

ὡσ-περ-εί
adv.,
as, as it were: 1Co.15:8.†
(AS)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ὡσπερεί
or ὥσπερ εἰ, adverb just as if, with indicative, ὥσπερ εἰ παρεστάτεις [Refs 5th c.BC+]; τὸ ὡσπερεὶ φάναι to say 'as it were', [Refs 3rd c.AD+]; ὅμοια ὥ. εἰ.. [Refs 5th c.BC+]: with Nouns or parts., as it were, ὡ. ψῆτταν [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II ὥσπερ ἂν εἰ or ὡσπερανεί (properly elliptical for ὥσπερ ἂν ἦν, εἰ.., or the like), [Refs 5th c.BC+]; Doric dialect ὥσπερ αἰκ ἐξ ἑνὸς κελεύματος κεχάναντι they gape as it were 'by numbers' [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ὥσπερ οὖν ἂν εἰ with imperfect, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
