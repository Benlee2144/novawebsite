ὄμμα

ómma — G3659

Transliteration: ómma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3659

Derivation: from G3700 (ὀπτάνομαι);

Definition

a sight, i.e. (by implication) the eye

KJV Translation

eye
