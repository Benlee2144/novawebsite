Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H2287

חָגַג

châgag — H2287

Transliteration: châgag

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2287

Derivation: a primitive root (compare H2283 (חָגָא), H2328 (חוּג));

Definition

properly, to move in acircle, i.e. (specifically) to march in asacred procession, to observe afestival; by implication, to be giddy

KJV Translation

celebrate, dance, (keep, hold) a (solemn) feast (holiday), reel to and fro.
