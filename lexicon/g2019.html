ἐπιφωνέω

epiphōnéō — G2019

Transliteration: epiphōnéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2019

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G5455 (φωνέω);

Definition

to call at something, i.e. exclaim

KJV Translation

cry (against), give a shout
