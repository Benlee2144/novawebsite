ἀνανήφω

ananḗphō — G366

Transliteration: ananḗphō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G366

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and G3525 (νήφω);

Definition

to become sober again, i.e. (figuratively) regain (one's) senses

KJV Translation

recover self

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀνανήφω,
become sober again, come to one's senses, [Refs 4th c.BC+]; return to sobriety of mind, [NT]; recover from a swoon, [Refs 1st c.AD+]
__2 transitive, make sober again, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]

Appears In (1 verse)

2 Timothy 2:26
