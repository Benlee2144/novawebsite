ὑποστρώννυμι

hypostrṓnnymi — G5291

Transliteration: hypostrṓnnymi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5291

Derivation: from G5259 (ὑπό) and G4766 (στρώννυμι);

Definition

to strew underneath (the feet as a carpet)

KJV Translation

spread
