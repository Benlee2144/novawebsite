Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H5848

עָטַף

ʻâṭaph — H5848

Transliteration: ʻâṭaph

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5848

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to shroud, i.e. clothe (whether transitive or reflex.); hence (from the idea of darkness) to languish

KJV Translation

cover (over), fail, faint, feebler, hide self, be overwhelmed, swoon.

Appears In (6 verses)

Isaiah 57:16Job 23:9Psalms 102:0Psalms 107:5Psalms 65:13Psalms 73:6

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

1) to be feeble, be faint, grow weak

1b) (Niphal) to faint
1c) (Hiphil) to show feebleness
1d) (Hithpael) to faint, faint away
