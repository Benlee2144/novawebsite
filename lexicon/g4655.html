σκότος

skótos — G4655

Transliteration: skótos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4655

Derivation: from the base of G4639 (σκιά);

Definition

shadiness, i.e. obscurity (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

darkness

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
