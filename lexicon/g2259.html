ἡνίκᾰ [ῐ],

__2 ἡνίκ᾽ ἄν, like{ὅταν}, with Subj., of future time, whenever, [Refs 5th c.BC+]: to denote repeated occurrence in present time, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ἄν should be restored in [Refs 4th c.BC+]; ἄν goes with Verb in ἡνίκ᾽ ἂν ὠφέλει when it would have been to his advantage, [Refs 4th c.BC+]; but ἡνίκ᾽ ἂν ἀνέβη ἡ νεφέλη, of repeated occurrence, [LXX]
