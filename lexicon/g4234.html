πρᾶξις

prâxis — G4234

Transliteration: prâxis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4234

Derivation: from G4238 (πράσσω);

Definition

practice, i.e. (concretely) an act; by extension, a function

KJV Translation

deed, office, work
