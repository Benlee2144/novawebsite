μέρος

méros — G3313

Transliteration: méros

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3313

Derivation: from an obsolete but more primary form of (to get as a section or allotment);

Definition

a division or share (literally or figuratively, in a wide application)

KJV Translation

behalf, course, coast, craft, particular (+ -ly), part (+ -ly), piece, portion, respect, side, some sort(-what)
