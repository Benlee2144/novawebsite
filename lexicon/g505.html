ἀνυπόκριτος

anypókritos — G505

Transliteration: anypókritos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G505

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G5271 (ὑποκρίνομαι);

Definition

undissembled, i.e. sincere

KJV Translation

without dissimulation (hypocrisy), unfeigned

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
