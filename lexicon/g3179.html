μεθίστημι

methístēmi — G3179

Transliteration: methístēmi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3179

Derivation: from G3326 (μετά) and G2476 (ἵστημι);

Definition

to transfer, i.e. carry away, depose or (figuratively) exchange, seduce

KJV Translation

put out, remove, translate, turn away
