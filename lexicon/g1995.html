ἐπιστροφή

epistrophḗ — G1995

Transliteration: epistrophḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1995

Derivation: from G1994 (ἐπιστρέφω);

Definition

reversion, i.e. morally, revolution

KJV Translation

conversion
