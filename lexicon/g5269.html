ὑποζώννυμι

hypozṓnnymi — G5269

Transliteration: hypozṓnnymi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5269

Derivation: from G5259 (ὑπό) and G2224 (ζώννυμι);

Definition

to gird under, i.e. frap (a vessel with cables across the keel, sides and deck)

KJV Translation

undergirt
