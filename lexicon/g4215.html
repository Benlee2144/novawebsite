ποταμός

potamós — G4215

Transliteration: potamós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4215

Derivation: probably from a derivative of the alternate of G4095 (πίνω) (compare G4224 (πότος));

Definition

a current, brook or freshet (as drinkable), i.e. running water

KJV Translation

flood, river, stream, water
