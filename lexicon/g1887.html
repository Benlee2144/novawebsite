ἐπαύριον

epaúrion — G1887

Transliteration: epaúrion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1887

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G839 (αὔριον);

Definition

occurring on the succeeding day, i.e. (G2250 (ἡμέρα) being implied) to-morrow

KJV Translation

day following, morrow, next day (after)

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
