σχολή

scholḗ — G4981

Transliteration: scholḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4981

Derivation: probably feminine of a presumed derivative of the alternate of G2192 (ἔχω);

Definition

properly, loitering (as a withholding of oneself from work) or leisure, i.e. (by implication) a \

KJV Translation

school
