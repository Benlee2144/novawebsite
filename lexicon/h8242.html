Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H8242

שַׂק

saq — H8242

Transliteration: saq

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H8242

Derivation: from H8264 (שָׁקַק);

Definition

properly, a mesh (as allowing a liquid to run through), i.e. coarse loose cloth or sacking (used in mourning and for bagging); hence, a bag (for grain, etc.)

KJV Translation

sack(-cloth, -clothes).
