καταπαύω

katapaúō — G2664

Transliteration: katapaúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2664

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G3973 (παύω);

Definition

to settle down, i.e. (literally) to colonize, or (figuratively) to (cause to) desist

KJV Translation

cease, (give) rest(-rain)
