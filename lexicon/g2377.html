θυρωρός

thyrōrós — G2377

Transliteration: thyrōrós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2377

Derivation: from G2374 (θύρα) and (a watcher);

Definition

a gate- warden

KJV Translation

that kept the door, porter
