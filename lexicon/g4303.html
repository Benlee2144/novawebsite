Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4303

προμαρτύρομαι

promartýromai — G4303

Transliteration: promartýromai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4303

Derivation: from G4253 (πρό) and G3143 (μαρτύρομαι);

Definition

to be a witness in advance i.e. predict

KJV Translation

testify beforehand

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

προμαρτύρομαι [ῡ],
bear witness to beforehand, τὰ εἰς Χριστὸν παθήματα [NT]

Appears In (1 verse)

1 Peter 1:11

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

προ-μαρτύρομαι
