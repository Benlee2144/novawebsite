Χαλδαῖος

Chaldaîos — G5466

Transliteration: Chaldaîos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5466

Derivation: probably of Hebrew or (H03778);

Definition

a Chaldæan (i.e. Kasdi), or native or the region of the lower Euphrates
