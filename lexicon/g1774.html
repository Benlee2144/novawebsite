ἐνοικέω

enoikéō — G1774

Transliteration: enoikéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1774

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and G3611 (οἰκέω);

Definition

to inhabit (figuratively)

KJV Translation

dwell in
