נַחֲלָה

nachălâh — H5159

Transliteration: nachălâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5159

Derivation: from H5157 (נָחַל) (in its usual sense);

Definition

properly, something inherited, i.e. (abstractly) occupancy, or (concretely) an heirloom; generally an estate, patrimony or portion

KJV Translation

heritage, to inherit, inheritance, possession. Compare H5158 (נַחַל).
