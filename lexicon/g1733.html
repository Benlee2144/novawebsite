ἕνδεκα

héndeka — G1733

Transliteration: héndeka

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1733

Derivation: from (the neuter of) G1520 (εἷς) and G1176 (δέκα);

Definition

one and ten, i.e. eleven

KJV Translation

eleven
