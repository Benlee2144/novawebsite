Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2433

ἱλάσκομαι

hiláskomai — G2433

Transliteration: hiláskomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2433

Derivation: middle voice from the same as G2436 (ἵλεως);

Definition

to conciliate, i.e. (transitively) to atone for (sin), or (intransitively) be propitious

KJV Translation

be merciful, make reconciliation for
