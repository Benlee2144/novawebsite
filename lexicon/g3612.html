οἴκημα

oíkēma — G3612

Transliteration: oíkēma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3612

Derivation: from G3611 (οἰκέω);

Definition

a tenement, i.e. (specially), a jail

KJV Translation

prison
