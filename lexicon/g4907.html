σύνεσις

sýnesis — G4907

Transliteration: sýnesis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4907

Derivation: from G4920 (συνίημι);

Definition

a mental putting together, i.e. intelligence or (concretely) the intellect

KJV Translation

knowledge, understanding
