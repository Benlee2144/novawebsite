Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H283

אַחְיוֹ

ʼAchyôw — H283

Transliteration: ʼAchyôw

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H283

Derivation: prolonged from H251 (אָח); brotherly;

Definition

Achio, the name of three Israelites

KJV Translation

Ahio.

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

A man of the tribe of Benjamin living at the time of United Monarchy, first mentioned at 1Ch.8.31;
son of: Abiel (H0022) and Maacah (H4601N);
brother of: Kish (H7027), Ner (H5369), Abdon (H5658J), Zur (H6698I), Baal (H1168B), Nadab (H5070J), Gedor (H1446I), Zecher (H2144) and Mikloth (H4732)
§ Ahio = "brotherly" or "fraternal"
1) son of Abinadab who sheltered the ark
2) a Benjamite
3) another Benjamite of Saul's ancestry
