ἐνδυναμόω

endynamóō — G1743

Transliteration: endynamóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1743

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and G1412 (δυναμόω);

Definition

to empower

KJV Translation

enable, (increase in) strength(-en), be (make) strong

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐνδυνᾰμ-όω,
strengthen, confirm, [LXX+NT]:—passive, ἐπὶ τῇ ματαιότητι [LXX]; τῇ πίστει [NT]
__II endow with vitality, in passive, metaphorically of scientific theorems, [Refs 3rd c.AD+]
