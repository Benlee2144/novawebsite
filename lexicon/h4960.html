מִשְׁתֶּה

mishteh — H4960

Transliteration: mishteh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4960

Derivation: from H8354 (שָׁתָה);

Definition

drink, by implication, drinking (the act); also (by implication) a banquet or (generally) feast

KJV Translation

banquet, drank, drink, feast((-ed), -ing).
