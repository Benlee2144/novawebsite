ἄμετρος

ámetros — G280

Transliteration: ámetros

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G280

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and G3358 (μέτρον);

Definition

immoderate

KJV Translation

(thing) without measure
