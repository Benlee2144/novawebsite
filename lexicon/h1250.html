בָּר

bâr — H1250

Transliteration: bâr

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1250

Derivation: or בַּר; from H1305 (בָּרַר) (in the sense of winnowing);

Definition

grain of any kind (even while standing in the field); by extension the open country

KJV Translation

corn, wheat.
