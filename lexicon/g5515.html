χλωρός

chlōrós — G5515

Transliteration: chlōrós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5515

Derivation: from the same as G5514 (Χλόη);

Definition

greenish, i.e. verdant, dun-colored

KJV Translation

green, pale
