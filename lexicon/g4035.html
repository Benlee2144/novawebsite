περιλείπω

perileípō — G4035

Transliteration: perileípō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4035

Derivation: from G4012 (περί) and G3007 (λείπω);

Definition

to leave all around, i.e. (passively) survive

KJV Translation

remain
