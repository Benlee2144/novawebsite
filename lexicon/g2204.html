ζέω

zéō — G2204

Transliteration: zéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2204

Derivation: a primary verb;

Definition

to be hot (boil, of liquids; or glow, of solids), i.e. (figuratively) be fervid (earnest)

KJV Translation

be fervent
