ἐπιτάσσω

epitássō — G2004

Transliteration: epitássō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2004

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G5021 (τάσσω);

Definition

to arrange upon, i.e. order

KJV Translation

charge, command, injoin
