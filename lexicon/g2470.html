ἴσος

ísos — G2470

Transliteration: ísos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2470

Derivation: probably from G1492 (εἴδω) (through the idea of seeming);

Definition

similar (in amount and kind)

KJV Translation

+ agree, as much, equal, like
