אֶתְמוֹל

ʼethmôwl — H865

Transliteration: ʼethmôwl

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H865

Derivation: or אִתְמוֹל; or אֶתְמוּל; probably from H853 (אֵת) or H854 (אֵת) and H4136 (מוּל);

Definition

heretofore; definitely yesterday

KJV Translation

[phrase] before (that) time, [phrase] heretofore, of late (old), [phrase] times past, yester(day).
