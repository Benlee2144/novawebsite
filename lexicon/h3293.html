יַעַר

yaʻar — H3293

Transliteration: yaʻar

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3293

Derivation: from an unused root probably meaning to thicken with verdure;

Definition

a copse of bushes; hence, a forest; hence, honey in the comb (as hived in trees)

KJV Translation

(honey-) comb, forest, wood.
