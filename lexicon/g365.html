ἀνανεόω

ananeóō — G365

Transliteration: ananeóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G365

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and a derivative of G3501 (νέος);

Definition

to renovate, i.e. reform

KJV Translation

renew
