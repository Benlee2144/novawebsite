διαπαντός

diapantós — G1275

Transliteration: diapantós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1275

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and the genitive case of G3956 (πᾶς);

Definition

through all time, i.e. (adverbially) constantly

KJV Translation

alway(-s), continually
