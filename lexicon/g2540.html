καιρός

kairós — G2540

Transliteration: kairós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2540

Derivation: of uncertain affinity;

Definition

an occasion, i.e. set or proper time

KJV Translation

X always, opportunity, (convenient, due) season, (due, short, while) time, a while
