יְשַׁעְיָה

Yᵉshaʻyâh — H3470

Transliteration: Yᵉshaʻyâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3470

Derivation: or יְשַׁעְיָהוּ; from H3467 (יָשַׁע) and H3050 (יָהּ); Jah has saved;

Definition

Jeshajah, the name of seven Israelites

KJV Translation

Isaiah, Jesaiah, Jeshaiah.

A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at Neh.11.7;
father of: Ithiel (H0384)
§ Isaiah or Jesaiah or Jeshaiah = "Jehovah has saved"
1) son of Hananiah, brother of Pelatiah, and grandson of Zerubbabel
2) a Benjamite
3) son of Athaliah and chief of the house of Elam who returned with Ezra
4) a chief of the descendants of Merari who returned with Ezra
