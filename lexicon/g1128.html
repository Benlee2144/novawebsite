γυμνάζω

gymnázō — G1128

Transliteration: gymnázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1128

Derivation: from G1131 (γυμνός);

Definition

to practise naked (in the games), i.e. train (figuratively)

KJV Translation

exercise
