Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H3371

יׇקְתְאֵל

Yoqthᵉʼêl — H3371

Transliteration: Yoqthᵉʼêl

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3371

Derivation: probably from the same as H3348 (יָקֶה) and H410 (אֵל); veneration of God (compare H3354 (יְקוּתִיאֵל));

Definition

Joktheel, the name of a place in Palestine, and of one in Idumaea

KJV Translation

Joktheel.

Appears In (1 verse)

2 Kings 14:7

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

Joktheel = "the blessedness of God"
the name given to the cliff which was the stronghold of Edom by king Amaziah after capturing it; also called 'Selah' and 'Petra'
Another name of se.la (סֶ֫לַע "Sela" H5554)
