οὗ

hoû — G3757

Transliteration: hoû

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3757

Derivation: genitive case of G3739 (ὅς) as adverb;

Definition

at which place, i.e. where

KJV Translation

where(-in), whither(-soever)

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

οὗ,
genitive of relative and possess. Prons. ὅς: as adverb where, see at {ὅς}, ἥ, ὅ A b. 1.
