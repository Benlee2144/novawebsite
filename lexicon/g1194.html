δέρω

dérō — G1194

Transliteration: dérō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1194

Derivation: a primary verb;

Definition

properly, to flay, i.e. (by implication) to scourge, or (by analogy) to thrash

KJV Translation

beat, smite
