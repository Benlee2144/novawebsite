νικάω

nikáō — G3528

Transliteration: nikáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3528

Derivation: from G3529 (νίκη);

Definition

to subdue (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

conquer, overcome, prevail, get the victory
