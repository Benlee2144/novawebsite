ἁγνίζω

hagnízō — G48

Transliteration: hagnízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G48

Derivation: from G53 (ἁγνός);

Definition

to make clean, i.e. (figuratively) sanctify (ceremonially or morally)

KJV Translation

purify (self)
