πετεινόν

peteinón — G4071

Transliteration: peteinón

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4071

Derivation: neuter of a derivative of G4072 (πέτομαι);

Definition

a flying animal, i.e. bird

KJV Translation

bird, fowl

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

πετεινός, ή, όν,
