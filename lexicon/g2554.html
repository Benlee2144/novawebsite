Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2554

κακοποιέω

kakopoiéō — G2554

Transliteration: kakopoiéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2554

Derivation: from G2555 (κακοποιός);

Definition

to be a bad-doer, i.e. (objectively) to injure, or (genitive) to sin

KJV Translation

do(ing) evil
