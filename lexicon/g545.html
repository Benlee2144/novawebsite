ἀπειθής

apeithḗs — G545

Transliteration: apeithḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G545

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and G3982 (πείθω);

Definition

unpersuadable, i.e. contumacious

KJV Translation

disobedient
