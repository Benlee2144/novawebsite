מַלּוּךְ

Mallûwk — H4409

Transliteration: Mallûwk

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4409

Derivation: or מַלּוּכִי; (Nehemiah 12:14), from H4427 (מָלַךְ); remnant;

Definition

Malluk, the name of five Israelites

KJV Translation

Malluch, Melichu (from the margin).

Appears In (6 verses)

1 Chronicles 6:44Ezra 10:29Ezra 10:32Nehemiah 10:27Nehemiah 10:4Nehemiah 12:2

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

A man living at the time of Exile and Return, first mentioned at Neh.10.4; also called Mallichi (Qere, KJV: Melicu; NIV: Malluk) at Neh.12.14;
Also named: me.li.khu (מְלִיכוּ "Mallichi" H4409B)
§ Malluch or Melicu = "counsellor"
a priest who returned from exile with Zerubbabel
