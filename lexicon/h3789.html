כָּתַב

kâthab — H3789

Transliteration: kâthab

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3789

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to grave, by implication, to write (describe, inscribe, prescribe, subscribe)

KJV Translation

describe, record, prescribe, subscribe, write(-ing, -ten).
