σκληρός

sklērós — G4642

Transliteration: sklērós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4642

Derivation: from the base of G4628 (σκέλος);

Definition

dry, i.e. hard or tough (figuratively, harsh, severe)

KJV Translation

fierce, hard

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

σκληρός, -ά, -όν
(σκέλλω, to dry), [in LXX chiefly for קָשָׁה ;]
hard to the touch, rough, harsh, (opposite to μαλακός); metaphorically, in various uses;
__(a) of men, hard, stern, severe: Mat.25:24;
__(b) of things, hard, rough, violent: σκληρόν σοι (ἐστι), Act.26:14; λόγος, Jhn.6:60; ἄνεμος, Jas.3:4; σκληρὰ λαλεῖν, Jude 15.†
