καταμαρτυρέω

katamartyréō — G2649

Transliteration: katamartyréō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2649

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G3140 (μαρτυρέω);

Definition

to testify against

KJV Translation

witness against

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

καταμαρτῠρέω,
bear witness against, τινος [Refs 5th c.BC+], etc.; κατά τινος [NT+5th c.BC+]:—passive, have evidence given against one, μὴ πιστῶς καταμαρτυρηθείς [Refs 5th c.BC+]; κ. ὑπὸ τοῦ βίου τοῦ ἑαυτοῦ to be convicted, [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__2 passive, of evidence, to be given against one, ἃ καταμαρτυρεῖται αὐτοῦ [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__II assert concerning, οὐδὲν κ. τῶν οὐ παρόντων [Refs 3rd c.AD+]
__III Astrology texts, exercise malign influence over, 'aspect', [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
