προσκεφάλαιον

proskephálaion — G4344

Transliteration: proskephálaion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4344

Derivation: neuter of a presumed compound of G4314 (πρός) and G2776 (κεφαλή);

Definition

something for the head, i.e. a cushion

KJV Translation

pillow
