אֵצֶל

ʼêtsel — H681

Transliteration: ʼêtsel

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H681

Derivation: from H680 (אָצַל) (in the sense of joining);

Definition

a side; (as a preposition) near

KJV Translation

at, (hard) by, (from) (beside), near (unto), toward, with. See also H1018 (בֵּית הָאֵצֶל).
