Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4792

συγκομίζω

synkomízō — G4792

Transliteration: synkomízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4792

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G2865 (κομίζω);

Definition

to convey together, i.e. collect or bear away in company with others

KJV Translation

carry
