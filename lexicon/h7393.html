Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H7393

רֶכֶב

rekeb — H7393

Transliteration: rekeb

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7393

Derivation: from H7392 (רָכַב);

Definition

a vehicle; by implication, a team; by extension, cavalry; by analogy a rider, i.e. the upper millstone

KJV Translation

chariot, (upper) millstone, multitude (from the margin), wagon.
