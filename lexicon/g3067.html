λουτρόν

loutrón — G3067

Transliteration: loutrón

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3067

Derivation: from G3068 (λούω);

Definition

a bath, i.e. (figuratively), baptism

KJV Translation

washing
