ἐκπληρόω

ekplēróō — G1603

Transliteration: ekplēróō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1603

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G4137 (πληρόω);

Definition

to accomplish entirely

KJV Translation

fulfill

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

__3 man completely, τριήρεις [Refs 4th c.BC+]

__5 pay off, τὸ χρέος [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II ἐ. λιμένα πλάτῃ make one's way over, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
