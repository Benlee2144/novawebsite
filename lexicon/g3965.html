πατριά

patriá — G3965

Transliteration: patriá

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3965

Derivation: as if feminine of a derivative of G3962 (πατήρ);

Definition

paternal descent, i.e. (concretely) a group of families or a whole race (nation)

KJV Translation

family, kindred, lineage

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
