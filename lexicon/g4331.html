προσεγγίζω

prosengízō — G4331

Transliteration: prosengízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4331

Derivation: from G4314 (πρός) and G1448 (ἐγγίζω);

Definition

to approach near

KJV Translation

come nigh

[in LXX chiefly for נָגַשׁ, קָרַב ;]
__1. trans., to bring near (Luc.).
__2. Intrans., to approach: with dative, Mrk.2:4 (WH, προσενέγκαι).†
