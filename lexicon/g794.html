ἄστοργος

ástorgos — G794

Transliteration: ástorgos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G794

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of (to cherish affectionately);

Definition

hard-hearted towards kindred

KJV Translation

without natural affection
