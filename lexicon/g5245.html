ὑπερνικάω

hypernikáō — G5245

Transliteration: hypernikáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5245

Derivation: from G5228 (ὑπέρ) and G3528 (νικάω);

Definition

to vanquish beyond, i.e. gain a decisive victory

KJV Translation

more than conquer
