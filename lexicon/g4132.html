πλήμμυρα

plḗmmyra — G4132

Transliteration: plḗmmyra

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4132

Derivation: prolonged from G4130 (πλήθω);

Definition

flood-tide, i.e. (by analogy) a freshet

KJV Translation

flood

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

πλήμῡρ-α, ἡ,
={πλημυρίς}, flood-tide, flood, [NT+4th c.BC+]; inundation of the Nile, [Refs 3rd c.AD+]: metaphorically, λόγου [Refs 1st c.AD+]
__2 in Medicine texts, accumulation, excessive flow, Lycusap.[Refs 2nd c.AD+]
