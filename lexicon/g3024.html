Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3024

Definition

KJV Translation

+ forget

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

λήθ-η,
Doric dialect λάθα, ἡ, forgetting, forgetfulness, personified in [Refs 8th c.BC+]; λήθην ποιεύμενος τά μιν ἐόργεε forgetting.., [Refs 5th c.BC+]; λήθη λαμβάνει, ἔχει τινά, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; εἰς λ. ἀφιγμένα forgotten, [Refs 1st c.BC+]
