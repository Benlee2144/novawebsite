γνήσιος

gnḗsios — G1103

Transliteration: gnḗsios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1103

Derivation: from the same as G1077 (γενέσια);

Definition

legitimate (of birth), i.e. genuine

KJV Translation

own, sincerity, true

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

γνήσιος, -α, -ον
(γίγνομαι), [in LXX: Sir.7:18, 3Ma.3:19 * ;]
__1. prop., lawfully begotten, born in wedlock (in π., γυνὴ γ., a lawful wife; MM, see word).
__2. true, genuine, sincere: Php.4:3, 1Ti.1:2, Tit.1:4; as subst., τὸ γ. = ἡ γνησιότης, sincerity, 2Co.8:8.
† (AS)
