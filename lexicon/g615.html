ἀποκτείνω

apokteínō — G615

Transliteration: apokteínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G615

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and (to slay);

Definition

to kill outright; figuratively, to destroy

KJV Translation

put to death, kill, slay
