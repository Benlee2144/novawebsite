יְרֻבַּעַל

Yᵉrubbaʻal — H3378

Transliteration: Yᵉrubbaʻal

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3378

Derivation: from H7378 (רִיב) and H1168 (בַּעַל); Baal will contend;

Definition

Jerubbaal, a symbolic name of Gideon

KJV Translation

Jerubbaal.

son of: Joash (H3101);
father of: Jether (H3500), Abimelech (H0040H) and Jotham (H3147); also called Jerubbaal at Jdg.6.32; 7.1; 8.29,35; 9.1,2,5,16,19,24,28,57; 1xJerubbesheth at 2Sa.11.21;
Another name of gid.on (גִּדְעוֹן "Gideon" H1439)

name given to Gideon by his father when he destroyed the altar of Baal
