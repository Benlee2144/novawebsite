זֶבַח

zebach — H2077

Transliteration: zebach

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2077

Derivation: from H2076 (זָבַח);

Definition

properly, a slaughter, i.e. the flesh of an animal; by implication, a sacrifice (the victim or the act)

KJV Translation

offer(-ing), sacrifice.
