ζωογονέω

zōogonéō — G2225

Transliteration: zōogonéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2225

Derivation: from the same as G2226 (ζῶον) and a derivative of G1096 (γίνομαι);

Definition

to engender alive, i.e. (by analogy) to rescue (passively, be saved) from death

KJV Translation

live, preserve
