Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G5288

ὑποστέλλω

hypostéllō — G5288

Transliteration: hypostéllō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5288

Derivation: from G5259 (ὑπό) and G4724 (στέλλω);

Definition

to withhold under (out of sight), i.e. (reflexively) to cower or shrink, (figuratively) to conceal (reserve)

KJV Translation

draw (keep) back, shun, withdraw
