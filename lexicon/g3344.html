μεταστρέφω

metastréphō — G3344

Transliteration: metastréphō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3344

Derivation: from G3326 (μετά) and G4762 (στρέφω);

Definition

to turn across, i.e. transmute or (figuratively) corrupt

KJV Translation

pervert, turn
