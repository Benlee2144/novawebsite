ἀνέγκλητος

anénklētos — G410

Transliteration: anénklētos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G410

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G1458 (ἐγκαλέω);

Definition

unaccused, i.e. (by implication) irreproachable

KJV Translation

blameless
