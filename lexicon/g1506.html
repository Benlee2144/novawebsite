εἰλικρινής

eilikrinḗs — G1506

Transliteration: eilikrinḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1506

Derivation: from (the sun's ray) and G2919 (κρίνω);

Definition

judged by sunlight, i.e. tested as genuine (figuratively)

KJV Translation

pure, sincere
