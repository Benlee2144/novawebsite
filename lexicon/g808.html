ἀσχημοσύνη

aschēmosýnē — G808

Transliteration: aschēmosýnē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G808

Derivation: from G809 (ἀσχήμων);

Definition

an indecency; by implication, the pudenda

KJV Translation

shame, that which is unseemly

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

__3 ἀ. φέρει brings discredit, disgrace, [Refs]
