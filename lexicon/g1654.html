ἐλεημοσύνη

eleēmosýnē — G1654

Transliteration: eleēmosýnē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1654

Derivation: from G1656 (ἔλεος);

Definition

compassionateness, i.e. (as exercised towards the poor) beneficence, or (concretely) a benefaction

KJV Translation

alms(-deeds)
