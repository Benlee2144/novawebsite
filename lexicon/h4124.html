מוֹאָב

Môwʼâb — H4124

Transliteration: Môwʼâb

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4124

Derivation: from a prolonged form of the prepositional prefix m-and H1 (אָב); from (her (the mother's)) father;

Definition

Moab, an incestuous son of Lot; also his territory and descendants

KJV Translation

Moab.
