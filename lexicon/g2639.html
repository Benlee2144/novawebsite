καταλέγω

katalégō — G2639

Transliteration: katalégō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2639

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G3004 (λέγω) (in its original meaning);

Definition

to lay down, i.e. (figuratively) to enrol

KJV Translation

take into the number
