κατήγορος

katḗgoros — G2725

Transliteration: katḗgoros

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2725

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G58 (ἀγορά);

Definition

against one in the assembly, i.e. a complainant at law; specially, Satan

KJV Translation

accuser
