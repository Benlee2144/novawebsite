Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H7683

שָׁגַג

shâgag — H7683

Transliteration: shâgag

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7683

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to stray, i.e. (figuratively) sin (with more or less apology)

KJV Translation

[idiom] also for that, deceived, err, go astray, sin ignorantly.

Appears In (3 verses)

Job 12:16Leviticus 5:18Psalms 119:67

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

1) to go astray, err, commit sin or error
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to err (mentally)
1a2) to sin (ignorantly or inadvertently)
