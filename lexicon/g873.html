ἀφορίζω

aphorízō — G873

Transliteration: aphorízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G873

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G3724 (ὁρίζω);

Definition

to set off by boundary, i.e. (figuratively) limit, exclude, appoint, etc.

KJV Translation

divide, separate, sever
