בְּעַד

bᵉʻad — H1157

Transliteration: bᵉʻad

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1157

Derivation: from H5704 (עַד) with prepositional prefix;

Definition

in up to or over against; generally at, beside, among, behind, for, etc.

KJV Translation

about, at by (means of), for, over, through, up (-on), within.
