ἐκκλίνω

ekklínō — G1578

Transliteration: ekklínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1578

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G2827 (κλίνω);

Definition

to deviate, i.e. (absolutely) to shun (literally or figuratively), or (relatively) to decline (from piety)

KJV Translation

avoid, eschew, go out of the way

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
