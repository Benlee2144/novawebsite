εὐτόνως

eutónōs — G2159

Transliteration: eutónōs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2159

Derivation: adverb from a compound of G2095 (εὖ) and a derivative of (to stretch);

Definition

in a well-strung manner, i.e. (figuratively) intensely (in a good sense, cogently; in a bad one, fiercely)

KJV Translation

mightily, vehemently
