πρόσκαιρος

próskairos — G4340

Transliteration: próskairos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4340

Derivation: from G4314 (πρός) and G2540 (καιρός);

Definition

for the occasion only, i.e. temporary

KJV Translation

dur-(eth) for awhile, endure for a time, for a season, temporal
