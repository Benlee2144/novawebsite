לָחַץ

lâchats — H3905

Transliteration: lâchats

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3905

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

properly, to press, i.e. (figuratively) to distress

KJV Translation

afflict, crush, force, hold fast, oppress(-or), thrust self.
