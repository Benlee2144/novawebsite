ʼâz — H227

Transliteration: ʼâz

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H227

Derivation: a demonstrative adverb;

Definition

at that time or place; also as a conjunction, therefore

KJV Translation

beginning, for, from, hitherto, now, of old, once, since, then, at which time, yet.
