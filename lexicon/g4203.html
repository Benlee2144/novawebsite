πορνεύω

porneúō — G4203

Transliteration: porneúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4203

Derivation: from G4204 (πόρνη);

Definition

to act the harlot, i.e. (literally) indulge unlawful lust (of either sex), or (figuratively) practise idolatry

KJV Translation

commit (fornication)

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
