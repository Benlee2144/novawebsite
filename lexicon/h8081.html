שֶׁמֶן

shemen — H8081

Transliteration: shemen

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H8081

Derivation: from H8080 (שָׁמַן);

Definition

grease, especially liquid (as from the olive, often perfumed); figuratively, richness

KJV Translation

anointing, [idiom] fat (things), [idiom] fruitful, oil(-ed), ointment, olive, [phrase] pine.

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

1) fat, oil
1a) fat, fatness
1b) oil, olive oil
1b1) as staple, medicament or unguent
1b2) for anointing
1c) fat (of fruitful land, valleys) (metaph)
