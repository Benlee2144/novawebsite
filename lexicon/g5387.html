φιλόστοργος

philóstorgos — G5387

Transliteration: philóstorgos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5387

Derivation: from G5384 (φίλος) and (cherishing one's kindred, especially parents or children);

Definition

fond of natural relatives, i.e. fraternal towards fellow Christian

KJV Translation

kindly affectioned
