θυμιαστήριον

thymiastḗrion — G2369

Transliteration: thymiastḗrion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2369

Derivation: from a derivative of G2370 (θυμιάω);

Definition

a place of fumigation, i.e. the alter of incense (in the Temple)

KJV Translation

censer
