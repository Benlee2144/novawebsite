Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1261

διαλογισμός

dialogismós — G1261

Transliteration: dialogismós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1261

Derivation: from G1260 (διαλογίζομαι);

Definition

discussion, i.e. (internal) consideration (by implication, purpose), or (external) debate

KJV Translation

dispute, doubtful(-ing), imagination, reasoning, thought

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
