צָוָה

tsâvâh — H6680

Transliteration: tsâvâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6680

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

(intensively) to constitute, enjoin

KJV Translation

appoint, (for-) bid, (give a) charge, (give a, give in, send with) command(-er, -ment), send a messenger, put, (set) in order.
