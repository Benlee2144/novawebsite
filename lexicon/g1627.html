ἐκφέρω

ekphérō — G1627

Transliteration: ekphérō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1627

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G5342 (φέρω);

Definition

to bear out (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

bear, bring forth, carry forth (out)
