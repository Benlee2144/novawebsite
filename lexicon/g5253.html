Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G5253

ὑπερῷον

hyperōion — G5253

Transliteration: hyperōion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5253

Derivation: neuter of a derivative of G5228 (ὑπέρ);

Definition

a higher part of the house, i.e. apartment in the third story

KJV Translation

upper chamber (room)
