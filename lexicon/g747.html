ἀρχηγός

archēgós — G747

Transliteration: archēgós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G747

Derivation: from G746 (ἀρχή) and G71 (ἄγω);

Definition

a chief leader

KJV Translation

author, captain, prince
