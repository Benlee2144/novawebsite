Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H7641

שִׁבֹּל

shibbôl — H7641

Transliteration: shibbôl

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7641

Derivation: or (feminine) שִׁבֹּלֶת; from the same as H7640 (שֹׁבֶל);

Definition

a stream (as flowing); also an ear of grain (as growing out); by analogy, a branch

KJV Translation

branch, channel, ear (of corn), (water-)flood, Shibboleth. Compare H5451 (סִבֹּלֶת).
