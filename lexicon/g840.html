Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G840

αὐστηρός

austērós — G840

Transliteration: austērós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G840

Derivation: from a (presumed) derivative of the same as G109 (ἀήρ) (meaning blown);

Definition

rough (properly as a gale), i.e. (figuratively) severe

KJV Translation

austere
