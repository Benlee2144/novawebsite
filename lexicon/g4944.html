Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4944

συνωδίνω

synōdínō — G4944

Transliteration: synōdínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4944

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G5605 (ὠδίνω);

Definition

to have (parturition) pangs in company (concert, simultaneously) with, i.e. (figuratively) to sympathize (in expectation of relief from suffering)

KJV Translation

travail in pain together

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
