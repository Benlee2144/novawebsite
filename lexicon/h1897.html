הָגָה

hâgâh — H1897

Transliteration: hâgâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1897

Derivation: a primitive root (compare H1901 (הָגִיג));

Definition

to murmur (in pleasure or anger); by implication, to ponder

KJV Translation

imagine, meditate, mourn, mutter, roar, [idiom] sore, speak, study, talk, utter.
