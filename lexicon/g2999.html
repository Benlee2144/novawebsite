λατρεία

latreía — G2999

Transliteration: latreía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2999

Derivation: from G3000 (λατρεύω);

Definition

ministration of God, i.e. worship

KJV Translation

(divine) service

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

λατρ-εία, ἡ,
the state of a hired labourer, service, [Refs 5th c.BC+], etc.: metaphorically, the business or duties of life, [Refs 1st c.AD+]
__2 λ. τοῦ θεοῦ, θεῶν, service to the gods, divine worship, [NT+5th c.BC+]
