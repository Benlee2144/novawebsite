προβάλλω

probállō — G4261

Transliteration: probállō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4261

Derivation: from G4253 (πρό) and G906 (βάλλω);

Definition

to throw forward, i.e. push to the front, germinate

KJV Translation

put forward, shoot forth

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
