μάλιστα

málista — G3122

Transliteration: málista

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3122

Derivation: neuter plural of the superlative of an apparently primary adverb (very);

Definition

(adverbially) most (in the greatest degree) or particularly

KJV Translation

chiefly, most of all, (e-)specially
