שָׁם

shâm — H8033

Transliteration: shâm

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H8033

Derivation: a primitive particle (rather from the relative pronoun, H834 (אֲשֶׁר));

Definition

there (transferring to time) then; often thither, or thence

KJV Translation

in it, [phrase] thence, there (-in, [phrase] of, [phrase] out), [phrase] thither, [phrase] whither.
