σαίνω

saínō — G4525

Transliteration: saínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4525

Derivation: akin to G4579 (σείω);

Definition

to wag (as a dog its tail fawningly), i.e. (generally) to shake (figuratively, disturb)

KJV Translation

move
