Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4878

συναντιλαμβάνομαι

synantilambánomai — G4878

Transliteration: synantilambánomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4878

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G482 (ἀντιλαμβάνομαι);

Definition

to take hold of opposite together, i.e. co-operate (assist)

KJV Translation

help

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

συναντι-λαμβάνομαι,
middle, help in gaining a thing, τῆς ἐλευθερίας [Refs 3rd c.BC+]; τῆς θεραπείας [Refs 1st c.BC+]; assist in supporting, τὴν ὁρμὴν τοῦ λαοῦ [LXX]: absolutely, render assistance, περί τινων [Refs 3rd c.BC+]; εἴς τι [Refs 3rd c.BC+]
__II with dative, take part with, help, [LXX+NT]
