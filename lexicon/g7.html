Ἀβιά

Abiá — G7

Transliteration: Abiá

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G7

Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H029);

Definition

Abijah, the name of two Israelites

KJV Translation

Abia

Abia, Abijah.
