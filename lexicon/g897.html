Βαβυλών

Babylṓn — G897

Transliteration: Babylṓn

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G897

Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H0894);

Definition

Babylon, the capitol of Chaldæa (literally or figuratively (as a type of tyranny))

KJV Translation

Babylon

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

Βαβυλών, -ῶνος, ἡ (בָּבֶל, Heb. form of Assyr. Bab-ili, Gate of God),
Babylon: Mat.1:11, 12 17, Act.7:43" (LXX) ; symbolically, of Rome: Rev.14:8 16:19 17:5 18:2, 10, 21, and prob. also 1Pe.5:13.†
