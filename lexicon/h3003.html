יָבֵשׁ

Yâbêsh — H3003

Transliteration: Yâbêsh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3003

Derivation: the same as H3002 (יָבֵשׁ) (also יָבֵישׁ; often with the addition of H1568 (גִּלְעָד), i.e. Jabesh of Gilad);

Definition

Jobesh, the name of an Israelite and of a place in Palestine

KJV Translation

Jobesh (-Gilead).
