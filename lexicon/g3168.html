μεγαλειότης

megaleiótēs — G3168

Transliteration: megaleiótēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3168

Derivation: from G3167 (μεγαλεῖος);

Definition

superbness, i.e. glory or splendor

KJV Translation

magnificence, majesty, mighty power
