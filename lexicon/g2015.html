ἐπιφάνεια

epipháneia — G2015

Transliteration: epipháneia

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2015

Derivation: from G2016 (ἐπιφανής);

Definition

a manifestation, i.e. (specially) the advent of Christ (past or future)

KJV Translation

appearing, brightness

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐπιφάν-εια
(i.e. ἱερά), τά, sacrifices in celebration of an ἐπιφάνεια, Δημήτριος τὰ ἐ. τοῦ ἀδελφοῦ θύων [Refs 2nd c.BC+]
