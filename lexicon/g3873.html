παράκειμαι

parákeimai — G3873

Transliteration: parákeimai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3873

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and G2749 (κεῖμαι);

Definition

to lie near, i.e. be at hand (figuratively, be prompt or easy)

KJV Translation

be present

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

παράκειμαι,
