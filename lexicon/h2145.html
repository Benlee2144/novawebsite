זָכָר

zâkâr — H2145

Transliteration: zâkâr

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2145

Derivation: from H2142 (זָכַר);

Definition

properly, remembered, i.e. a male (of man or animals, as being the most noteworthy sex)

KJV Translation

[idiom] him, male, man(child, -kind).
