καταράομαι

kataráomai — G2672

Transliteration: kataráomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2672

Derivation: middle voice from G2671 (κατάρα);

Definition

to execrate; by analogy, to doom

KJV Translation

curse

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
