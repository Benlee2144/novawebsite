ἐπιγίνομαι

epigínomai — G1920

Transliteration: epigínomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1920

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G1096 (γίνομαι);

Definition

to arrive upon, i.e. spring up (as a wind)

KJV Translation

blow
