Βαραββᾶς

Barabbâs — G912

Transliteration: Barabbâs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G912

Derivation: of Chaldee origin (H01347 and H05);

Definition

son of Abba; Bar-abbas, an Israelite

KJV Translation

Barabbas

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

Βαραββᾶς, -ᾶ, ὁ (Aram. בַּר־אַבָּא, lit., son of a father, i.e. accusative to Jerome, filius magistri),
Barabbas: Mat.27:16, 17 20, 21, 26, Mrk.15:7, 11 15, Luk.23:18, Jhn.18:40. (In Mat.27:16, some MSS. read Ἰησοῦν Β.; see WH, App., 19f.).†
