Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4893

συνείδησις

syneídēsis — G4893

Transliteration: syneídēsis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4893

Derivation: from a prolonged form of G4894 (συνείδω);

Definition

co-perception, i.e. moral consciousness

KJV Translation

conscience
