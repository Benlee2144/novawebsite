דָּמָה

dâmâh — H1819

Transliteration: dâmâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1819

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to compare; by implication, to resemble, liken, consider

KJV Translation

compare, devise, (be) like(-n), mean, think, use similitudes.
