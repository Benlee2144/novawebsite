κατακλίνω

kataklínō — G2625

Transliteration: kataklínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2625

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G2827 (κλίνω);

Definition

to recline down, i.e. (specially) to take a place at table

KJV Translation

(make) sit down (at meat)
