ἐπισκιάζω

episkiázō — G1982

Transliteration: episkiázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1982

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and a derivative of G4639 (σκιά);

Definition

to cast a shade upon, i.e. (by analogy) to envelop in a haze of brilliancy; figuratively, to invest with preternatural influence

KJV Translation

overshadow
