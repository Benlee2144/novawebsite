ʼôwr — H215

Transliteration: ʼôwr

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H215

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to be (causative, make) luminous (literally and metaphorically)

KJV Translation

[idiom] break of day, glorious, kindle, (be, en-, give, show) light (-en, -ened), set on fire, shine.
