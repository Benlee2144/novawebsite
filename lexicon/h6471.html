פַּעַם

paʻam — H6471

Transliteration: paʻam

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6471

Derivation: or (feminine) פַּעֲמָה; from H6470 (פָּעַם);

Definition

a stroke, literally or figuratively (in various applications, as follow)

KJV Translation

anvil, corner, foot(-step), going, (hundred-) fold, [idiom] now, (this) [phrase] once, order, rank, step, [phrase] thrice, (often-), second, this, two) time(-s), twice, wheel.
