Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4042

περιοχή

periochḗ — G4042

Transliteration: periochḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4042

Derivation: from G4023 (περιέχω);

Definition

a being held around, i.e. (concretely) a passage (of Scripture, as circumscribed)

KJV Translation

place
