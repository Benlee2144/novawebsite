Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3114

μακροθυμέω

makrothyméō — G3114

Transliteration: makrothyméō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3114

Derivation: from the same as G3116 (μακροθυμώς);

Definition

to be long-spirited, i.e. (objectively) forbearing or (subjectively) patient

KJV Translation

bear (suffer) long, be longsuffering, have (long) patience, be patient, patiently endure
