Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4802

συζητέω

syzētéō — G4802

Transliteration: syzētéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4802

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G2212 (ζητέω);

Definition

to investigate jointly, i.e. discuss, controvert, cavil

KJV Translation

dispute (with), enquire, question (with), reason (together)
