סָרַח

çârach — H5628

Transliteration: çârach

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5628

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to extend (even to excess)

KJV Translation

exceeding, hand, spread, stretch self, banish.
