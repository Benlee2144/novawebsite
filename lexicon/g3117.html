μακρός

makrós — G3117

Transliteration: makrós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3117

Derivation: from G3372 (μῆκος);

Definition

long (in place (distant) or time (neuter plural))

KJV Translation

far, long

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

μακρός, ά, όν [ᾰ
