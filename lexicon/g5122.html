τοὔνομα

toúnoma — G5122

Transliteration: toúnoma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5122

Derivation: contraction for the neuter of G3588 (ὁ) and G3686 (ὄνομα);

Definition

the name (is)

KJV Translation

named
