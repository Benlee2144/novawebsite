חֵיק

chêyq — H2436

Transliteration: chêyq

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2436

Derivation: or חֵק; and חוֹק; from an unused root, apparently meaning to inclose;

Definition

the bosom (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

bosom, bottom, lap, midst, within.
