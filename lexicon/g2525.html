καθίστημι

kathístēmi — G2525

Transliteration: kathístēmi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2525

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G2476 (ἵστημι);

Definition

to place down (permanently), i.e. (figuratively) to designate, constitute, convoy

KJV Translation

appoint, be, conduct, make, ordain, set
