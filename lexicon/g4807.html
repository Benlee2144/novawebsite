συκάμινος

sykáminos — G4807

Transliteration: sykáminos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4807

Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H08256) in imitation of G4809 (συκομωραία);

Definition

a sycamore-fig tree

KJV Translation

sycamine tree

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

σῡκᾰμιν-ος, ἡ,
more rarely ὁ,= μορέα, mulberry-tree, ὁ σ. συκάμιν᾽, ὁρᾷς, φορεῖ [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__II σ. ἡ Αἰγυπτία,= συκόμορος,[NT+3rd c.BC+]
__III σ. ἀγρία, ={βάτος} (A), [Refs 4th c.BC+]
