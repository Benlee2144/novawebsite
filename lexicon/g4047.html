περιποίησις

peripoíēsis — G4047

Transliteration: peripoíēsis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4047

Derivation: from G4046 (περιποιέομαι);

Definition

acquisition (the act or the thing); by extension, preservation

KJV Translation

obtain(-ing), peculiar, purchased, possession, saving
