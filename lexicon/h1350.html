Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H1350

גָּאַל

gâʼal — H1350

Transliteration: gâʼal

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1350

Derivation: a primitive root, to redeem (according to the Oriental law of kinship), i.e.

Definition

to be the next of kin (and as such to buy back a relative's property, marry his widow, etc.)

KJV Translation

[idiom] in any wise, [idiom] at all, avenger, deliver, (do, perform the part of near, next) kinsfolk(-man), purchase, ransom, redeem(-er), revenger.
