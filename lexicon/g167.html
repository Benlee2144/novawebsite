ἀκαθαρσία

akatharsía — G167

Transliteration: akatharsía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G167

Derivation: from G169 (ἀκάθαρτος);

Definition

impurity (the quality), physically or morally

KJV Translation

uncleanness

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀκᾰθαρ-σία, ἡ,
uncleanness, foulness, of a wound or sore, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__b dirt, filth, [Refs 1st c.BC+]
__2 in moral sense, depravity, [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__3 ceremonial impurity, [LXX]
