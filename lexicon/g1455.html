ἐγκάθετος

enkáthetos — G1455

Transliteration: enkáthetos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1455

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and a derivative of G2524 (καθίημι);

Definition

subinduced, i.e. surreptitiously suborned as a lier-in-wait

KJV Translation

spy

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐγκάθ-ετος, ον,
(ἐγκαθίημι) put in secretly, suborned, προέδρους ἐ. ὑφέντες [NT+5th c.BC+]. adverb -τως, δημηγορεῖν [Refs 1st c.BC+]
__II of a child, ={εἰσποιητός}, [Refs 4th c.BC+]
