Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4788

συγκλείω

synkleíō — G4788

Transliteration: synkleíō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4788

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G2808 (κλείω);

Definition

to shut together, i.e. include or (figuratively) embrace in a common subjection to

KJV Translation

conclude, inclose, shut up
