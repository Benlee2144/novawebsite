ὠφέλεια

ōphéleia — G5622

Transliteration: ōphéleia

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5622

Derivation: from a derivative of the base of G5624 (ὠφέλιμος);

Definition

usefulness, i.e. benefit

KJV Translation

advantage, profit
