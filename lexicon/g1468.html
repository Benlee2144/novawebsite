ἐγκρατής

enkratḗs — G1468

Transliteration: enkratḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1468

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and G2904 (κράτος);

Definition

strong in a thing (masterful), i.e. (figuratively and reflexively) self-controlled (in appetite, etc.)

KJV Translation

temperate
