μεταβαίνω

metabaínō — G3327

Transliteration: metabaínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3327

Derivation: from G3326 (μετά) and the base of G939 (βάσις);

Definition

to change place

KJV Translation

depart, go, pass, remove
