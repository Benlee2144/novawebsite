Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G5081

τηλαυγῶς

tēlaugōs — G5081

Transliteration: tēlaugōs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5081

Derivation: adverb from a compound of a derivative of G5056 (τέλος) and G827 (αὐγή);

Definition

in a far-shining manner, i.e. plainly

KJV Translation

clearly

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

Included with: τηλαυγ-ής, ές,
(τῆλε, αὐγή) far-shining, far-beaming, πρόσωπον, of the sun, [Refs]; φάος, φέγγος, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ἀκτίς, ἀκτίνων σέλας, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; πρόσωπον θέμεν τ. to make it beam from afar, [Refs] luminous meaning, [Refs 1st c.BC+]
__II of distant objects, far-seen, conspicuous, σκοπιή [Refs 6th c.BC+]; of leprosy, [LXX]
__III far-seeing, αἴσθησις, ψυχή, [Refs 5th c.BC+] adverb -γῶς, τηλαυγέστερον ὁρᾶν to see more clearly, [NT+1st c.BC+]

Mark 8:25
