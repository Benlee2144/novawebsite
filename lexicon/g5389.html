φιλοτιμέομαι

philotiméomai — G5389

Transliteration: philotiméomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5389

Derivation: middle voice from a compound of G5384 (φίλος) and G5092 (τιμή);

Definition

to be fond of honor, i.e. emulous (eager or earnest to do something)

KJV Translation

labour, strive, study
