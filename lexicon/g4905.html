συνέρχομαι

synérchomai — G4905

Transliteration: synérchomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4905

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G2064 (ἔρχομαι);

Definition

to convene, depart in company with, associate with, or (specially), cohabit (conjugally)

KJV Translation

accompany, assemble (with), come (together), come (company, go) with, resort
