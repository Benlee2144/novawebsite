Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4433

πτωχεύω

ptōcheúō — G4433

Transliteration: ptōcheúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4433

Derivation: from G4434 (πτωχός);

Definition

to be a beggar, i.e. (by implication) to become indigent (figuratively)

KJV Translation

become poor

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

__2 to be as poor as a beggar, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__3 metaphorically with genitive, to be badly off for, πραγμάτων, of historians, [Refs 2nd c.BC+]
