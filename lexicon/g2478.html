ἰσχυρός

ischyrós — G2478

Transliteration: ischyrós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2478

Derivation: from G2479 (ἰσχύς);

Definition

forcible (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

boisterous, mighty(-ier), powerful, strong(-er, man), valiant
