Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H6446

פַּס

paç — H6446

Transliteration: paç

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6446

Derivation: from H6461 (פָּסַס); properly, the palm (of the hand) or sole (of the foot) (compare H6447 (פַּס)); by implication (plural)

Definition

a long and sleeved tunic (perhaps simply a wide one; from the original sense of the root, i.e. of many breadths)

KJV Translation

(divers) colours.

Appears In (2 verses)

2 Samuel 13:18Genesis 37:3

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

1) flat (of the hand or foot), palm, sole
1a) of the tunic reaching to palms and soles (fig.)
