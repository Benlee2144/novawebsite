מַחֲלֹקֶת

machălôqeth — H4256

Transliteration: machălôqeth

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4256

Derivation: from H2505 (חָלַק);

Definition

a section (of Levites, people or soldiers)

KJV Translation

company, course, division, portion. See also H5555 (סֶלַע הַמַּחְלְקוֹת).
