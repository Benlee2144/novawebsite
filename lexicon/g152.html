αἰσχύνη

aischýnē — G152

Transliteration: aischýnē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G152

Derivation: from G153 (αἰσχύνομαι);

Definition

shame or disgrace (abstractly or concretely)

KJV Translation

dishonesty, shame
