πρεσβυτέριον

presbytérion — G4244

Transliteration: presbytérion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4244

Derivation: neuter of a presumed derivative of G4245 (πρεσβύτερος);

Definition

the order of elders, i.e. (specially), Israelite Sanhedrin or Christian \

KJV Translation

(estate of) elder(-s), presbytery
