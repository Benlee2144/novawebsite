ἐξηγέομαι

exēgéomai — G1834

Transliteration: exēgéomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1834

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G2233 (ἡγέομαι);

Definition

to consider out (aloud), i.e. rehearse, unfold

KJV Translation

declare, tell
