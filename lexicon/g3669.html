ὁμοίωσις

homoíōsis — G3669

Transliteration: homoíōsis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3669

Derivation: from G3666 (ὁμοιόω);

Definition

assimilation, i.e. resemblance

KJV Translation

similitude

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

ὁμοίωσις, -εως, ἡ
(ὁμοιόω) [in LXX chiefly for דְּמוּת, Psa.58:4, al. ;]
__1. a making like, becoming like (Plat.).
__2. likeness: Jas.3:9 (LXX).†
SYN.: see: ὁμοίωμα, and cf. Tr., Syn., § xv (AS)
