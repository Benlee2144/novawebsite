καλῶς

kalōs — G2573

Transliteration: kalōs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2573

Derivation: adverb from G2570 (καλός);

Definition

well (usually morally)

KJV Translation

(in a) good (place), honestly, + recover, (full) well
