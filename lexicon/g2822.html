κλητός

klētós — G2822

Transliteration: klētós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2822

Derivation: from the same as G2821 (κλῆσις);

Definition

invited, i.e. appointed, or (specially), a saint

KJV Translation

called

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

κλητ-ός, ή, όν,
invited, [Refs 4th c.BC+]; welcome, [Refs 8th c.BC+]
__2 called out, chosen, [Refs 8th c.BC+]
__3 invoked, [Refs]
__4 summoned to court, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
__II substantive κλητή (i.e. ἐκκλησία), ἡ, convocation, [LXX]
