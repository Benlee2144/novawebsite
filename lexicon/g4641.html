σκληροκαρδία

sklērokardía — G4641

Transliteration: sklērokardía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4641

Derivation: feminine of a compound of G4642 (σκληρός) and G2588 (καρδία);

Definition

hard-heartedness, i.e. (specially), destitution of (spiritual) perception

KJV Translation

hardness of heart
