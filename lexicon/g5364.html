φιλανθρώπως

philanthrṓpōs — G5364

Transliteration: philanthrṓpōs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5364

Derivation: adverb from a compound of G5384 (φίλος) and G444 (ἄνθρωπος);

Definition

fondly to man (\

KJV Translation

courteously
