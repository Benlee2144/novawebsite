κατακλύζω

kataklýzō — G2626

Transliteration: kataklýzō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2626

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and the base of G2830 (κλύδων);

Definition

to dash (wash) down, i.e. (by implication) to deluge

KJV Translation

overflow
