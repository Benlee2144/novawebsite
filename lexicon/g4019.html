περιδέω

peridéō — G4019

Transliteration: peridéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4019

Derivation: from G4012 (περί) and G1210 (δέω);

Definition

to bind around one, i.e. enwrap

KJV Translation

bind about

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

περιδέω,

__2 bind round, bandage with, ἱμᾶσι πόδα [Refs 5th c.BC+]
