ὀφθαλμός

ophthalmós — G3788

Transliteration: ophthalmós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3788

Derivation: from G3700 (ὀπτάνομαι);

Definition

the eye (literally or figuratively); by implication, vision; figuratively, envy (from the jealous side-glance)

KJV Translation

eye, sight
