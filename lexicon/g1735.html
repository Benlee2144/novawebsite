ἐνδέχεται

endéchetai — G1735

Transliteration: endéchetai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1735

Derivation: third person singular present of a compound of G1722 (ἐν) and G1209 (δέχομαι);

Definition

(impersonally) it is accepted in, i.e. admitted (possible)

KJV Translation

can (+ not) be
