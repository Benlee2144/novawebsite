ἀτιμάζω

atimázō — G818

Transliteration: atimázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G818

Derivation: from G820 (ἄτιμος);

Definition

to render infamous, i.e. (by implication) contemn or maltreat

KJV Translation

despise, dishonour, suffer shame, entreat shamefully
