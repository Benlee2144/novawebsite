Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1075

γενεαλογέω

genealogéō — G1075

Transliteration: genealogéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1075

Derivation: from G1074 (γενεά) and G3056 (λόγος);

Definition

to reckon by generations, i.e. trace in genealogy

KJV Translation

count by descent
