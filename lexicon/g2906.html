κραυγή

kraugḗ — G2906

Transliteration: kraugḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2906

Derivation: from G2896 (κράζω);

Definition

an outcry (in notification, tumult or grief)

KJV Translation

clamour, cry(-ing)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

κραυγ-ή, ἡ,
crying, screaming, shouting, τίς ἥδε κ.; [Refs 6th c.BC+]; rarely of a shout of joy, [NT+5th c.BC+]
