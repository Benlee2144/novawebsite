στερεόω

stereóō — G4732

Transliteration: stereóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4732

Derivation: from G4731 (στερεός);

Definition

to solidify, i.e. confirm (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

establish, receive strength, make strong
