πορνεία

porneía — G4202

Transliteration: porneía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4202

Derivation: from G4203 (πορνεύω);

Definition

harlotry (including adultery and incest); figuratively, idolatry

KJV Translation

fornication
