Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3573

νυστάζω

nystázō — G3573

Transliteration: nystázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3573

Derivation: from a presumed derivative of G3506 (νεύω);

Definition

to nod, i.e. (by implication) to fall asleep; figuratively, to delay

KJV Translation

slumber

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
