ὑπομένω

hypoménō — G5278

Transliteration: hypoménō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5278

Derivation: from G5259 (ὑπό) and G3306 (μένω);

Definition

to stay under (behind), i.e. remain; figuratively, to undergo, i.e. bear (trials), have fortitude, persevere

KJV Translation

abide, endure, (take) patient(-ly), suffer, tarry behind
