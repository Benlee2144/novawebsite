ἐκτός

ektós — G1622

Transliteration: ektós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1622

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ);

Definition

the exterior; figuratively (as a preposition) aside from, besides

KJV Translation

but, except(-ed), other than, out of, outside, unless, without
