ἐμβλέπω

emblépō — G1689

Transliteration: emblépō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1689

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and G991 (βλέπω);

Definition

to look on, i.e. (relatively) to observe fixedly, or (absolutely) to discern clearly

KJV Translation

behold, gaze up, look upon, (could) see
