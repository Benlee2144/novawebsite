ὑπόδειγμα

hypódeigma — G5262

Transliteration: hypódeigma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5262

Derivation: from G5263 (ὑποδείκνυμι);

Definition

an exhibit for imitation or warning (figuratively, specimen, adumbration)

KJV Translation

en-(ex-)ample, pattern

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ὑπόδειγ-μα, ατος, τό,
sign, token, indication, see reading in [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 illustration, picture showing how something is to be done, [Refs 1st c.BC+]
__II pattern, [NT+2nd c.BC+]; καλὸν ὑ. τῆς ἰδίας προαιρέσεως καταβαλλόμενος [Refs 1st c.BC+]: but also ὑ. ἀπειθίας [Refs 2nd c.AD+]; ὑ. μελλόντων ἀσεβεῖν [NT]
__III example, instance, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; specimen, [Refs 1st c.BC+], etc.: plural, [Refs 5th c.BC+] —rejected as less correct than παράδειγμα by the Atticists, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
