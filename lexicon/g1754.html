ἐνεργέω

energéō — G1754

Transliteration: energéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1754

Derivation: from G1756 (ἐνεργής);

Definition

to be active, efficient

KJV Translation

do, (be) effectual (fervent), be mighty in, shew forth self, work (effectually in)
