ποίμνη

poímnē — G4167

Transliteration: poímnē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4167

Derivation: contraction from G4165 (ποιμαίνω);

Definition

a flock (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

flock, fold

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ποίμν-η, ἡ,
flock, [Refs 8th c.BC+]; properly of sheep, βουκολίας ἀγέλας τε καὶ αἰπόλια πλατἔ αἰγῶν ποίμνας τ᾽ είροπόκων ὀΐων [Refs 8th c.BC+]; of a single animal, χρυσεόμαλλος π., of the ram with golden fleece, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 metaphorically of persons, [Refs 4th c.BC+]; ποίμνη δίπους, of mankind, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
