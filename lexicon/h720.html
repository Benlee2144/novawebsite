אֲרוֹד

ʼĂrôwd — H720

Transliteration: ʼĂrôwd

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H720

Derivation: an orthographical variation of H719 (אַרְוַד); fugitive;

Definition

Arod, an Israelite

KJV Translation

Arod.

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

A man of the tribe of Gad living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, first mentioned at Gen.46.16;
son of: Gad (H1410);
brother of: Ziphion (H6837), Haggi (H2291), Shuni (H7764), Ezbon (H0675), Eri (H6179) and Areli (H0692); also called Arod at Num.26.17;
Also named: a.ro.di (אֲרוֹדִי "Arodi" H0722)
§ Arod = "I shall subdue: I shall roam"
a son of Gad
