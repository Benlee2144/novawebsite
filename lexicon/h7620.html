שָׁבוּעַ

shâbûwaʻ — H7620

Transliteration: shâbûwaʻ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7620

Derivation: or שָׁבֻעַ; also (feminine) שְׁבֻעָה; properly, passive participle of H7650 (שָׁבַע) as a denominative of H7651 (שֶׁבַע);

Definition

literally, sevened, i.e. a week (specifically, of years)

KJV Translation

seven, week.
