θησαυρός

thēsaurós — G2344

Transliteration: thēsaurós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2344

Derivation: from G5087 (τίθημι);

Definition

a deposit, i.e. wealth (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

treasure
