ἐπέρχομαι

epérchomai — G1904

Transliteration: epérchomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1904

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G2064 (ἔρχομαι);

Definition

to supervene, i.e. arrive, occur, impend, attack, (figuratively) influence

KJV Translation

come (in, upon)

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

ἐπ-έρχομαι,
[in LXX for בּוֹא, עָבַר, etc. ;]
__1. to come to, arrive, come on: before ἀπό, with genitive loc., Act.14:19 (ἐπῆλθαν; cf. M, Pr., 65; Deiss., BS, 191); of time, Eph.2:7.
__2. to come upon (as in Hom.): of calamities, Luk.21:26, Act.8:24 13:40, Jas.5:1; of an enemy, Luk.11:22; of the Holy Spirit, Luk.1:35, Act.1:8, [in LXX: γίγνομαι ἐπί, Jdg.14:6, 3Ki.11:6, al.].†
