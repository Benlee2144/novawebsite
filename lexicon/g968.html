βῆμα

bēma — G968

Transliteration: bēma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G968

Derivation: from the base of G939 (βάσις);

Definition

a step, i.e. foot-breath; by implication, a rostrum, i.e. a tribunal

KJV Translation

judgment-seat, set (foot) on, throne
