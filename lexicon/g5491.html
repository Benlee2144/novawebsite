χεῖλος

cheîlos — G5491

Transliteration: cheîlos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5491

Derivation: from a form of the same as G5490 (χάσμα);

Definition

a lip (as a pouring place); figuratively, a margin (of water)

KJV Translation

lip, shore
