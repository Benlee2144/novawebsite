ἀδιάλειπτος

adiáleiptos — G88

Transliteration: adiáleiptos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G88

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a derivative of a compound of G1223 (διά) and G3007 (λείπω);

Definition

unintermitted, i.e. permanent

KJV Translation

without ceasing, continual
