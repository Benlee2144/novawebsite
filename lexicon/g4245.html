πρεσβύτερος

presbýteros — G4245

Transliteration: presbýteros

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4245

Derivation: comparative of (elderly);

Definition

older; as noun, a senior; specially, an Israelite Sanhedrist (also figuratively, member of the celestial council) or Christian \

KJV Translation

elder(-est), old
