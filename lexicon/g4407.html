πρωϊνός

prōïnós — G4407

Transliteration: prōïnós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4407

Derivation: from G4404 (πρωΐ);

Definition

pertaining to the dawn, i.e. matutinal

KJV Translation

morning
