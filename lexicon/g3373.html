Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3373

μηκύνω

mēkýnō — G3373

Transliteration: mēkýnō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3373

Derivation: from G3372 (μῆκος);

Definition

to lengthen, i.e. (middle voice) to enlarge

KJV Translation

grow up
