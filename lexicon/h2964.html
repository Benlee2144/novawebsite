ṭereph — H2964

Transliteration: ṭereph

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2964

Derivation: from H2963 (טָרַף);

Definition

something torn, i.e. a fragment, e.g. a fresh leaf, prey, food

KJV Translation
