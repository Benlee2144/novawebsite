ἀπόλλυμι

apóllymi — G622

Transliteration: apóllymi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G622

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and the base of G3639 (ὄλεθρος);

Definition

to destroy fully (reflexively, to perish, or lose), literally or figuratively

KJV Translation

destroy, die, lose, mar, perish
