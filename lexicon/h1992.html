הֵם

hêm — H1992

Transliteration: hêm

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1992

Derivation: or (prolonged) הֵמָּה; masculine plural from H1981 (הֲלַךְ);

Definition

they (only used when emphatic)

KJV Translation

it, like, [idiom] (how, so) many (soever, more as) they (be), (the) same, [idiom] so, [idiom] such, their, them, these, they, those, which, who, whom, withal, ye.
