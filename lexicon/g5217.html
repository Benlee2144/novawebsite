ὑπάγω

hypágō — G5217

Transliteration: hypágō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5217

Derivation: from G5259 (ὑπό) and G71 (ἄγω);

Definition

to lead (oneself) under, i.e. withdraw or retire (as if sinking out of sight), literally or figuratively

KJV Translation

depart, get hence, go (a-)way
