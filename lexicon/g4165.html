ποιμαίνω

poimaínō — G4165

Transliteration: poimaínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4165

Derivation: from G4166 (ποιμήν);

Definition

to tend as a shepherd of (figuratively, superviser)

KJV Translation

feed (cattle), rule
