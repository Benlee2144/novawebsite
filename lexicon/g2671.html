κατάρα

katára — G2671

Transliteration: katára

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2671

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) (intensive) and G685 (ἀρά);

Definition

imprecation, execration

KJV Translation

curse(-d, ing)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

κατ-άρα [ᾰρ],
Ionic dialect κατ-άρη, ἡ, curse, κατάρας ποιέεσθαί τινι to lay curses upon one, [NT+5th c.BC+]; τὴν κ. ἀναγράψαι, στηλιτεῦσαι, [Refs 1st c.BC+] 2.354b; cursing, [Refs 1st c.BC+] O.
