θεωρία

theōría — G2335

Transliteration: theōría

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2335

Derivation: from the same as G2334 (θεωρέω);

Definition

spectatorship, i.e. (concretely) a spectacle

KJV Translation

sight
