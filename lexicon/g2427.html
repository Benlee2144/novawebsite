Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2427

ἱκανόω

hikanóō — G2427

Transliteration: hikanóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2427

Derivation: from G2425 (ἱκανός);

Definition

to enable, i.e. qualify

KJV Translation

make able (meet)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἱκᾰν-όω,
make sufficient, qualify, [NT]:—passive, to be empowered, [Refs 2nd c.BC+]; to be made complete, brought to perfection, of the soul, τῷ περιέχοντι [Refs 4th c.AD+]
__II passive, to be satisfied, content, τινι [LXX+1st c.BC+]; ἱκανούσθω ὑμῖν, with infinitive, let it suffice you.., i.e. do it no more, [LXX]

Appears In (2 verses)

2 Corinthians 3:6Colossians 1:12
