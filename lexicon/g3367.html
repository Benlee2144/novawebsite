μηδείς

mēdeís — G3367

Transliteration: mēdeís

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3367

Derivation: from G3361 (μή) and G1520 (εἷς);

Definition

not even one (man, woman, thing)

KJV Translation

any (man, thing), no (man), none, not (at all, any man, a whit), nothing, + without delay
