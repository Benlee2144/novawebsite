ἐνδεής

endeḗs — G1729

Transliteration: endeḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1729

Derivation: from a compound of G1722 (ἐν) and G1210 (δέω) (in the sense of lacking);

Definition

deficient in

KJV Translation

lacking
