κενοφωνία

kenophōnía — G2757

Transliteration: kenophōnía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2757

Derivation: from a presumed compound of G2756 (κενός) and G5456 (φωνή);

Definition

empty sounding, i.e. fruitless discussion

KJV Translation
