אִשָּׁה

ʼishshâh — H801

Transliteration: ʼishshâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H801

Derivation: the same as H800 (אֶשָּׁה), but used in a liturgical sense;

Definition

properly, a burnt-offering; but occasionally of any sacrifice

KJV Translation

(offering, sacrifice), (made) by fire.
