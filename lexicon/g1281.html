Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1281

διαπραγματεύομαι

diapragmateúomai — G1281

Transliteration: diapragmateúomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1281

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G4231 (πραγματεύομαι);

Definition

to thoroughly occupy oneself, i.e. (transitively and by implication) to earn in business

KJV Translation

gain by trading
