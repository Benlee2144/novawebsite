עֲשָׂהאֵל

ʻĂsâhʼêl — H6214

Transliteration: ʻĂsâhʼêl

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6214

Derivation: from H6213 (עָשָׂה) and H410 (אֵל); God has made;

Definition

Asahel, the name of four Israelites

KJV Translation

Asahel.

Appears In (14 verses)

1 Chronicles 11:261 Chronicles 2:161 Chronicles 27:72 Samuel 2:192 Samuel 2:202 Samuel 2:212 Samuel 2:222 Samuel 2:232 Samuel 2:302 Samuel 2:322 Samuel 23:242 Samuel 3:272 Samuel 3:30Ezra 10:15

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at Ezr.10.15;
father of: Jonathan (H3129I)
§ Asahel = "God-made"
1) nephew of David, son of David's sister Zeruiah, and brother of Joab and Abishai; swift of foot he was killed by Abner when he pursued him in battle and caught him
2) a Levite in the reign of king Jehoshaphat of Judah who went through the kingdom giving instruction in the law
3) a Levite in the reign of king Hezekiah of Judah in charge of the tithes and dedicated things in the temple
4) a priest, father of Jonathan, in the time of Ezra
