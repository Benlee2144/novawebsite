μνημονεύω

mnēmoneúō — G3421

Transliteration: mnēmoneúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3421

Derivation: from a derivative of G3420 (μνήμη);

Definition

to exercise memory, i.e. recollect; by implication, to punish; also to rehearse

KJV Translation

make mention; be mindful, remember
