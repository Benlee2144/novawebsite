τανῦν

tanŷn — G3569

Transliteration: tanŷn

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3569

Derivation: from neuter plural of G3588 (ὁ) and G3568 (νῦν);

Definition

the things now, i.e. (adverbially) at present

KJV Translation

(but) now

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

concerning the present, now

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

Included with: νῦν
