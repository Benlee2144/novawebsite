Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1461

ἐγκεντρίζω

enkentrízō — G1461

Transliteration: enkentrízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1461

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and a derivative of G2759 (κέντρον);

Definition

to prick in, i.e. ingraft

KJV Translation

graff in(-to)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐγκεντ-ρίζω,
goad, spur on, [LXX]
__II of plants, graft, [Refs 4th c.BC+], etc.: metaphorically, [NT+3rd c.AD+]; of a Centaur, ὃν φύσις ἐνεκέντρισεν ἵππῳ [Refs]
__III (κέντρον) concentrate, [Refs 5th c.AD+] —passive [Refs]
