ἐπισιτισμός

episitismós — G1979

Transliteration: episitismós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1979

Derivation: from a compound of G1909 (ἐπί) and a derivative of G4621 (σῖτος);

Definition

a provisioning, i.e. (concretely) food
