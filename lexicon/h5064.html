Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H5064

נָגַר

nâgar — H5064

Transliteration: nâgar

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5064

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to flow; figuratively, to stretch out; causatively, to pour out or down; figuratively, to deliver over

KJV Translation

fall, flow away, pour down (out), run, shed, spilt, trickle down.

Appears In (5 verses)

Job 20:28Lamentations 3:49Micah 1:4Psalms 63:10Psalms 77:2

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

1) to pour, run, flow, pour down
1a) (Niphal)
1a1) to be poured, be spilt
1a2) to pour oneself, flow, trickle
1a3) to vanish (fig.)
1a4) to be stretched out
1b) (Hiphil) to pour down
1c) (Hophal) to melt
