אַדִּיר

ʼaddîyr — H117

Transliteration: ʼaddîyr

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H117

Derivation: from H142 (אָדַר);

Definition

wide or (generally) large; figuratively, powerful

KJV Translation

excellent, famous, gallant, glorious, goodly, lordly, mighty(-ier one), noble, principal, worthy.
