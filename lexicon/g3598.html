ὁδός

hodós — G3598

Transliteration: hodós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3598

Derivation: apparently a primary word;

Definition

a road; by implication, a progress (the route, act or distance); figuratively, a mode or means

KJV Translation

journey, (high-)way
