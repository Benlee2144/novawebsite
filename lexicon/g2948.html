κυλλός

kyllós — G2948

Transliteration: kyllós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2948

Derivation: from the same as G2947 (κυλιόω);

Definition

rocking about, i.e. crippled (maimed, in feet or hands)

KJV Translation

maimed

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
