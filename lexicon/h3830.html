Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H3830

לְבוּשׁ

lᵉbûwsh — H3830

Transliteration: lᵉbûwsh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3830

Derivation: or לְבֻשׁ; from H3847 (לָבַשׁ);

Definition

a garment (literally or figuratively); by implication (euphemistically) a wife

KJV Translation

apparel, clothed with, clothing, garment, raiment, vestment, vesture.
