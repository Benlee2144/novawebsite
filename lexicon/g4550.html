σαπρός

saprós — G4550

Transliteration: saprós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4550

Derivation: from G4595 (σήπω);

Definition

rotten, i.e. worthless (literally or morally)

KJV Translation

bad, corrupt
