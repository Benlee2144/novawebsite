רִאשׁוֹן

riʼshôwn — H7223

Transliteration: riʼshôwn

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7223

Derivation: or רִאשֹׁן; from H7221 (רִאשָׁה);

Definition

first, in place, time or rank (as adjective or noun)

KJV Translation

ancestor, (that were) before(-time), beginning, eldest, first, fore(-father) (-most), former (thing), of old time, past.
