Μωσεύς

Mōseús — G3475

Transliteration: Mōseús

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3475

Derivation: of Hebrew origin; (H04872);

Definition

Moseus, Moses, or Mouses (i.e. Mosheh), the Hebrew lawgiver

KJV Translation

Moses

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

Μωυσῆς (Μωϋσῆς, T; Μωσῆς, Rec.), -έως, dative - (as LXX: Exo.5:20, al.), and -εῖ, accusative -ῆν (ac LXX) and -έα (Luk.16:29 only) (Heb. מֹשֶׁה),
Moses: Mat.8:4 17:3-4, al.; νόμος Μωυσέως, Luk.2:22 24:44, Jhn.7:23, Act.13:39 15:5 28:23, 1Co.9:9, Heb.10:28; by meton., of the books of Moses, Luk.16:29 24:27, Act.15:21, 2Co.3:15
