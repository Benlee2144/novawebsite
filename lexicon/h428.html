אֵלֶּה

ʼêl-leh — H428

Transliteration: ʼêl-leh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H428

Derivation: prolonged from H411 (אֵל);

Definition

KJV Translation

an-(the) other; one sort, so, some, such, them, these (same), they, this, those, thus, which, who(-m).
