οὐδέπω

oudépō — G3764

Transliteration: oudépō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3764

Derivation: from G3761 (οὐδέ) and G4452 (-πω);

Definition

not even yet

KJV Translation

as yet not, never before (yet), (not) yet

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

οὐδέ πω,
conjunction and adverb and not yet, not as yet, [Refs 8th c.BC+]: later with present, [Refs 5th c.BC+]: with future, ὅσον οὐδέπω συμβήσεσθαι [Refs 1st c.BC+]; compare οὐδέποτε.
