εὔχομαι

eúchomai — G2172

Transliteration: eúchomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2172

Derivation: middle voice of a primary verb;

Definition

to wish; by implication, to pray to God

KJV Translation

pray, will, wish
