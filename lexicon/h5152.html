Nâchôwr — H5152

Transliteration: Nâchôwr

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5152

Derivation: from the same as H5170 (נַחַר); snorer;

Definition

Nochor, the name of the grandfather and a brother of Abraham

KJV Translation
