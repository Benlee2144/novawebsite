ἐκλογή

eklogḗ — G1589

Transliteration: eklogḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1589

Derivation: from G1586 (ἐκλέγομαι);

Definition

(divine) selection (abstractly or concretely)

KJV Translation

chosen, election

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

__2 levying of troops, [Refs 2nd c.BC+]
__3 collection of tribute, etc., κριθῶν Legal Attica cited in [Refs 2nd c.AD+]; σίτου Cratesap.[Refs 2nd c.AD+]
__4 Theol., election[NT], etc.; σκεῦος ἐκλογῆς [NT]
__5 balancing of accounts, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
__II extract, quotation from a book, [Refs 3rd c.BC+]
__II.2 choice collection of passages, such as the Eclogae or 'Elegant Extracts' of Stobaeus: ἐκλογαὶ Ἀρχιγένους select prescriptions of [Refs 4th c.BC+]
