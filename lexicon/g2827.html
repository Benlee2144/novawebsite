κλίνω

klínō — G2827

Transliteration: klínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2827

Derivation: a primary verb;

Definition

to slant or slope, i.e. incline or recline (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

bow (down), be far spent, lay, turn to flight, wear away
