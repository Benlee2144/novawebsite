Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3878

παρακούω

parakoúō — G3878

Transliteration: parakoúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3878

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and G191 (ἀκούω);

Definition

to mishear, i.e. (by implication) to disobey

KJV Translation

neglect to hear

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

[in LXX: Est.3:3, 8 (עָבַר, עָשָׂה, with neg.) Est.4:14 7:4 (חָרַשׁ hi.), Isa.65:12 (שָׁמַע, with neg.), I Est.4:11, Tob.3:4 * ;]
__1. to overhear.
__2. to hear amiss or imperfectly.
__3. Later (as in LXX, Polyb., Plut.; also in π., see MM, xviii), to hear without heeding, take no heed: Mrk.5:36; with genitive, Mat.18:17.†
