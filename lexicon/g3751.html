Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3751

ὀσφῦς

osphŷs — G3751

Transliteration: osphŷs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3751

Derivation: of uncertain affinity;

Definition

the loin (externally), i.e. the hip; internally (by extension) procreative power

KJV Translation

loin
