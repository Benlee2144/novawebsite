πανοῦργος

panoûrgos — G3835

Transliteration: panoûrgos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3835

Derivation: from G3956 (πᾶς) and G2041 (ἔργον);

Definition

all-working, i.e. adroit (shrewd)

KJV Translation

crafty
