ἱμάς

himás — G2438

Transliteration: himás

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2438

Derivation: perhaps from the same as G260 (ἅμα);

Definition

a strap, i.e. (specially) the tie (of a sandal) or the lash (of a scourge)

KJV Translation

latchet, thong
