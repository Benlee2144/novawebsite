Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2545

καίω

kaíō — G2545

Transliteration: kaíō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2545

Derivation: apparently a primary verb;

Definition

to set on fire, i.e. kindle or (by implication) consume

KJV Translation

burn, light
