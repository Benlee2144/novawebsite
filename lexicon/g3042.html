λιμός

limós — G3042

Transliteration: limós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3042

Derivation: probably from G3007 (λείπω) (through the idea of destitution);

Definition

a scarcity of food

KJV Translation

dearth, famine, hunger
