ἀναπέμπω

anapémpō — G375

Transliteration: anapémpō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G375

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and G3992 (πέμπω);

Definition

to send up or back

KJV Translation

send (again)
