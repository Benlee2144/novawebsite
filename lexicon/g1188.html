δεξιός

dexiós — G1188

Transliteration: dexiós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1188

Derivation: from G1209 (δέχομαι);

Definition

the right side or (feminine) hand (as that which usually takes)

KJV Translation

right (hand, side)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
