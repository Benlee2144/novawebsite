כּוּשׁ

Kûwsh — H3568

Transliteration: Kûwsh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3568

Derivation: probably of foreign origin;

Definition

Cush (or Ethiopia), the name of a son of Ham, and of his territory; also of an Israelite

KJV Translation

Chush, Cush, Ethiopia.
