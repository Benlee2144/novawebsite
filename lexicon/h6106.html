עֶצֶם

ʻetsem — H6106

Transliteration: ʻetsem

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6106

Derivation: from H6105 (עָצַם);

Definition

a bone (as strong); by extension, the body; figuratively, the substance, i.e. (as pron.) selfsame

KJV Translation

body, bone, [idiom] life, (self-) same, strength, [idiom] very.
