ἐργάτης

ergátēs — G2040

Transliteration: ergátēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2040

Derivation: from G2041 (ἔργον);

Definition

a toiler; figuratively, a teacher

KJV Translation

labourer, worker(-men)
