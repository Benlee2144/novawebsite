Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3579

ξενίζω

xenízō — G3579

Transliteration: xenízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3579

Derivation: from G3581 (ξένος);

Definition

to be a host (passively, a guest); by implication, be (make, appear) strange

KJV Translation

entertain, lodge, (think it) strange
