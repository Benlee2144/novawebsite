יָצַת

yâtsath — H3341

Transliteration: yâtsath

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3341

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to burn or set on fire; figuratively, to desolate

KJV Translation

burn (up), be desolate, set (on) fire (fire), kindle.
