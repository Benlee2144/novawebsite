τελείωσις

teleíōsis — G5050

Transliteration: teleíōsis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5050

Derivation: from G5448 (φυσιόω);

Definition

(the act) completion, i.e. (of prophecy) verification, or (of expiation) absolution

KJV Translation

perfection, performance
