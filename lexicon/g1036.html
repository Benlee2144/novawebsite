βυθίζω

bythízō — G1036

Transliteration: bythízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1036

Derivation: from G1037 (βυθός);

Definition

to sink; by implication, to drown

KJV Translation

begin to sink, drown

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

βῠθ-ίζω,

__II overwhelm, submerge, of a flood, οἰκίας [Refs 1st c.AD+]:—passive, [Refs]
__III metaphorically, β. ἀνθρώπους εἰς ὄλεθρον [NT]:—passive, to be ruined, τοὺς διὰ φιλοκερδίαν βυθιζομένους [Refs 1st c.BC+]
