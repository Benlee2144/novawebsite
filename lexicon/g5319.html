φανερόω

phaneróō — G5319

Transliteration: phaneróō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5319

Derivation: from G5318 (φανερός);

Definition

to render apparent (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

appear, manifestly declare, (make) manifest (forth), shew (self)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

φᾰνερ-όω,
make manifest, σεαυτὸν τῷ κόσμῳ [NT]; reveal, make clear, τινὶ τὴν ἀλήθειαν [Refs 5th c.AD+]; ἀληθῆ πεφανέρωται have been demonstrated, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II make known or famous, [Refs 1st c.BC+]:—passive, become so, ἐφανερώθη ἐς τοὺς Ἕλληνας δαπάνῃσι μεγίστῃσι [Refs 5th c.BC+]
