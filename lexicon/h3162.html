יַחַד

yachad — H3162

Transliteration: yachad

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3162

Derivation: from H3161 (יָחַד);

Definition

properly, a unit, i.e. (adverb) unitedly

KJV Translation

alike, at all (once), both, likewise, only, (al-) together, withal.
