Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3404

μισέω

miséō — G3404

Transliteration: miséō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3404

Derivation: from a primary (hatred);

Definition

to detest (especially to persecute); by extension, to love less

KJV Translation

hate(-ful)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
