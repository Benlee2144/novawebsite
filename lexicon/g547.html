ἀπειλή

apeilḗ — G547

Transliteration: apeilḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G547

Derivation: from G546 (ἀπειλέω);

Definition

a menace

KJV Translation

X straitly, threatening

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀπειλ-ή, ἡ,
mostly in plural, boastful promises, boasts, ποῦ τοι ἀπειλαὶ ἃς.. ὑπίσχεο οἰνοποτάζων;[Refs 8th c.BC+]
__II commonly in plural, threats, ποῦ τοι ἀπειλαὶ οἴχονται τὰς Τρωσὶν ἀπείλεον υἷες Ἀχαιῶ;[Refs 8th c.BC+]
__II.2 of threatening conditions, ἀ. πνιγμοῦ[Refs 3rd c.AD+]; τὰ ἐν ἀπειλῇ ἀποστήματα Heras cited in [Refs 2nd c.AD+]; of storms, [Refs 1st c.AD+]
