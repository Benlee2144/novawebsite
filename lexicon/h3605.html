Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H3605

כֹּל

kôl — H3605

Transliteration: kôl

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3605

Derivation: or (Jeremiah 33:8) כּוֹל; from H3634 (כָּלַל);

Definition

properly, the whole; hence, all, any or every (in the singular only, but often in a plural sense)

KJV Translation

(in) all (manner, (ye)), altogether, any (manner), enough, every (one, place, thing), howsoever, as many as, (no-) thing, ought, whatsoever, (the) whole, whoso(-ever).
