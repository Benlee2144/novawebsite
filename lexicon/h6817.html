Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H6817

צָעַק

tsâʻaq — H6817

Transliteration: tsâʻaq

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6817

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to shriek; (by implication) to proclaim (an assembly)

KJV Translation

[idiom] at all, call together, cry (out), gather (selves) (together).
