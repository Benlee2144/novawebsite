שָׁתָה

shâthâh — H8354

Transliteration: shâthâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H8354

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to imbibe (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

[idiom] assuredly, banquet, [idiom] certainly, drink(-er, -ing), drunk ([idiom] -ard), surely. (Prop. intensive of H8248 (שָׁקָה).)
