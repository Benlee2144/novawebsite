κατακρίνω

katakrínō — G2632

Transliteration: katakrínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2632

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G2919 (κρίνω);

Definition

to judge against, i.e. sentence

KJV Translation

condemn, damn

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

κατα-κρίνω,
