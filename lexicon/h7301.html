Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H7301

רָוָה

râvâh — H7301

Transliteration: râvâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7301

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to slake the thirst (occasionally of other appetites)

KJV Translation

bathe, make drunk, (take the) fill, satiate, (abundantly) satisfy, soak, water (abundantly).

Appears In (10 verses)

1) to be satiated or saturated, have or drink one's fill
1a) (Qal) to take one's fill
1b) (Piel)
1b1) to be drunk, be intoxicated
1b2) to drench, water abundantly, saturate
