φαίνω

phaínō — G5316

Transliteration: phaínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5316

Derivation: prolongation for the base of G5457 (φῶς);

Definition

to lighten (shine), i.e. show (transitive or intransitive, literal or figurative)

KJV Translation

appear, seem, be seen, shine, X think
