עֶרְוָה

ʻervâh — H6172

Transliteration: ʻervâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6172

Derivation: from H6168 (עָרָה);

Definition

nudity, literally (especially the pudenda) or figuratively (disgrace, blemish)

KJV Translation

nakedness, shame, unclean(-ness).
