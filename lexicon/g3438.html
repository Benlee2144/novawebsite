μονή

monḗ — G3438

Transliteration: monḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3438

Derivation: from G3306 (μένω);

Definition

a staying, i.e. residence (the act or the place)

KJV Translation

abode, mansion
