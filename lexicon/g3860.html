παραδίδωμι

paradídōmi — G3860

Transliteration: paradídōmi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3860

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and G1325 (δίδωμι);

Definition

to surrender, i.e yield up, intrust, transmit

KJV Translation

betray, bring forth, cast, commit, deliver (up), give (over, up), hazard, put in prison, recommend
