πόλις

pólis — G4172

Transliteration: pólis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4172

Derivation: probably from the same as G4171 (πόλεμος), or perhaps from G4183 (πολύς);

Definition

a town (properly, with walls, of greater or less size)

KJV Translation

city
