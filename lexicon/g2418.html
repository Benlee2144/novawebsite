Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2418

ἱερουργέω

hierourgéō — G2418

Transliteration: hierourgéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2418

Derivation: from a compound of G2411 (ἱερόν) and the base of G2041 (ἔργον);

Definition

to be a temple-worker, i.e. officiate as a priest (figuratively)

KJV Translation

minister
