חָגַר

châgar — H2296

Transliteration: châgar

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2296

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to gird on (as a belt, armor, etc.)

KJV Translation

be able to put on, be afraid, appointed, gird, restrain, [idiom] on every side.
