ἄγε

áge — G33

Transliteration: áge

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G33

Derivation: imperative of G71 (ἄγω);

Definition

properly, lead, i.e. come on

KJV Translation

go to

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

ἄγε, prop. imperat. of ἄγω,
come! used as adv. and addressed, like φέρε, to one or more persons: Jas.4:13, 5:1.†
(AS)
