πόῤῥωθεν

pórrhōthen — G4207

Transliteration: pórrhōthen

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4207

Derivation: from G4206 (πόῤῥω) with adverbial enclitic of source;

Definition

from far, or (by implication) at a distance, i.e. distantly

KJV Translation

afar off
