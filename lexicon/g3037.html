λίθος

líthos — G3037

Transliteration: líthos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3037

Derivation: apparently a primary word;

Definition

a stone (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

(mill-, stumbling-)stone
