περιβλέπω

periblépō — G4017

Transliteration: periblépō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4017

Derivation: from G4012 (περί) and G991 (βλέπω);

Definition

to look all around

KJV Translation

look (round) about (on)
