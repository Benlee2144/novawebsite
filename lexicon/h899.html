בֶּגֶד

beged — H899

Transliteration: beged

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H899

Derivation: from H898 (בָּגַד);

Definition

a covering, i.e. clothing; also treachery or pillage

KJV Translation

apparel, cloth(-es, ing), garment, lap, rag, raiment, robe, [idiom] very (treacherously), vesture, wardrobe.
