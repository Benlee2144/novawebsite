ἐπισυνάγω

episynágō — G1996

Transliteration: episynágō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1996

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G4863 (συνάγω);

Definition

to collect upon the same place

KJV Translation

gather (together)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
