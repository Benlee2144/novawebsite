προέπω

proépō — G4277

Transliteration: proépō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4277

Derivation: from G4253 (πρό) and G2036 (ἔπω);

Definition

to say already, to predict

KJV Translation

forewarn, say (speak, tell) before

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

προ-εῖπον 2 aor. from unused Pres. (see: εἶπον, and pf., -είρηκα (3Ma.6:35 *), pass., -είρημαι (2Ma.2:32, 3Ma.1:26, al.),
__1. to say before: Gal.1:9, 1Th.4:6 (on the form -αμεν, see WH, App., 164), Heb.4:7; before ὅτι, 2Co.7:3 13:2, Gal.5:21; of prophecy, Mat.24:25, Mrk.13:23, Act.1:16, Rom.9:29, 2Pe.3:2, Ju 17.
__2. to proclaim publicly, declare openly or plainly (cl.): so R, mg., in 2Co.13:2, Gal.5:21, 1Th.4:6 (cf. προλέγω; but see supr., and cf. M, Th., 38).†
(AS)
