συμπέμπω

sympémpō — G4842

Transliteration: sympémpō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4842

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G3992 (πέμπω);

Definition

to despatch in company

KJV Translation

send with
