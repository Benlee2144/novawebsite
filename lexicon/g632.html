ἀπονέμω

aponémō — G632

Transliteration: aponémō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G632

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and the base of G3551 (νόμος);

Definition

to apportion, i.e. bestow

KJV Translation

give
