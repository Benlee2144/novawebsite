ἐπιτελέω

epiteléō — G2005

Transliteration: epiteléō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2005

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G5055 (τελέω);

Definition

to fulfill further (or completely), i.e. execute; by implication, to terminate, undergo

KJV Translation

accomplish, do, finish, (make) (perfect), perform(X -ance)
