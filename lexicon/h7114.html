קָצַר

qâtsar — H7114

Transliteration: qâtsar

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7114

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to dock off, i.e. curtail (transitive or intransitive, literal or figurative); especially to harvest (grass or grain)

KJV Translation

[idiom] at all, cut down, much discouraged, grieve, harvestman, lothe, mourn, reap(-er), (be, wax) short(-en, -er), straiten, trouble, vex.
