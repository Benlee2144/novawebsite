κατόρθωμα

katórthōma — G2735

Transliteration: katórthōma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2735

Derivation: from a compound of G2596 (κατά) and a derivative of G3717 (ὀρθός) (compare G1357 (διόρθωσις));

Definition

something made fully upright, i.e. (figuratively) rectification (specially, good public administration)

KJV Translation

very worthy deed

κατόρθωμα, -τος, τό
