σύνδουλος

sýndoulos — G4889

Transliteration: sýndoulos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4889

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G1401 (δοῦλος);

Definition

a co-slave, i.e. servitor or ministrant of the same master (human or divine)

KJV Translation

fellowservant

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

σύνδουλ-ος, ὁ, ἡ,
fellow-slave, masculine, [NT+5th c.BC+]; also feminine συνδούλη, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 metaphorically in NT, [NT] (The statement of Moeris [Refs 2nd c.AD+] distinguishes ς. 'slave of the same master' from ὁμόδουλος 'companion in slavery'.)
