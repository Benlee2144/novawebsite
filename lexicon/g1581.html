ἐκκόπτω

ekkóptō — G1581

Transliteration: ekkóptō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1581

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G2875 (κόπτω);

Definition

to exscind; figuratively, to frustrate

KJV Translation

cut down (off, out), hew down, hinder
