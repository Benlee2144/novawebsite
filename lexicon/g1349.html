δίκη

díkē — G1349

Transliteration: díkē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1349

Derivation: probably from G1166 (δεικνύω);

Definition

right (as self-evident), i.e. justice (the principle, a decision, or its execution)

KJV Translation

judgment, punish, vengeance

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

δίκη, -ης, ἡ
[in LXX for נָקָם, רִיב, etc. ;]
__1. custom.
__2. right.
__3. a judicial hearing; hence its result, the execution of a sentence, punishment: δ. τίνειν, 2Th.1:9; δ. υπεχειν, Jude 7.
__4. Personified (cf. Lat. Justitia), justice, vengeance: Act.28:4.†
(AS)
