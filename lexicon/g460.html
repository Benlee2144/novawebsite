ἀνόμως

anómōs — G460

Transliteration: anómōs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G460

Derivation: adverb from G459 (ἄνομος);

Definition

lawlessly, i.e. (specially) not amenable to (the Jewish) law

KJV Translation

without law
