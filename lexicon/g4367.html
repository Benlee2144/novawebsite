προστάσσω

prostássō — G4367

Transliteration: prostássō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4367

Derivation: from G4314 (πρός) and G5021 (τάσσω);

Definition

to arrange towards, i.e. (figuratively) enjoin

KJV Translation

bid, command

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

[in LXX chiefly for צָוָה pi. ;]
