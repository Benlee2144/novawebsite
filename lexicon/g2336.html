θήκη

thḗkē — G2336

Transliteration: thḗkē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2336

Derivation: from G5087 (τίθημι);

Definition

a receptacle, i.e. scabbard

KJV Translation

sheath
