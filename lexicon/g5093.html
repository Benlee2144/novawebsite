τίμιος

tímios — G5093

Transliteration: tímios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5093

Derivation: from G5092 (τιμή);

Definition

valuable, i.e. (objectively) costly, or (subjectively) honored, esteemed, or (figuratively) beloved

KJV Translation

dear, honourable, (more, most) precious, had in reputation
