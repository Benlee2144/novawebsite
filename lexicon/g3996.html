πενθέω

penthéō — G3996

Transliteration: penthéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3996

Derivation: from G3997 (πένθος);

Definition

to grieve (the feeling or the act)

KJV Translation

mourn, (be-)wail

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
