θάνατος

thánatos — G2288

Transliteration: thánatos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2288

Derivation: from G2348 (θνήσκω);

Definition

(properly, an adjective used as a noun) death (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

X deadly, (be…) death
