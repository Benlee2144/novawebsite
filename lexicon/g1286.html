διασείω

diaseíō — G1286

Transliteration: diaseíō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1286

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G4579 (σείω);

Definition

to shake thoroughly, i.e. (figuratively) to intimidate

KJV Translation

do violence to
