Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2017

ἐπιφαύω

epiphaúō — G2017

Transliteration: epiphaúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2017

Derivation: a form of G2014 (ἐπιφαίνω);

Definition

to illuminate (figuratively)

KJV Translation

give light

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐπιφαύσκω,
future -φαύσω (see. below):— shine out, of the sun or moon, [LXX]: also in passive form,[Refs]; ἐπιφαύσει σοι ὁ Χριστός will shine out for thee,[NT]

Appears In (1 verse)

Ephesians 5:14
