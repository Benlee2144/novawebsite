Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1684

ἐμβαίνω

embaínō — G1684

Transliteration: embaínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1684

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and the base of G939 (βάσις);

Definition

to walk on, i.e. embark (aboard a vessel), reach (a pool)

KJV Translation

come (get) into, enter (into), go (up) into, step in, take ship
