ἀναζώννυμι

anazṓnnymi — G328

Transliteration: anazṓnnymi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G328

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and G2224 (ζώννυμι);

Definition

to gird afresh

KJV Translation

gird up

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀναζώννῡμι
or ἀναζωννύω, future -ζώσω, gird up again, recall to service, metaphorically, τινὰ ἐπὶ τοὺς λόγους[Refs 4th c.AD+] —middle, ἀ. τὰς ὀσφύας gird up one's loins, [NT+4th c.AD+]; ἀνεζωσμένοι, Latin alte praecincti, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]:—passive, to be held in check, of passions, [Refs 1st c.AD+]
__2 middle, with accusative, πόλεμον embark on, [Refs 4th c.BC+]

Appears In (1 verse)

1 Peter 1:13
