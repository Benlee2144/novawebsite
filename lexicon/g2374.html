θύρα

thýra — G2374

Transliteration: thýra

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2374

Derivation: apparently a primary word (compare \

Definition

a portal or entrance (the opening or the closure, literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

door, gate
