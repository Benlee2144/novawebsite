עֵדָה

ʻêdâh — H5712

Transliteration: ʻêdâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5712

Derivation: feminine of H5707 (עֵד) in the original sense of fixture;

Definition

a stated assemblage (specifically, a concourse, or generally, a family or crowd)

KJV Translation

assembly, company, congregation, multitude, people, swarm. Compare H5713 (עֵדָה).
