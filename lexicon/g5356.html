φθορά

phthorá — G5356

Transliteration: phthorá

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5356

Derivation: from G5351 (φθείρω);

Definition

decay, i.e. ruin (spontaneous or inflicted, literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

corruption, destroy, perish
