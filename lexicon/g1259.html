διαλλάσσω

diallássō — G1259

Transliteration: diallássō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1259

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G236 (ἀλλάσσω);

Definition

to change thoroughly, i.e. (mentally) to conciliate

KJV Translation

reconcile
