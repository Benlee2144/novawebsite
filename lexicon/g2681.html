Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2681

κατασκηνόω

kataskēnóō — G2681

Transliteration: kataskēnóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2681

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G4637 (σκηνόω);

Definition

to camp down, i.e. haunt; figuratively, to remain

KJV Translation

lodge, rest

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

κατασκην-όω,
take up one's quarters, encamp, εἰς.. [LXX+6th c.BC+]: generally, rest, [LXX]; settle, of birds, [NT]: metaphorically, [Refs 3rd c.AD+].
