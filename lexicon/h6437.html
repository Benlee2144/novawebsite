Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H6437

פָּנָה

pânâh — H6437

Transliteration: pânâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6437

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to turn; by implication, to face, i.e. appear, look, etc.

KJV Translation

appear, at (even-) tide, behold, cast out, come on, [idiom] corner, dawning, empty, go away, lie, look, mark, pass away, prepare, regard, (have) respect (to), (re-) turn (aside, away, back, face, self), [idiom] right (early).

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

This name means corner, to turn, to turn back
Another spelling of pin.nah (פִּנָּה "Corner( Gate)" H6438)
