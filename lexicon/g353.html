ἀναλαμβάνω

analambánō — G353

Transliteration: analambánō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G353

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and G2983 (λαμβάνω);

Definition

to take up

KJV Translation

receive up, take (in, unto, up)
