κωλύω

kōlýō — G2967

Transliteration: kōlýō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2967

Derivation: from the base of G2849 (κολάζω);

Definition

to estop, i.e. prevent (by word or act)

KJV Translation

forbid, hinder, keep from, let, not suffer, withstand
