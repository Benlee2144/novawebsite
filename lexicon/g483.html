ἀντιλέγω

antilégō — G483

Transliteration: antilégō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G483

Derivation: from G473 (ἀντί) and G3004 (λέγω);

Definition

to dispute, refuse

KJV Translation

answer again, contradict, deny, gainsay(-er), speak against
