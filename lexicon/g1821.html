Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1821

ἐξαποστέλλω

exapostéllō — G1821

Transliteration: exapostéllō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1821

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G649 (ἀποστέλλω);

Definition

to send away forth, i.e. (on a mission) to despatch, or (peremptorily) to dismiss

KJV Translation

send (away, forth, out)
