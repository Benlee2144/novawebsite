ἔριφος

ériphos — G2056

Transliteration: ériphos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2056

Derivation: perhaps from the same as G2053 (ἔριον) (through the idea of hairiness);

Definition

a kid or (genitive case) goat

KJV Translation

goat, kid
