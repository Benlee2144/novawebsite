Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4796

συγχαίρω

synchaírō — G4796

Transliteration: synchaírō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4796

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G5463 (χαίρω);

Definition

to sympathize in gladness, congratulate

KJV Translation

rejoice in (with)
