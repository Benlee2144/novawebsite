Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4790

συγκοινωνέω

synkoinōnéō — G4790

Transliteration: synkoinōnéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4790

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G2841 (κοινωνέω);

Definition

to share in company with, i.e. co-participate in

KJV Translation

communicate (have fellowship) with, be partaker of

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

συγκοινων-έω,
have a joint share of, τῆς δόξης ταύτης [Refs 4th c.BC+]; to be connected with, τῆς κνήμης [Refs 5th c.BC+]; σ. τινί τινος go shares with one in a thing, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
__2 in “NT” with dative, take part in, have fellowship with, ταῖς ἁμαρτίαις [NT]

Appears In (3 verses)

Ephesians 5:11Philippians 4:14Revelation 18:4
