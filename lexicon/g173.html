ἄκανθα

ákantha — G173

Transliteration: ákantha

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G173

Derivation: probably from the same as G188 (ἀκμήν);

Definition

a thorn

KJV Translation

thorn

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

ἄκανθα, -ης, ἡ
(ἀκή, a point), [in LXX chiefly for קוֹץ, also for סִיר, שַׁיִת, etc. ;]
a prickly plant, thorn, brier; in NT always pl.: Mat.7:16 13:7,22 27:29, Mrk.4:7,18, Luk.6:44 8:7,14, Jhn.19:2, Heb.6:8 (see MM, VGT, see word).†
