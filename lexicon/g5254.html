ὑπέχω

hypéchō — G5254

Transliteration: hypéchō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5254

Derivation: from G5259 (ὑπό) and G2192 (ἔχω);

Definition

to hold oneself under, i.e. endure with patience

KJV Translation

suffer
