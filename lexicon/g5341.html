Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G5341

φελόνης

phelónēs — G5341

Transliteration: phelónēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5341

Derivation: by transposition for a derivative probably of G5316 (φαίνω) (as showing outside the other garments);

Definition

a mantle (surtout)

KJV Translation

cloke

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

φαιλόνης,
={φαινόλης}, [NT+5th c.AD+]; written φελόνης in [NT+2nd c.AD+] [same place]:—diminutive φαιλόνιον [Refs 2nd c.AD+]; written φ[ε]λόνιον in [Refs 4th c.AD+]
φαινόλη,
Doric dialect -όλα, ἡ, = Latin paenula, thick upper garment, cloak, [Refs 3rd c.BC+], compare φαινόλα· τὸ ὕφασμα, [Refs 5th c.AD+]; φαίνουλα [Refs], παίνουλα [Refs]πένουλα [Refs]:—but usually φαινόλης, ου, ὁ, [Refs 1st c.AD+]

Appears In (1 verse)
