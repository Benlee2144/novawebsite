ἄζυμος

ázymos — G106

Transliteration: ázymos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G106

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and G2219 (ζύμη);

Definition

unleavened, i.e. (figuratively) uncorrupted; (in the neutral plural) specially (by implication) the Passover week

KJV Translation

unleavened (bread)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
