Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H2351

חוּץ

chûwts — H2351

Transliteration: chûwts

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2351

Derivation: or (shortened) חֻץ; (both forms feminine in the plural) from an unused root meaning to sever;

Definition

properly, separate by awall, i.e. outside, outdoors

KJV Translation

abroad, field, forth, highway, more, out(-side, -ward), street, without.
