ἐκβάλλω

ekbállō — G1544

Transliteration: ekbállō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1544

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G906 (βάλλω);

Definition

to eject (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

bring forth, cast (forth, out), drive (out), expel, leave, pluck (pull, take, thrust) out, put forth (out), send away (forth, out)
