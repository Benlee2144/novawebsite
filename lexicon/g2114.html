εὐθυμέω

euthyméō — G2114

Transliteration: euthyméō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2114

Derivation: from G2115 (εὔθυμος);

Definition

to cheer up, i.e. (intransitively) be cheerful; neuter comparative (adverbially) more cheerfully

KJV Translation

be of good cheer (merry)
