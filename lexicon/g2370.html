θυμιάω

thymiáō — G2370

Transliteration: thymiáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2370

Derivation: from a derivative of G2380 (θύω) (in the sense of smoking);

Definition

to fumigate, i.e. offer aromatic fumes

KJV Translation

burn incense
