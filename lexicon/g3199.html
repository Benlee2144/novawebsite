Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3199

μέλω

mélō — G3199

Transliteration: mélō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3199

Derivation: a primary verb;

Definition

to be of interest to, i.e. to concern (only third person singular present indicative used impersonally, it matters)

KJV Translation

(take) care

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

μέλω,
middle μέλομαι, used in both voices, either in neuter sense, to be an object of care or thought, or in active sense, care for, take an interest in.
