Ἰωσήφ

Iōsḗph — G2501

Transliteration: Iōsḗph

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2501

Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H03130);

Definition

Joseph, the name of seven Israelites

KJV Translation

Joseph
