ἐξάγω

exágō — G1806

Transliteration: exágō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1806

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G71 (ἄγω);

Definition

to lead forth

KJV Translation

bring forth (out), fetch (lead) out

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
