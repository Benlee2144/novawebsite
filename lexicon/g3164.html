μάχομαι

máchomai — G3164

Transliteration: máchomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3164

Derivation: middle voice of an apparently primary verb;

Definition

to war, i.e. (figuratively) to quarrel, dispute

KJV Translation

fight, strive
