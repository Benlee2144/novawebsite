καθόλου

kathólou — G2527

Transliteration: kathólou

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2527

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G3650 (ὅλος);

Definition

on the whole, i.e. entirely

KJV Translation

at all
