ἄφθαρτος

áphthartos — G862

Transliteration: áphthartos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G862

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G5351 (φθείρω);

Definition

undecaying (in essence or continuance)

KJV Translation

not (in-, un-)corruptible, immortal
