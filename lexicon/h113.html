אָדוֹן

ʼâdôwn — H113

Transliteration: ʼâdôwn

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H113

Derivation: or (shortened) אָדֹן; from an unused root (meaning to rule);

Definition

sovereign, i.e. controller (human or divine)

KJV Translation

lord, master, owner. Compare also names beginning with 'Adoni-'.
