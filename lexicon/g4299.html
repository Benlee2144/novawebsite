πρόκριμα

prókrima — G4299

Transliteration: prókrima

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4299

Derivation: from a compound of G4253 (πρό) and G2919 (κρίνω);

Definition

a prejudgment (prejudice), i.e. prepossession

KJV Translation

prefer one before another

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

πρό-κρῐμα, ατος, τό,

__2 = praejudicium, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
