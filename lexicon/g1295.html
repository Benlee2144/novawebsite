διασώζω

diasṓzō — G1295

Transliteration: diasṓzō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1295

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G4982 (σώζω);

Definition

to save thoroughly, i.e. (by implication or analogy) to cure, preserve, rescue, etc.

KJV Translation

bring safe, escape (safe), heal, make perfectly whole, save
