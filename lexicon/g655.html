ἀποστυγέω

apostygéō — G655

Transliteration: apostygéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G655

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and the base of G4767 (στυγνητός);

Definition

to detest utterly

KJV Translation

abhor
