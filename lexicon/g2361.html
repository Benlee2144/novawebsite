θρόμβος

thrómbos — G2361

Transliteration: thrómbos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2361

Derivation: perhaps from G5142 (τρέφω) (in the sense of thickening);

Definition

a clot

KJV Translation

great drop
