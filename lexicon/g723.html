ἄροτρον

árotron — G723

Transliteration: árotron

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G723

Derivation: from (to till);

Definition

a plow

KJV Translation

plough

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἄροτρον [ᾰ], τό,
(ἀρόω) plough, [Refs 8th c.BC+]
__b ἡ ἀπ᾽ ἀρότρου πληγή, in boxing, right-handed blow, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
__2 in plural, metaphorically of the organs of generation, [Refs 4th c.AD+]
__II ἄροτρα· γῆ, χώρα, πλέθρα, [Refs 5th c.AD+]
