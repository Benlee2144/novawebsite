בַּעַל

baʻal — H1167

Transliteration: baʻal

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1167

Derivation: from H1166 (בָּעַל);

Definition

a master; hence, a husband, or (figuratively) owner (often used with another noun in modifications of this latter sense)
