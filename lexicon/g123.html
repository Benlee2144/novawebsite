αἰγιαλός

aigialós — G123

Transliteration: aigialós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G123

Derivation: from (to rush) and G251 (ἅλς) (in the sense of the sea;

Definition

a beach (on which the waves dash)

KJV Translation

shore

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

αἰγιᾰλός, ὁ,
sea-shore, beach, [Refs 8th c.BC+]; distinguished from ἀκτή, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; αἰγιαλὸν ἔνδον τρέφει a whole beach of voting-pelbles, [Refs 5th c.BC+], of deaf persons, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
