כָּחַד

kâchad — H3582

Transliteration: kâchad

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3582

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to secrete, by act or word; hence (intensively) to destroy

KJV Translation

conceal, cut down (off), desolate, hide.
