ἁρπάζω

harpázō — G726

Transliteration: harpázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G726

Derivation: from a derivative of G138 (αἱρέομαι);

Definition

to seize (in various applications)

KJV Translation

catch (away, up), pluck, pull, take (by force)
