Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G5178

τυμπανίζω

tympanízō — G5178

Transliteration: tympanízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5178

Derivation: from a derivative of G5180 (τύπτω) (meaning a drum, \

Definition

to stretch on an instrument of torture resembling a drum, and thus beat to death

KJV Translation

torture

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

τυμπᾰν-ίζω,

__2 τ. ἐπὶ ταῖς θύραις drum with the hand on them, [LXX]
__II perhaps ={ἀποτυμπανίζω} (which is variant), [NT]; coupled with ἀνασκολοπίζεσθαι in [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
