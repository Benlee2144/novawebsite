βουλεύω

bouleúō — G1011

Transliteration: bouleúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1011

Derivation: from G1012 (βουλή);

Definition

to advise, i.e. (reflexively) deliberate, or (by implication) resolve

KJV Translation

consult, take counsel, determine, be minded, purpose
