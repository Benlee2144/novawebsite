εὐαρεστέω

euarestéō — G2100

Transliteration: euarestéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2100

Derivation: from G2101 (εὐάρεστος);

Definition

to gratify entirely

KJV Translation

please (well)
