φθόνος

phthónos — G5355

Transliteration: phthónos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5355

Derivation: probably akin to the base of G5351 (φθείρω);

Definition

ill-will (as detraction), i.e. jealousy (spite)

KJV Translation

envy
