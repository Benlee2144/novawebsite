καταγγέλλω

katangéllō — G2605

Transliteration: katangéllō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2605

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and the base of G32 (ἄγγελος);

Definition

to proclaim, promulgate

KJV Translation

declare, preach, shew, speak of, teach
