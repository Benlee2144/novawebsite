ἔσχατος

éschatos — G2078

Transliteration: éschatos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2078

Derivation: a superlative probably from G2192 (ἔχω) (in the sense of contiguity);

Definition

farthest, final (of place or time)

KJV Translation

ends of, last, latter end, lowest, uttermost
