Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H8193

שָׂפָה

sâphâh — H8193

Transliteration: sâphâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H8193

Derivation: or (in dual and plural) שֶׂפֶת; probably from H5595 (סָפָה) or H8192 (שָׁפָה) through the idea of termination (compare H5490 (סוֹף));

Definition

the lip (as a natural boundary); by implication, language; by analogy, a margin (of a vessel, water, cloth, etc.)

KJV Translation

band, bank, binding, border, brim, brink, edge, language, lip, prating, (sea-)shore, side, speech, talk, (vain) words.
