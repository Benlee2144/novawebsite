Βαπτιστής

Baptistḗs — G910

Transliteration: Baptistḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G910

Derivation: from G907 (βαπτίζω);

Definition

a baptizer, as an epithet of Christ's forerunner
