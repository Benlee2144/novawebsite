φοβέω

phobéō — G5399

Transliteration: phobéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5399

Derivation: from G5401 (φόβος);

Definition

to frighten, i.e. (passively) to be alarmed; by analogy, to be in awe of, i.e. revere

KJV Translation

be (+ sore) afraid, fear (exceedingly), reverence
