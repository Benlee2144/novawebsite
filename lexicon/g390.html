ἀναστρέφω

anastréphō — G390

Transliteration: anastréphō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G390

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and G4762 (στρέφω);

Definition

to overturn; also to return; by implication, to busy oneself, i.e. remain, live

KJV Translation

abide, behave self, have conversation, live, overthrow, pass, return, be used
