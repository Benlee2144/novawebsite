ἀληθεύω

alētheúō — G226

Transliteration: alētheúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G226

Derivation: from G227 (ἀληθής);

Definition

to be true (in doctrine and profession)

KJV Translation

speak (tell) the truth
