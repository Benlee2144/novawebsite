קָצֶה

qâtseh — H7097

Transliteration: qâtseh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7097

Derivation: or (negative only) קֵצֶה; from H7096 (קָצָה); (used in a great variety of applications and idioms; compare H7093 (קֵץ))

Definition

an extremity

KJV Translation

[idiom] after, border, brim, brink, edge, end, (in-) finite, frontier, outmost coast, quarter, shore, (out-) side, [idiom] some, ut(-ter-) most (part).
