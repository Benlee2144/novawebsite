ἥμισυ

hḗmisy — G2255

Transliteration: hḗmisy

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2255

Derivation: neuter of a derivative from an inseparable prefix akin to G260 (ἅμα) (through the idea of partition involved in connection) and meaning semi-;

Definition

(as noun) half

KJV Translation

half
