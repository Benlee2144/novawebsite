μεσίτης

mesítēs — G3316

Transliteration: mesítēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3316

Derivation: from G3319 (μέσος);

Definition

a go-between, i.e. (simply) an internunciator, or (by implication) a reconciler (intercessor)

KJV Translation

mediator
