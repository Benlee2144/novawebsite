ματαιότης

mataiótēs — G3153

Transliteration: mataiótēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3153

Derivation: from G3152 (μάταιος);

Definition

inutility; figuratively, transientness; morally, depravity

KJV Translation

vanity
