ὑποκάτω

hypokátō — G5270

Transliteration: hypokátō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5270

Derivation: from G5259 (ὑπό) and G2736 (κάτω);

Definition

down under, i.e. beneath

KJV Translation

under

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ὑποκάτω [ᾰ],
adverb below, under, with genitive, ὑ. τῆς ἐκροῆς [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ὑ. παραγράψαι τι [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__II in Logic, τὸ ὑ. γένος the subordinate genus, opposed to τὸ ἐπάνω, [Refs 4th c.BC+]
