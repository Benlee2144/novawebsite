ἀναγγέλλω

anangéllō — G312

Transliteration: anangéllō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G312

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and the base of G32 (ἄγγελος);

Definition

to announce (in detail)

KJV Translation

declare, rehearse, report, show, speak, tell

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀναγγέλλω,
(see. ἀγγέλλω) carry back tidings of, report, τι [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ἐν ἁλία, of valuers, [Refs 2nd c.BC+]: with participle, tell of person doing, [Refs 5th c.BC+]:—passive, ὡς ἀνηγγέλθη τεθνεώς[Refs 1st c.AD+]
__II proclaim, τοὺς στεφάνους[Refs]:—passive, of rewards, ἀνηγγέλθαι αὐτῷ ἀργύριον[Refs 4th c.BC+]
