μᾶλλον

mâllon — G3123

Transliteration: mâllon

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3123

Derivation: neuter of the comparative of the same as G3122 (μάλιστα);

Definition

(adverbially) more (in a greater degree)) or rather

KJV Translation

+ better, X far, (the) more (and more), (so) much (the more), rather
