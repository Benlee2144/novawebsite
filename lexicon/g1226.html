Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1226

διαβεβαιόομαι

diabebaióomai — G1226

Transliteration: diabebaióomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1226

Derivation: middle voice of a compound of G1223 (διά) and G950 (βεβαιόω);

Definition

to confirm thoroughly (by words), i.e. asseverate

KJV Translation

affirm constantly

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

διαβεβαι-όω,
confirm, ὑπόληψιν [Refs 3rd c.AD+]:—usually middle διαβεβαι-όομαι, maintain strongly, affirm, confirm, [Refs 4th c.BC+]; to be positive, περί τινος [Refs 2nd c.BC+]:—passive, [Refs 1st c.BC+]
