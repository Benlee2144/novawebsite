προάγω

proágō — G4254

Transliteration: proágō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4254

Derivation: from G4253 (πρό) and G71 (ἄγω);

Definition

to lead forward (magisterially); intransitively, to precede (in place or time (participle, previous))

KJV Translation

bring (forth, out), go before
