Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H3733

כַּר

kar — H3733

Transliteration: kar

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3733

Derivation: from H3769 (כָּרַר) in the sense of plumpness;

Definition

a ram (as full-grown and fat), including a battering-ram (as butting); hence, a meadow (as for sheep); also a pad or camel's saddle (as puffed out)

KJV Translation

captain, furniture, lamb, (large) pasture, ram. See also H1033 (בֵּית כַּר), H3746 (כָּרִי).
