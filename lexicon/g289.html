ἀμπελουργός

ampelourgós — G289

Transliteration: ampelourgós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G289

Derivation: from G288 (ἄμπελος) and G2041 (ἔργον);

Definition

a vine-worker, i.e. pruner

KJV Translation

vine-dresser

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

vine-dresser, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; title of plays by [Refs 4th c.BC+] and Alexis; compare ἀμπελοεργός.
