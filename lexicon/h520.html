אַמָּה

ʼammâh — H520

Transliteration: ʼammâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H520

Derivation: prolonged from H517 (אֵם);

Definition

properly, a mother (i.e. unit of measure, or the fore-arm (below the elbow), i.e. a cubit; also a door-base (as a bond of the entrance)

KJV Translation

cubit, [phrase] hundred (by exchange for H3967 (מֵאָה)), measure, post.
