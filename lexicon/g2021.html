ἐπιχειρέω

epicheiréō — G2021

Transliteration: epicheiréō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2021

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G5495 (χείρ);

Definition

to put the hand upon, i.e. undertake

KJV Translation

go about, take in hand (upon)
