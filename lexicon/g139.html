αἵρεσις

haíresis — G139

Transliteration: haíresis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G139

Derivation: from G138 (αἱρέομαι);

Definition

properly, a choice, i.e. (specially) a party or (abstractly) disunion

KJV Translation

heresy (which is the Greek word itself), sect
