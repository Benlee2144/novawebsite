πυγμή

pygmḗ — G4435

Transliteration: pygmḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4435

Derivation: from a primary (the fist as a weapon);

Definition

the clenched hand, i.e. (only in dative case as adverb) with the fist (hard scrubbing)

KJV Translation

oft
