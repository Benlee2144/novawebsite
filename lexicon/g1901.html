ἐπεκτείνομαι

epekteínomai — G1901

Transliteration: epekteínomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1901

Derivation: middle voice from G1909 (ἐπί) and G1614 (ἐκτείνω);

Definition

to stretch (oneself) forward upon

KJV Translation

reach forth
