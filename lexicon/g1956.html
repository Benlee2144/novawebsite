ἐπιλύω

epilýō — G1956

Transliteration: epilýō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1956

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G3089 (λύω);

Definition

to solve further, i.e. (figuratively) to explain, decide

KJV Translation

determine, expound
