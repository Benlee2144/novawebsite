κατευθύνω

kateuthýnō — G2720

Transliteration: kateuthýnō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2720

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G2116 (εὐθύνω);

Definition

to straighten fully, i.e. (figuratively) direct

KJV Translation

guide, direct
