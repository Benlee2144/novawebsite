πολυλογία

polylogía — G4180

Transliteration: polylogía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4180

Derivation: from a compound of G4183 (πολύς) and G3056 (λόγος);

Definition

loquacity, i.e. prolixity
