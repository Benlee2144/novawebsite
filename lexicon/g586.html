ἀποδεκατόω

apodekatóō — G586

Transliteration: apodekatóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G586

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G1183 (δεκατόω);

Definition

to tithe (as debtor or creditor)

KJV Translation

(give, pay, take) tithe

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
