ἡσυχάζω

hēsycházō — G2270

Transliteration: hēsycházō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2270

Derivation: from the same as G2272 (ἡσύχιος);

Definition

to keep still (intransitively), i.e. refrain from labor, meddlesomeness or speech

KJV Translation

cease, hold peace, be quiet, rest
