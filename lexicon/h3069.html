Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H3069

יְהֹוִה

Yᵉhôvih — H3069

Transliteration: Yᵉhôvih

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3069

Derivation: a variation of H3068 (יְהֹוָה) (used after H136 (אֲדֹנָי), and pronounced by Jews as H430 (אֱלֹהִים), in order to prevent the repetition of the same sound, since they elsewhere pronounce H3068 (יְהֹוָה) as H136 (אֲדֹנָי))

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

1) Jehovah-used primarily in the combination 'Lord Jehovah'
1a) equal to ye.ho.vah (יהוה "LORD" H3068) but pointed with the vowels of e.lo.him (אֱלֹהִים "God" H0430)
