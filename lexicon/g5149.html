τρίζω

trízō — G5149

Transliteration: trízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5149

Derivation: apparently a primary verb;

Definition

to creak (squeak), i.e. (by analogy) to grate the teeth (in frenzy)

KJV Translation

gnash
