ἀλυσιτελής

alysitelḗs — G255

Transliteration: alysitelḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G255

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and the base of G3081 (λυσιτελεῖ);

Definition

gainless, i.e. (by implication) pernicious

KJV Translation

unprofitable
