εἱλίσσω

heilíssō — G1507

Transliteration: heilíssō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1507

Derivation: a prolonged form of a primary but defective verb (of the same meaning);

Definition

to coil or wrap

KJV Translation

roll together
