ἐπιφαίνω

epiphaínō — G2014

Transliteration: epiphaínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2014

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G5316 (φαίνω);

Definition

to shine upon, i.e. become (literally) visible or (figuratively) known

KJV Translation

appear, give light
