εὐχάριστος

eucháristos — G2170

Transliteration: eucháristos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2170

Derivation: from G2095 (εὖ) and a derivative of G5483 (χαρίζομαι);

Definition

well favored, i.e. (by implication) grateful

KJV Translation

thankful
