נֵס

nêç — H5251

Transliteration: nêç

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5251

Derivation: from H5264 (נָסַס);

Definition

a flag; also a sail; by implication, a flagstaff; generally a signal; figuratively, a token

KJV Translation

banner, pole, sail, (en-) sign, standard.
