θάλπω

thálpō — G2282

Transliteration: thálpō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2282

Derivation: probably akin to (to warm);

Definition

to brood, i.e. (figuratively) to foster

KJV Translation

cherish
