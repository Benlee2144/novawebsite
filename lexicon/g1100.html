Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1100

γλῶσσα

glōssa — G1100

Transliteration: glōssa

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1100

Derivation: of uncertain affinity;

Definition

the tongue; by implication, a language (specially, one naturally unacquired)

KJV Translation

tongue
