κάλαμος

kálamos — G2563

Transliteration: kálamos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2563

Derivation: of uncertain affinity;

Definition

a reed (the plant or its stem, or that of a similar plant); by implication, a pen

KJV Translation

pen, reed
