θαυμαστός

thaumastós — G2298

Transliteration: thaumastós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2298

Derivation: from G2296 (θαυμάζω);

Definition

wondered at, i.e. (by implication) wonderful

KJV Translation

marvel(-lous)
