Φίλιππος

Phílippos — G5376

Transliteration: Phílippos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5376

Derivation: from G5384 (φίλος) and G2462 (ἵππος);

Definition

fond of horses; Philippus, the name of four Israelites

KJV Translation
