στρατολογέω

stratologéō — G4758

Transliteration: stratologéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4758

Derivation: from a compound of the base of G4756 (στρατιά) and G3004 (λέγω) (in its original sense);

Definition

to gather (or select) as a warrior, i.e. enlist in the army

KJV Translation

choose to be a soldier
