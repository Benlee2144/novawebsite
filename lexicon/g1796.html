ἐνυβρίζω

enybrízō — G1796

Transliteration: enybrízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1796

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and G5195 (ὑβρίζω);

Definition

to insult

KJV Translation

do despite unto

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐνυβρ-ίζω,
insult or mock one in a thing, τινά τινι [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 with dative person, insult, γυναιξίν [Refs 2nd c.BC+]; εἴς τινα [Refs 1st c.BC+]
__3 absolutely, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__4 in passive, in Medicine texts, of ulcers, to be irritated, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]

Hebrews 10:29
