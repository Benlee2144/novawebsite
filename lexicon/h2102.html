Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H2102

זוּד

zûwd — H2102

Transliteration: zûwd

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2102

Derivation: or (by permutation) זִיד; a primitive root;

Definition

to seethe; figuratively, to be insolent

KJV Translation

be proud, deal proudly, presume, (come) presumptuously, sod.

Appears In (8 verses)

Deuteronomy 17:13Deuteronomy 18:20Exodus 18:11Exodus 21:14Jeremiah 50:29Nehemiah 9:10Nehemiah 9:16Nehemiah 9:29
