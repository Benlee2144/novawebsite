εὐθύνω

euthýnō — G2116

Transliteration: euthýnō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2116

Derivation: from G2117 (εὐθύς);

Definition

to straighten (level); technically, to steer

KJV Translation

governor, make straight
