πρόδηλος

pródēlos — G4271

Transliteration: pródēlos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4271

Derivation: from G4253 (πρό) and G1212 (δῆλος);

Definition

plain before all men, i.e. obvious

KJV Translation

evident, manifest (open) beforehand
