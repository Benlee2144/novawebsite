ὑπόδικος

hypódikos — G5267

Transliteration: hypódikos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5267

Derivation: from G5259 (ὑπό) and G1349 (δίκη);

Definition

under sentence, i.e. (by implication) condemned

KJV Translation

guilty

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
