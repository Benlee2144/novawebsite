ἐπιχορηγέω

epichorēgéō — G2023

Transliteration: epichorēgéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2023

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G5524 (χορηγέω);

Definition

to furnish besides, i.e. fully supply, (figuratively) aid or contribute

KJV Translation

add, minister (nourishment, unto)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐπιχορηγ-έω,
supply, furnish, [NT+2nd c.BC+]; especially of a husband, provide for a wife, ἐ. τῇ γαμουμένῃ τὰ δέοντα [Refs 1st c.AD+]; conversely, ἐὰν [γυνὴ] ἐ. τῷ ἀνδρὶ αὐτῆς [LXX]:—passive, τὰ ἀπὸ Λιμυρικῆς -ούμενα [NT+1st c.BC+]
