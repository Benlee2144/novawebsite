הֵן

hên — H2005

Transliteration: hên

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2005

Derivation: a primitive particle;

Definition

lo!; also (as expressing surprise) if

KJV Translation

behold, if, lo, though.

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

interj
1) behold, lo, though hypothetical part
2) if
Aramaic equivalent: hen (הֵן "look!" H2006A)
