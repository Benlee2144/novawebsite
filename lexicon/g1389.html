δολόω

dolóō — G1389

Transliteration: dolóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1389

Derivation: from G1388 (δόλος);

Definition

to ensnare, i.e. (figuratively) adulterate

KJV Translation

handle deceitfully
