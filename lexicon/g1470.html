ἐγκρύπτω

enkrýptō — G1470

Transliteration: enkrýptō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1470

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and G2928 (κρύπτω);

Definition

to conceal in, i.e. incorporate with

KJV Translation

hid in
