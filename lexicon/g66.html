ἄγριος

ágrios — G66

Transliteration: ágrios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G66

Derivation: from G68 (ἀγρός);

Definition

wild (as pertaining to the country), literally (natural) or figuratively (fierce)

KJV Translation

wild, raging
