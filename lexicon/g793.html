ἀστήρικτος

astḗriktos — G793

Transliteration: astḗriktos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G793

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G4741 (στηρίζω);

Definition

unfixed, i.e. (figuratively) vacillating

KJV Translation

unstable
