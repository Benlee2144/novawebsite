εὔσημος

eúsēmos — G2154

Transliteration: eúsēmos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2154

Derivation: from G2095 (εὖ) and the base of G4591 (σημαίνω);

Definition

well indicated, i.e. (figuratively) significant

KJV Translation

easy to be understood
