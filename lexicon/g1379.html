Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1379

δογματίζω

dogmatízō — G1379

Transliteration: dogmatízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1379

Derivation: from G1378 (δόγμα);

Definition

to prescribe by statute, i.e. (reflexively) to submit to, ceremonially rule

KJV Translation

be subject to ordinances
