γαζοφυλάκιον

gazophylákion — G1049

Transliteration: gazophylákion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1049

Derivation: from G1047 (γάζα) and G5438 (φυλακή);

Definition

a treasure-house, i.e. a court in the temple for the collection-boxes

KJV Translation

treasury
