καταμένω

kataménō — G2650

Transliteration: kataménō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2650

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G3306 (μένω);

Definition

to stay fully, i.e. reside

KJV Translation

abide
