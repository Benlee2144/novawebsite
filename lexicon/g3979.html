πεζῇ

pezēi — G3979

Transliteration: pezēi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3979

Derivation: dative case feminine of a derivative of G4228 (πούς) (as adverb);

Definition

foot-wise, i.e. by walking

KJV Translation

a- (on) foot
