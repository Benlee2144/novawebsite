ἀποτάσσομαι

apotássomai — G657

Transliteration: apotássomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G657

Derivation: middle voice from G575 (ἀπό) and G5021 (τάσσω);

Definition

literally, to say adieu (by departing or dismissing); figuratively, to renounce

KJV Translation

bid farewell, forsake, take leave, send away
