Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H6828

צָפוֹן

tsâphôwn — H6828

Transliteration: tsâphôwn

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6828

Derivation: or צָפֹן; from H6845 (צָפַן);

Definition

properly, hidden, i.e. dark; used only of the north as aquarter (gloomy and unknown)

KJV Translation

north(-ern, side, -ward, wind).
