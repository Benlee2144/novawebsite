αὐχμηρός

auchmērós — G850

Transliteration: auchmērós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G850

Derivation: from (probably from a base akin to that of G109 (ἀήρ)) (dust, as dried by wind);

Definition

properly, dirty, i.e. (by implication) obscure

KJV Translation

dark
