πρόσφατος

prósphatos — G4372

Transliteration: prósphatos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4372

Derivation: from G4253 (πρό) and a derivative of G4969 (σφάζω);

Definition

previously (recently) slain (fresh), i.e. (figuratively) lately made

KJV Translation

new
