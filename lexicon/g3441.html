μόνος

mónos — G3441

Transliteration: mónos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3441

Derivation: probably from G3306 (μένω);

Definition

remaining, i.e. sole or single; by implication, mere

KJV Translation

alone, only, by themselves
