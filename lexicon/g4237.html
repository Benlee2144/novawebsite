πρασιά

prasiá — G4237

Transliteration: prasiá

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4237

Derivation: perhaps from (a leek, and so an onion-patch);

Definition

a garden plot, i.e. (by implication, of regular beds) a row (repeated in plural by Hebraism, to indicate an arrangement)

KJV Translation

in ranks

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
