פָּלַל

pâlal — H6419

Transliteration: pâlal

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6419

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to judge (officially or mentally); by extension, to intercede, pray

KJV Translation

intreat, judge(-ment), (make) pray(-er, -ing), make supplication.
