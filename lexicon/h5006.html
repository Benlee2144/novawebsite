נָאַץ

nâʼats — H5006

Transliteration: nâʼats

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5006

Derivation: a primitive root; in Ecclesiastes 12:5, by interchange for H5132 (נוּץ), to bloom

Definition

to scorn;

KJV Translation

abhor, (give occasion to) blaspheme, contemn, despise, flourish, [idiom] great, provoke.
