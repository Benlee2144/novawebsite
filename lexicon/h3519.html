כָּבוֹד

kâbôwd — H3519

Transliteration: kâbôwd

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3519

Derivation: rarely כָּבֹד; from H3513 (כָּבַד);

Definition

properly, weight, but only figuratively in a good sense, splendor or copiousness

KJV Translation

glorious(-ly), glory, honour(-able).
