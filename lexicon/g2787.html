κιβωτός

kibōtós — G2787

Transliteration: kibōtós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2787

Derivation: of uncertain derivation;

Definition

a box, i.e. the sacred ark and that of Noah

KJV Translation
