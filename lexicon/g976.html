βίβλος

bíblos — G976

Transliteration: bíblos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G976

Derivation: properly, the inner bark of the papyrus plant, i.e. (by implication) a sheet or scroll of writing

KJV Translation

book
