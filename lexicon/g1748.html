ἐνεδρεύω

enedreúō — G1748

Transliteration: enedreúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1748

Derivation: from G1747 (ἐνέδρα);

Definition

to lurk, i.e. (figuratively) plot assassination

KJV Translation

lay wait for
