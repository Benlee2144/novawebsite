ἀποκόπτω

apokóptō — G609

Transliteration: apokóptō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G609

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G2875 (κόπτω);

Definition

to amputate; reflexively (by irony) to mutilate (the privy parts)

KJV Translation

cut off
