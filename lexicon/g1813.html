ἐξαλείφω

exaleíphō — G1813

Transliteration: exaleíphō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1813

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G218 (ἀλείφω);

Definition

to smear out, i.e. obliterate (erase tears, figuratively, pardon sin)

KJV Translation

blot out, wipe away
