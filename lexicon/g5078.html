τέχνη

téchnē — G5078

Transliteration: téchnē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5078

Derivation: from the base of G5088 (τίκτω);

Definition

art (as productive), i.e. (specially), a trade, or (generally) skill

KJV Translation

art, craft, occupation
