Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G5575

ψευδομάρτυρ

pseudomártyr — G5575

Transliteration: pseudomártyr

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5575

Derivation: from G5571 (ψευδής) and a kindred form of G3144 (μάρτυς);

Definition

a spurious witness, i.e. bearer of untrue testimony

KJV Translation

false witness

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ψευδομάρτυς, ῠρος, ὁ,
