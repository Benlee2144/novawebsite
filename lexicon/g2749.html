κεῖμαι

keîmai — G2749

Transliteration: keîmai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2749

Derivation: middle voice of a primary verb;

Definition

to lie outstretched (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

be (appointed, laid up, made, set), lay, lie
