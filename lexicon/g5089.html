τίλλω

tíllō — G5089

Transliteration: tíllō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5089

Derivation: perhaps akin to the alternate of G138 (αἱρέομαι), and thus to G4951 (σύρω);

Definition

to pull off

KJV Translation

pluck
