καύχησις

kaúchēsis — G2746

Transliteration: kaúchēsis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2746

Derivation: from G2744 (καυχάομαι);

Definition

boasting (properly, the act; by implication, the object), in a good or a bad sense

KJV Translation

boasting, whereof I may glory, glorying, rejoicing

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
