ἐλαττόω

elattóō — G1642

Transliteration: elattóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1642

Derivation: from G1640 (ἐλάσσων);

Definition

to lessen (in rank or influence)

KJV Translation

decrease, make lower
