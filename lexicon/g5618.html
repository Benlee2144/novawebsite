ὥσπερ

hṓsper — G5618

Transliteration: hṓsper

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5618

Derivation: from G5613 (ὡς) and G4007 (περ);

Definition

just as, i.e. exactly like

KJV Translation

(even, like) as
