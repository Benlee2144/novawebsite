δοκίμιον

dokímion — G1383

Transliteration: dokímion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1383

Derivation: neuter of a presumed derivative of G1382 (δοκιμή);

Definition

a testing; by implication, trustworthiness

KJV Translation

trial, trying

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

δοκῐμ-εῖον, τό,
test, means of testing, [Refs 5th c.BC+] (variant δοκίμιον, as in [NT+1st c.BC+]; proof, εὐσεβείας [Refs 1st c.BC+]
