מָחָה

mâchâh — H4229

Transliteration: mâchâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4229

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

properly, to stroke or rub; by implication, to erase; also to smooth (as if with oil), i.e. grease or make fat; also to touch, i.e. reach to

KJV Translation

abolish, blot out, destroy, full of marrow, put out, reach unto, [idiom] utterly, wipe (away, out).
