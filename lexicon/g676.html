ἀπρόσιτος

aprósitos — G676

Transliteration: aprósitos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G676

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a derivative of a comparative of G4314 (πρός) and (to go);

Definition

inaccessible

KJV Translation

which no man can approach
