Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H2656

חֵפֶץ

chêphets — H2656

Transliteration: chêphets

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2656

Derivation: from H2654 (חָפֵץ);

Definition

pleasure; hence (abstractly) desire; concretely, a valuable thing; hence (by extension) a matter (as something in mind)

KJV Translation

acceptable, delight(-some), desire, things desired, matter, pleasant(-ure), purpose, willingly.
