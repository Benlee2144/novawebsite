ἅγιος

hágios — G40

Transliteration: hágios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G40

Derivation: from (an awful thing) (compare G53 (ἁγνός), G2282 (θάλπω));

Definition

sacred (physically, pure, morally blameless or religious, ceremonially, consecrated)

KJV Translation

(most) holy (one, thing), saint
