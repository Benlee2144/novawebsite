προαιρέομαι

proairéomai — G4255

Transliteration: proairéomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4255

Derivation: from G4253 (πρό) and G138 (αἱρέομαι);

Definition

to choose for oneself before another thing (prefer), i.e. (by implication) to propose (intend)

KJV Translation

purpose
