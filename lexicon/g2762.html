κεραία

keraía — G2762

Transliteration: keraía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2762

Derivation: feminine of a presumed derivative of the base of G2768 (κέρας);

Definition

something horn-like, i.e. (specially) the apex of a Hebrew letter (figuratively, the least particle)

KJV Translation

tittle
