ὅπλον

hóplon — G3696

Transliteration: hóplon

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3696

Derivation: probably from a primary (to be busy about);

Definition

an implement or utensil or tool (literally or figuratively, especially, offensive for war)

KJV Translation

armour, instrument, weapon

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
