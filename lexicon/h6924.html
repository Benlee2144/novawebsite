Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H6924

קֶדֶם

qedem — H6924

Transliteration: qedem

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6924

Derivation: or קֵדְמָה; from H6923 (קָדַם);

Definition

the front, of place (absolutely, the fore part, relatively the East) or time (antiquity); often used adverbially (before, anciently, eastward)

KJV Translation

aforetime, ancient (time), before, east (end, part, side, -ward), eternal, [idiom] ever(-lasting), forward, old, past. Compare H6926 (קִדְמָה).
