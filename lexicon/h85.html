אַבְרָהָם

ʼAbrâhâm — H85

Transliteration: ʼAbrâhâm

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H85

Derivation: contracted from H1 (אָב) and an unused root (probably meaning to be populous); father of a multitude;

Definition
