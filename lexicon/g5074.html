τετράπους

tetrápous — G5074

Transliteration: tetrápous

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5074

Derivation: from G5064 (τέσσαρες) and G4228 (πούς);

Definition

a quadruped

KJV Translation

fourfooted beast

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

τετρά-πους, ὁ, ἡ, πουν, τό,
genitive ποδος, plural ποδα, four-footed, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; λεία τ. a booty of cattle, [Refs 2nd c.BC+]; τετράποδα ζῴδια, viz. Aries, Taurus, Leo, Sagittarius, [Refs]
__2 τετράπουν, τό, quadruped, beast, [NT+5th c.BC+]
__II of things, δίφρος τ. [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II.2 of four feet in length or area, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
