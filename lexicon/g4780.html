Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4780

συγκαλύπτω

synkalýptō — G4780

Transliteration: synkalýptō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4780

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G2572 (καλύπτω);

Definition

to conceal altogether

KJV Translation

cover

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

συγ-κᾰλύπτω,
cover or veil completely, σὺν δὲ νεφέεσσι κάλυψε γαῖαν [Refs 8th c.BC+]; ὁ συγκεκαλυμμένος πατήρ, with reference to a well-known fallacy [Refs 4th c.BC+]; ἐξάγει συγκεκαλυμμένην muffled up, [LXX+1st c.AD+]middle, aorist συγκαλύψασθαι, wrap oneself up, cover one's face, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 intransitive in active, λόγος συγκαλύψας ἀχλύϊ [Refs 4th c.AD+]

Appears In (1 verse)

Luke 12:2
