שָׁכַח

shâkach — H7911

Transliteration: shâkach

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7911

Derivation: or שָׁכֵחַ; a primitive root;

Definition

to mislay, i.e. to be oblivious of, from want of memory or attention

KJV Translation

[idiom] at all, (cause to) forget.
