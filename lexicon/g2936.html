κτίζω

ktízō — G2936

Transliteration: ktízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2936

Derivation: probably akin to G2932 (κτάομαι) (through the idea of proprietorship of the manufacturer);

Definition

to fabricate, i.e. found (form originally)

KJV Translation

create, Creator, make
