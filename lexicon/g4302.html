προλέγω

prolégō — G4302

Transliteration: prolégō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4302

Derivation: from G4253 (πρό) and G3004 (λέγω);

Definition

to say beforehand, i.e. predict, forewarn

KJV Translation

foretell, tell before

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
