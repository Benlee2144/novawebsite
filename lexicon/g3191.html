Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3191

μελετάω

meletáō — G3191

Transliteration: meletáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3191

Derivation: from a presumed derivative of G3199 (μέλω);

Definition

to take care of, i.e. (by implication) revolve in the mind

KJV Translation

imagine, (pre-)meditate
