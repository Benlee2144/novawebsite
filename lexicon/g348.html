ἀνακόπτω

anakóptō — G348

Transliteration: anakóptō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G348

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and G2875 (κόπτω);

Definition

to beat back, i.e. check

KJV Translation

hinder

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

1. to drive back , (Odyssey by Homer)
2. to beat back an assailant, (Thucydides)
3. to stop:—; Pass. to be stopped, stop short , τινός from a thing, (Lucian) (ML)
