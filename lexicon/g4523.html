Σαδδουκαῖος

Saddoukaîos — G4523

Transliteration: Saddoukaîos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4523

Derivation: probably from G4524 (Σαδώκ);

Definition

a Sadducæan (i.e. Tsadokian), or follower of a certain heretical Israelite

KJV Translation

Sadducee
