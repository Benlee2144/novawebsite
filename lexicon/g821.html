ἀτιμόω

atimóō — G821

Transliteration: atimóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G821

Derivation: from G820 (ἄτιμος);

Definition

used like G818 (ἀτιμάζω), to maltreat

KJV Translation

handle shamefully
