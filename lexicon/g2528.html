καθοπλίζω

kathoplízō — G2528

Transliteration: kathoplízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2528

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά);

Definition

and G3695 (ὁπλίζω); to equip fully with armor

KJV Translation

arm

__II array, set in order: metaphorically, τὸ μὴ καλὸν καθοπλίσασα δύο φέρειν so ordering that which is not well as to.., [Refs 5th c.BC+]
