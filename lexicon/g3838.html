παντελής

pantelḗs — G3838

Transliteration: pantelḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3838

Derivation: from G3956 (πᾶς) and G5056 (τέλος);

Definition

full-ended, i.e. entire (neuter as noun, completion)

KJV Translation

+ in (no) wise, uttermost
