πραιτώριον

praitṓrion — G4232

Transliteration: praitṓrion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4232

Derivation: of Latin origin;

Definition

the prætorium or governor's courtroom (sometimes including the whole edifice and camp)

KJV Translation

(common, judgment) hall (of judgment), palace, prætorium

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

πραιτώριον, τό,
= Latin Praetorium, official residence of a governor, [NT]; later, of private residences, [Refs 6th c.AD+]
__II praetorian guard: ἔπαρχος πραιτωρίον, τοῦ π., = praefectus praetorio, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
__II.2 imperial household, Ep. Phil.1.13.
