διαδίδωμι

diadídōmi — G1239

Transliteration: diadídōmi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1239

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G1325 (δίδωμι);

Definition

to give throughout a crowd, i.e. deal out; also to deliver over (as to a successor)

KJV Translation

(make) distribute(-ion), divide, give
