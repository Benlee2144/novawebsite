ἀθετέω

athetéō — G114

Transliteration: athetéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G114

Derivation: from a compound of G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G5087 (τίθημι);

Definition

to set aside, i.e. (by implication) to disesteem, neutralize or violate

KJV Translation

cast off, despise, disannul, frustrate, bring to nought, reject

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
