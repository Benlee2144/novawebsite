ἐρημόω

erēmóō — G2049

Transliteration: erēmóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2049

Derivation: from G2048 (ἔρημος);

Definition

to lay waste (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

(bring to, make) desolate(-ion), come to nought
