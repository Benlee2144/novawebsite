λείπω

leípō — G3007

Transliteration: leípō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3007

Derivation: a primary verb;

Definition

to leave, i.e. (intransitively or passively) to fail or be absent

KJV Translation

be destitute (wanting), lack
