ὑμῖν

hymîn — G5213

Transliteration: hymîn

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5213

Derivation: irregular dative case of G5210 (ὑμεῖς);

Definition

to (with or by) you

KJV Translation

ye, you, your(-selves)
