אֶפְרַיִם

ʼEphrayim — H669

Transliteration: ʼEphrayim

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H669

Derivation: dual of masculine form of H672 (אֶפְרָת); double fruit;

Definition

Ephrajim, a son of Joseph; also the tribe descended from him, and its territory

KJV Translation

Ephraim, Ephraimites.
