τελώνιον

telṓnion — G5058

Transliteration: telṓnion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5058

Derivation: neuter of a presumed derivative of G5057 (τελώνης);

Definition

a tax-gatherer's place of business

KJV Translation

receipt of custom
