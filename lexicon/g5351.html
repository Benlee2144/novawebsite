φθείρω

phtheírō — G5351

Transliteration: phtheírō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5351

Derivation: probably strengthened from (to pine or waste);

Definition

properly, to shrivel or wither, i.e. to spoil (by any process) or (generally) to ruin (especially figuratively, by moral influences, to deprave)

KJV Translation

corrupt (self), defile, destroy
