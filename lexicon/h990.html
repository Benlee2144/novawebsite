בֶּטֶן

beṭen — H990

Transliteration: beṭen

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H990

Derivation: from an unused root probably meaning to be hollow;

Definition

the belly, especially the womb; also the bosom or body of anything

KJV Translation

belly, body, [phrase] as they be born, [phrase] within, womb.
