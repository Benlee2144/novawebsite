ἐπάγγελμα

epángelma — G1862

Transliteration: epángelma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1862

Derivation: from G1861 (ἐπαγγέλλω);

Definition

a self-committal (by assurance of conferring some good)

KJV Translation

promise
