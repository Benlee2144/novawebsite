Σιδών

Sidṓn — G4605

Transliteration: Sidṓn

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4605

Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H06721);

Definition

Sidon (i.e. Tsidon), a place in Palestine

KJV Translation

Sidon

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

Σῑδών, ῶνος, ἡ,
[Refs 8th c.BC+]: hence adjective Σῑδόνιος, α, ον, [Refs 8th c.BC+]; later Σιδώνιος, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; feminine Σιδωνιάς, άδος, [Refs 5th c.BC+]:—Σῐδόνες, οἱ, men of Sidon, [Refs 8th c.BC+]; also Σιδόνιοι, [Refs 8th c.BC+]
