κατασύρω

katasýrō — G2694

Transliteration: katasýrō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2694

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G4951 (σύρω);

Definition

to drag down, i.e. arrest judicially
