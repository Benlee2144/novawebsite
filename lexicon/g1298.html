διαταράσσω

diatarássō — G1298

Transliteration: diatarássō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1298

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G5015 (ταράσσω);

Definition

to disturb wholly, i.e. agitate (with alarm)

KJV Translation

trouble
