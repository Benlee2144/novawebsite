בָּרַח

bârach — H1272

Transliteration: bârach

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1272

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to bolt, i.e. figuratively, to flee suddenly

KJV Translation

chase (away); drive away, fain, flee (away), put to flight, make haste, reach, run away, shoot.
