φανερός

phanerós — G5318

Transliteration: phanerós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5318

Derivation: from G5316 (φαίνω);

Definition

shining, i.e. apparent (literally or figuratively); neuter (as adverb) publicly, externally

KJV Translation

abroad, + appear, known, manifest, open (+ -ly), outward (+ -ly)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
