ἀποδοκιμάζω

apodokimázō — G593

Transliteration: apodokimázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G593

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G1381 (δοκιμάζω);

Definition

to disapprove, i.e. (by implication) to repudiate

KJV Translation

disallow, reject
