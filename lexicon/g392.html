ἀνατάσσομαι

anatássomai — G392

Transliteration: anatássomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G392

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and the middle voice of G5021 (τάσσω);

Definition

to arrange

KJV Translation

set in order

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀνατάσσω,
Attic dialect ἀνα-τάττω, aorist 2 passive ἀνετάγην, countermand expenditure, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]:—middle, go regularly through again, rehearse, [Refs 1st c.AD+]; set in order, διήγησιν[NT]
