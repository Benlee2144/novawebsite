τετρακισχίλιοι

tetrakischílioi — G5070

Transliteration: tetrakischílioi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5070

Derivation: from the multiplicative adverb of G5064 (τέσσαρες) and G5507 (χίλιοι);

Definition

four times a thousand

KJV Translation

four thousand
