תְּהוֹם

tᵉhôwm — H8415

Transliteration: tᵉhôwm

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H8415

Derivation: or תְּהֹם; (usually feminine) from H1949 (הוּם);

Definition

an abyss (as a surging mass of water), especially the deep (the main sea or the subterranean watersupply)

KJV Translation

deep (place), depth.
