ἀπείραστος

apeírastos — G551

Transliteration: apeírastos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G551

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G3987 (πειράω);

Definition

untried, i.e. not temptable

KJV Translation

not to be tempted

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀπείραστος, ον,
incapable of being tempted, κακῶν[NT]
__II without experience, τῶν ἀβουλήτων[Refs 4th c.AD+]
__III not experienced, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]; untried, τέχνη[Refs 5th c.BC+]
