ἔκβασις

ékbasis — G1545

Transliteration: ékbasis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1545

Derivation: from a compound of G1537 (ἐκ) and the base of G939 (βάσις) (meaning to go out);

Definition

an exit (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

end, way to escape
