πλεονάζω

pleonázō — G4121

Transliteration: pleonázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4121

Derivation: from G4119 (πλείων);

Definition

to do, make or be more, i.e. increase (transitively or intransitively); by extension, to superabound

KJV Translation

abound, abundant, make to increase, have over
