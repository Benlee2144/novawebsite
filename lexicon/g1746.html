ἐνδύω

endýō — G1746

Transliteration: endýō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1746

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and G1416 (δύνω) (in the sense of sinking into a garment);

Definition

to invest with clothing (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

array, clothe (with), endue, have (put) on
