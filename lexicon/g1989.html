ἐπιστέλλω

epistéllō — G1989

Transliteration: epistéllō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1989

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G4724 (στέλλω);

Definition

to enjoin (by writing), i.e. (genitive case) to communicate by letter (for any purpose)

KJV Translation

write (a letter, unto)
