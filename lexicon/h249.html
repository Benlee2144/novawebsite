Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H249

אֶזְרָח

ʼezrâch — H249

Transliteration: ʼezrâch

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H249

Derivation: from H2224 (זָרַח) (in the sense of springing up);

Definition

a spontaneous growth, i.e. native (tree or persons)

KJV Translation

bay tree, (home-) born (in the land), of the (one's own) country (nation).

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

1) a native (one rising from the soil)
1a) of man, native Israelites
1b) of tree, native (to Israel)
