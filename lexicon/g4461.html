Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4461

ῥαββί

rhabbí — G4461

Transliteration: rhabbí

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4461

Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H07227 with pronominal suffix);

Definition

my master, i.e Rabbi, as an official title of honor

KJV Translation

Master, Rabbi
