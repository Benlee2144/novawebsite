διαίρεσις

diaíresis — G1243

Transliteration: diaíresis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1243

Derivation: from G1244 (διαιρέω);

Definition

a distinction or (concretely) variety

KJV Translation

difference, diversity

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

δι-αίρεσις, -εως, ἡ
(διαιρέω), [in LXX chiefly for מַחֲלֹקֶת ;]
__1. a distinction, diference.
__2. a division, distribution: 1Co.12:4-6 (cf. διαιρέω; Cremer, 616).†
