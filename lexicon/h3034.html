Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H3034

יָדָה

yâdâh — H3034

Transliteration: yâdâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3034

Derivation: a primitive root; used only as denominative from H3027 (יָד); literally, to use (i.e. hold out) the hand;

Definition

physically, to throw (a stone, an arrow) at or away; especially to revere or worship (with extended hands); intensively, to bemoan (by wringing the hands)

KJV Translation

cast (out), (make) confess(-ion), praise, shoot, (give) thank(-ful, -s, -sgiving).
