στατήρ

statḗr — G4715

Transliteration: statḗr

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4715

Derivation: from the base of G2746 (καύχησις);

Definition

a stander (standard of value), i.e. (specially), a stater or certain coin

KJV Translation

piece of money
