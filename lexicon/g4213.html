πόσις

pósis — G4213

Transliteration: pósis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4213

Derivation: from the alternate of G4095 (πίνω);

Definition

a drinking (the act), i.e. (concretely) a draught

KJV Translation

drink

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

πόσῐς, ιος,

__2 draught, αἷμα πίεται τρίτην πόσιν [Refs 5th c.BC+]
