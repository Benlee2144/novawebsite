פֶּטֶר

peṭer — H6363

Transliteration: peṭer

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6363

Derivation: or פִּטְרָה; from H6362 (פָּטַר);

Definition

a fissure, i.e. (concretely) firstling (as opening the matrix)

KJV Translation

firstling, openeth, such as open.

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

firstborn, firstling, that which separates or first opens
Another spelling of pe.ter (פֶּ֫טֶר "firstborn" H6363A)
