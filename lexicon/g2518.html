καθεύδω

katheúdō — G2518

Transliteration: katheúdō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2518

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and (to sleep);

Definition

to lie down to rest, i.e. (by implication) to fall asleep (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

(be a-)sleep
