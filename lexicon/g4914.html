συνήθεια

synḗtheia — G4914

Transliteration: synḗtheia

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4914

Derivation: from a compound of G4862 (σύν) and G2239 (ἦθος);

Definition

mutual habituation, i.e. usage

KJV Translation

custom
