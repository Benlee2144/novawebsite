πρωτοκαθεδρία

prōtokathedría — G4410

Transliteration: prōtokathedría

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4410

Derivation: from G4413 (πρῶτος) and G2515 (καθέδρα);

Definition

a sitting first (in the front row), i.e. preeminence in council

KJV Translation

chief (highest, uppermost) seat
