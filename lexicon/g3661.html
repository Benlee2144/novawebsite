ὁμοθυμαδόν

homothymadón — G3661

Transliteration: homothymadón

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3661

Derivation: adverb from a compound of the base of G3674 (ὁμοῦ) and G2372 (θυμός);

Definition

unanimously

KJV Translation

with one accord (mind)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
