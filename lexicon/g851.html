ἀφαιρέω

aphairéō — G851

Transliteration: aphairéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G851

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G138 (αἱρέομαι);

Definition

to remove (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

cut (smite) off, take away
