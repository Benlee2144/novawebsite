ἀνθομολογέομαι

anthomologéomai — G437

Transliteration: anthomologéomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G437

Derivation: from G473 (ἀντί) and the middle voice of G3670 (ὁμολογέω);

Definition

to confess in turn, i.e. respond in praise

KJV Translation

give thanks

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀνθομολογ-έομαι,
make a mutual agreement or covenant, πρός τινα[Refs 4th c.BC+]
__II confess freely and openly, τὰς ἀρετάς τινος[Refs 2nd c.BC+]
__II.2 admit, signify, πρός τινα μηδὲν ἑωρακέναι[Refs 1st c.AD+]
__II.3 assent, agree, τοῖς εἰρημένοις[Refs 2nd c.BC+]
__II.4 return thanks to God, [LXX+NT]; χάριν ἀ. return thanks, [Refs 1st c.AD+] —active, -λογέω admit a claim, is late, [Refs 3rd c.AD+]
