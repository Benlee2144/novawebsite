בָּקָר

bâqâr — H1241

Transliteration: bâqâr

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1241

Derivation: from H1239 (בָּקַר);

Definition

beef cattle or an animal of the ox family of either gender (as used for plowing); collectively, a herd

KJV Translation
