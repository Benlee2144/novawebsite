σκοπός

skopós — G4649

Transliteration: skopós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4649

Derivation: from (to peer about (\

Definition

perhaps akin to G4626 (σκάπτω) through the idea of concealment; compare G4629 (σκέπασμα)); a watch (sentry or scout), i.e. (by implication) a goal
