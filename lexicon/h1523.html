Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H1523

גִּיל

gîyl — H1523

Transliteration: gîyl

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1523

Derivation: or (by permutation) גּוּל; a primitive root;

Definition

properly, to spin round (under the influence of any violent emotion), i.e. usually rejoice, or (as cringing) fear

KJV Translation

be glad, joy, be joyful, rejoice.
