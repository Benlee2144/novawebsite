στάχυς

stáchys — G4719

Transliteration: stáchys

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4719

Derivation: from the base of G2476 (ἵστημι);

Definition

a head of grain (as standing out from the stalk)

KJV Translation

ear (of corn)
