Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2168

εὐχαριστέω

eucharistéō — G2168

Transliteration: eucharistéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2168

Derivation: from G2170 (εὐχάριστος);

Definition

to be grateful, i.e. (actively) to express gratitude (towards); specially, to say grace at a meal

KJV Translation

(give) thank(-ful, -s)
