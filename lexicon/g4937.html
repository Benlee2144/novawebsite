Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4937

συντρίβω

syntríbō — G4937

Transliteration: syntríbō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4937

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and the base of G5147 (τρίβος);

Definition

to crush completely, i.e. to shatter (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

break (in pieces), broken to shivers (+ -hearted), bruise
