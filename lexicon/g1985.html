ἐπίσκοπος

epískopos — G1985

Transliteration: epískopos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1985

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G4649 (σκοπός) (in the sense of G1983 (ἐπισκοπέω));

Definition

a superintendent, i.e. Christian officer in genitive case charge of a (or the) church (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

bishop, overseer
