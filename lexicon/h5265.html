נָסַע

nâçaʻ — H5265

Transliteration: nâçaʻ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5265

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

properly, to pull up, especially the tent-pins, i.e. start on ajourney

KJV Translation

cause to blow, bring, get, (make to) go (away, forth, forward, onward, out), (take) journey, march, remove, set aside (forward), [idiom] still, be on his (go their) way.
