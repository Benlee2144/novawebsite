Ἰουδαία

Ioudaía — G2449

Transliteration: Ioudaía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2449

Derivation: feminine of G2453 (Ἰουδαῖος) (with G1093 (γῆ) implied);

Definition

the Judæan land (i.e. Judæa), a region of Palestine

KJV Translation

Judæa

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

Ἰουδαῖος, ὁ,
