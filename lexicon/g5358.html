φιλάγαθος

philágathos — G5358

Transliteration: philágathos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5358

Derivation: from G5384 (φίλος) and G18 (ἀγαθός);

Definition

fond to good, i.e. a promoter of virtue

KJV Translation

love of good men
