זִמָּה

zimmâh — H2154

Transliteration: zimmâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2154

Derivation: or זַמָּה; from H2161 (זָמַם);

Definition

a plan, especially a bad one

KJV Translation

heinous crime, lewd(-ly, -ness), mischief, purpose, thought, wicked (device, mind, -ness).
