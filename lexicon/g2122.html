εὐκαίρως

eukaírōs — G2122

Transliteration: eukaírōs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2122

Derivation: adverb from G2121 (εὔκαιρος);

Definition

opportunely

KJV Translation

conveniently, in season
