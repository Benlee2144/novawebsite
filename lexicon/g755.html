ἀρχιτρίκλινος

architríklinos — G755

Transliteration: architríklinos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G755

Derivation: from G746 (ἀρχή) and a compound of G5140 (τρεῖς) and G2827 (κλίνω) (a dinner-bed, because composed of three couches);

Definition

director of the entertainment

KJV Translation

governor (ruler) of the feast

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀρχι-τρίκλῑνος, ὁ,
president of a banquet (triclinium), [NT]
__2 head-waiter, [Refs 3rd c.AD+]
