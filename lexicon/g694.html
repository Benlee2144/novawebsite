ἀργύριον

argýrion — G694

Transliteration: argýrion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G694

Derivation: neuter of a presumed derivative of G696 (ἄργυρος);

Definition

silvery, i.e. (by implication) cash; specially, a silverling (i.e. drachma or shekel)

KJV Translation

money, (piece of) silver (piece)
