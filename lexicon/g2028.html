ἐπονομάζω

eponomázō — G2028

Transliteration: eponomázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2028

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G3687 (ὀνομάζω);

Definition

to name further, i.e. denominate

KJV Translation

call
