βάρος

báros — G922

Transliteration: báros

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G922

Derivation: probably from the same as G939 (βάσις) (through the notion of going down;

Definition

compare G899 (βάθος)); weight; in the New Testament only, figuratively, a load, abundance, authority

KJV Translation

burden(-some), weight
