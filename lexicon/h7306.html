Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H7306

רוּחַ

rûwach — H7306

Transliteration: rûwach

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7306

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

properly, to blow, i.e. breathe; only (literally) to smell or (by implication, perceive (figuratively, to anticipate, enjoy)

KJV Translation

accept, smell, [idiom] touch, make of quick understanding.

Appears In (6 verses)

1 Samuel 26:19Amos 5:21Deuteronomy 4:28Job 39:25Leviticus 26:31Psalms 115:6

1b) of delight (metaph)
