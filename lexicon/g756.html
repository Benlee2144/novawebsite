ἄρχομαι

árchomai — G756

Transliteration: árchomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G756

Derivation: middle voice of G757 (ἄρχω) (through the implication, of precedence);

Definition

to commence (in order of time)

KJV Translation

(rehearse from the) begin(-ning)
