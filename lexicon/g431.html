ἀνεψιός

anepsiós — G431

Transliteration: anepsiós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G431

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a particle of union) and an obsolete (a brood);

Definition

properly, akin, i.e. (specially) a cousin

KJV Translation

sister's son

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀνεψι-ός, ὁ,
first cousin, or generally, cousin, [Refs 8th c.BC+].] (Cf. Sanskrit ναπᾱτ 'grandson', Latin nepos, etc.)

Appears In (1 verse)

Colossians 4:10
