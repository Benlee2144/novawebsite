ἀναγεννάω

anagennáō — G313

Transliteration: anagennáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G313

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and G1080 (γεννάω);

Definition

to beget or (by extension) bear (again)

KJV Translation

beget, (bear) X (again)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀναγενν-άω,
