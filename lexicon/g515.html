ἀξιόω

axióō — G515

Transliteration: axióō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G515

Derivation: from G514 (ἄξιος);

Definition

to deem entitled or fit

KJV Translation

desire, think good, count (think) worthy
