Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G5623

ὠφελέω

ōpheléō — G5623

Transliteration: ōpheléō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5623

Derivation: from the same as G5622 (ὠφέλεια);

Definition

to be useful, i.e. to benefit

KJV Translation

advantage, better, prevail, profit
