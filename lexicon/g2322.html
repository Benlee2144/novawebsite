θεραπεία

therapeía — G2322

Transliteration: therapeía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2322

Derivation: from G2323 (θεραπεύω);

Definition

attendance (specially, medical, i.e. cure); figuratively and collectively, domestics

KJV Translation

healing, household

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

Ionic dialect θερᾰπ-ηΐη (θερᾰπ-είη [Refs 5th c.BC+], ἡ, service, attendance:
