Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H2534

חֵמָה

chêmâh — H2534

Transliteration: chêmâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2534

Derivation: or (Daniel 11:44) חֵמָא; from H3179 (יָחַם);

Definition

heat; figuratively, anger, poison (from its fever)

KJV Translation

anger, bottles, hot displeasure, furious(-ly, -ry), heat, indignation, poison, rage, wrath(-ful). See H2529 (חֶמְאָה).
