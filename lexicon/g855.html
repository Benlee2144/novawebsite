ἄφαντος

áphantos — G855

Transliteration: áphantos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G855

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G5316 (φαίνω);

Definition

non-manifested, i.e. invisible

KJV Translation

vanished out of sight

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
