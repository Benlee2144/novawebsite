λαλέω

laléō — G2980

Transliteration: laléō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2980

Derivation: a prolonged form of an otherwise obsolete verb;

Definition

to talk, i.e. utter words

KJV Translation

preach, say, speak (after), talk, tell, utter

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
