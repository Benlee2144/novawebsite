λάσχω

láschō — G2997

Transliteration: láschō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2997

Derivation: a strengthened form of a primary verb, which only occurs in this and another prolonged form as alternate in certain tenses;

Definition

to crack open (from a fall)

KJV Translation

burst asunder
