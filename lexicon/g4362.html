προσπήγνυμι

prospḗgnymi — G4362

Transliteration: prospḗgnymi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4362

Derivation: from G4314 (πρός) and G4078 (πήγνυμι);

Definition

to fasten to, i.e. (specially), to impale (on a cross)

KJV Translation

crucify
