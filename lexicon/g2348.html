θνήσκω

thnḗskō — G2348

Transliteration: thnḗskō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2348

Derivation: a strengthened form of a simpler primary (which is used for it only in certain tenses);

Definition

to die (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

be dead, die
