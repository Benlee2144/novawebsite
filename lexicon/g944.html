🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

__II ={ἁλιεύς}, a kind of fish, fishing-frog or sea-angler, Lophius piscatorius, [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__III frog of a horse's hoof, [Refs]: hence Astron., of the star β Centauri, [Refs 1st c.BC+]
