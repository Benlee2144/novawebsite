Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2132

εὐνοέω

eunoéō — G2132

Transliteration: eunoéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2132

Derivation: from a compound of G2095 (εὖ) and G3563 (νοῦς);

Definition

to be well- minded, i.e. reconcile

KJV Translation

agree
