κλέπτης

kléptēs — G2812

Transliteration: kléptēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2812

Derivation: from G2813 (κλέπτω);

Definition

a stealer (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

thief

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

κλέπτ-ης, ου, ὁ,
thief, [Refs 8th c.BC+]; opposed to ἅρπαξ (a robber), [NT+5th c.BC+]; ὁ τοῦ κ. λόγος, a logical fallacy, [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__2 generally, cheat, knave, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
