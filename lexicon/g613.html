ἀποκρύπτω

apokrýptō — G613

Transliteration: apokrýptō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G613

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G2928 (κρύπτω);

Definition

to conceal away (i.e. fully); figuratively, to keep secret

KJV Translation

hide
