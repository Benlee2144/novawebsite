Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4798

συγχράομαι

synchráomai — G4798

Transliteration: synchráomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4798

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G5530 (χράομαι);

Definition

to use jointly, i.e. (by implication) to hold intercourse in common

KJV Translation

have dealings with

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

συγχράομαι,
