ἀναίτιος

anaítios — G338

Transliteration: anaítios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G338

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and G159 (αἴτιος) (in the sense of G156 (αἰτία));

Definition

innocent

KJV Translation

blameless, guiltless

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀναίτιος, ον,

__2 with genitive of things, guiltless of a thing, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; φόνου, κακῶν, [Refs 5th c.BC+] with infinitive, it is blamable to do, [Refs]
