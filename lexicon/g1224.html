διαβαίνω

diabaínō — G1224

Transliteration: diabaínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1224

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and the base of G939 (βάσις);

Definition

to cross

KJV Translation

come over, pass (through)

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
