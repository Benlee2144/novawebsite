ἀποπίπτω

apopíptō — G634

Transliteration: apopíptō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G634

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G4098 (πίπτω);

Definition

to fall off

KJV Translation

fall
