ἁπλότης

haplótēs — G572

Transliteration: haplótēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G572

Derivation: from G573 (ἁπλοῦς);

Definition

singleness, i.e. (subjectively) sincerity (without dissimulation or self-seeking), or (objectively) generosity (copious bestowal)

KJV Translation

bountifulness, liberal(-ity), simplicity, singleness
