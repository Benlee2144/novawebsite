σωρεύω

sōreúō — G4987

Transliteration: sōreúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4987

Derivation: from another form of G4673 (σορός);

Definition

to pile up (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

heap, load
