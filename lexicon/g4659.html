σκυθρωπός

skythrōpós — G4659

Transliteration: skythrōpós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4659

Derivation: from (sullen) and a derivative of G3700 (ὀπτάνομαι);

Definition

angry-visaged, i.e. gloomy or affecting a mournful appearance

KJV Translation

of a sad countenance

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

σκυθρωπ-ός, όν,
