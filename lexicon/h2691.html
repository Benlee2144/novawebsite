חָצֵר

châtsêr — H2691

Transliteration: châtsêr

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2691

Derivation: (masculine and feminine); from H2690 (חָצַר) in its original sense;

Definition

a yard (as inclosed by a fence); also a hamlet (as similarly surrounded with walls)

KJV Translation

court, tower, village.
