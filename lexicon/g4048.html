περιῤῥήγνυμι

perirrhḗgnymi — G4048

Transliteration: perirrhḗgnymi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4048

Derivation: from G4012 (περί) and G4486 (ῥήγνυμι);

Definition

to tear all around, i.e. completely away

KJV Translation

rend off
