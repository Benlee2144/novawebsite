ὠνέομαι

ōnéomai — G5608

Transliteration: ōnéomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5608

Derivation: middle voice from an apparently primary (a sum or price);

Definition

to purchase (synonymous with the earlier G4092 (πίμπρημι))

KJV Translation

buy
