πολλαπλασίων

pollaplasíōn — G4179

Transliteration: pollaplasíōn

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4179

Derivation: from G4183 (πολύς) and probably a derivative of G4120 (πλέκω);

Definition

manifold, i.e. (neuter as noun) very much more

KJV Translation

manifold more
