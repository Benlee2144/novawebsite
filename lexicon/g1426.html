δυσφημία

dysphēmía — G1426

Transliteration: dysphēmía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1426

Derivation: from a compound of G1418 (δυσ-) and G5345 (φήμη);

Definition

defamation

KJV Translation

evil report

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

δυσφημ-ία, ἡ,
ill language, especially words of ill omen, κατεῖχε.. πᾶν στρατόπεδον δυσφημίαις [Refs 5th c.BC+]: plural, curses, [Refs 1st c.AD+]; but, unsavoury details, [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__II blasphemy, slander, [Refs 1st c.BC+]
__III ill fame, obloquy, [Refs 5th c.BC+]

Appears In (1 verse)

2 Corinthians 6:8
