σκῆνος

skēnos — G4636

Transliteration: skēnos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4636

Derivation: from G4633 (σκηνή);

Definition

a hut or temporary residence, i.e. (figuratively) the human body (as the abode of the spirit)

KJV Translation

tabernacle
