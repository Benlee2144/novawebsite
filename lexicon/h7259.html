Ribqâh — H7259

Transliteration: Ribqâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7259

Derivation: from an unused root probably meaning to clog by tying up the fetlock; fettering (by beauty);

Definition

Ribkah, the wife of Isaac

KJV Translation

Rebekah.
