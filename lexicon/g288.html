ἄμπελος

ámpelos — G288

Transliteration: ámpelos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G288

Derivation: probably from the base of G297 (ἀμφότερος) and that of G257 (ἅλων);

Definition

a vine (as coiling about a support)

KJV Translation

vine
