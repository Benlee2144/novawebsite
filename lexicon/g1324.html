Δίδυμος

Dídymos — G1324

Transliteration: Dídymos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1324

Derivation: prolongation from G1364 (δίς);

Definition

double, i.e. twin; Didymus, a Christian

KJV Translation

Didymus
