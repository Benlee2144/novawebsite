Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H5930

עֹלָה

ʻôlâh — H5930

Transliteration: ʻôlâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5930

Derivation: or עוֹלָה; feminine active participle of H5927 (עָלָה);

Definition

a step or (collectively, stairs, as ascending); usually a holocaust (as going up in smoke)

KJV Translation

ascent, burnt offering (sacrifice), go up to. See also H5766 (עֶוֶל).
