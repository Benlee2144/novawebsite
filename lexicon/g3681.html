ὄνειδος

óneidos — G3681

Transliteration: óneidos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3681

Derivation: probably akin to the base of G3686 (ὄνομα);

Definition

notoriety, i.e. a taunt (disgrace)

KJV Translation

reproach
