ἀποκλείω

apokleíō — G608

Transliteration: apokleíō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G608

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G2808 (κλείω);

Definition

to close fully

KJV Translation

shut up
