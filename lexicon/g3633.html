οἴομαι

oíomai — G3633

Transliteration: oíomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3633

Derivation: middle voice apparently from G3634 (οἷος);

Definition

to make like (oneself), i.e. imagine (be of the opinion)

KJV Translation

suppose, think
