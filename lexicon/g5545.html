χρῖσμα

chrîsma — G5545

Transliteration: chrîsma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5545

Derivation: from G5548 (χρίω);

Definition

an unguent or smearing, i.e. (figuratively) the special endowment (\

KJV Translation

anointing, unction
