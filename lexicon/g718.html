ἁρμόζω

harmózō — G718

Transliteration: harmózō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G718

Derivation: from G719 (ἁρμός);

Definition

to joint, i.e. (figuratively) to woo (reflexively, to betroth)

KJV Translation

espouse
