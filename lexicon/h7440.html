רִנָּה

rinnâh — H7440

Transliteration: rinnâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7440

Derivation: from H7442 (רָנַן);

Definition

properly, a creaking (or shrill sound), i.e. shout (of joy or grief)

KJV Translation

cry, gladness, joy, proclamation, rejoicing, shouting, sing(-ing), triumph.
