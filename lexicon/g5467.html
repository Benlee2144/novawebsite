χαλεπός

chalepós — G5467

Transliteration: chalepós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5467

Derivation: perhaps from G5465 (χαλάω) through the idea of reducing the strength;

Definition

difficult, i.e. dangerous, or (by implication) furious

KJV Translation

fierce, perilous
