Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1997

ἐπισυναγωγή

episynagōgḗ — G1997

Transliteration: episynagōgḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1997

Derivation: from G1996 (ἐπισυνάγω);

Definition

a complete collection; especially a Christian meeting (for worship)

KJV Translation

assembling (gathering) together
