Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3166

μεγαλαυχέω

megalauchéō — G3166

Transliteration: megalauchéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3166

Derivation: from a compound of G3173 (μέγας) and (to boast;

Definition

akin to G837 (αὐξάνω) and G2744 (καυχάομαι)); to talk big, i.e. be grandiloquent (arrogant, egotistic)

KJV Translation

boast great things
