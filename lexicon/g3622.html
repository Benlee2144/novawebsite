οἰκονομία

oikonomía — G3622

Transliteration: oikonomía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3622

Derivation: from G3623 (οἰκονόμος);

Definition

administration (of a household or estate); specially, a (religious) \

KJV Translation

dispensation, stewardship
