ἐπάγω

epágō — G1863

Transliteration: epágō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1863

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G71 (ἄγω);

Definition

to superinduce, i.e. inflict (an evil), charge (a crime)

KJV Translation

bring upon
