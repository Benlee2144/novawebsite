κοίτη

koítē — G2845

Transliteration: koítē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2845

Derivation: from G2749 (κεῖμαι);

Definition

a couch; by extension, cohabitation; by implication, the male sperm

KJV Translation

bed, chambering, X conceive
