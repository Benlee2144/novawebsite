παρηγορία

parēgoría — G3931

Transliteration: parēgoría

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3931

Derivation: from a compound of G3844 (παρά) and a derivative of G58 (ἀγορά) (meaning to harangue an assembly);

Definition

an address alongside, i.e. (specially), consolation

KJV Translation

comfort
