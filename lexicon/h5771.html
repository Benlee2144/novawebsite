Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H5771

עָוֺן

ʻâvôn — H5771

Transliteration: ʻâvôn

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5771

Derivation: or עָווֹן; (2 Kings 7:9; Psalm 51:5 (H7 (אֲבַד))), from H5753 (עָוָה);

Definition

perversity, i.e. (moral) evil

KJV Translation

fault, iniquity, mischeif, punishment (of iniquity), sin.

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

: punishment
1) perversity, depravity, iniquity, guilt or punishment of iniquity
1a) iniquity
1b) guilt of iniquity, guilt (as great), guilt (of condition)
1c) consequence of or punishment for iniquity
