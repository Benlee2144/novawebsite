Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2903

κράτιστος

krátistos — G2903

Transliteration: krátistos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2903

Derivation: superlative of a derivative of G2904 (κράτος);

Definition

strongest, i.e. (in dignity) very honorable

KJV Translation

most excellent (noble)
