Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H8414

תֹּהוּ

tôhûw — H8414

Transliteration: tôhûw

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H8414

Derivation: from an unused root meaning to lie waste;

Definition

a desolation (of surface), i.e. desert; figuratively, a worthless thing; adverbially, in vain

KJV Translation

confusion, empty place, without form, nothing, (thing of) nought, vain, vanity, waste, wilderness.
