Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4932

συντέμνω

syntémnō — G4932

Transliteration: syntémnō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4932

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and the base of G5114 (τομώτερος);

Definition

to contract by cutting, i.e. (figuratively) do concisely (speedily)

KJV Translation

(cut) short
