σκηνή

skēnḗ — G4633

Transliteration: skēnḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4633

Derivation: apparently akin to G4632 (σκεῦος) and G4639 (σκιά);

Definition

a tent or cloth hut (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

habitation, tabernacle
