διερμηνεύω

diermēneúō — G1329

Transliteration: diermēneúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1329

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G2059 (ἑρμηνεύω);

Definition

to explain thoroughly, by implication, to translate

KJV Translation

expound, interpret(-ation)
