διότι

dióti — G1360

Transliteration: dióti

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1360

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G3754 (ὅτι);

Definition

on the very account that, or inasmuch as

KJV Translation

because (that), for, therefore

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

διότι,
conjunction for διὰ τοῦτο ὅτι.. [Refs 2nd c.AD+], because, for the reason that, since, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; οὐδὲ δι᾽ ἓν ἄλλο ἢ διότι.. [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 indirect, wherefore, for what reason, φράσω διότι.. [Refs 5th c.BC+]; σκοπεῖν διότι.. [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ἐρωτᾶν διότι.. [Refs 4th c.AD+]
__II ={ὅτι}, that, [Refs 5th c.BC+]: followed by infinitive, [Refs 2nd c.BC+]
