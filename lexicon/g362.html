ἀναμένω

anaménō — G362

Transliteration: anaménō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G362

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and G3306 (μένω);

Definition

to await

KJV Translation

wait for

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀναμένω,
poetry ἀμμένω, Aeolic dialect ὀμμένω [Refs 7th c.BC+]:— wait for, await, ἀνέμεινα..
