Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H3169

יְחִזְקִיָּה

Yᵉchizqîyâh — H3169

Transliteration: Yᵉchizqîyâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3169

Derivation: or יְחִזְקִיָּהוּ; from H3388 (יְרוּשָׁא) and H3050 (יָהּ); strengthened of Jah;

Definition

Jechizkijah, the name of five Israelites

KJV Translation

Hezekiah, Jehizkiah. Compare H2396 (חִזְקִיָּה).

Appears In (2 verses)

Hosea 1:1Micah 1:1

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

A man living at the time of Exile and Return, first mentioned at Ezr.2.16;
son of: Ater (H0333)
Another spelling of chiz.qiy.yah (חִזְקִיָּהוּ "Hezekiah" H2396I)
§ Hezekiah or Jehizkiah = "Jehovah has made strong"
1) son of king Ahazby Abi or Abijah and the 12th king of Judah; his reign was characterised by his godly conduct; reigned for 29 years
2) head of a family of returning exiles in the time of Nehemiah
3) an Ephraimite in the time of Ahaz
