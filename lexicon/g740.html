ἄρτος

ártos — G740

Transliteration: ártos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G740

Derivation: from G142 (αἴρω);

Definition

bread (as raised) or a loaf

KJV Translation

(shew-)bread, loaf

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἄρτος, ὁ,
cake or loaf of wheat-bread, mostly in plural, [Refs 8th c.BC+]; ἄρτος οὖλος a whole loaf, [Refs]; collectively, bread, δούλιον ἄρτον ἔδων[Refs 7th c.BC+]; opposed to μᾶζα (porridge), [Refs 5th c.BC+] —Freq. in all writers.
__II ἄρτος· βόλος τις, καὶ ὁ Ἀθηναίων ξένος, [Refs 5th c.AD+]
