τάχος

táchos — G5034

Transliteration: táchos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5034

Derivation: from the same as G5036 (ταχύς);

Definition

a brief space (of time), i.e. (with G1722 (ἐν) prefixed) in haste

KJV Translation

+ quickly, + shortly, + speedily
