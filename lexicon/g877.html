ἀφροσύνη

aphrosýnē — G877

Transliteration: aphrosýnē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G877

Derivation: from G878 (ἄφρων);

Definition

senselessness, i.e. (euphemistically) egotism; (morally) recklessness

KJV Translation

folly, foolishly(-ness)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀφροσύνη, ἡ,
(ἄφρων) folly, thoughtlessness, frequently in plural, παῖδας καταπαυέμεν ἀφροσυνάων[Refs 8th c.BC+]; opposed to σωφροσύνη and σοφία, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
