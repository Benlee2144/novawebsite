נָבַט

nâbaṭ — H5027

Transliteration: nâbaṭ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5027

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to scan, i.e. look intently at; by implication, to regard with pleasure, favor or care

KJV Translation

(cause to) behold, consider, look (down), regard, have respect, see.
