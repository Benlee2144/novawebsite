οἰκητήριον

oikētḗrion — G3613

Transliteration: oikētḗrion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3613

Derivation: neuter of a presumed derivative of G3611 (οἰκέω) (equivalent to G3612 (οἴκημα));

Definition

a residence (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation
