ῥώννυμι

rhṓnnymi — G4517

Transliteration: rhṓnnymi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4517

Derivation: prolongation from (to dart;

Definition

probably akin to G4506 (ῥύομαι)); to strengthen, i.e. (impersonal passive) have health (as a parting exclamation, good-bye)

KJV Translation

farewell
