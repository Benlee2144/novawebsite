Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4883

συναρμολογέω

synarmologéō — G4883

Transliteration: synarmologéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4883

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and a derivative of a compound of G719 (ἁρμός) and G3004 (λέγω) (in its original sense of laying);

Definition

to render close-jointed together, i.e. organize compactly

KJV Translation

be fitly framed (joined) together

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
