Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1405

δράσσομαι

drássomai — G1405

Transliteration: drássomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1405

Derivation: perhaps akin to the base of G1404 (δράκων) (through the idea of capturing);

Definition

to grasp, i.e. (figuratively) entrap

KJV Translation

take
