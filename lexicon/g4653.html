σκοτία

skotía — G4653

Transliteration: skotía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4653

Derivation: from G4655 (σκότος);

Definition

dimness, obscurity (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

dark(-ness)

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

σκοτί-α, ἡ,
(σκότος) darkness, gloom, [NT+3rd c.BC+]
__II in Architecture, scotia, cavetto, a sunken moulding, so called from the dark shadow it casts, [Refs 1st c.BC+]
__III Σκοτιά, epithet of Aphrodite in Egypt, [Refs]
