ἄῤῥητος

árrhētos — G731

Transliteration: árrhētos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G731

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and the same as G4490 (ῥητῶς);

Definition

unsaid, i.e. (by implication) inexpressible

KJV Translation

unspeakable
