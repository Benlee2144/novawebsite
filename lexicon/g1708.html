ἐμπλοκή

emplokḗ — G1708

Transliteration: emplokḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1708

Derivation: from G1707 (ἐμπλέκω);

Definition

elaborate braiding of the hair

KJV Translation

plaiting
