νομοθετέω

nomothetéō — G3549

Transliteration: nomothetéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3549

Derivation: from G3550 (νομοθέτης);

Definition

to legislate, i.e. (passively) to have (the Mosaic) enactments injoined, be sanctioned (by them)

KJV Translation

establish, receive the law
