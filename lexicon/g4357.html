προσμένω

prosménō — G4357

Transliteration: prosménō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4357

Derivation: from G4314 (πρός) and G3306 (μένω);

Definition

to stay further, i.e. remain in a place, with a person; figuratively, to adhere to, persevere in

KJV Translation

abide still, be with, cleave unto, continue in (with)
