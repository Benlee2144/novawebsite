רָפָא

râphâʼ — H7495

Transliteration: râphâʼ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7495

Derivation: or רָפָה; a primitive root;

Definition

properly, to mend (by stitching), i.e. (figuratively) to cure

KJV Translation

cure, (cause to) heal, physician, repair, [idiom] thoroughly, make whole. See H7503 (רָפָה).
