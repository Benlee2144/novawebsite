Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H3603

כִּכָּר

kikkâr — H3603

Transliteration: kikkâr

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3603

Derivation: from H3769 (כָּרַר);

Definition

a circle, i.e. (by implication) a circumjacent tract or region, especially the Ghor or valley of the Jordan; also a (round) loaf; also a talent (or large [round] coin)

KJV Translation

loaf, morsel, piece, plain, talent.
