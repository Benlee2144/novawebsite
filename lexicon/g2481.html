ἴσως

ísōs — G2481

Transliteration: ísōs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2481

Derivation: adverb from G2470 (ἴσος);

Definition

likely, i.e. perhaps

KJV Translation

it may be

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἴσως,
Laconian dialect ϝίὡρ (see. βίωρ), adverb of ἴσος, equally, in like manner, [Refs 7th c.BC+]; evenly, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II equally, with reference to equality, τὸ ὀρθὸν ληπτέον ἴσως [Refs 4th c.BC+]; fairly, equitably, ἴ. καὶ κοινῶς Aen.[Refs 4th c.BC+]
__III probably, perhaps, [Refs 7th c.BC+] with optative is falsa lectio in [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ἴ. μέν.., ἴ. δέ.. perhaps so or so, [Refs 5th c.BC+]: used to soften or qualify a positive assertion, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__IV with numerals, about, [Refs 5th c.BC+]

Luke 20:13
