ἀγρός

agrós — G68

Transliteration: agrós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G68

Derivation: from G71 (ἄγω);

Definition

a field (as a drive for cattle); genitive case, the country; specially, a farm, i.e. hamlet

KJV Translation

country, farm, piece of ground, land
