ἐντυγχάνω

entynchánō — G1793

Transliteration: entynchánō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1793

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and G5177 (τυγχάνω);

Definition

to chance upon, i.e. (by implication) confer with; by extension to entreat (in favor or against)

KJV Translation

deal with, make intercession
