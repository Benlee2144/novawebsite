ψηφίζω

psēphízō — G5585

Transliteration: psēphízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5585

Derivation: from G5586 (ψῆφος);

Definition

to use pebbles in enumeration, i.e. (generally) to compute

KJV Translation

count
