Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G807

ἀσχημονέω

aschēmonéō — G807

Transliteration: aschēmonéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G807

Derivation: from G809 (ἀσχήμων);

Definition

to be (i.e. act) unbecoming

KJV Translation

behave self uncomely (unseemly)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀσχημ-ονέω,
behave unseemly, disgrace oneself, [Refs 5th c.BC+]: with participle, [Refs 1st c.AD+] —passive, πολλὰ ἀσχημονεῖται many unseemly things are done, [Refs 1st c.BC+]

1 Corinthians 13:51 Corinthians 7:36
