παραζηλόω

parazēlóō — G3863

Transliteration: parazēlóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3863

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and G2206 (ζηλόω);

Definition

to stimulate alongside, i.e. excite to rivalry

KJV Translation

provoke to emulation (jealousy)
