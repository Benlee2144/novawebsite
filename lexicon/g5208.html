ὕλη

hýlē — G5208

Transliteration: hýlē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5208

Derivation: perhaps akin to G3586 (ξύλον);

Definition

a forest, i.e. (by implication) fuel

KJV Translation

matter

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

__II wood cut down, [Refs 8th c.BC+]; firewood, fuel, [Refs 8th c.BC+]; brushwood, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; timber, ὕ. ναυπηγησίμη [Refs 5th c.BC+]; also, twigs for birds' nests, [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__III the stuff of which a thing is made, material, (perhaps so of wood), [Refs 8th c.BC+]; rarely of other material, as metal, οἱ παρ᾽ ἄκμονι.. ὕλην ἄψυχον δημιουργοῦντες [
