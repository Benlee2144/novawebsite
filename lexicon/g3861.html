παράδοξος

parádoxos — G3861

Transliteration: parádoxos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3861

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and G1391 (δόξα) (in the sense of seeming);

Definition

contrary to expectation, i.e. extraordinary (\

KJV Translation

strange
