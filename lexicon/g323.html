ἀνάδειξις

anádeixis — G323

Transliteration: anádeixis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G323

Derivation: from G322 (ἀναδείκνυμι);

Definition

(the act of) exhibition

KJV Translation

shewing
