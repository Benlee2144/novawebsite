προβατικός

probatikós — G4262

Transliteration: probatikós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4262

Derivation: from G4263 (πρόβατον);

Definition

relating to sheep, i.e. (a gate) through which they were led into Jerusalem

KJV Translation

sheep (market)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

προβᾰτ-ικός, ή, όν,
of sheep or goats, π. χορός a chorus of goats, as in the Αἶγες of Eupolis, [Refs]; ἡ π. πύλη the sheep-gate, [LXX]; without πύλη, [NT+2nd c.AD+]

John 5:2
