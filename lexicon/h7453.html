רֵעַ

rêaʻ — H7453

Transliteration: rêaʻ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7453

Derivation: or רֵיעַ; from H7462 (רָעָה);

Definition

an associate (more or less close)

KJV Translation

brother, companion, fellow, friend, husband, lover, neighbour, [idiom] (an-) other.
