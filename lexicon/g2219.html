ζύμη

zýmē — G2219

Transliteration: zýmē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2219

Derivation: probably from G2204 (ζέω);

Definition

ferment (as if boiling up)

KJV Translation

leaven

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ζύμ-η [ῡ], ἡ,
leaven, [LXX+4th c.BC+]; beer-yeast, [Refs 2nd c.AD+], etc.: metaphorically, of corruption, falsehood, NT.Matt.16.6, etc. (ζῡ-μᾱ or ζῡσ-μᾱ, cf. Sanskrit yauti 'mix', Sanskrit yūs, Latin jūs 'soup', Lett. jaut 'stir dough', javs 'dough'.)
