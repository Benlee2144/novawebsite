ὑποβάλλω

hypobállō — G5260

Transliteration: hypobállō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5260

Derivation: from G5259 (ὑπό) and G906 (βάλλω);

Definition

to throw in stealthily, i.e. introduce by collusion

KJV Translation

suborn

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
