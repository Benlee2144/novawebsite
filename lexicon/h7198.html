קֶשֶׁת

qesheth — H7198

Transliteration: qesheth

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7198

Derivation: from H7185 (קָשָׁה) in the original sense (of H6983 (קוֹשׁ)) of bending;

Definition

a bow, forshooting (hence, figuratively, strength) or the iris

KJV Translation

[idiom] arch(-er), [phrase] arrow, bow(-man, -shot).
