διηνεκής

diēnekḗs — G1336

Transliteration: diēnekḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1336

Derivation: neuter of a compound of G1223 (διά) and a derivative of an alternate of G5342 (φέρω);

Definition

carried through, i.e. (adverbially with G1519 (εἰς) and G3588 (ὁ) prefixed) perpetually

KJV Translation

+ continually, for ever
