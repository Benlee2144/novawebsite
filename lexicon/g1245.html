διακαθαρίζω

diakatharízō — G1245

Transliteration: diakatharízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1245

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G2511 (καθαρίζω);

Definition

to cleanse perfectly, i.e. (specially) winnow

KJV Translation

thoroughly purge
