Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1173

δεῖπνον

deîpnon — G1173

Transliteration: deîpnon

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1173

Derivation: from the same as G1160 (δαπάνη);

Definition

dinner, i.e. the chief meal (usually in the evening)

KJV Translation

feast, supper

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
