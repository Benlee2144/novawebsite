Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4879

συναπάγω

synapágō — G4879

Transliteration: synapágō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4879

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G520 (ἀπάγω);

Definition

to take off together, i.e. transport with (seduce, passively, yield)

KJV Translation

carry (lead) away with, condescend
