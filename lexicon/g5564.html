χωρίον

chōríon — G5564

Transliteration: chōríon

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5564

Derivation: diminutive of G5561 (χώρα);

Definition

a spot or plot of ground

KJV Translation

field, land, parcel of ground, place, possession
