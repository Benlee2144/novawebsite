εἰσφέρω

eisphérō — G1533

Transliteration: eisphérō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1533

Derivation: from G1519 (εἰς) and G5342 (φέρω);

Definition

to carry inward (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

bring (in), lead into
