θλίβω

thlíbō — G2346

Transliteration: thlíbō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2346

Derivation: akin to the base of G5147 (τρίβος);

Definition

to crowd (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

afflict, narrow, throng, suffer tribulation, trouble
