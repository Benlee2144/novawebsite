οὐδαμῶς

oudamōs — G3760

Transliteration: oudamōs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3760

Derivation: adverb from (the feminine) of G3762 (οὐδείς);

Definition

by no means

KJV Translation

not

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
