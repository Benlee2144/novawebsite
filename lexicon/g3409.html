μισθόω

misthóō — G3409

Transliteration: misthóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3409

Derivation: from G3408 (μισθός);

Definition

to let out for wages, i.e. (middle voice) to hire

KJV Translation

hire
