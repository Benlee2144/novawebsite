συμπολίτης

sympolítēs — G4847

Transliteration: sympolítēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4847

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G4177 (πολίτης);

Definition

a native of the same town, i.e. (figuratively) co-religionist (fellow-Christian)

KJV Translation

fellow- citizen

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
