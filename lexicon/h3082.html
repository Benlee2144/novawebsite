יְהוֹנָדָב

Yᵉhôwnâdâb — H3082

Transliteration: Yᵉhôwnâdâb

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3082

Derivation: from H3068 (יְהֹוָה) and H5068 (נָדַב); Jehovah-largessed;

Definition

Jehonadab, the name of an Israelite and of an Arab

KJV Translation

Jehonadab, Jonadab. Compare H3122 (יוֹנָדָב).

Appears In (6 verses)

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

A man of the tribe of Judah living at the time of United Monarchy, first mentioned at 2Sa.13.3;
son of: Shimeah (H8093); also called Jonathan at 2Sa.21.21; 1x27.32;
Another spelling of yo.na.dav (יוֹנָדָב "Jonadab" H3122)
§ Jehonadab = "Jehovah is willing"
1) a son of Rechab, chief of the Rechabites, in the time of Jehu and Ahab
2) a nephew of David
