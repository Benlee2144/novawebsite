διατηρέω

diatēréō — G1301

Transliteration: diatēréō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1301

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G5083 (τηρέω);

Definition

to watch thoroughly, i.e. (positively and transitively) to observe strictly, or (negatively and reflexively) to avoid wholly

KJV Translation

keep
