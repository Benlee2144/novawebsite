κατάγω

katágō — G2609

Transliteration: katágō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2609

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G71 (ἄγω);

Definition

to lead down; specially, to moor a vessel

KJV Translation

bring (down, forth), (bring to) land, touch
