Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3679

ὀνειδίζω

oneidízō — G3679

Transliteration: oneidízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3679

Derivation: from G3681 (ὄνειδος);

Definition

to defame, i.e. rail at, chide, taunt

KJV Translation

cast in teeth, (suffer) reproach, revile, upbraid
