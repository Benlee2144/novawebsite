ἀνεξίκακος

anexíkakos — G420

Transliteration: anexíkakos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G420

Derivation: from G430 (ἀνέχομαι) and G2556 (κακός);

Definition

enduring of ill, i.e. forbearing

KJV Translation

patient
