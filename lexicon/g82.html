ἄδηλος

ádēlos — G82

Transliteration: ádēlos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G82

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and G1212 (δῆλος);

Definition

hidden, figuratively, indistinct

KJV Translation

appear not, uncertain

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

ἄ-δηλος, -ον
(δῆλος), [in LXX: Ps 50 (51):6 (בַּטֻחוֹת) ;]
__1. unseen, unobserved, not manifest (Ps, l.with): Luk.11:44.
