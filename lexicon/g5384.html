Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G5384

φίλος

phílos — G5384

Transliteration: phílos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5384

Derivation: properly, dear, i.e. a friend;

Definition

actively, fond, i.e. friendly (still as a noun, an associate, neighbor, etc.)

KJV Translation

friend
