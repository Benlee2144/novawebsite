ἕνεκα

héneka — G1752

Transliteration: héneka

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1752

Derivation: of uncertain affinity;

Definition

on account of

KJV Translation

because, for (cause, sake), (where-)fore, by reason of, that
