Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H1413

גָּדַד

gâdad — H1413

Transliteration: gâdad

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1413

Derivation: a primitive root (compare H1464 (גּוּד));

Definition

to crowd; also to gash (as if by pressing into)

KJV Translation

assemble (selves by troops), gather (selves together, self in troops), cut selves.

Appears In (6 verses)

Deuteronomy 14:1Jeremiah 16:6Jeremiah 47:5Jeremiah 5:7Micah 5:1Psalms 94:21

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

1) to penetrate, cut, attack, invade
1a) (Qal) to penetrate, cut into
1b)(Hithpoel)
1b1) to cut oneself
1b2) to gather in troops or crowds
Aramaic equivalent: ge.dad (גְּדַד "to chop" H1414)
