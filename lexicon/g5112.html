τολμηρότερον

tolmēróteron — G5112

Transliteration: tolmēróteron

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5112

Derivation: neuter of the compound of a derivative of the base of 5111 (as adverb);

Definition

more daringly, i.e. with greater confidence than otherwise

KJV Translation

the more boldly

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

Included with: τολμ-ηρός, ά, όν,
