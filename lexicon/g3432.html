μοιχός

moichós — G3432

Transliteration: moichós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3432

Derivation: perhaps a primary word;

Definition

a (male) paramour; figuratively, apostate

KJV Translation

adulterer

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

μοιχός, ὁ,
adulterer, paramour, [Refs 6th c.BC+]; κεκαρμένος μοιχὸν μιᾷ μαχαίρᾳ having the head close shaven with a razor, as was done by way of punishment to persons taken in adultery, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II generally, paramour, of a sodomite, [Refs 3rd c.AD+]
__III idolatrous person, [NT]
