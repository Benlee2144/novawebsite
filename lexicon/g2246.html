ἥλιος

hḗlios — G2246

Transliteration: hḗlios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2246

Derivation: from (a ray;

Definition

perhaps akin to the alternate of G138 (αἱρέομαι)); the sun; by implication, light

KJV Translation

+ east, sun
