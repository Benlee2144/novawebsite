παραπίπτω

parapíptō — G3895

Transliteration: parapíptō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3895

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and G4098 (πίπτω);

Definition

to fall aside, i.e. (figuratively) to apostatize

KJV Translation

fall away
