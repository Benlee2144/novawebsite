ὑποχωρέω

hypochōréō — G5298

Transliteration: hypochōréō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5298

Derivation: from G5259 (ὑπό) and G5562 (χωρέω);

Definition

to vacate down, i.e. retire quietly

KJV Translation

go aside, withdraw self
