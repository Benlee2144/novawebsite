Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H5153

נָחוּשׁ

nâchûwsh — H5153

Transliteration: nâchûwsh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5153

Derivation: apparently passive participle of H5172 (נָחַשׁ) (perhaps in the sense of ringing), i.e. bell-metal; or from the red color of the throat of a serpent (H5175 (נָחָשׁ), as denominative) when hissing);

Definition

coppery, i.e. (figuratively) hard

KJV Translation

of brass.

Appears In (1 verse)

Job 6:12
