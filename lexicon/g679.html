ἄπταιστος

áptaistos — G679

Transliteration: áptaistos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G679

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G4417 (πταίω);

Definition

not stumbling, i.e. (figuratively) without sin

KJV Translation

from falling
