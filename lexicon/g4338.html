προσηλόω

prosēlóō — G4338

Transliteration: prosēlóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4338

Derivation: from G4314 (πρός) and a derivative of G2247 (ἧλος);

Definition

to peg to, i.e. spike fast

KJV Translation

nail to
