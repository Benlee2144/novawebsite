ou — G3756

Transliteration: ou

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3756

Derivation: a primary word;

Definition

the absolute negative (compare G3361 (μή)) adverb; no or not

KJV Translation

+ long, nay, neither, never, no (X man), none, (can-)not, + nothing, + special, un(-worthy), when, + without, + yet but
