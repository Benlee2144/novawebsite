Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H5068

נָדַב

nâdab — H5068

Transliteration: nâdab

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5068

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to impel; hence, to volunteer (as a soldier), to present spontaneously

KJV Translation

offer freely, be (give, make, offer self) willing(-ly).

Appears In (9 verses)
