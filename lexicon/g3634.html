οἷος

hoîos — G3634

Transliteration: hoîos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3634

Derivation: probably akin to G3588 (ὁ), G3739 (ὅς), and G3745 (ὅσος);

Definition

such or what sort of (as a correlation or exclamation); especially the neuter (adverbially) with negative, not so

KJV Translation

so (as), such as, what (manner of), which

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
