φρέαρ

phréar — G5421

Transliteration: phréar

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5421

Derivation: of uncertain derivation;

Definition

a hole in the ground (dug for obtaining or holding water or other purposes), i.e. a cistern or well; figuratively, an abyss (as a prison)

KJV Translation

well, pit
