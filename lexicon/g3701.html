ὀπτασία

optasía — G3701

Transliteration: optasía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3701

Derivation: from a presumed derivative of G3700 (ὀπτάνομαι);

Definition

visuality, i.e. (concretely) an apparition

KJV Translation

vision

ὀπτᾰσία, ἡ
