οὐράνιος

ouránios — G3770

Transliteration: ouránios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3770

Derivation: from G3772 (οὐρανός);

Definition

celestial, i.e. belonging to or coming from the sky

KJV Translation

heavenly
