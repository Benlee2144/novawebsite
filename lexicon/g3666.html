ὁμοιόω

homoióō — G3666

Transliteration: homoióō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3666

Derivation: from G3664 (ὅμοιος);

Definition

to assimilate, i.e. compare; passively, to become similar

KJV Translation

be (make) like, (in the) liken(-ess), resemble
