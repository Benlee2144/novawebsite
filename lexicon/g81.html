ἀδελφότης

adelphótēs — G81

Transliteration: adelphótēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G81

Derivation: from G80 (ἀδελφός);

Definition

brotherhood (properly, the feeling of brotherliness), i.e. the (Christian) fraternity

KJV Translation

brethren, brotherhood
