καταρτίζω

katartízō — G2675

Transliteration: katartízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2675

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and a derivative of G739 (ἄρτιος);

Definition

to complete thoroughly, i.e. repair (literally or figuratively) or adjust

KJV Translation

fit, frame, mend, (make) perfect(-ly join together), prepare, restore
