κακοήθεια

kakoḗtheia — G2550

Transliteration: kakoḗtheia

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2550

Derivation: from a compound of G2556 (κακός) and G2239 (ἦθος);

Definition

bad character, i.e. (specially) mischievousness

KJV Translation

malignity
