סָגַר

çâgar — H5462

Transliteration: çâgar

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5462

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to shut up; figuratively, to surrender

KJV Translation

close up, deliver (up), give over (up), inclose, [idiom] pure, repair, shut (in, self, out, up, up together), stop, [idiom] straitly.
