σώφρων

sṓphrōn — G4998

Transliteration: sṓphrōn

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4998

Derivation: from the base of G4982 (σώζω) and that of G5424 (φρήν);

Definition

safe (sound) in mind, i.e. self-controlled (moderate as to opinion or passion)

KJV Translation

discreet, sober, temperate
