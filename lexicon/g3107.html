Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3107

μακάριος

makários — G3107

Transliteration: makários

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3107

Derivation: a prolonged form of the poetical (meaning the same);

Definition

supremely blest; by extension, fortunate, well off

KJV Translation

blessed, happy(X -ier)
