Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2706

καταφρονέω

kataphronéō — G2706

Transliteration: kataphronéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2706

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G5426 (φρονέω);

Definition

to think against, i.e. disesteem

KJV Translation

despise
