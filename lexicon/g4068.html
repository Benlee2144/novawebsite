περπερεύομαι

perpereúomai — G4068

Transliteration: perpereúomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4068

Derivation: middle voice from (braggart;

Definition

perhaps by reduplication of the base of G4008 (πέραν)); to boast

KJV Translation

vaunt itself

περπερ-εύομαι,
boast, brag, [NT+2nd c.AD+]

Appears In (1 verse)
