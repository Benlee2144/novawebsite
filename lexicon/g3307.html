Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3307

μερίζω

merízō — G3307

Transliteration: merízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3307

Derivation: from G3313 (μέρος);

Definition

to part, i.e. (literally) to apportion, bestow, share, or (figuratively) to disunite, differ

KJV Translation

deal, be difference between, distribute, divide, give participle
