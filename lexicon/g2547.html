κἀκεῖθεν

kakeîthen — G2547

Transliteration: kakeîthen

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2547

Derivation: from G2532 (καί) and G1564 (ἐκεῖθεν);

Definition

likewise from that place (or time)

KJV Translation

and afterward (from) (thence), thence also

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

κἀκεῖθεν
(by crasis for καὶ ἐκεῖθεν, and so sometimes written, see: ἐκεῖθεν),
of place, and from thence, and thence: Mrk.9:30, Luk.11:53, Act.7:4 14:26 16:12 20:15 21:1 27:4 28:15.†
(AS)
