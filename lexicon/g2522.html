καθημερινός

kathēmerinós — G2522

Transliteration: kathēmerinós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2522

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G2250 (ἡμέρα);

Definition

quotidian

KJV Translation

daily

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

καθημέρ-ιος,
Doric dialect καθᾱμ-, α, ον, day by day, daily (καθ᾽ ἡμέραν), neuter as adverb, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; μοῖρα κ. [Refs 5th c.BC+]:—later also καθημερινός, ή, όν, δίαιτα [LXX+1st c.AD+]; of fevers, quotidian, later word for ἀμφημερινός (which see), especially of non-remittent quotidians, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]; φρίξ [Refs 4th c.AD+]
