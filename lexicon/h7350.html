Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H7350

רָחוֹק

râchôwq — H7350

Transliteration: râchôwq

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7350

Derivation: or רָחֹק; from H7368 (רָחַק);

Definition

remote, literally or figuratively, of place or time; specifically, precious; often used adverbially (with preposition)

KJV Translation

(a-) far (abroad, off), long ago, of old, space, great while to come.
