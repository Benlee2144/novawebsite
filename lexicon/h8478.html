תַּחַת

tachath — H8478

Transliteration: tachath

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H8478

Derivation: from the same as H8430 (תּוֹחַ);

Definition

the bottom (as depressed); only adverbially, below (often with prepositional prefix underneath), in lieu of, etc.

KJV Translation

as, beneath, [idiom] flat, in(-stead), (same) place (where...is), room, for...sake, stead of, under, [idiom] unto, [idiom] when...was mine, whereas, (where-) fore, with.
