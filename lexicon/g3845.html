παραβαίνω

parabaínō — G3845

Transliteration: parabaínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3845

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and the base of G939 (βάσις);

Definition

to go contrary to, i.e. violate a command

KJV Translation

(by) transgress(-ion)

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

παρα-βαίνω
[in LXX for סוּר, עָבַר, פָּרַר, שָׂטָה, etc. ;]
__1. in Hom. (twice), to go by the side of, stand beside.
__2. In Æsch., Herod., Thuc., al., to go past or pass over, chiefly metaphorically, to overstep, violate, transgress (Jos.7:11, Eze.16:59, \2, al.) : τ. παράδοσιν, Mat.15:2; τ. ἐντολήν, Mat.15:3; before ἀπό (as מִן סוּר, Deu.17:20, al.), to turn aside, fall away (cf. π. τῆς ἀληθείας, Arist., Cael., i, 5, 2): Act.1:25.†
