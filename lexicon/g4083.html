πῆχυς

pēchys — G4083

Transliteration: pēchys

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4083

Derivation: of uncertain affinity;

Definition

the fore-arm, i.e. (as a measure) a cubit

KJV Translation

cubit

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

[Refs 5th c.BC+]; λαιὸν ἔπαιρε π. [Refs]
__2 Anatomy texts, ulna, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
__II centrepiece, which joined the two horns of the bow, τόν ῥ᾽ [ὀϊστὸν] ἐπὶ πήχει ἑλὼν ἕλκεν νευρήν [Refs 8th c.BC+]
