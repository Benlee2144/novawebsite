συνεφίστημι

synephístēmi — G4911

Transliteration: synephístēmi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4911

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G2186 (ἐφίστημι);

Definition

to stand up together, i.e. to resist (or assault) jointly

KJV Translation

rise up together
