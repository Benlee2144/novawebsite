ἀχλύς

achlýs — G887

Transliteration: achlýs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G887

Derivation: of uncertain derivation;

Definition

dimness of sight, i.e. (probably) a cataract

KJV Translation

mist
