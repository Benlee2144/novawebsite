Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H2220

זְרוֹעַ

zᵉrôwaʻ — H2220

Transliteration: zᵉrôwaʻ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2220

Derivation: or (shortened) זְרֹעַ; and (feminine) זְרוֹעָה; or זְרֹעָה; from H2232 (זָרַע);

Definition

the arm (as stretched out), or (of animals) the foreleg; figuratively, force

KJV Translation

arm, [phrase] help, mighty, power, shoulder, strength.
