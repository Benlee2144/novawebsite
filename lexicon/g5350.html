φθέγγομαι

phthéngomai — G5350

Transliteration: phthéngomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5350

Derivation: probably akin to G5338 (φέγγος) and thus to G5346 (φημί);

Definition

to utter a clear sound, i.e. (generally) to proclaim
