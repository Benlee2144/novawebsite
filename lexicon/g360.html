ἀναλύω

analýō — G360

Transliteration: analýō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G360

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and G3089 (λύω);

Definition

to break up, i.e. depart (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

depart, return
