ἀγοράζω

agorázō — G59

Transliteration: agorázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G59

Derivation: from G58 (ἀγορά);

Definition

properly, to go to market, i.e. (by implication) to purchase; specially, to redeem

KJV Translation

buy, redeem
