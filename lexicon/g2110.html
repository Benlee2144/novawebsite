εὐεργέτης

euergétēs — G2110

Transliteration: euergétēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2110

Derivation: from G2095 (εὖ) and the base of G2041 (ἔργον);

Definition

a worker of good, i.e. (specially) a philanthropist

KJV Translation

benefactor
