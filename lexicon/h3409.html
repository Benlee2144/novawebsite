Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H3409

יָרֵךְ

yârêk — H3409

Transliteration: yârêk

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3409

Derivation: from an unused root meaning to be soft;

Definition

the thigh (from its fleshy softness); by euphemistically the generative parts; figuratively, a shank, flank, side

KJV Translation

[idiom] body, loins, shaft, side, thigh.
