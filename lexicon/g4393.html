προφέρω

prophérō — G4393

Transliteration: prophérō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4393

Derivation: from G4253 (πρό) and G5342 (φέρω);

Definition

to bear forward, i.e. produce

KJV Translation

bring forth
