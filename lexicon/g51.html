ἀγνόημα

agnóēma — G51

Transliteration: agnóēma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G51

Derivation: from G50 (ἀγνοέω);

Definition

a thing ignored, i.e. shortcoming

KJV Translation

error
