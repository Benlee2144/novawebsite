πεῖρα

peîra — G3984

Transliteration: peîra

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3984

Derivation: from the base of G4008 (πέραν) (through the idea of piercing);

Definition

a test, i.e. attempt, experience

KJV Translation

assaying, trial
