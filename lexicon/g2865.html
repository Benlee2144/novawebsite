κομίζω

komízō — G2865

Transliteration: komízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2865

Derivation: from a primary (to tend, i.e. take care of);

Definition

properly, to provide for, i.e. (by implication) to carry off (as if from harm; genitive case obtain)

KJV Translation

bring, receive
