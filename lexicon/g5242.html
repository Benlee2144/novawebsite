ὑπερέχω

hyperéchō — G5242

Transliteration: hyperéchō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5242

Derivation: from G5228 (ὑπέρ) and G2192 (ἔχω);

Definition

to hold oneself above, i.e. (figuratively) to excel; participle (as adjective, or neuter as noun) superior, superiority

KJV Translation

better, excellency, higher, pass, supreme
