ἀσύνθετος

asýnthetos — G802

Transliteration: asýnthetos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G802

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G4934 (συντίθεμαι);

Definition

properly, not agreed, i.e. treacherous to compacts

KJV Translation

covenant-breaker

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀσύν-θετος, ον,
(συντίθημι) uncompounded, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ἀ. φωνή a word standing alone, [Refs 3rd c.BC+]. adverb -τως[Refs]
__II (συντίθεμαι) bound by no covenant, faithless, ὁ δῆμός ἐστιν πρᾶγμα τῶν πάντων ἀσυνθετώτατον[NT+4th c.BC+]; making no covenants, ἀ. διατελοῦσι[Refs 1st c.BC+]
