μαλακός

malakós — G3120

Transliteration: malakós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3120

Derivation: of uncertain affinity;

Definition

soft, i.e. fine (clothing); figuratively, a catamite

KJV Translation

effeminate, soft
