בָּכָה

bâkâh — H1058

Transliteration: bâkâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1058

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to weep; generally to bemoan

KJV Translation

[idiom] at all, bewail, complain, make lamentation, [idiom] more, mourn, [idiom] sore, [idiom] with tears, weep.
