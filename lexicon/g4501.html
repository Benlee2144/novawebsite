ῥομφαία

rhomphaía — G4501

Transliteration: rhomphaía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4501

Derivation: probably of foreign origin;

Definition

a sabre, i.e. a long and broad cutlass (any weapon of the kind, literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

sword
