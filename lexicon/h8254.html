שָׁקַל

shâqal — H8254

Transliteration: shâqal

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H8254

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to suspend or poise (especially in trade)

KJV Translation

pay, receive(-r), spend, [idiom] throughly, weigh.
