σός

sós — G4674

Transliteration: sós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4674

Derivation: from G4771 (σύ);

Definition

thine

KJV Translation

thine (own), thy (friend)

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

σός, -ή, -όν
possess. prop. of second of person(s),
thy, thine: Mat.7:3, al.; as subst., οἱ σοί, thy kinsfolk, friends, Mrk.5:19; τὸ σόν, what is thine, Mat.20:14 25:25; τὰ σά, thy goods, Luk.6:30
(AS)
