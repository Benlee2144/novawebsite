πέραν

péran — G4008

Transliteration: péran

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4008

Derivation: apparently accusative case of an obsolete derivative of (to \

Definition

through (as adverb or preposition), i.e. across

KJV Translation

beyond, farther (other) side, over

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
