αὐτάρκεια

autárkeia — G841

Transliteration: autárkeia

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G841

Derivation: from G842 (αὐτάρκης);

Definition

self-satisfaction, i.e. (abstractly) contentedness, or (concretely) a competence

KJV Translation

contentment, sufficiency
