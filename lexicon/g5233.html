ὑπερβαίνω

hyperbaínō — G5233

Transliteration: hyperbaínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5233

Derivation: from G5228 (ὑπέρ) and the base of G939 (βάσις);

Definition

to transcend, i.e. (figuratively) to overreach

KJV Translation

go beyond
