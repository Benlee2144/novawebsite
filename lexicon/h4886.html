מָשַׁח

mâshach — H4886

Transliteration: mâshach

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4886

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to rub with oil, i.e. to anoint; by implication, to consecrate; also to paint

KJV Translation

anoint, paint.
