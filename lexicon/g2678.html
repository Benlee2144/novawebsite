κατασείω

kataseíō — G2678

Transliteration: kataseíō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2678

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G4579 (σείω);

Definition

to sway downward, i.e. make a signal

KJV Translation

beckon
