ἀπόλαυσις

apólausis — G619

Transliteration: apólausis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G619

Derivation: from a comparative of G575 (ἀπό) and (to enjoy);

Definition

full enjoyment

KJV Translation

enjoy(-ment)

act of enjoying, fruition, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ἀ. ἀνεύφραντος[Refs 2nd c.AD+]
__II result of enjoying, pleasure, αἱ ἀ. αἱ σωματικαί[Refs 4th c.BC+]; ὁ κατ᾽ ἀπόλαυσιν βίος a life of pleasure, [Refs]
__II.2 with genitive, aduantage got from a thing, σίτων καὶ ποτῶν[Refs 5th c.BC+]; ἀπόλαυσιν εἰκοῦς (accusative absolutely) as a reward for your resemblance, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ἀ. ἀδικημάτων advantage, fruit of them, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
