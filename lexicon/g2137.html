εὐοδόω

euodóō — G2137

Transliteration: euodóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2137

Derivation: from a compound of G2095 (εὖ) and G3598 (ὁδός);

Definition

to help on the road, i.e. (passively) succeed in reaching; figuratively, to succeed in business affairs

KJV Translation

(have a) prosper(-ous journey)
