πυρέσσω

pyréssō — G4445

Transliteration: pyréssō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4445

Derivation: from G4443 (πυρά);

Definition

to be on fire, i.e. (specially), to have a fever

KJV Translation

be sick of a fever
