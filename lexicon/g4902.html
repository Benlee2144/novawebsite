συνέπομαι

synépomai — G4902

Transliteration: synépomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4902

Derivation: middle voice from G4862 (σύν) and a primary (to follow);

Definition

to attend (travel) in company with

KJV Translation

accompany
