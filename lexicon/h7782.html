שׁוֹפָר

shôwphâr — H7782

Transliteration: shôwphâr

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7782

Derivation: or שֹׁפָר; from H8231 (שָׁפַר) in the original sense of incising;

Definition

a cornet (as giving a clear sound) or curved horn

KJV Translation

cornet, trumpet.
