Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H1730

דּוֹד

dôwd — H1730

Transliteration: dôwd

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1730

Derivation: or (shortened) דֹּד; from an unused root meaning properly, to boil, i.e.

Definition

(figuratively) to love; by implication, a love-token, lover, friend; specifically an uncle

KJV Translation

(well-) beloved, father's brother, love, uncle.
