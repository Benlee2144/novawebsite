ὀμνύω

omnýō — G3660

Transliteration: omnýō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3660

Derivation: a prolonged form of a primary, but obsolete , for which another prolonged form is used in certain tenses;

Definition

to swear, i.e. take (or declare on) oath

KJV Translation

swear
