προσκοπή

proskopḗ — G4349

Transliteration: proskopḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4349

Derivation: from G4350 (προσκόπτω);

Definition

a stumbling, i.e. (figuratively and concretely) occasion of sin

KJV Translation

offence

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

προσ-κοπή, ἡ,
= πρόσκομμα,
__B offence taken, φθόνος καὶ π. [Refs 2nd c.BC+]; πρός τινα ἀλλοτριότης καὶ π. [Refs 1st c.BC+]; νοσήματα κατὰ προσκοπὴν γινόμενα, i.e. antipathies, [Refs 3rd c.BC+] give no cause of offence, [NT]
