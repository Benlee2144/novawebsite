Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H6438

פִּנָּה

pinnâh — H6438

Transliteration: pinnâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6438

Derivation: feminine of H6434 (פֵּן);

Definition

an angle; by implication, a pinnacle; figuratively, a chieftain

KJV Translation

bulwark, chief, corner, stay, tower.
