עוּר

ʻûwr — H5782

Transliteration: ʻûwr

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5782

Derivation: a primitive root (rather identical with through the idea of opening the eyes);

Definition

to wake (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

(a-) wake(-n, up), lift up (self), [idiom] master, raise (up), stir up (self).
