κατακληροδοτέω

kataklērodotéō — G2624

Transliteration: kataklērodotéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2624

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and a derivative of a compound of G2819 (κλῆρος) and G1325 (δίδωμι);

Definition

to be a giver of lots to each, i.e. (by implication) to apportion an estate

KJV Translation

divide by lot

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

κατακληρο-δοτέω,
