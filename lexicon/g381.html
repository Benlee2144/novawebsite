ἀνάπτω

anáptō — G381

Transliteration: anáptō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G381

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and G681 (ἅπτω);

Definition

to enkindle

KJV Translation

kindle, light
