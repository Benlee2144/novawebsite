βρέχω

bréchō — G1026

Transliteration: bréchō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1026

Derivation: a primary verb;

Definition

to moisten (especially by a shower)

KJV Translation

(send) rain, wash

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
