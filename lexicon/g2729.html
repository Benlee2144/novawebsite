κατισχύω

katischýō — G2729

Transliteration: katischýō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2729

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G2480 (ἰσχύω);

Definition

to overpower

KJV Translation

prevail (against)
