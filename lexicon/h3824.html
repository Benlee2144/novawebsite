לֵבָב

lêbâb — H3824

Transliteration: lêbâb

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3824

Derivation: from H3823 (לָבַב); used also like H3820 (לֵב)

Definition

the heart (as the most interior organ);

KJV Translation

[phrase] bethink themselves, breast, comfortably, courage, ((faint), (tender-) heart(-ed), midst, mind, [idiom] unawares, understanding.
