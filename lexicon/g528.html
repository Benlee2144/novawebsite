ἀπαντάω

apantáō — G528

Transliteration: apantáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G528

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and a derivative of G473 (ἀντί);

Definition

to meet away, i.e. encounter

KJV Translation

meet
