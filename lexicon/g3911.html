παραφέρω

paraphérō — G3911

Transliteration: paraphérō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3911

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and G5342 (φέρω) (including its alternate forms);

Definition

to bear along or aside, i.e. carry off (literally or figuratively); by implication, to avert

KJV Translation

remove, take away
