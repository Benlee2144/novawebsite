μετρέω

metréō — G3354

Transliteration: metréō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3354

Derivation: from G3358 (μέτρον);

Definition

to measure (i.e. ascertain in size by a fixed standard); by implication, to admeasure (i.e. allot by rule)

KJV Translation

figuratively, to estimate:--measure, mete
