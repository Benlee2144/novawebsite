κἀκεῖνος

kakeînos — G2548

Transliteration: kakeînos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2548

Derivation: from G2532 (καί) and G1565 (ἐκεῖνος);

Definition

likewise that (or those)

KJV Translation

and him (other, them), even he, him also, them (also), (and) they

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

κἀκεῖνος, -είνη, -εῖνο
