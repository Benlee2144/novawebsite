ἄφιξις

áphixis — G867

Transliteration: áphixis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G867

Derivation: from G864 (ἀφικνέομαι);

Definition

properly, arrival, i.e. (by implication) departure

KJV Translation

departing
