ἀλαλάζω

alalázō — G214

Transliteration: alalázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G214

Derivation: from ( a shout, \

Definition

to vociferate, i.e. (by implication) to wail; figuratively, to clang

KJV Translation

tinkle, wail
