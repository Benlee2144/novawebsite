μήτε

mḗte — G3383

Transliteration: mḗte

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3383

Derivation: from G3361 (μή) and G5037 (τέ);

Definition

not too, i.e. (in continued negation) neither or nor; also, not even

KJV Translation

neither, (n-)or, so as much

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
