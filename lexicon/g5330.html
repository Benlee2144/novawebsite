Φαρισαῖος

Pharisaîos — G5330

Transliteration: Pharisaîos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5330

Derivation: of Hebrew origin (compare H06567);

Definition

a separatist, i.e. exclusively religious; a Pharisean, i.e. Jewish sectary

KJV Translation

Pharisee

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

a Pharisee, Separatist (from pharash, to distinguish) , one of a sect who separated themselves from other Jews as affecting superior holiness. (from Middle LSJ)
