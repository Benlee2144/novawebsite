σύσσημον

sýssēmon — G4953

Transliteration: sýssēmon

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4953

Derivation: neuter of a compound of G4862 (σύν) and the base of G4591 (σημαίνω);

Definition

a sign in common, i.e. preconcerted signal

KJV Translation

token

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
