νομίμως

nomímōs — G3545

Transliteration: nomímōs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3545

Derivation: adverb from a derivative of G3551 (νόμος);

Definition

legitimately (specially, agreeably to the rules of the lists)

KJV Translation

lawfully
