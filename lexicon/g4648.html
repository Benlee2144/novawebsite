Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4648

σκοπέω

skopéō — G4648

Transliteration: skopéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4648

Derivation: from G4649 (σκοπός);

Definition

to take aim at (spy), i.e. (figuratively) regard

KJV Translation

consider, take heed, look at (on), mark
