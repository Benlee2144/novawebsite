יְהוֹשָׁפָט

Yᵉhôwshâphâṭ — H3092

Transliteration: Yᵉhôwshâphâṭ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3092

Derivation: from H3068 (יְהֹוָה) and H8199 (שָׁפַט); Jehovah-judged;

Definition

Jehoshaphat, the name of six Israelites; also of a valley near Jerusalem

KJV Translation

Jehoshaphat. Compare H3146 (יוֹשָׁפָט).

Jehoshaphat = "Jehovah has judged"
son of king Asa and himself king of Judah for 25 years; one of the best, most pious, and prosperous kings of Judah, symbolical name of a valley near Jerusalem which is the place of ultimate judgment; maybe the deep ravine which separates Jerusalem from the Mount of Olives through which the Kidron flowed
