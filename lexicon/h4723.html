Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H4723

מִקְוֶה

miqveh — H4723

Transliteration: miqveh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4723

Derivation: or מִקְוֵה; (1 Kings 10:28), or מִקְוֵאlemma מִּקְוֵא extra dagesh, corrected to מִקְוֵא; (2 Chronicles 1:16), from H6960 (קָוָה);

Definition

something waited for, i.e. confidence (objective or subjective); also a collection, i.e. (of water) a pond, or (of men and horses) a caravan or drove

KJV Translation

abiding, gathering together, hope, linen yarn, plenty (of water), pool.
