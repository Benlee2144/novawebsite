Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H6942

קָדַשׁ

qâdash — H6942

Transliteration: qâdash

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6942

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to be (causatively, make, pronounce or observe as) clean (ceremonially or morally)

KJV Translation

appoint, bid, consecrate, dedicate, defile, hallow, (be, keep) holy(-er, place), keep, prepare, proclaim, purify, sanctify(-ied one, self), [idiom] wholly.
