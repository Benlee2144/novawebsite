δηνάριον

dēnárion — G1220

Transliteration: dēnárion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1220

Derivation: of Latin origin;

Definition

a denarius (or ten asses)

KJV Translation

pence, penny(-worth)

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

δηνάριον, τό,
= Latin denarius (usually represented by δραχμή (which see)), [Refs 2nd c.AD+]; = one tetradrachm, [Refs 4th c.AD+]
