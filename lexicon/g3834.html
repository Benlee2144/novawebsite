πανουργία

panourgía — G3834

Transliteration: panourgía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3834

Derivation: from G3835 (πανοῦργος);

Definition

adroitness, i.e. (in a bad sense) trickery or sophistry

KJV Translation

(cunning) craftiness, subtilty
