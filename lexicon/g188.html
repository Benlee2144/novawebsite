ἀκμήν

akmḗn — G188

Transliteration: akmḗn

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G188

Derivation: accusative case of a noun (\

Definition

adverbially, just now, i.e. still

KJV Translation

yet

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

accusative of ἀκμή, used as adverb, as yet, still, [Refs 4th c.BC+]; τὰ σκευοφόρα.. ἀκμὴν διέβαινε were just crossing the river, [NT+5th c.BC+]
