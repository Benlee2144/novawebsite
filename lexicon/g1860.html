ἐπαγγελία

epangelía — G1860

Transliteration: epangelía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1860

Derivation: from G1861 (ἐπαγγέλλω);

Definition

an announcement (for information, assent or pledge; especially a divine assurance of good)

KJV Translation

message, promise
