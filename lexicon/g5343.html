Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G5343

φεύγω

pheúgō — G5343

Transliteration: pheúgō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5343

Derivation: apparently a primary verb;

Definition

to run away (literally or figuratively); by implication, to shun; by analogy, to vanish

KJV Translation

escape, flee (away)
