ἐπιφέρω

epiphérō — G2018

Transliteration: epiphérō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2018

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G5342 (φέρω);

Definition

to bear upon (or further), i.e. adduce (personally or judicially (accuse, inflict)), superinduce

KJV Translation

add, bring (against), take
