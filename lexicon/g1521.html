εἰσάγω

eiságō — G1521

Transliteration: eiságō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1521

Derivation: from G1519 (εἰς) and G71 (ἄγω);

Definition

to introduce (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

bring in(-to), (+ was to) lead into
