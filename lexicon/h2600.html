חִנָּם

chinnâm — H2600

Transliteration: chinnâm

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2600

Derivation: from H2580 (חֵן);

Definition

gratis, i.e. devoid of cost, reason or advantage

KJV Translation

without a cause (cost, wages), causeless, to cost nothing, free(-ly), innocent, for nothing (nought, in vain.
