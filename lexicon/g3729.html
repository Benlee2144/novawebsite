ὁρμάω

hormáō — G3729

Transliteration: hormáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3729

Derivation: from G3730 (ὁρμή);

Definition

to start, spur or urge on, i.e. (reflexively) to dash or plunge

KJV Translation

run (violently), rush
