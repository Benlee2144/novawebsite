ἀπαλλάσσω

apallássō — G525

Transliteration: apallássō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G525

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G236 (ἀλλάσσω);

Definition

to change away, i.e. release, (reflexively) remove

KJV Translation

deliver, depart
