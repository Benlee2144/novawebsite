Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H7167

קָרַע

qâraʻ — H7167

Transliteration: qâraʻ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7167

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to rend, literally or figuratively (revile, paint the eyes, as if enlarging them)

KJV Translation

cut out, rend, [idiom] surely, tear.
