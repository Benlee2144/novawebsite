נָשִׂיא

nâsîyʼ — H5387

Transliteration: nâsîyʼ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5387

Derivation: or נָשִׂא; from H5375 (נָשָׂא);

Definition

properly, an exalted one, i.e. a king or sheik; also a rising mist

KJV Translation

captain, chief, cloud, governor, prince, ruler, vapour.
