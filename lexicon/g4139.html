πλησίον

plēsíon — G4139

Transliteration: plēsíon

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4139

Derivation: neuter of a derivative of (near);

Definition

(adverbially) close by; as noun, a neighbor, i.e. fellow (as man, countryman, Christian or friend)

KJV Translation

near, neighbour
