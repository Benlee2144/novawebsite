ἀνατίθεμαι

anatíthemai — G394

Transliteration: anatíthemai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G394

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and the middle voice of G5087 (τίθημι);

Definition

to set forth (for oneself), i.e propound

KJV Translation

communicate, declare
