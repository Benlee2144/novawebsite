אֵל

ʼêl — H410

Transliteration: ʼêl

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H410

Derivation: shortened from H352 (אַיִל);

Definition

strength; as adjective, mighty; especially the Almighty (but used also of any deity)

KJV Translation

God (god), [idiom] goodly, [idiom] great, idol, might(-y one), power, strong. Compare names in '-el.'
