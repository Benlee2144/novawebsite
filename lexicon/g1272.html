Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1272

διανοίγω

dianoígō — G1272

Transliteration: dianoígō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1272

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G455 (ἀνοίγω);

Definition

to open thoroughly, literally (as a first-born) or figuratively (to expound)

KJV Translation

open
