מַדּוּעַ

maddûwaʻ — H4069

Transliteration: maddûwaʻ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4069

Derivation: or מַדֻּעַ; from H4100 (מָה) and the passive participle of H3045 (יָדַע);

Definition

what (is) known?; i.e. (by implication) (adverbially) why?

KJV Translation

how, wherefore, why.
