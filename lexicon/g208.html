ἀκυρόω

akyróō — G208

Transliteration: akyróō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G208

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and G2964 (κυρόω);

Definition

to invalidate

KJV Translation

disannul, make of none effect
