πατροπαράδοτος

patroparádotos — G3970

Transliteration: patroparádotos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3970

Derivation: from G3962 (πατήρ) and a derivative of G3860 (παραδίδωμι) (in the sense of handing over or down);

Definition

traditionary
