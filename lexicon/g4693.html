σπήλαιον

spḗlaion — G4693

Transliteration: spḗlaion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4693

Derivation: neuter of a presumed derivative of (a grotto);

Definition

a cavern; by implication, a hiding-place or resort

KJV Translation

cave, den
