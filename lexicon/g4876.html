συναντάω

synantáō — G4876

Transliteration: synantáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4876

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and a derivative of G473 (ἀντί);

Definition

to meet with; figuratively, to occur

KJV Translation

befall, meet
