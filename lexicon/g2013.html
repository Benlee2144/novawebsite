ἐπιτυγχάνω

epitynchánō — G2013

Transliteration: epitynchánō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2013

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G5177 (τυγχάνω);

Definition

to chance upon, i.e. (by implication) to attain

KJV Translation

obtain
