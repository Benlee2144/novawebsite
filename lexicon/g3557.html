νοσφίζομαι

nosphízomai — G3557

Transliteration: nosphízomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3557

Derivation: middle voice from (apart or clandestinely);

Definition

to sequestrate for oneself, i.e. embezzle

KJV Translation

keep back, purloin
