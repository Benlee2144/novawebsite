προσεδρεύω

prosedreúō — G4332

Transliteration: prosedreúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4332

Derivation: from a compound of G4314 (πρός) and the base of G1476 (ἑδραῖος);

Definition

to sit near, i.e. attend as a servant

KJV Translation

wait at
