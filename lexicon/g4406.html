πρώϊμος

prṓïmos — G4406

Transliteration: prṓïmos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4406

Derivation: from G4404 (πρωΐ);

Definition

dawning, i.e. (by analogy) autumnal (showering, the first of the rainy season)

KJV Translation

early

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

__2 metaphorically, precocious, π. πονηρία [Refs 4th c.AD+]. adverb comparative -ώτερον[Refs 2nd c.BC+]
