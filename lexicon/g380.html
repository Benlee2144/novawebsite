ἀναπτύσσω

anaptýssō — G380

Transliteration: anaptýssō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G380

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) (in the sense of reversal) and G4428 (πτύσσω);

Definition

to unroll (a scroll or volume)

KJV Translation

open
