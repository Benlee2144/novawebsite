ζημιόω

zēmióō — G2210

Transliteration: zēmióō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2210

Derivation: from G2209 (ζημία);

Definition

to injure, i.e. (reflexively or passively) to experience detriment

KJV Translation

be cast away, receive damage, lose, suffer loss
