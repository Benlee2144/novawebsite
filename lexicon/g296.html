ἄμφοδον

ámphodon — G296

Transliteration: ámphodon

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G296

Derivation: from the base of G297 (ἀμφότερος) and G3598 (ὁδός);

Definition

a fork in the road

KJV Translation

where two ways meet

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἄμφοδον, τό,
street, [NT+5th c.BC+]; = compitum, [Refs]
__II block of houses surrounded by streets, [Refs 4th c.BC+]: hence, ward, quarter of a town, [LXX+3rd c.BC+], etc.:—also ἄμφοδος, ἡ, [LXX+2nd c.AD+]

Appears In (1 verse)

Mark 11:4
