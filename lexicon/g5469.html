Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G5469

χαλινός

chalinós — G5469

Transliteration: chalinós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5469

Derivation: from G5465 (χαλάω);

Definition

a curb or head-stall (as curbing the spirit)

KJV Translation

bit, bridle

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

χᾰλῑνός
