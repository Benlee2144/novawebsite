μένω

ménō — G3306

Transliteration: ménō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3306

Derivation: a primary verb;

Definition

to stay (in a given place, state, relation or expectancy)

KJV Translation

abide, continue, dwell, endure, be present, remain, stand, tarry (for), X thine own
