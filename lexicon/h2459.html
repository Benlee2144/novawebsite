חֶלֶב

cheleb — H2459

Transliteration: cheleb

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2459

Derivation: or חֵלֶב; from an unused root meaning to be fat;

Definition

fat, whether literally or figuratively; hence, the richest or choice part

KJV Translation

[idiom] best, fat(-ness), [idiom] finest, grease, marrow.
