Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4537

σαλπίζω

salpízō — G4537

Transliteration: salpízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4537

Derivation: from G4536 (σάλπιγξ);

Definition

to trumpet, i.e. sound a blast (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

(which are yet to) sound (a trumpet)
