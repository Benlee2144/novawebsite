Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3341

μετάνοια

metánoia — G3341

Transliteration: metánoia

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3341

Derivation: from G3340 (μετανοέω);

Definition

(subjectively) compunction (for guilt, including reformation); by implication, reversal (of (another's) decision)

KJV Translation

repentance

⚠ Our Analysis Differs

Traditional lexicons define μετάνοια through a theological lens rather than its literal meaning: “a change of mind.” The concept of “penitence” was imported from the Latin poenitentia. See our study for the historical evidence.
