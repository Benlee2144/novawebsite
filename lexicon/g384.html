ἀνασκευάζω

anaskeuázō — G384

Transliteration: anaskeuázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G384

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) (in the sense of reversal) and a derivative of G4632 (σκεῦος);

Definition

properly, to pack up (baggage), i.e. (by implication, and figuratively) to upset

KJV Translation

subvert
