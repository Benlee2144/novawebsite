ὑποδέχομαι

hypodéchomai — G5264

Transliteration: hypodéchomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5264

Derivation: from G5259 (ὑπό) and G1209 (δέχομαι);

Definition

to admit under one's roof, i.e. entertain hospitably

KJV Translation

receive
