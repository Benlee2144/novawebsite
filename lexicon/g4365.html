προσπορεύομαι

prosporeúomai — G4365

Transliteration: prosporeúomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4365

Derivation: from G4314 (πρός) and G4198 (πορεύομαι);

Definition

to journey towards, i.e. approach (not the same as G4313 (προπορεύομαι))

KJV Translation

go before

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
