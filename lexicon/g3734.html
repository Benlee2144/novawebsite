ὁροθεσία

horothesía — G3734

Transliteration: horothesía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3734

Derivation: from a compound of the base of G3725 (ὅριον) and a derivative of G5087 (τίθημι);

Definition

a limit-placing, i.e. (concretely) boundary-line

KJV Translation

bound
