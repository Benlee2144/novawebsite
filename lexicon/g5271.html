ὑποκρίνομαι

hypokrínomai — G5271

Transliteration: hypokrínomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5271

Derivation: middle voice from G5259 (ὑπό) and G2919 (κρίνω);

Definition

to decide (speak or act) under a false part, i.e. (figuratively) dissemble (pretend)

KJV Translation

feign
