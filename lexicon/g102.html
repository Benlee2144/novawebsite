ἀδύνατος

adýnatos — G102

Transliteration: adýnatos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G102

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and G1415 (δυνατός);

Definition

unable, i.e. weak (literally or figuratively); passively, impossible

KJV Translation

could not do, impossible, impotent, not possible, weak
