Ἰσραήλ

Israḗl — G2474

Transliteration: Israḗl

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2474

Derivation: of Hebrew origin (H03478);

Definition

Israel (i.e. Jisrael), the adopted name of Jacob, including his descendants (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

Israel
