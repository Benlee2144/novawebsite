σύνδεσμος

sýndesmos — G4886

Transliteration: sýndesmos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4886

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G1199 (δεσμόν);

Definition

a joint tie, i.e. ligament, (figuratively) uniting principle, control

KJV Translation

band, bond
