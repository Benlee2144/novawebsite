στερεός

stereós — G4731

Transliteration: stereós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4731

Derivation: from G2476 (ἵστημι);

Definition

stiff, i.e. solid, stable (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

stedfast, strong, sure
