Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G5240

ὑπερεκχύνω

hyperekchýnō — G5240

Transliteration: hyperekchýnō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5240

Derivation: from G5228 (ὑπέρ) and the alternate form of G1632 (ἐκχέω);

Definition

to pour out over, i.e. (passively) to overflow

KJV Translation

run over

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

Included with: ὑπερεκ-χέω,
pour out over, metaphorically, εἰς τὴν ἐπαρχίαν τὰς τιμάς [Refs 1st c.AD+]:—passive, overflow, [LXX+1st c.BC+]:—also ὑπερεκ-χύνομαι in [NT]; future -χυθήσομαι variant in [LXX]

Appears In (1 verse)

Luke 6:38
