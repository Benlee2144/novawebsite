זָהָב

zâhâb — H2091

Transliteration: zâhâb

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2091

Derivation: from an unused root meaning to shimmer;

Definition

gold, figuratively, something gold-colored (i.e. yellow), as oil, a clear sky

KJV Translation

gold(-en), fair weather.
