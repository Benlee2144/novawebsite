προσδέομαι

prosdéomai — G4326

Transliteration: prosdéomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4326

Derivation: from G4314 (πρός) and G1189 (δέομαι);

Definition

to require additionally, i.e. want further

KJV Translation

need

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

__A.2 with accusative only, attach, τοὺς κάλους [Refs 5th c.BC+]
