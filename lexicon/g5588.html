ψιθυριστής

psithyristḗs — G5588

Transliteration: psithyristḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5588

Derivation: from the same as G5587 (ψιθυρισμός);

Definition

a secret calumniator

KJV Translation

whisperer
