Καπερναούμ

Kapernaoúm — G2584

Transliteration: Kapernaoúm

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2584

Derivation: of Hebrew origin (probably H03723 and H05151);

Definition

Capernaum (i.e. Caphanachum), a place in Palestine

KJV Translation

Capernaum
