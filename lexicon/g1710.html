ἐμπορεύομαι

emporeúomai — G1710

Transliteration: emporeúomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1710

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and G4198 (πορεύομαι);

Definition

to travel in (a country as a pedlar), i.e. (by implication) to trade

KJV Translation

buy and sell, make merchandise
