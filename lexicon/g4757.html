στρατιώτης

stratiṓtēs — G4757

Transliteration: stratiṓtēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4757

Derivation: from a presumed derivative of the same as G4756 (στρατιά);

Definition

a camper-out, i.e. a (common) warrior (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

soldier
