βαστάζω

bastázō — G941

Transliteration: bastázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G941

Derivation: perhaps remotely derived from the base of G939 (βάσις) (through the idea of removal);

Definition

to lift, literally or figuratively (endure, declare, sustain, receive, etc.)

KJV Translation

bear, carry, take up
