συμπνίγω

sympnígō — G4846

Transliteration: sympnígō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4846

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G4155 (πνίγω);

Definition

to strangle completely, i.e. (literally) to drown, or (figuratively) to crowd

KJV Translation

choke, throng

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

συν-πνίγω (Rec. συμπ-),
to choke: of thronging by a crowd, Luk.8:42. Fig., of seed, τ. λόγον, Mat.13:22, Mrk.4:7, 19, Luk.8:14.†
