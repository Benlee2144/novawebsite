Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H8585

תְּעָלָה

tᵉʻâlâh — H8585

Transliteration: tᵉʻâlâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H8585

Derivation: from H5927 (עָלָה);

Definition

a channel (into which water is raised for irrigation); also a bandage or plaster (as placed upon a wound)

KJV Translation

conduit, cured, healing, little river, trench, watercourse.

Appears In (6 verses)

1 Kings 18:32Ezekiel 31:4Isaiah 7:3Jeremiah 30:13Jeremiah 46:11Job 38:25

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

1) healing (of new flesh and skin forming over wound)
1a) (CLBL) bandage, plaister (for healing of wound)
