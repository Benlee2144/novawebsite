ἐπιστομίζω

epistomízō — G1993

Transliteration: epistomízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1993

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G4750 (στόμα);

Definition

to put something over the mouth, i.e. (figuratively) to silence

KJV Translation

stop mouths
