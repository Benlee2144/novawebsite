μόδιος

módios — G3426

Transliteration: módios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3426

Derivation: of Latin origin;

Definition

a modius, i.e. certain measure for things dry (the quantity or the utensil)

KJV Translation

bushel

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

μόδιος, ὁ,
= Latin modius, a dry measure, = 1/6 μέδιμνος, for which it is falsa lectio in [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__2 vessel of this capacity, [NT]
__II a measure of length, = 200 ὀργυιαί, [Refs 2nd c.BC+]
