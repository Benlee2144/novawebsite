אָפַד

ʼâphad — H640

Transliteration: ʼâphad

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H640

Derivation: a primitive root (rather a denominative from H646 (אֵפוֹד));

Definition

to gird on (the ephod)

KJV Translation

bind, gird.

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

1) (Qal) bind, gird
1a) to gird on (ephod)
1b) (TWOT) ephod
