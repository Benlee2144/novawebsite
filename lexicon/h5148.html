נָחָה

nâchâh — H5148

Transliteration: nâchâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5148

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to guide; by implication, to transport (into exile, or as colonists)

KJV Translation

bestow, bring, govern, guide, lead (forth), put, straiten.
