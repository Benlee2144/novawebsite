יִשְׂחָק

Yischâq — H3446

Transliteration: Yischâq

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3446

Derivation: from H7831 (שַׁחֲצוֹם); he will laugh;

Definition

Jischak, the heir of Abraham

KJV Translation

Isaac. Compare H3327 (יִצְחָק).
