πρόθυμος

próthymos — G4289

Transliteration: próthymos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4289

Derivation: from G4253 (πρό) and G2372 (θυμός);

Definition

forward in spirit, i.e. predisposed; neuter (as noun) alacrity

KJV Translation

ready, willing
