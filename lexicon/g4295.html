πρόκειμαι

prókeimai — G4295

Transliteration: prókeimai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4295

Derivation: from G4253 (πρό) and G2749 (κεῖμαι);

Definition

to lie before the view, i.e. (figuratively) to be present (to the mind), to stand forth (as an example or reward)

KJV Translation

be first, set before (forth)
