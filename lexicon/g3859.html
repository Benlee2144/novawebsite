παραδιατριβή

paradiatribḗ — G3859

Transliteration: paradiatribḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3859

Derivation: from a compound of G3844 (παρά) and G1304 (διατρίβω);

Definition

misemployment, i.e. meddlesomeness

KJV Translation

perverse disputing
