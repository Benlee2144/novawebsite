ἀποτολμάω

apotolmáō — G662

Transliteration: apotolmáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G662

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G5111 (τολμάω);

Definition

to venture plainly

KJV Translation

be very bold

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀποτολμ-άω,
make a bold venture upon, τινί[Refs 5th c.BC+]: with infinitive, ἀ. ἐπιχειρῆσαι[Refs 5th c.BC+] —perfect participle passive in active sense, δι᾽ ἐλευθερίας λίαν ἀποτετολμημένης too presumptuous liberty, [NT+5th c.BC+]

Appears In (1 verse)

Romans 10:20
