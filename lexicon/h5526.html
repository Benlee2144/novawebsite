סָכַךְ

çâkak — H5526

Transliteration: çâkak

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5526

Derivation: or שָׂכַךְ; (Exodus 33:22), a primitive root;

Definition

properly, to entwine as ascreen; by implication, to fence in, cover over, (figuratively) protect

KJV Translation

cover, defence, defend, hedge in, join together, set, shut up.
