ἐξεγείρω

exegeírō — G1825

Transliteration: exegeírō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1825

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G1453 (ἐγείρω);

Definition

to rouse fully, i.e. (figuratively) to resuscitate (from death), release (from infliction)

KJV Translation

raise up
