ἀνώτερος

anṓteros — G511

Transliteration: anṓteros

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G511

Derivation: comparative degree of G507 (ἄνω);

Definition

upper, i.e. (neuter as adverb) to a more conspicuous place, in a former part of the book

KJV Translation

above, higher

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀνώτερος, α, ον,
comparative adjective from ἄνω (B), upper, higher, [Refs 4th c.BC+]; ἐπιβουλῆς ἀ. γέγονεν got the better of,[Refs 4th c.BC+]; neuter as adverb, [Refs 4th c.BC+]; above, [LXX]; earlier in a book, [NT+2nd c.BC+]; to a higher place, [NT]. adverb ἀνωτέρω, see at {ἄνω}.
