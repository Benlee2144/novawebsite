ἀνακαλύπτω

anakalýptō — G343

Transliteration: anakalýptō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G343

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) (in the sense of reversal) and G2572 (καλύπτω);

Definition

to unveil

KJV Translation

open, (un-)taken away
