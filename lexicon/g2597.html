καταβαίνω

katabaínō — G2597

Transliteration: katabaínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2597

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and the base of G939 (βάσις);

Definition

to descend (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

come (get, go, step) down, fall (down)
