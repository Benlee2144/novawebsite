κοιμάω

koimáō — G2837

Transliteration: koimáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2837

Derivation: from G2749 (κεῖμαι);

Definition

to put to sleep, i.e. (passively or reflexively) to slumber; figuratively, to decease

KJV Translation

(be a-, fall a-, fall on) sleep, be dead
