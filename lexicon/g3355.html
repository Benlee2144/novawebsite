μετρητής

metrētḗs — G3355

Transliteration: metrētḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3355

Derivation: from G3354 (μετρέω);

Definition

a measurer, i.e. (specially), a certain standard measure of capacity for liquids

KJV Translation

firkin
