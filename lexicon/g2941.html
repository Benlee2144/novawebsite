Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2941

κυβέρνησις

kybérnēsis — G2941

Transliteration: kybérnēsis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2941

Derivation: from (of Latin origin, to steer);

Definition

pilotage, i.e. (figuratively) directorship (in the church)

KJV Translation

government

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

κῠβέρν-ησις,
Doric dialect κῠβέρν-ᾱσις, εως, ἡ, steering, pilotage, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 metaphorically, government, πολίων of cities, [NT+5th c.BC+]; θεοῦ by a god, [Refs 1st c.AD+]

Appears In (1 verse)

1 Corinthians 12:28
