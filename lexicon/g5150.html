τρίμηνον

trímēnon — G5150

Transliteration: trímēnon

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5150

Derivation: neuter of a compound of G5140 (τρεῖς) and G3376 (μήν) as noun;

Definition

a three months' space

KJV Translation

three months

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

τρίμηνος, -ον
(τρεῖς, μήν) [in LXX (neut.): Gen.38:24, 4Ki.23:31 B 4Ki.24:8, 2Ch.36:2 36:9 (חֹדֶשׁ שָׁלוֹשׁ) * ;]
of three months; as subst., τὸ τ. (= cl. ἡ τ.), a space of three months (Polyb., al.; LXX): Heb.11:23.†
