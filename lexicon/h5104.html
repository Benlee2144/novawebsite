נָהָר

nâhâr — H5104

Transliteration: nâhâr

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5104

Derivation: from H5102 (נָהַר);

Definition

a stream (including the sea; expectation the Nile, Euphrates, etc.); figuratively, prosperity

KJV Translation

flood, river.
