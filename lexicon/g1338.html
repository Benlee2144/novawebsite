Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1338

διϊκνέομαι

diïknéomai — G1338

Transliteration: diïknéomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1338

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and the base of G2425 (ἱκανός);

Definition

to reach through, i.e. penetrate

KJV Translation

pierce

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

διϊκνέομαι,
future -ίξομαι: aorist -ικόμην:— go through, penetrate, ποτὶ τὰν ψυχάν δι᾽ ὤτων [Refs 5th c.BC+]; reach, with missiles, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 in speaking, go through, tell of, πάντα δ. [Refs 8th c.BC+]
__3 of Time, intervene, διετοῦς χρόνου διικνουμένου [Refs 3rd c.AD+]
