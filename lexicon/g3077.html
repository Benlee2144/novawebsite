λύπη

lýpē — G3077

Transliteration: lýpē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3077

Derivation: apparently a primary word;

Definition

sadness

KJV Translation

grief, grievous, + grudgingly, heaviness, sorrow

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

λῡπ-η, ἡ,
pain of body, opposed to ἡδονή, [Refs]; also, sad plight or condition, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 pain of mind, grief,[Refs 4th c.BC+]; τί γὰρ καλὸν ζῆν βίοτον, ὃς λύπας φέρε; [Refs 5th c.BC+]; opposed to χαρά, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
