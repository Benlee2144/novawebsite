Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H3335

יָצַר

yâtsar — H3335

Transliteration: yâtsar

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3335

Derivation: probably identical with H3334 (יָצַר) (through the squeezing into shape); (compare H3331 (יַצַע));

Definition

to mould into a form; especially as apotter; figuratively, to determine (i.e. form a resolution)

KJV Translation

[idiom] earthen, fashion, form, frame, make(-r), potter, purpose.
