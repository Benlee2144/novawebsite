שָׁחַר

shâchar — H7836

Transliteration: shâchar

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7836

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

properly, to dawn, i.e. (figuratively) be (up) early at any task (with the implication of earnestness); by extension, to search for (with painstaking)

KJV Translation

(do something) betimes, enquire early, rise (seek) betimes, seek diligently) early, in the morning).
