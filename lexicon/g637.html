ἀποπλύνω

apoplýnō — G637

Transliteration: apoplýnō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G637

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G4150 (πλύνω);

Definition

to rinse off

KJV Translation
