סָתַר

Transliteration: çâthar

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5641

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

KJV Translation

be absent, keep close, conceal, hide (self), (keep) secret, [idiom] surely.
