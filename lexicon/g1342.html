δίκαιος

díkaios — G1342

Transliteration: díkaios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1342

Derivation: from G1349 (δίκη);

Definition

equitable (in character or act); by implication, innocent, holy (absolutely or relatively)

KJV Translation

just, meet, right(-eous)
