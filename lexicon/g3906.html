παρατηρέω

paratēréō — G3906

Transliteration: paratēréō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3906

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and G5083 (τηρέω);

Definition

to inspect alongside, i.e. note insidiously or scrupulously

KJV Translation

observe, watch
