Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H2836

חָשַׁק

châshaq — H2836

Transliteration: châshaq

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2836

Derivation: a primitive root; also interchangeable for H2820 (חָשַׂךְ)

Definition

to cling, i.e. join, (figuratively) to love, delight in; elliptically; to deliver

KJV Translation

have a delight, (have a) desire, fillet, long, set (in) love.
