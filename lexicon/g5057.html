τελώνης

telṓnēs — G5057

Transliteration: telṓnēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5057

Derivation: from G5056 (τέλος) and G5608 (ὠνέομαι);

Definition

a tax-farmer, i.e. collector of public revenue

KJV Translation

publican

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

τελών-ης, ου, ὁ,
(τέλος 1.8, ὠνέομαι) farmer or collector of tolls, customs, or taxes, [Refs 5th c.BC+], of the Oropians, [Refs 2nd c.BC+]; = Latin publicanus, [NT]; πορνοβοσκοὶ καὶ τ. [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
