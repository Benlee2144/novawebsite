καταφεύγω

katapheúgō — G2703

Transliteration: katapheúgō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2703

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G5343 (φεύγω);

Definition

to flee down (away)

KJV Translation

flee

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

κατα-φεύγω
[in LXX for נוּס, etc. ;]
to flee for refuge: before εἰς, Act.14:6; metaphorically, with inf., Heb.6:18.†
(AS)
