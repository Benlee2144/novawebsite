μύριοι

mýrioi — G3463

Transliteration: mýrioi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3463

Derivation: plural of an apparently primary word (properly, meaning very many);

Definition

ten thousand; by extension, innumerably many

KJV Translation

ten thousand
