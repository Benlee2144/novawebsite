ἀξίως

axíōs — G516

Transliteration: axíōs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G516

Derivation: adverb from G514 (ἄξιος);

Definition

appropriately

KJV Translation

as becometh, after a godly sort, worthily(-thy)
