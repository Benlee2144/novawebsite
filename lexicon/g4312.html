προπετής

propetḗs — G4312

Transliteration: propetḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4312

Derivation: from a compound of G4253 (πρό) and G4098 (πίπτω);

Definition

falling forward, i.e. headlong (figuratively, precipitate)

KJV Translation

heady, rash(-ly)
