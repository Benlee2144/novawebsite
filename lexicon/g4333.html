Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4333

προσεργάζομαι

prosergázomai — G4333

Transliteration: prosergázomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4333

Derivation: from G4314 (πρός) and G2038 (ἐργάζομαι);

Definition

to work additionally, i.e. (by implication) acquire besides

KJV Translation

gain

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

προσεργάζομαι,
work besides, μηδὲν τοῖς δεδραμένοις [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ἀγαθὰ π. τινί do good service to one besides, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 make, earn in addition, [Refs 5th c.BC+]

Appears In (1 verse)

Luke 19:16

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)
