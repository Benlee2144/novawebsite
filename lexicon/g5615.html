ὡσαύτως

hōsaútōs — G5615

Transliteration: hōsaútōs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5615

Derivation: from G5613 (ὡς) and an adverb from G846 (αὐτός);

Definition

as thus, i.e. in the same way

KJV Translation

even so, likewise, after the same (in like) manner

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ὡσαύτως,
adverb of ὁ αὐτός, in like manner, just so, used by [Refs 8th c.BC+].. in like manner as.. [Refs 5th c.BC+]; opposed to ὡς ἑτέρως, [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__2 further strengthened, ὡ. οὕτως so in like manner, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
