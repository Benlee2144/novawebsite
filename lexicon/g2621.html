κατάκειμαι

katákeimai — G2621

Transliteration: katákeimai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2621

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G2749 (κεῖμαι);

Definition

to lie down, i.e. (by implication) be sick; specially, to recline at a meal

KJV Translation

keep, lie, sit at meat (down)
