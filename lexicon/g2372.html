θυμός

thymós — G2372

Transliteration: thymós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2372

Derivation: from G2380 (θύω);

Definition

passion (as if breathing hard)

KJV Translation

fierceness, indignation, wrath

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
