Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4980

σχολάζω

scholázō — G4980

Transliteration: scholázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4980

Derivation: from G4981 (σχολή);

Definition

to take a holiday, i.e. be at leisure for (by implication, devote oneself wholly to); figuratively, to be vacant (of a house)

KJV Translation

empty, give self
