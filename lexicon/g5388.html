φιλότεκνος

philóteknos — G5388

Transliteration: philóteknos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5388

Derivation: from G5384 (φίλος) and G5043 (τέκνον);

Definition

fond of one's children, i.e. maternal

KJV Translation

love their children

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

φῐλότεκν-ος, ον,
loving one's children or offspring, [NT+5th c.BC+]: comparative, αἱ μητέρες -ότεραι [Refs 4th c.BC+]; superlative -ότατος[Refs 1st c.AD+]

Appears In (1 verse)

Titus 2:4
