נְאֻם

nᵉʼum — H5002

Transliteration: nᵉʼum

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5002

Derivation: from H5001 (נָאַם);

Definition

an oracle

KJV Translation

(hath) said, saith.

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

1) (Qal) utterance, declaration (of prophet)
1a) utterance, declaration, revelation (of prophet in ecstatic state)
1b) utterance, declaration (elsewhere always preceding divine name)
