ἐπιταγή

epitagḗ — G2003

Transliteration: epitagḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2003

Derivation: from G2004 (ἐπιτάσσω);

Definition

an injunction or decree; by implication, authoritativeness

KJV Translation

authority, commandment

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐπι-τᾰγή, ἡ,
(ἐπιτάσσω) ={-ταγμα}, [LXX+2nd c.BC+]; imposition of taxes, αἱ ἐ. τῶν εἰσφορῶν [Refs 1st c.BC+]
__2 especially of oracles or divine commands, κατ᾽ ἐπιταγήν [Refs]; κατ᾽ ἐ. τοῦ θεοῦ [NT]
