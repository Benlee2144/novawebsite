ἀναντίῤῥητος

anantírrhētos — G368

Transliteration: anantírrhētos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G368

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of a compound of G473 (ἀντί) and G4483 (ῥέω);

Definition

indisputable

KJV Translation

cannot be spoken against
