νότος

nótos — G3558

Transliteration: nótos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3558

Derivation: of uncertain affinity;

Definition

the south(-west) wind; by extension, the southern quarter itself

KJV Translation

south (wind)
