Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4787

συγκινέω

synkinéō — G4787

Transliteration: synkinéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4787

Derivation: from G4682 (σπαράσσω) and G2795 (κινέω);

Definition

to move together, i.e. (specially), to excite as a mass (to sedition)

KJV Translation

stir up

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

συγκῑν-έω,

__II apparently intransitive, [Refs 4th c.BC+]
