ἀποστοματίζω

apostomatízō — G653

Transliteration: apostomatízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G653

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and a (presumed) derivative of G4750 (στόμα);

Definition

to speak off-hand (properly, dictate), i.e. to catechize (in an invidious manner)

KJV Translation

provoke to speak

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

__2 interrogate, catechize, as a master his pupil, [NT+5th c.BC+]
