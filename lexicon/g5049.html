τελείως

teleíōs — G5049

Transliteration: teleíōs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5049

Derivation: adverb from G5046 (τέλειος);

Definition

completely, i.e. (of hope) without wavering

KJV Translation

to the end
