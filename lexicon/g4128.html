πλῆθος

plēthos — G4128

Transliteration: plēthos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4128

Derivation: from G4130 (πλήθω);

Definition

a fulness, i.e. a large number, throng, populace

KJV Translation

bundle, company, multitude
