Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H2645

חָפָה

châphâh — H2645

Transliteration: châphâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2645

Derivation: a primitive root (compare H2644 (חָפָא), H2653 (חׇפַף));

Definition

to cover; by implication, to veil, to encase, protect

KJV Translation

ceil, cover, overlay.

Appears In (6 verses)

2 Chronicles 3:52 Chronicles 3:92 Samuel 15:30Esther 7:8Jeremiah 14:4Psalms 68:13
