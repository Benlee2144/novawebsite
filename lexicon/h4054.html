Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H4054

מִגְרָשׁ

migrâsh — H4054

Transliteration: migrâsh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4054

Derivation: also (in plural) feminine (Ezekiel 27:28) מִגְרָשָׁה; from H1644 (גָּרַשׁ);

Definition

a suburb (i.e. open country whither flocks are driven from pasture); hence, the area around abuilding, or the margin of the sea

KJV Translation

cast out, suburb.
