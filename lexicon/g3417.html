μνεία

mneía — G3417

Transliteration: mneía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3417

Derivation: from G3415 (μνάομαι) or G3403 (μιμνήσκω);

Definition

recollection; by implication, recital

KJV Translation

mention, remembrance

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

μνεία, ἡ,
={μνήμη}, remembrance, βίου δὲ τοῦ παρόντος οὐ μνείαν ἔχεις [Refs 5th c.BC+]; κατά γε τὴν ἐμὴν μ. uncertain in [Refs 2nd c.AD+]; μνείας χάριν, frequently in late epitaphs, [Refs]
