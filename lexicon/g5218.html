ὑπακοή

hypakoḗ — G5218

Transliteration: hypakoḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5218

Derivation: from G5219 (ὑπακούω);

Definition

attentive hearkening, i.e. (by implication) compliance or submission

KJV Translation

obedience, (make) obedient, obey(-ing)

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ὑπᾰκοή, ἡ,
(ὑπακούω) obedience, [NT+6th c.AD+]; answer to prayer, [LXX]
