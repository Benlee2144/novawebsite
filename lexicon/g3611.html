οἰκέω

oikéō — G3611

Transliteration: oikéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3611

Derivation: from G3624 (οἶκος);

Definition

to occupy a house, i.e. reside (figuratively, inhabit, remain, inhere); by implication, to cohabit

KJV Translation

dwell
