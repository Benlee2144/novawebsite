κυκλόω

kyklóō — G2944

Transliteration: kyklóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2944

Derivation: from the same as G2945 (κύκλῳ);

Definition

to encircle, i.e. surround

KJV Translation

compass (about), come (stand) round about
