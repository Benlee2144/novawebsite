κατασκάπτω

kataskáptō — G2679

Transliteration: kataskáptō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2679

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G4626 (σκάπτω);

Definition

to undermine, i.e. (by implication) destroy

KJV Translation

dig down, ruin
