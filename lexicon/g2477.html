ἱστορέω

historéō — G2477

Transliteration: historéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2477

Derivation: from a derivative of G1492 (εἴδω);

Definition

to be knowing (learned), i.e. (by implication) to visit for information (interview)
