Τρωάς

Trōás — G5174

Transliteration: Trōás

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5174

Derivation: from (a Trojan);

Definition

the Troad (or plain of Troy), i.e. Troas, a place in Asia Minor

KJV Translation

Troas

__II γῆ Τρῳάς the Troad, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ἡ Τρωάς alone, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
