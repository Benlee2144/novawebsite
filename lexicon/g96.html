ἀδόκιμος

adókimos — G96

Transliteration: adókimos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G96

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and G1384 (δόκιμος);

Definition

unapproved, i.e. rejected; by implication, worthless (literally or morally)

KJV Translation

castaway, rejected, reprobate
