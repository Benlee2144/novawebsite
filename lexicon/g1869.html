ἐπαίρω

epaírō — G1869

Transliteration: epaírō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1869

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G142 (αἴρω);

Definition

to raise up (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

exalt self, poise (lift, take) up
