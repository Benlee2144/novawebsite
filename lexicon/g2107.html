εὐδοκία

eudokía — G2107

Transliteration: eudokía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2107

Derivation: from a presumed compound of G2095 (εὖ) and the base of G1380 (δοκέω);

Definition

satisfaction, i.e. (subjectively) delight, or (objectively) kindness, wish, purpose

KJV Translation

desire, good pleasure (will), X seem good
