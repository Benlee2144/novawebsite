Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1465

ἐγκόπτω

enkóptō — G1465

Transliteration: enkóptō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1465

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and G2875 (κόπτω);

Definition

to cut into, i.e. (figuratively) impede, detain

KJV Translation

hinder, be tedious unto
