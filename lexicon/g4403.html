πρύμνα

prýmna — G4403

Transliteration: prýmna

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4403

Derivation: feminine of (hindmost);

Definition

the stern of a ship

KJV Translation

hinder part, stern

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

πρύμνα, -ης, ἡ
prop. fem. of adj. πρύμνο, -η, -ον, (sc. ναῦς), the hindmost part of a ship, the stern: Mrk.4:38, Act.27:29; opposite to πρῷρα, Act.27:41.†
(AS)
