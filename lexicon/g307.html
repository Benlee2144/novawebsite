ἀναβιβάζω

anabibázō — G307

Transliteration: anabibázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G307

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and a derivative of the base of G939 (βάσις);

Definition

to cause to go up, i.e. haul (a net)

KJV Translation

draw
