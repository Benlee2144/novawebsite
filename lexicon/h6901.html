Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H6901

קָבַל

qâbal — H6901

Transliteration: qâbal

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6901

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to admit, i.e. take (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

choose, (take) hold, receive, (under-) take.

Appears In (5 verses)

1 Chronicles 21:11Esther 4:4Exodus 26:5Exodus 36:12Job 2:10

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

1) to take, receive, be before
1a) (Piel)
1a1) to take, choose, receive
1a2) to accept
1a3) to accept, assume
1b) (Hiphil)
1b1) to show oppositeness
1b2) to correspond, receive one to the other
Aramaic equivalent: qe.val (קַבֵּל "to receive" H6902)
