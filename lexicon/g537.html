ἅπας

hápas — G537

Transliteration: hápas

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G537

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a particle of union) and G3956 (πᾶς);

Definition

absolutely all or (singular) every one

KJV Translation

all (things), every (one), whole

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

ἅπας, -ασα, -αν
(strengthened form of πᾶς, see: ἅ-),
all, the whole, altogether: bef. subst. with art., as Luk.3:21; or after, as Mrk.16:15]; absol., in masc., as Luk.5:26; in neut., as Act.2:44; ἅ. οὗτοι, Act.2:7 (LT); ἅ. ὑμεῖς, Gal.3:28 (TTr.). Most frequently in Lk, Ac (see MM, VGT, see word)
(AS)
