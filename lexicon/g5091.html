τιμάω

timáō — G5091

Transliteration: timáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5091

Derivation: from G5093 (τίμιος);

Definition

to prize, i.e. fix a valuation upon; by implication, to revere

KJV Translation

honour, value
