ἀδυνατέω

adynatéō — G101

Transliteration: adynatéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G101

Derivation: from G102 (ἀδύνατος);

Definition

to be unable, i.e. (passively) impossible

KJV Translation

be impossible

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀδῠνᾰτ-έω,
of persons, to be ἀδύνατος, lack strength, [Refs 5th c.BC+]: with infinitive, to be unable to do, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II of things, to be impossible, [LXX+1st c.BC+]
