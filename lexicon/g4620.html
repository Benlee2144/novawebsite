Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4620

σιτόμετρον

sitómetron — G4620

Transliteration: sitómetron

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4620

Derivation: from G4621 (σῖτος) and G3358 (μέτρον);

Definition

a grain-measure, i.e. (by implication) ration (allowance of food)

KJV Translation

portion of meat

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

σῑτομετρ-ία, ἡ,
measured allowance of corn, rations, [Refs 3rd c.BC+], Mélanges Glotz [Refs 2nd c.BC+]: so σῑτομέτρ-ιον, τό, [NT]; ἔπαρχος -μετρίου δήμου Ῥωμαίων, = Latin praefectus annonae, [Refs]; σῑτομέτρ-μετρον, τό, [Refs 1st c.AD+]

Appears In (1 verse)

Luke 12:42
