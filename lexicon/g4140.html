πλησμονή

plēsmonḗ — G4140

Transliteration: plēsmonḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4140

Derivation: from a presumed derivative of G4130 (πλήθω);

Definition

a filling up, i.e. (figuratively) gratification

KJV Translation

satisfying

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

πλησμον-ή, ἡ,
a being filled, satiety, opposed to ἔνδεια, κένωσις, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; especially with food, repletion, surfeit, [LXX+5th c.BC+]
__II abundance, [LXX]

Appears In (1 verse)

Colossians 2:23
