ἀσθενέω

asthenéō — G770

Transliteration: asthenéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G770

Derivation: from G772 (ἀσθενής);

Definition

to be feeble (in any sense)

KJV Translation

be diseased, impotent folk (man), (be) sick, (be, be made) weak
