Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G5339

φείδομαι

pheídomai — G5339

Transliteration: pheídomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5339

Derivation: of uncertain affinity;

Definition

to be chary of, i.e. (subjectively) to abstain or (objectively) to treat leniently

KJV Translation

forbear, spare

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

φείδομαι,
