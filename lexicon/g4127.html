πληγή

plēgḗ — G4127

Transliteration: plēgḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4127

Derivation: from G4141 (πλήσσω);

Definition

a stroke; by implication, a wound; figuratively, a calamity

KJV Translation

plague, stripe, wound(-ed)
