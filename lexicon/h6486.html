פְּקֻדָּה

pᵉquddâh — H6486

Transliteration: pᵉquddâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6486

Derivation: feminine passive participle of H6485 (פָּקַד);

Definition

visitation (in many senses, chiefly official)

KJV Translation

account, (that have the) charge, custody, that which...laid up, numbers, office(-r), ordering, oversight, [phrase] prison, reckoning, visitation.
