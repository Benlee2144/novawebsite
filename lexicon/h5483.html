סוּס

çûwç — H5483

Transliteration: çûwç

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5483

Derivation: or סֻס; from an unused root meaning to skip (properly, for joy);

Definition

a horse (as leaping); also a swallow (from its rapid flight)

KJV Translation

crane, horse (-back, -hoof). Compare H6571 (פָּרָשׁ).
