Αἰθίοψ

Aithíops — G128

Transliteration: Aithíops

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G128

Derivation: from (to scorch) and (the face, from G3700 (ὀπτάνομαι));

Definition

an Æthiopian (as a blackamoor)

KJV Translation

Ethiopian
