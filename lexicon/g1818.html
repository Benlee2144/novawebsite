Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1818

ἐξαπατάω

exapatáō — G1818

Transliteration: exapatáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1818

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G538 (ἀπατάω);

Definition

to seduce wholly

KJV Translation

beguile, deceive

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐξᾰπᾰτάω,
