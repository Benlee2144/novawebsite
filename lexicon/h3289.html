יָעַץ

yâʻats — H3289

Transliteration: yâʻats

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3289

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to advise; reflexively, to deliberate or resolve

KJV Translation

advertise, take advise, advise (well), consult, (give, take) counsel(-lor), determine, devise, guide, purpose.
