Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1268

διανέμω

dianémō — G1268

Transliteration: dianémō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1268

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and the base of G3551 (νόμος);

Definition

to distribute, i.e. (of information) to disseminate

KJV Translation

spread
