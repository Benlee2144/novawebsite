ἱππικόν

hippikón — G2461

Transliteration: hippikón

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2461

Derivation: neuter of a derivative of G2462 (ἵππος);

Definition

the cavalry force

KJV Translation

horse(-men)

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

ἱππικός, -ή, -όν
(ἵππος), [in LXX: 1Ma.15:33, 3Ma.1:1 * ;]
of a horse or of horsemen, equestrian; as subst., τὸ ἱ (sc. στράτευμα), cavalry: Heb.9:16.†
(AS)
