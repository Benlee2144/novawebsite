αὔριον

aúrion — G839

Transliteration: aúrion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G839

Derivation: from a derivative of the same as G109 (ἀήρ) (meaning a breeze, i.e. the morning air);

Definition

properly, fresh, i.e. (adverb with ellipsis of G2250 (ἡμέρα)) to-morrow

KJV Translation

(to-)morrow, next day
