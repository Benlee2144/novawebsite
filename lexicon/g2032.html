ἐπουράνιος

epouránios — G2032

Transliteration: epouránios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2032

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G3772 (οὐρανός);

Definition

above the sky

KJV Translation

celestial, (in) heaven(-ly), high
