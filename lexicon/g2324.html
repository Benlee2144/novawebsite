θεράπων

therápōn — G2324

Transliteration: therápōn

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2324

Derivation: apparently a participle from an otherwise obsolete derivative of the base of G2330 (θέρος);

Definition

a menial attendant (as if cherishing)

KJV Translation

servant
