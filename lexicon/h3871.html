לוּחַ

lûwach — H3871

Transliteration: lûwach

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3871

Derivation: or לֻחַ; from a primitive root;

Definition

probably meaning to glisten; a tablet (as polished), of stone, wood or metal

KJV Translation

board, plate, table.
