προσποιέομαι

prospoiéomai — G4364

Transliteration: prospoiéomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4364

Derivation: middle voice from G4314 (πρός) and G4160 (ποιέω);

Definition

to do forward for oneself, i.e. pretend (as if about to do a thing)

KJV Translation

make as though
