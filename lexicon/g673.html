ἀποχωρίζω

apochōrízō — G673

Transliteration: apochōrízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G673

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G5563 (χωρίζω);

Definition

to rend apart; reflexively, to separate

KJV Translation

depart (asunder)
