κατενώπιον

katenṓpion — G2714

Transliteration: katenṓpion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2714

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G1799 (ἐνώπιον);

Definition

directly in front of

KJV Translation

before (the presence of), in the sight of
