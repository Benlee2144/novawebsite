ἱλαστήριον

hilastḗrion — G2435

Transliteration: hilastḗrion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2435

Derivation: neuter of a derivative of G2433 (ἱλάσκομαι);

Definition

an expiatory (place or thing), i.e. (concretely) an atoning victim, or (specially) the lid of the Ark (in the Temple)

KJV Translation

mercyseat, propitiation
