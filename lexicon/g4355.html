προσλαμβάνω

proslambánō — G4355

Transliteration: proslambánō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4355

Derivation: from G4314 (πρός) and G2983 (λαμβάνω);

Definition

to take to oneself, i.e. use (food), lead (aside), admit (to friendship or hospitality)

KJV Translation

receive, take (unto)
