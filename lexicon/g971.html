Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G971

βιάζω

biázō — G971

Transliteration: biázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G971

Derivation: from G979 (βίος);

Definition

to force, i.e. (reflexively) to crowd oneself (into), or (passively) to be seized

KJV Translation

press, suffer violence

βιάζω (βία), and depon. -ομαι,
[in LXX for פּרץ, פּצר, etc. ;]
to force, constrain, rare in active (poet, and late prose), but found in cl. in pass., and so perh. βιάζεται, suffereth violence, Mat.11:12, EV (but see infr.), whether
__(a) in good sense, of disciples (Thayer, al.), or
