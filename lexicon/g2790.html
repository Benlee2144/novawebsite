Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2790

κιθαρῳδός

kitharōidós — G2790

Transliteration: kitharōidós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2790

Derivation: from G2788 (κιθάρα) and a derivative of the same as G5603 (ᾠδή);

Definition

a lyre-singer(-player), i.e. harpist

KJV Translation

harper

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

(κιθάρα, ἀοιδός) one who plays and sings to the cithara, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II a fish, found in the Red Sea, with body striped like the strings of a lyre, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
