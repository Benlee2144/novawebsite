נְכוֹ

Nᵉkôw — H5224

Transliteration: Nᵉkôw

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5224

Derivation: probably of Egyptian origin;

Definition

Neko, an Egyptian king

KJV Translation

Necho. Compare H6549 (פַּרְעֹה נְכֹה).

Appears In (8 verses)

2 Chronicles 35:202 Chronicles 35:222 Chronicles 36:42 Kings 23:292 Kings 23:332 Kings 23:342 Kings 23:35Jeremiah 46:2

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

A man of the Egyptians living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at 2Ki.23.29; also called Pharaoh at 2Ki.23.35 etc.;
Combined with ne.kho (נְכוֹ "Neco" H5224)
§ Necho = "lame"
the Pharaoh of Egypt in the time of king Josiah of Judah
