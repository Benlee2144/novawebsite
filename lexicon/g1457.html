ἐγκαινίζω

enkainízō — G1457

Transliteration: enkainízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1457

Derivation: from G1456 (ἐγκαίνια);

Definition

to renew, i.e. inaugurate

KJV Translation

consecrate, dedicate
