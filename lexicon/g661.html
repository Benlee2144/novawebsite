ἀποτίνω

apotínō — G661

Transliteration: apotínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G661

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G5099 (τίνω);

Definition

to pay in full

KJV Translation

repay
