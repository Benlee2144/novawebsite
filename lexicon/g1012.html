βουλή

boulḗ — G1012

Transliteration: boulḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1012

Derivation: from G1014 (βούλομαι);

Definition

volition, i.e. (objectively) advice, or (by implication) purpose

KJV Translation

+ advise, counsel, will
