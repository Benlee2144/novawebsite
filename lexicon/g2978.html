λαῖλαψ

laîlaps — G2978

Transliteration: laîlaps

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2978

Derivation: of uncertain derivation;

Definition

a whirlwind (squall)

KJV Translation

storm, tempest

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

λαῖλαψ, ᾰπος, ἡ,
furious storm, hurricane, βαῖνον ἐρεμνῇ λαίλαπι ἶσοι [LXX+8th c.BC+], a whirlwind sweeping upwards: metaphorically, ἔτλης λαίλαπα δυσμενέων [Refs 1st c.BC+]—Not found in early Prose, but common later,[NT+1st c.AD+]:—a form λαιλαπετός, ὁ, occurs in [Refs 8th c.BC+]
