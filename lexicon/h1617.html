Gêrâʼ — H1617

Transliteration: Gêrâʼ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1617

Derivation: perhaps from H1626 (גֵּרָה); a grain;

Definition

Gera, the name of six Israelites

KJV Translation

Gera.

Appears In (6 verses)

1 Kings 2:82 Samuel 16:52 Samuel 19:162 Samuel 19:18Genesis 46:21Judges 3:15

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

A man of the tribe of Benjamin living before Israel's Monarchy, only mentioned at 1Ch.8.7;
son of: Ehud (H0164);
brother of: Naaman (H5283J), Ahijah (H0281K), Uzza (H5798H) and Ahihud (H0284); also called Heglam (KJV: "removed"; NIV: "deported") at 1Ch.8.7;
Also named: ga.lah (הֶגְלָם "Heglam" H1540)
§ Gera = "a grain"
1) a son of Benjamin
2) a son of Bela and grandson of Benjamin
3) any member of the Gera family of the Benjamite tribe
