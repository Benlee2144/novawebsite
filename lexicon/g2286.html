θανάσιμος

thanásimos — G2286

Transliteration: thanásimos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2286

Derivation: from G2288 (θάνατος);

Definition

fatal, i.e. poisonous

KJV Translation

deadly
