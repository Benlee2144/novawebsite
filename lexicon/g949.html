βέβαιος

bébaios — G949

Transliteration: bébaios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G949

Derivation: from the base of G939 (βάσις) (through the idea of basality);

Definition

stable (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

firm, of force, stedfast, sure
