ἐξανίστημι

exanístēmi — G1817

Transliteration: exanístēmi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1817

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G450 (ἀνίστημι);

Definition

objectively, to produce, i.e. (figuratively) beget; subjectively, to arise, i.e. (figuratively) object

KJV Translation

raise (rise) up
