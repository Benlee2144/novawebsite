συνδρομή

syndromḗ — G4890

Transliteration: syndromḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4890

Derivation: from (the alternate of) G4936 (συντρέχω);

Definition

a running together, i.e. (riotous) concourse

KJV Translation

run together

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

__2 of things, στενὴ πορθμοῦ σ.
