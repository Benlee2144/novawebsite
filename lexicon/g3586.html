ξύλον

xýlon — G3586

Transliteration: xýlon

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3586

Derivation: from another form of the base of G3582 (ξέστης);

Definition

timber (as fuel or material); by implication, a stick, club or tree or other wooden article or substance

KJV Translation

staff, stocks, tree, wood

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
