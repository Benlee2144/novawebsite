παρέρχομαι

parérchomai — G3928

Transliteration: parérchomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3928

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and G2064 (ἔρχομαι);

Definition

to come near or aside, i.e. to approach (arrive), go by (or away), (figuratively) perish or neglect, (causative) avert

KJV Translation

come (forth), go, pass (away, by, over), past, transgress
