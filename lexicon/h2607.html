חֲנָנִי

Chănânîy — H2607

Transliteration: Chănânîy

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2607

Derivation: from H2603 (חָנַן); gracious;

Definition

Chanani, the name of six Israelites

KJV Translation

Hanani.

Appears In (10 verses)

1 Chronicles 25:41 Kings 16:11 Kings 16:72 Chronicles 16:72 Chronicles 19:22 Chronicles 20:34Ezra 10:20Nehemiah 1:2Nehemiah 12:36Nehemiah 7:2

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at Neh.12.36;
son of: Jonathan (H3129K);
brother of: Zechariah (H2148Z), Shemaiah (H8098V), Azarel (H5832J), Milalai (H4450), Gilalai (H1562), Maai (H4597), Nethanel (H5417P) and Judah (H3063K)
§ Hanani = "gracious"
1) one of the sons of Heman, a chief musician of David, and head of the 18th course of the service
2) a seer who rebuked Asa, king of Judah, and was imprisoned
2a) maybe, father of Jehu the seer who testified against Baasha
3) one of the priests who in the time of Ezra took a foreign wife
4) brother of Nehemiah whom Nehemiah appointed governor of Jerusalem
5) another priest who was the chief musician under Nehemiah
