πάροδος

párodos — G3938

Transliteration: párodos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3938

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and G3598 (ὁδός);

Definition

a by-road, i.e. (actively) a route

KJV Translation

way
