ἀποτρέπω

apotrépō — G665

Transliteration: apotrépō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G665

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and the base of G5157 (τροπή);

Definition

to deflect, i.e. (reflexively) avoid

KJV Translation

turn away

[in LXX: Sir.20:29 48:18, 3Ma.1:23, 4Ma.1:33 4Mac 16:12 * ;]
