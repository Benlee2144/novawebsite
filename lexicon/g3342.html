μεταξύ

metaxý — G3342

Transliteration: metaxý

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3342

Derivation: from G3326 (μετά) and a form of G4862 (σύν);

Definition

betwixt (of place or person); (of time) as adjective, intervening, or (by implication) adjoining

KJV Translation

between, mean while, next

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
