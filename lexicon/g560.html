ἀπελπίζω

apelpízō — G560

Transliteration: apelpízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G560

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G1679 (ἐλπίζω);

Definition

to hope out, i.e. fully expect

KJV Translation

hope for again

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀπελπ-ίζω,
Attic dialect future -ιῶ[Refs 1st c.BC+]perfect -ήλπικα:—despair of, τῆς πόλεως τὴν σωτηρίαν[Refs 4th c.BC+]:—passive, to be given up in despair, τὰ πράγματα[Refs]; of persons, to be despaired of, οἱ ἀπηλπισμένοι[LXX+2nd c.BC+] (ἀφηλπ-).
__2 ἀ. τινός despair of,[Refs 2nd c.BC+] οὐκ ἀ. τινός to be confident of, [Refs 1st c.BC+]
__3 absolutely, hope that a thing will not happen, [Refs 3rd c.AD+]
__II causal, drive to despair, τινά[Refs 1st c.AD+]
__III hope to receive back, μηδὲν (variant{μηδένα}) ἀπελπίζοντες[NT]
