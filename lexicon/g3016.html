Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3016

λεπτόν

leptón — G3016

Transliteration: leptón

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3016

Derivation: neuter of a derivative of the same as G3013 (λεπίς);

Definition

something scaled (light), i.e. a small coin

KJV Translation

mite
