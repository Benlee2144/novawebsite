κάμνω

kámnō — G2577

Transliteration: kámnō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2577

Derivation: apparently a primary verb;

Definition

properly, to toil, i.e. (by implication) to tire (figuratively, faint, sicken)

KJV Translation

faint, sick, be wearied
