Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4838

συμπαραλαμβάνω

symparalambánō — G4838

Transliteration: symparalambánō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4838

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G3880 (παραλαμβάνω);

Definition

to take along in company

KJV Translation

take with

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
