γνώμη

gnṓmē — G1106

Transliteration: gnṓmē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1106

Derivation: from G1097 (γινώσκω);

Definition

cognition, i.e. (subjectively) opinion, or (objectively) resolve (counsel, consent, etc.)

KJV Translation

advice, + agree, judgment, mind, purpose, will
