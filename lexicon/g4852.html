σύμφημι

sýmphēmi — G4852

Transliteration: sýmphēmi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4852

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G5346 (φημί);

Definition

to say jointly, i.e. assent to

KJV Translation

consent unto
