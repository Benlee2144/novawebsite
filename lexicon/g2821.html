κλῆσις

klēsis — G2821

Transliteration: klēsis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2821

Derivation: from a shorter form of G2564 (καλέω);

Definition

an invitation (figuratively)

KJV Translation

calling
