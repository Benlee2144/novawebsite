ἀχρειόω

achreióō — G889

Transliteration: achreióō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G889

Derivation: from G888 (ἀχρεῖος);

Definition

to render useless, i.e. spoil

KJV Translation

become unprofitable

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀχρει-όω
or ἀχρει-χρεόω [Refs]perfect ἠχρείωκα:— render useless, disable, damage, [LXX+4th c.BC+] [same place]:—passive, ἀχρεῶσθαι[Refs 3rd c.BC+]; to be corrupted, [LXX+1st c.BC+]

Appears In (1 verse)

Romans 3:12
