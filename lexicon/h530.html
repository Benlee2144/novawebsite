אֱמוּנָה

ʼĕmûwnâh — H530

Transliteration: ʼĕmûwnâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H530

Derivation: or (shortened) אֱמֻנָה; feminine of H529 (אֵמוּן);

Definition

literally firmness; figuratively security; morally fidelity

KJV Translation

faith(-ful, -ly, -ness, (man)), set office, stability, steady, truly, truth, verily.
