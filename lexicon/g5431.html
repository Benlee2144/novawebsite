Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G5431

φροντίζω

phrontízō — G5431

Transliteration: phrontízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5431

Derivation: from a derivative of G5424 (φρήν);

Definition

to exercise thought, i.e. be anxious

KJV Translation

be careful
