παραπλήσιον

paraplḗsion — G3897

Transliteration: paraplḗsion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3897

Derivation: neuter of a compound of G3844 (παρά) and the base of G4139 (πλησίον) (as adverb);

Definition

close by, i.e. (figuratively) almost

KJV Translation

nigh unto
