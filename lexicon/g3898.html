παραπλησίως

paraplēsíōs — G3898

Transliteration: paraplēsíōs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3898

Derivation: adverb from the same as G3897 (παραπλήσιον);

Definition

in a manner near by, i.e. (figuratively) similarly

KJV Translation

likewise

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

Included with: παραπλήσιος, α, ον
