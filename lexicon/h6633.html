צָבָא

tsâbâʼ — H6633

Transliteration: tsâbâʼ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6633

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to mass (an army or servants)

KJV Translation

assemble, fight, perform, muster, wait upon, war.

Appears In (3 verses)

Exodus 38:8Isaiah 29:7Zechariah 14:12

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

1) to go forth, wage war, fight, serve
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to wage war, fight, go forth to war
1a2) to serve (at sacred tent)
1b) (Hiphil) to cause to go forth, muster
