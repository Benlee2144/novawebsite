Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G762

ἄσβεστος

ásbestos — G762

Transliteration: ásbestos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G762

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G4570 (σβέννυμι);

Definition

not extinguished, i.e. (by implication) perpetual

KJV Translation

not to be quenched, unquenchable

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἄσβεστος, ον,
