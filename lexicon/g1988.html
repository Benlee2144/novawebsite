ἐπιστάτης

epistátēs — G1988

Transliteration: epistátēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1988

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and a presumed derivative of G2476 (ἵστημι);

Definition

an appointee over, i.e. commander (teacher)

KJV Translation

master
