κυκλόθεν

kyklóthen — G2943

Transliteration: kyklóthen

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2943

Derivation: adverb from the same as G2945 (κύκλῳ);

Definition

from the circle, i.e. all around

KJV Translation

(round) about
