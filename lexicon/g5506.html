χιλίαρχος

Transliteration: chilíarchos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5506

Derivation: from G5507 (χίλιοι) and G757 (ἄρχω);

Definition

the commander of a thousand soldiers (\

KJV Translation
