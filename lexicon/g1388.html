δόλος

dólos — G1388

Transliteration: dólos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1388

Derivation: from an obsolete primary verb, (probably meaning to decoy;

Definition

compare G1185 (δελεάζω)); a trick (bait), i.e. (figuratively) wile

KJV Translation

craft, deceit, guile, subtilty
