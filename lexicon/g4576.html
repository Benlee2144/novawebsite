Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4576

σέβομαι

sébomai — G4576

Transliteration: sébomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4576

Derivation: middle voice of an apparently primary verb;

Definition

to revere, i.e. adore

KJV Translation

devout, religious, worship
