οἰκέτης

oikétēs — G3610

Transliteration: oikétēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3610

Derivation: from G3611 (οἰκέω);

Definition

a fellow resident, i.e. menial domestic

KJV Translation

(household) servant

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

οἰκέτ-ης, ου, ὁ,
(οἶκος) household slave, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 οἱ οἰκέται also, ={οἰκετεία}, household, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; δοῦλος μεῖζον οἰκέτου φρονῶν [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__II as epithet of Apollo, ἱερέως.. Καρνείου Βοικέτα [Β = ϝ] [Refs]
