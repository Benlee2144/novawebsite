πωλέω

pōléō — G4453

Transliteration: pōléō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4453

Derivation: probably ultimately from (to be busy, to trade);

Definition

to barter (as a pedlar), i.e. to sell

KJV Translation

sell, whatever is sold
