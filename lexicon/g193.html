ἀκρατής

akratḗs — G193

Transliteration: akratḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G193

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and G2904 (κράτος);

Definition

powerless, i.e. without self-control

KJV Translation

incontinent

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
