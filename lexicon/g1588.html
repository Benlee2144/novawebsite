ἐκλεκτός

eklektós — G1588

Transliteration: eklektós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1588

Derivation: from G1586 (ἐκλέγομαι);

Definition

select; by implication, favorite

KJV Translation

chosen, elect

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐκλεκτ-ός, ή, όν,
picked out, select, [Refs 6th c.BC+]; ἐ. δικασταί,=Latin iudices selecti, [Refs 2nd c.AD+] adverb -τῶς interpolated in Suid. see at {ἐπίλεκτος}.
__2 choice, pure, σμύρνη [LXX+3rd c.BC+]; ἐκλεκτόν,= corn, [LXX+2nd c.AD+]
__II chosen of God, elect, [LXX+NT]
