Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4784

συγκατατίθεμαι

synkatatíthemai — G4784

Transliteration: synkatatíthemai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4784

Derivation: middle from G4862 (σύν) and G2698 (κατατίθημι);

Definition

to deposit (one's vote or opinion) in company with, i.e. (figuratively) to accord with

KJV Translation

consent
