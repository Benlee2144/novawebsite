κοράσιον

korásion — G2877

Transliteration: korásion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2877

Derivation: neuter of a presumed derivative of (a maiden);

Definition

a (little) girl

KJV Translation

damsel, maid

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

κορᾱσ-ιον, τό,
in later Gr., diminutive of κόρη, little girl, maiden, [NT+4th c.BC+]
