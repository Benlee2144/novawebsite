Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4624

σκανδαλίζω

skandalízō — G4624

Transliteration: skandalízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4624

Derivation: from G4625 (σκάνδαλον);

Definition

to entrap, i.e. trip up (figuratively, stumble (transitively) or entice to sin, apostasy or displeasure)

KJV Translation

(make to) offend

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

σκανδαλ-ίζω,
cause to stumble, give offence or scandal to any one, τινα [NT]:— passive, to be made to stumble, take offence, [Refs], etc.; ἔν τινι [LXX+NT]
