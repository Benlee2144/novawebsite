Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4506

ῥύομαι

rhýomai — G4506

Transliteration: rhýomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4506

Derivation: middle voice of an obsolete verb, akin to G4482 (ῥέω) (through the idea of a current;

Definition

compare G4511 (ῥύσις)); to rush or draw (for oneself), i.e. rescue

KJV Translation

deliver(-er)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ῥύομαι,
see at {ἐρύω} (B). ῥύπα, τά, irregular plural of ῥύπος (which see).
