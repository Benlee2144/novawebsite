ἐξέρχομαι

exérchomai — G1831

Transliteration: exérchomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1831

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G2064 (ἔρχομαι);

Definition

to issue (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

come (forth, out), depart (out of), escape, get out, go (abroad, away, forth, out, thence), proceed (forth), spread abroad

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
