Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4934

συντίθεμαι

syntíthemai — G4934

Transliteration: syntíthemai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4934

Derivation: middle voice from G4862 (σύν) and G5087 (τίθημι);

Definition

to place jointly, i.e. (figuratively) to consent (bargain, stipulate), concur

KJV Translation

agree, assent, covenant
