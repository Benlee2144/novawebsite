ζωή

zōḗ — G2222

Transliteration: zōḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2222

Derivation: from G2198 (ζάω);

Definition

life (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

life(-time)

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

ζωή, -ῆς, ἡ (ζάω),
[in LXX chiefly for חַיִּים ;]

__2.
