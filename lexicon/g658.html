ἀποτελέω

apoteléō — G658

Transliteration: apoteléō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G658

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G5055 (τελέω);

Definition

to complete entirely, i.e. consummate

KJV Translation

finish
