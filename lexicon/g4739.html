Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4739

στήκω

stḗkō — G4739

Transliteration: stḗkō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4739

Derivation: from the perfect tense of G2476 (ἵστημι);

Definition

to be stationary, i.e. (figuratively) to persevere

KJV Translation

stand (fast)

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

στήκω,
late present formed from ἕστηκα (perfect of ἵστημι), stand, [LXX+NT]; participle, παρὼν καὶ στήγων (sic) [Refs 4th c.AD+]; so ἑστήκω, [Refs 3rd c.BC+].
