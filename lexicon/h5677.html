עֵבֵר

ʻÊbêr — H5677

Transliteration: ʻÊbêr

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5677

Derivation: the same as H5676 (עֵבֶר);

Definition

Eber, the name of two patriarchs and four Israelites

KJV Translation

Eber, Heber.

Appears In (11 verses)

1 Chronicles 1:181 Chronicles 1:251 Chronicles 8:12Genesis 10:21Genesis 10:24Genesis 11:14Genesis 11:15Genesis 11:16Genesis 11:17Nehemiah 12:20Numbers 24:24

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at Neh.12.20
§ Eber or Heber = "the region beyond"
1) son of Salah, great grandson of Shem, father of Peleg and Joktan
2) a Gadite chief
3) a Benjamite, son of Elpaal and descendant of Sharahaim
4) a Benjamite, son of Shashak
5) a priest in the days of Joiakim the son of Jeshua
