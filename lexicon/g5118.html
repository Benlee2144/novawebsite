τοσοῦτος

tosoûtos — G5118

Transliteration: tosoûtos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5118

Derivation: from (so much;

Definition

apparently from G3588 (ὁ) and G3739 (ὅς)) and G3778 (οὗτος) (including its variations); so vast as this, i.e. such (in quantity, amount, number of space)

KJV Translation

as large, so great (long, many, much), these many
