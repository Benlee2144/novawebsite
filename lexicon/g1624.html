ἐκτρέπω

ektrépō — G1624

Transliteration: ektrépō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1624

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and the base of G5157 (τροπή);

Definition

to deflect, i.e. turn away (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

avoid, turn (aside, out of the way)
