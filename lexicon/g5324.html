φαντάζω

phantázō — G5324

Transliteration: phantázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5324

Derivation: from a derivative of G5316 (φαίνω);

Definition

to make apparent, i.e. (passively) to appear (neuter participle as noun, a spectacle)

KJV Translation

sight
