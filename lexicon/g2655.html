καταναρκάω

katanarkáō — G2655

Transliteration: katanarkáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2655

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and (to be numb);

Definition

to grow utterly torpid, i.e. (by implication) slothful (figuratively, expensive)

KJV Translation

be burdensome (chargeable)

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

καταναρκάομαι,
Ionic dialect καταναρκ-έομαι, passive, grow quite numb, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; to be reduced to a torpid condition, [Refs 1st c.BC+]
__II active, καταναρκᾶν τινος to be slothful towards, press heavily upon.. , [NT]

__1. to cause to grow numb.
