Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G616

ἀποκυέω

apokyéō — G616

Transliteration: apokyéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G616

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and the base of G2949 (κῦμα);

Definition

to breed forth, i.e. (by transference) to generate (figuratively)

KJV Translation

beget, produce

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀποκῠ-έω,
bear young, bring forth, with accusative, [NT+4th c.BC+]:—passive, of the child, [Refs 1st c.AD+] (passive participle ἀποκυόμενα[Refs 1st c.AD+]
