γένεσις

génesis — G1078

Transliteration: génesis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1078

Derivation: from the same as G1074 (γενεά);

Definition

nativity; figuratively, nature

KJV Translation

generation, nature(-ral)
