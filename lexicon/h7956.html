שֵׁלָה

Shêlâh — H7956

Transliteration: Shêlâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7956

Derivation: the same as H7596 (שְׁאֵלָה) (shortened); request;

Definition

Shelah, the name of a postdiluvian patriarch and of an Israelite

KJV Translation

Shelah.
