Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4843

συμπεριλαμβάνω

symperilambánō — G4843

Transliteration: symperilambánō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4843

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and a compound of G4012 (περί) and G2983 (λαμβάνω);

Definition

to take by enclosing altogether, i.e. earnestly throw the arms about one

KJV Translation

embrace
