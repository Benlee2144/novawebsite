συντρέχω

syntréchō — G4936

Transliteration: syntréchō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4936

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G5143 (τρέχω) (including its alternate);

Definition

to rush together (hastily assemble) or headlong (figuratively)

KJV Translation

run (together, with)
