μεταδίδωμι

metadídōmi — G3330

Transliteration: metadídōmi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3330

Derivation: from G3326 (μετά) and G1325 (δίδωμι);

Definition

to give over, i.e. share

KJV Translation

give, impart

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

μεταδίδωμι [δῐ],
future μεταδια-δώσω, give part of, give a share, with genitive of things, τοῦ μεταδοῦν (poetry aorist 2 infinitive) [Refs 6th c.BC+]; γῆς (i.e. αὐτοῖσι) [Refs 5th c.BC+]; τῆς ἀρχῆς (i.e. αὐτοῖσι) [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 with accusative of the part given, μ. τὸ τριτημόριόν τινι [Refs 5th c.BC+]; μ. πυρούς distribute, [Refs]
__3 intransitive, μ. τινὶ ὑπέρ, περί τινος, communicate with one about.., [Refs 2nd c.BC+]; ὅτι.. [Refs 1st c.AD+]:—passive, to be communicated, transmitted, of notices, memoranda, etc., [Refs 2nd c.AD+]; of diseases, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
