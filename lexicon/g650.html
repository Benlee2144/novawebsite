ἀποστερέω

aposteréō — G650

Transliteration: aposteréō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G650

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and (to deprive);

Definition

to despoil

KJV Translation

defraud, destitute, kept back by fraud

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

ἀπο-στερέω, -ῶ
(στερέω, to rob), [in LXX: Exo.21:10 (גָּרַע), Deu.24:14 (עָשַׁק), Mal.3:5, Sir.4:1 29:6-7 31:21-22 * ;]
to defraud, deprive of, despoil (in cl. chiefly of the misappropriation of trust funds, Field, Notes, 33; cf. MM, see word): absol., Mrk.10:19, 1Co.6:8; with accusative of person(s), 1Co.7:5. Mid., endure deprivation: 1Co.6:7 (WM, § 38, 3; but see Bl., § 54, 5; M, Pr., 162); pass., ἀπεστερημένοι, bereft of: 1Ti.6:5.†
(AS)
