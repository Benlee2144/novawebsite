בָּזָה

bâzâh — H959

Transliteration: bâzâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H959

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to disesteem

KJV Translation

despise, disdain, contemn(-ptible), [phrase] think to scorn, vile person.
