Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3560

νουθετέω

nouthetéō — G3560

Transliteration: nouthetéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3560

Derivation: from the same as G3559 (νουθεσία);

Definition

to put in mind, i.e. (by implication) to caution or reprove gently

KJV Translation

admonish, warn

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
