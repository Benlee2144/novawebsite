Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2942

κυβερνήτης

kybernḗtēs — G2942

Transliteration: kybernḗtēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2942

Derivation: from the same as G2941 (κυβέρνησις);

Definition

helmsman, i.e. (by implication) captain

KJV Translation

(ship) master

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

κῠβερν-ήτης
(Aeolic dialect κυμερνήτης, which see), ου, ὁ, steersman, pilot, [Refs 8th c.BC+]; skipper of Nileboat, ναύκληρος καὶ κ. [Refs 3rd c.BC+]
__2 metaphorically, guide, governor, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; as an official title, [Refs 6th c.AD+]

Appears In (2 verses)

Acts 27:11Revelation 18:17
