Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H2986

יָבַל

yâbal — H2986

Transliteration: yâbal

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2986

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

properly, to flow; causatively, to bring (especially with pomp)

KJV Translation

bring (forth), carry, lead (forth).
