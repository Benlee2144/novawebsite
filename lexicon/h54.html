Transliteration: ʼEbyâthâr

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H54

Derivation: contracted from H1 (אָב) and H3498 (יָתַר); father of abundance (i.e. liberal);

Definition

Ebjathar, an Israelite

KJV Translation

Abiathar.

son of: Ahimelech (H0288);
