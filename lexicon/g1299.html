διατάσσω

diatássō — G1299

Transliteration: diatássō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1299

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G5021 (τάσσω);

Definition

to arrange thoroughly, i.e. (specially) institute, prescribe, etc.

KJV Translation

appoint, command, give, (set in) order, ordain
