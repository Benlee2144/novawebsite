ὑγιαίνω

hygiaínō — G5198

Transliteration: hygiaínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5198

Derivation: from G5199 (ὑγιής);

Definition

to have sound health, i.e. be well (in body); figuratively, to be uncorrupt (true in doctrine)

KJV Translation

be in health, (be safe and) sound, (be) whole(-some)
