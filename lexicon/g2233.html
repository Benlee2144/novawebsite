ἡγέομαι

hēgéomai — G2233

Transliteration: hēgéomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2233

Derivation: middle voice of a (presumed) strengthened form of G71 (ἄγω);

Definition

to lead, i.e. command (with official authority); figuratively, to deem, i.e. consider

KJV Translation

account, (be) chief, count, esteem, governor, judge, have the rule over, suppose, think
