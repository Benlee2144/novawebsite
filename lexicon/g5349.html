φθαρτός

phthartós — G5349

Transliteration: phthartós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5349

Derivation: from G5351 (φθείρω);

Definition

decayed, i.e. (by implication) perishable

KJV Translation

corruptible
