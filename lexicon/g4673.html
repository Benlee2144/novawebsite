Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4673

σορός

sorós — G4673

Transliteration: sorós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4673

Derivation: probably akin to the base of G4987 (σωρεύω);

Definition

a funereal receptacle (urn, coffin), i.e. (by analogy) a bier

KJV Translation

bier
