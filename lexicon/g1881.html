ἐπανίσταμαι

epanístamai — G1881

Transliteration: epanístamai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1881

Derivation: middle voice from G1909 (ἐπί) and G450 (ἀνίστημι);

Definition

to stand up on, i.e. (figuratively) to attack

KJV Translation

rise up against
