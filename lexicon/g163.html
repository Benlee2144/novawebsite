Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G163

αἰχμαλωτίζω

aichmalōtízō — G163

Transliteration: aichmalōtízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G163

Derivation: from G164 (αἰχμάλωτος);

Definition

to make captive

KJV Translation

lead away captive, bring into captivity

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
