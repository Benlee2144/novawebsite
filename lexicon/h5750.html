Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H5750

עוֹד

ʻôwd — H5750

Transliteration: ʻôwd

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5750

Derivation: or עֹד; from H5749 (עוּד);

Definition

properly, iteration or continuance; used only adverbially (with or without preposition), again, repeatedly, still, more

KJV Translation

again, [idiom] all life long, at all, besides, but, else, further(-more), henceforth, (any) longer, (any) more(-over), [idiom] once, since, (be) still, when, (good, the) while (having being), (as, because, whether, while) yet (within).
