גָּבַהּ

gâbahh — H1361

Transliteration: gâbahh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1361

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to soar, i.e. be lofty; figuratively, to be haughty

KJV Translation

exalt, be haughty, be (make) high(-er), lift up, mount up, be proud, raise up great height, upward.
