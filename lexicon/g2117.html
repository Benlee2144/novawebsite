εὐθύς

euthýs — G2117

Transliteration: euthýs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2117

Derivation: perhaps from G2095 (εὖ) and G5087 (τίθημι);

Definition

straight, i.e. (literally) level, or (figuratively) true; adverbially (of time) at once

KJV Translation

anon, by and by, forthwith, immediately, straightway
