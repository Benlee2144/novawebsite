Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1309

διαφεύγω

diapheúgō — G1309

Transliteration: diapheúgō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1309

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G5343 (φεύγω);

Definition

to flee through, i.e. escape

KJV Translation

escape

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

δια-φεύγω,
future -φεύξομαι[Refs 5th c.BC+]:—get away from, escape, τινά or τι, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; survive, [νόσημα] [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ἐκ τῆς Μήλου [Refs 5th c.BC+]; διαφεύγει δ᾽ οὐδὲ νῦν but it is not even now too late, [Refs 4th c.BC+]:—passive, διεφεύχθη ὁ κίνδυνος [Refs 1st c.AD+]
__2 escape one's notice or memory, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
