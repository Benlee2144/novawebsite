κατακαίω

katakaíō — G2618

Transliteration: katakaíō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2618

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G2545 (καίω);

Definition

to burn down (to the ground), i.e. consume wholly

KJV Translation

burn (up, utterly)
