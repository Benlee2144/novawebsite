Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1369

διχάζω

dicházō — G1369

Transliteration: dicházō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1369

Derivation: from a derivative of G1364 (δίς);

Definition

to make apart, i.e. sunder (figuratively, alienate)

KJV Translation

set at variance
