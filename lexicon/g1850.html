ἐξουσιάζω

exousiázō — G1850

Transliteration: exousiázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1850

Derivation: from G1849 (ἐξουσία);

Definition

to control

KJV Translation

exercise authority upon, bring under the (have) power of
