συγκεράννυμι

synkeránnymi — G4786

Transliteration: synkeránnymi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4786

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G2767 (κεράννυμι);

Definition

to commingle, i.e. (figuratively) to combine or assimilate

KJV Translation

mix with, temper together
