μέτοχος

métochos — G3353

Transliteration: métochos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3353

Derivation: from G3348 (μετέχω);

Definition

participant, i.e. (as noun) a sharer; by implication, an associate

KJV Translation

fellow, partaker, partner

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

μέτοχος, ον,
sharing in, partaking of, with genitive, [τῆς συμφορῆς] τὸ πλεῦν μέτοχος [Refs 5th c.BC+]; μ. ἐλπίδων, τέχνης, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; τοῦ βίου, of a wife, [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__II substantive, partner, accomplice in, τοῦ φόνου [Refs 5th c.BC+]; partner in business, [NT+3rd c.BC+]
__II.2 member of a board of officials, frequently in phrase ὁ δεῖνα καὶ μέτοχοι πράκτορες, ἐπιτηρηταί, ἀγορανόμοι, τραπεζῖται, etc., [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
__II.3 joint owner of a house, [Refs 3rd c.AD+]
__III θεῶν μέτοχοι, of the demigods, [Refs 4th c.BC+]
