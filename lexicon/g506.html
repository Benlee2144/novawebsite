ἀνυπότακτος

anypótaktos — G506

Transliteration: anypótaktos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G506

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G5293 (ὑποτάσσω);

Definition

unsubdued, i.e. insubordinate (in fact or temper)

KJV Translation

disobedient, that is not put under, unruly

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
