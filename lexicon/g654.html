ἀποστρέφω

apostréphō — G654

Transliteration: apostréphō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G654

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G4762 (στρέφω);

Definition

to turn away or back (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

bring again, pervert, turn away (from)
