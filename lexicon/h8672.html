תֵּשַׁע

têshaʻ — H8672

Transliteration: têshaʻ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H8672

Derivation: or (masculine) תִּשְׁעָה; perhaps from H8159 (שָׁעָה) through the idea of a turn to the next or full number ten;
