δεύτερος

deúteros — G1208

Transliteration: deúteros

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1208

Derivation: as the comparative of G1417 (δύο);

Definition

(ordinal) second (in time, place, or rank; also adverb)

KJV Translation

afterward, again, second(-arily, time)
