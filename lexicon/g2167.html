εὐφροσύνη

euphrosýnē — G2167

Transliteration: euphrosýnē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2167

Derivation: from the same as G2165 (εὐφραίνω);

Definition

joyfulness

KJV Translation

gladness, joy

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

εὐφροσύνη,

[Refs 4th c.BC+]: also in later Prose, [LXX+NT+4th c.BC+]; εὐ. ψυχῆς οἶνος πινόμενος [LXX]
__II proper name, [Refs], one of the Graces, [Refs 8th c.BC+]
