Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G940

βασκαίνω

baskaínō — G940

Transliteration: baskaínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G940

Derivation: akin to G5335 (φάσκω);

Definition

to malign, i.e. (by extension) to fascinate (by false representations)

KJV Translation

bewitch
