παραιτέομαι

paraitéomai — G3868

Transliteration: paraitéomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3868

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and the middle voice of G154 (αἰτέω);

Definition

to beg off, i.e. deprecate, decline, shun

KJV Translation

avoid, (make) excuse, intreat, refuse, reject

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
