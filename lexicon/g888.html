ἀχρεῖος

achreîos — G888

Transliteration: achreîos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G888

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G5534 (χρή) (compare G5532 (χρεία));

Definition

useless, i.e. (euphemistically) unmeritorious

KJV Translation

unprofitable
