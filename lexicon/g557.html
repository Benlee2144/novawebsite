ἀπελεγμός

apelegmós — G557

Transliteration: apelegmós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G557

Derivation: from a compound of G575 (ἀπό) and G1651 (ἐλέγχω);

Definition

refutation, i.e. (by implication) contempt

KJV Translation

nought
