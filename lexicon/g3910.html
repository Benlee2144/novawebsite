παραυτίκα

parautíka — G3910

Transliteration: parautíka

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3910

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and a derivative of G846 (αὐτός);

Definition

at the very instant, i.e. momentary

KJV Translation

but for a moment
