ὑπόμνησις

hypómnēsis — G5280

Transliteration: hypómnēsis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5280

Derivation: from G5279 (ὑπομιμνήσκω);

Definition

a reminding or (reflexively) recollection

KJV Translation

remembrance
