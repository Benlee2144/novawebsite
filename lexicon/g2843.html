κοινωνικός

koinōnikós — G2843

Transliteration: koinōnikós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2843

Derivation: from G2844 (κοινωνός);

Definition

communicative, i.e. (pecuniarily) liberal

KJV Translation

willing to communicate
