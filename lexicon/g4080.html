πηλίκος

pēlíkos — G4080

Transliteration: pēlíkos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4080

Derivation: a quantitative form (the feminine) of the base of G4225 (πού);

Definition

how much (as an indefinite), i.e. in size or (figuratively) dignity

KJV Translation

how great (large)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
