ἀδικέω

adikéō — G91

Transliteration: adikéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G91

Derivation: from G94 (ἄδικος);

Definition

to be unjust, i.e. (actively) do wrong (morally, socially or physically)

KJV Translation

hurt, injure, be an offender, be unjust, (do, suffer, take) wrong
