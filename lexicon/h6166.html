עֲרָד

ʻĂrâd — H6166

Transliteration: ʻĂrâd

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6166

Derivation: from an unused root meaning to sequester itself; fugitive;

Definition

Arad, the name of a place near Palestine, also of a Canaanite and an Israelite

KJV Translation

Arad.
