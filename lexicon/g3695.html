ὁπλίζω

hoplízō — G3695

Transliteration: hoplízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3695

Derivation: from G3696 (ὅπλον);

Definition

to equip (with weapons (middle voice and figuratively))

KJV Translation

arm self

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

ὁπλίζω
(ὅπλον), [in Sm.: Jer.52:25 * ;]
to make ready, equip; of soldiers, to arm. Mid., to arm oneself; figuratively, ἔννοιαν: 1Pe.4:1 (cf. θράσος ὁ., Soph., Elec., 995), (cf. καθ-ὁπλίζω).†
