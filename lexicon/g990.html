βλέμμα

blémma — G990

Transliteration: blémma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G990

Derivation: from G991 (βλέπω);

Definition

vision (properly concrete; by implication, abstract)

KJV Translation

seeing

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

βλέμμα, ατος, τό,
look, glance, [NT+5th c.BC+]; eyesight, [Refs 4th c.BC+]

Appears In (1 verse)

2 Peter 2:8

(βλέπω),
a look, a glance: βλέμματι καὶ ἀκοῇ, 2Pe.2:8, sight and hearing, a sense not found for β. in Gk. lit., but perh. recognized in the vernacular (ICC, in l.)†
