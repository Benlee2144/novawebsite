ἀπάγω

apágō — G520

Transliteration: apágō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G520

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G71 (ἄγω);

Definition

to take off (in various senses)

KJV Translation

bring, carry away, lead (away), put to death, take away

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
