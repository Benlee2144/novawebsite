κῶλον

kōlon — G2966

Transliteration: kōlon

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2966

Derivation: from the base of G2849 (κολάζω);

Definition

a limb of the body (as if lopped)

KJV Translation

carcase
