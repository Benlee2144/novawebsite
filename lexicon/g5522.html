χόος

chóos — G5522

Transliteration: chóos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5522

Derivation: from the base of G5494 (χειμών);

Definition

a heap (as poured out), i.e. rubbish; loose dirt

KJV Translation

dust
