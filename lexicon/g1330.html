διέρχομαι

diérchomai — G1330

Transliteration: diérchomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1330

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G2064 (ἔρχομαι);

Definition

to traverse (literally)

KJV Translation

come, depart, go (about, abroad, everywhere, over, through, throughout), pass (by, over, through, throughout), pierce through, travel, walk through

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

διέρχομαι,

__2 pass through, complete, τὸ πέμπτον μέρος τῆς ὁδοῦ [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__3 of reports, βάξις διῆλθ᾽ Ἀχαιούς [Refs 5th c.BC+]went abroad, spread, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__4 of pain, shoot through one, [Refs]; of passion, ἵμερος δ. Ἡρακλῆ [Refs]; ἐμὲ διῆλθέ τι a thought shot through me, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
