חָנָה

chânâh — H2583

Transliteration: chânâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2583

Derivation: a primitive root (compare H2603 (חָנַן));

Definition

properly, to incline; by implication, to decline (of the slanting rays of evening); specifically, to pitch atent; gen. to encamp (for abode or siege)

KJV Translation

abide (in tents), camp, dwell, encamp, grow to an end, lie, pitch (tent), rest in tent.
