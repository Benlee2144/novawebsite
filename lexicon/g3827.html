πάμπολυς

pámpolys — G3827

Transliteration: pámpolys

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3827

Derivation: from G3956 (πᾶς) and G4183 (πολύς);

Definition

full many, i.e. immense

KJV Translation

very great

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

πάμπολυς, -πόλλη, -τολυ
(πᾶς, πολύς), [in Sm.: Job.36:31, Psa.40:6 89:51 * ;]
very much, very great: Mrk.8:1, Rec. (RV, Edd., πάλιν πολλοῦ).†
