ἄῤῥωστος

árrhōstos — G732

Transliteration: árrhōstos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G732

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G4517 (ῥώννυμι);

Definition

infirm

KJV Translation

sick (folk, -ly)

__3 ἀρρωστότερος ἐς τὴν μισθοδοσίαν remiss in payment, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
