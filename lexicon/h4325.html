מַיִם

mayim — H4325

Transliteration: mayim

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4325

Derivation: dual of a primitive noun (but used in a singular sense);

Definition

water; figuratively, juice; by euphemism, urine, semen

KJV Translation

[phrase] piss, wasting, water(-ing, (-course, -flood, -spring)).
