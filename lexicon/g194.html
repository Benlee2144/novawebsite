ἄκρατος

ákratos — G194

Transliteration: ákratos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G194

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G2767 (κεράννυμι);

Definition

undiluted

KJV Translation

without mixture
