Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H5606

סָפַק

çâphaq — H5606

Transliteration: çâphaq

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5606

Derivation: or שָׂפַק; (1 Kings 20:10; Job 27:23; Isaiah 2:6), a primitive root;

Definition

to clap the hands (in token of compact, derision, grief, indignation, or punishment); by implication of satisfaction, to be enough; by implication of excess, to vomit

KJV Translation

clap, smite, strike, suffice, wallow.
