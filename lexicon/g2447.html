ἰός

iós — G2447

Transliteration: iós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2447

Derivation: perhaps from (to go) or (to send);

Definition

rust (as if emitted by metals); also venom (as emitted by serpents)

KJV Translation

poison, rust

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἰός [ῑ], ὁ,

__C rust on iron, or verdigris on copper and bronze, [Refs 7th c.BC+]; patina on bronze statues, ὅπως καθαρὸς ἰοῦ ἔσται ὁ ἀνδριάς [Refs 5th c.BC+]
