ῥυπαρός

rhyparós — G4508

Transliteration: rhyparós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4508

Derivation: from G4509 (ῥύπος);

Definition

dirty, i.e. (relatively) cheap or shabby; morally, wicked

KJV Translation

vile
