σφάγιον

sphágion — G4968

Transliteration: sphágion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4968

Derivation: neuter of a derivative of G4967 (σφαγή);

Definition

a victim (in sacrifice)

KJV Translation

slain beast
