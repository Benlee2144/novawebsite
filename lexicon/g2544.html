καίτοιγε

kaítoige — G2544

Transliteration: kaítoige

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2544

Derivation: from G2543 (καίτοι) and G1065 (γέ);

Definition

and yet indeed, i.e. although really

KJV Translation

nevertheless, though
