ὀλοθρεύω

olothreúō — G3645

Transliteration: olothreúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3645

Derivation: from G3639 (ὄλεθρος);

Definition

to spoil, i.e. slay

KJV Translation

destroy

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ὀλοθρ-εύω,
destroy, variant for{ὀλεθρεύω} in [LXX+1st c.AD+]; compare ἐξολοθρεύω:—hence ὀλοθρ-ευτής, οῦ, ὁ, destroyer, [NT]:—feminine ὀλοθρ-εύτρια, gloss on{λοιγίστρια}, [Refs 5th c.AD+]

Appears In (1 verse)

Hebrews 11:28
