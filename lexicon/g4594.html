σήμερον

sḗmeron — G4594

Transliteration: sḗmeron

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4594

Derivation: neuter (as adverb) of a presumed compound of the article G3588 (ὁ) (t changed to s) and G2250 (ἡμέρα);

Definition

on the (i.e. this) day (or night current or just passed); generally, now (i.e. at present, hitherto)

KJV Translation

this (to-)day

adverb to-day, [NT+8th c.BC+]; Doric dialect σάμερον [ᾱ] [Refs 5th c.BC+]; Attic dialect τήμερον [Refs 5th c.BC+] is sometimes found in Comedy texts, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; εἰς τ. Lithuanian šis 'this', Latin ci-tra: σήμερον (τήμερον) is to ἡμέρα as σῆτες (τῆτες) to ἔτος.)
