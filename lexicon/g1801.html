ἐνωτίζομαι

enōtízomai — G1801

Transliteration: enōtízomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1801

Derivation: middle voice from a compound of G1722 (ἐν) and G3775 (οὖς);

Definition

to take in one's ear, i.e. to listen

KJV Translation

hearken

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐνωτίζομαι,
(οὖς) give ear, hearken to, λόγους [LXX+NT]

Acts 2:14
