Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1398

δουλεύω

douleúō — G1398

Transliteration: douleúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1398

Derivation: from G1401 (δοῦλος);

Definition

to be a slave to (literal or figurative, involuntary or voluntary)

KJV Translation

be in bondage, (do) serve(-ice)

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

δουλ-εύω,
(δοῦλος) to be a slave, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 serve, be subject, τό τ᾽ ἄρχειν καὶ τὸ δ. δίχα [Refs 4th c.BC+]; δ. ζεύγλαις[Refs 5th c.BC+]; δ. γαστρί, ὕπνῳ, λαγνείᾳ, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; τῇ γῇ δ. make oneself a slave to one's land, i. e. give up rights that one may keep it, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; δ. τῷ καιρῷ accommodate oneself to the occasion, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
__3 render a service, τινά [Refs 6th c.AD+]
