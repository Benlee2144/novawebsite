ὄψις

ópsis — G3799

Transliteration: ópsis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3799

Derivation: from G3700 (ὀπτάνομαι);

Definition

properly, sight (the act), i.e. (by implication) the visage, an external show

KJV Translation

appearance, countenance, face
