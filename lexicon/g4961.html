συστρατιώτης

systratiṓtēs — G4961

Transliteration: systratiṓtēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4961

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G4757 (στρατιώτης);

Definition

a co-campaigner, i.e. (figuratively) an associate in Christian toil

KJV Translation

fellowsoldier
