Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3133

μαραίνω

maraínō — G3133

Transliteration: maraínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3133

Derivation: of uncertain affinity;

Definition

to extinguish (as fire), i.e. (figuratively and passively) to pass away

KJV Translation

fade away
