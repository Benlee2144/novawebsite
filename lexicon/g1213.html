δηλόω

dēlóō — G1213

Transliteration: dēlóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1213

Derivation: from G1212 (δῆλος);

Definition

to make plain (by words)

KJV Translation

declare, shew, signify

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

δηλ-όω:—

__2 make known, disclose, reveal, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; prove, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; explain, set forth, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; signify, ἐδήλουν οὐδὲν ὅτι ἴσασιν gave no sign of knowing, [Refs]: indicate, τὰς μεγίστας καὶ ἐλαχίστας [Refs 5th c.BC+]; δ. τι πρός or εἴς τινα, [Refs 5th c.BC+]: with accusative et infinitive, [Refs 3rd c.AD+]: followed by a relative clause, δ. ὅτι [Refs 5th c.BC+]: with accusative et participle, σκευή τε γάρ σε καὶ τὸ δύστηνον κάρα δηλοῦτον.. ὄνθ᾽ ὃς εἶ [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ὥς σε δηλώσω κακόν [ὄντα[Refs]participle nominative, refer
