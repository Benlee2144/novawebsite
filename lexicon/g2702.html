καταφέρω

kataphérō — G2702

Transliteration: kataphérō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2702

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G5342 (φέρω) (including its alternate);

Definition

to bear down, i.e. (figuratively) overcome (with drowsiness); specially, to cast a vote

KJV Translation

fall, give, sink down
