βοήθεια

boḗtheia — G996

Transliteration: boḗtheia

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G996

Derivation: from G998 (βοηθός);

Definition

aid; specially, a rope or chain for frapping a vessel

KJV Translation

help
