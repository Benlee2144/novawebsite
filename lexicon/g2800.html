κλάσις

klásis — G2800

Transliteration: klásis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2800

Derivation: from G2806 (κλάω);

Definition

fracture (the act)

KJV Translation

breaking

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

κλάσ-ις [ᾰ], εως, ἡ,
(κλάω A) breaking, fracture, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ἡ κ. τῶν ἀμπέλων breaking off the shoots and tendrils of vines, [NT+4th c.BC+]
__2 bending of the knee joint, [Refs 4th c.BC+]; κ. ὄψεων refraction, [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__2.b κλάσιν λαβεῖν to be deflected, [Refs 3rd c.AD+]; ὅταν κλάσιν ποιῇ καὶ γωνίαν, of a bandage. Erot. see at {σκέπαρνος}; of the labyrinth of the ear, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
__II modulation of the voice, [Refs 1st c.AD+]
