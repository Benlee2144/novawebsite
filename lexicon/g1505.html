εἰλικρίνεια

eilikríneia — G1505

Transliteration: eilikríneia

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1505

Derivation: from G1506 (εἰλικρινής);

Definition

clearness, i.e. (by implication) purity (figuratively)

KJV Translation

sincerity

__II sincerity, uprightness, [NT+3rd c.AD+]
