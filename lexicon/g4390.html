προτρέχω

protréchō — G4390

Transliteration: protréchō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4390

Derivation: from G4253 (πρό) and G5143 (τρέχω) (including its alternate);

Definition

to run forward, i.e. outstrip, precede

KJV Translation

outrun, run before
