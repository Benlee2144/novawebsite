Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H4975

מֹתֶן

môthen — H4975

Transliteration: môthen

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4975

Derivation: from an unused root meaning to be slender;

Definition

properly, the waist or small of the back; only in plural the loins

KJV Translation

[phrase] greyhound, loins, side.
