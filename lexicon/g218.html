ἀλείφω

aleíphō — G218

Transliteration: aleíphō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G218

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as particle of union) and the base of G3045 (λιπαρός);

Definition

to oil (with perfume)

KJV Translation

anoint
