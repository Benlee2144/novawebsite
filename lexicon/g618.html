ἀπολαμβάνω

apolambánō — G618

Transliteration: apolambánō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G618

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G2983 (λαμβάνω);

Definition

to receive (specially, in full, or as a host); also to take aside

KJV Translation

receive, take
