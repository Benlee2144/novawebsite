παρακαταθήκη

parakatathḗkē — G3872

Transliteration: parakatathḗkē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3872

Derivation: from a compound of G3844 (παρά) and G2698 (κατατίθημι);

Definition

something put down alongside, i.e. a deposit (sacred trust)

KJV Translation

that (thing) which is committed (un-)to (trust)

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

παρα-κατα-θήκη, -ης, ἡ
(παρακατατίθημι) [in LXX: Exo.22:8, 11 (מְלָאכָה), and elsewhere as seel. for παραθήκη, which see ;]
