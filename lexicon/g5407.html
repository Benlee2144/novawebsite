Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G5407

φονεύω

phoneúō — G5407

Transliteration: phoneúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5407

Derivation: from G5406 (φονεύς);

Definition

to be a murderer (of)

KJV Translation

kill, do murder, slay

__2 of an animal, ἐὰν.. ζῷον.. τι φονεύσῃ τινά [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__3 stain with blood, φασγάνῳ δέρην [Refs 5th c.BC+]
