χειμάζω

cheimázō — G5492

Transliteration: cheimázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5492

Derivation: from the same as G5494 (χειμών);

Definition

to storm, i.e. (passively) to labor under a gale

KJV Translation

be tossed with tempest
