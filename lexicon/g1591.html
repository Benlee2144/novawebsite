ἐκμάσσω

ekmássō — G1591

Transliteration: ekmássō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1591

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and the base of G3145 (μασσάομαι);

Definition

to knead out, i.e. (by analogy) to wipe dry

KJV Translation

wipe
