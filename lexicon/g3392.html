μιαίνω

miaínō — G3392

Transliteration: miaínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3392

Derivation: perhaps a primary verb;

Definition

to sully or taint, i.e. contaminate (ceremonially or morally)

KJV Translation

defile
