בַּת

bath — H1323

Transliteration: bath

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1323

Derivation: from H1129 (בָּנָה) (as feminine of H1121 (בֵּן));

Definition

a daughter (used in the same wide sense as other terms of relationship, literally and figuratively)

KJV Translation

apple (of the eye), branch, company, daughter, [idiom] first, [idiom] old, [phrase] owl, town, village.

A woman living at the time of the Patriarchs, first mentioned at Gen.38.2;
daughter of: Shua (H7770); married to Judah (H3063);
mother of: Er (H6147), Onan (H0209) and Shelah (H7956)
the wife of Uriah whom David had murdered, having had adulterous relations with her; subsequently wife of David and mother of Solomon, Shimea, Shobab, and Nathan (alternate spelling to 'Bathsheba')
