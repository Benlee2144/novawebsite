ἀπαγγέλλω

apangéllō — G518

Transliteration: apangéllō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G518

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and the base of G32 (ἄγγελος);

Definition

to announce

KJV Translation

bring word (again), declare, report, shew (again), tell
