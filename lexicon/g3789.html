ὄφις

óphis — G3789

Transliteration: óphis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3789

Derivation: probably from G3700 (ὀπτάνομαι) (through the idea of sharpness of vision);

Definition

a snake, figuratively, (as a type of sly cunning) an artful malicious person, especially Satan

KJV Translation

serpent
