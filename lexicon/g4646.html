σκολιός

skoliós — G4646

Transliteration: skoliós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4646

Derivation: from the base of G4628 (σκέλος);

Definition

warped, i.e. winding; figuratively, perverse

KJV Translation

crooked, froward, untoward

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

σκολιός, -ά, -όν
[in LXX: Deu.32:5 (עִקֵּשׁ), Pro.28:18 (σ. ὁδοῖς πορεύεσθαι, for: דֶּרֶךְ עָקַשׁ), Isa.40:4 (עָקֹב), etc. ;]
curved, bent, winding (opposite to ὀρθός, εὐθύς): Luk.3:5 (LXX). Metaphorical, crooked, perverse, unjust: Act.2:40, Php.2:15, 1Pe.2:18.†
