καταγινώσκω

kataginṓskō — G2607

Transliteration: kataginṓskō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2607

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G1097 (γινώσκω);

Definition

to note against, i.e. find fault with

KJV Translation

blame, condemn

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
