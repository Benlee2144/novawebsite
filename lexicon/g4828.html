Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4828

συμμαρτυρέω

symmartyréō — G4828

Transliteration: symmartyréō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4828

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G3140 (μαρτυρέω);

Definition

to testify jointly, i.e. corroborate by (concurrent) evidence

KJV Translation

testify unto, (also) bear witness (with)

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

συμμαρτῠρέω,
bear witness with or in support of another, ξυμμαρτυρῶ σοι [Refs 5th c.BC+]; τι to a fact, [Refs 6th c.BC+]; followed by a relative, ὡς.. σύ μοι ξυμμαρτυρῇς οἵα πέφυκα [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 Astrology texts, to be in aspect with, configurate with, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
