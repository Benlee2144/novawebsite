בְּרִית

bᵉrîyth — H1285

Transliteration: bᵉrîyth

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1285

Derivation: from H1262 (בָּרָה) (in the sense of cutting (like H1254 (בָּרָא)));

Definition

a compact (because made by passing between pieces of flesh)

KJV Translation

confederacy, (con-) feder(-ate), covenant, league.
