Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4463

ῥαβδίζω

rhabdízō — G4463

Transliteration: rhabdízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4463

Derivation: from G4464 (ῥάβδος);

Definition

to strike with a stick, i.e. bastinado

KJV Translation

beat (with rods)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
