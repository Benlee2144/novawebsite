גָּנַב

gânab — H1589

Transliteration: gânab

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1589

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to thieve (literally or figuratively); by implication, to deceive

KJV Translation

carry away, [idiom] indeed, secretly bring, steal (away), get by stealth.
