βλασφημία

blasphēmía — G988

Transliteration: blasphēmía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G988

Derivation: from G989 (βλάσφημος);

Definition

vilification (especially against God)

KJV Translation

blasphemy, evil speaking, railing

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
