ἄφρων

áphrōn — G878

Transliteration: áphrōn

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G878

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and G5424 (φρήν);

Definition

properly, mindless, i.e. stupid, (by implication) ignorant, (specially) egotistic, (practically) rash, or (morally) unbelieving

KJV Translation

fool(-ish), unwise
