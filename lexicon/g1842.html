ἐξολοθρεύω

exolothreúō — G1842

Transliteration: exolothreúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1842

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G3645 (ὀλοθρεύω);

Definition

to extirpate

KJV Translation

destroy
