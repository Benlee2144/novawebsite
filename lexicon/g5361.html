φιλάδελφος

philádelphos — G5361

Transliteration: philádelphos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5361

Derivation: from G5384 (φίλος) and G80 (ἀδελφός);

Definition

fond of brethren, i.e. fraternal

KJV Translation

love as brethren
