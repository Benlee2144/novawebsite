עֲזַזְיָהוּ

ʻĂzazyâhûw — H5812

Transliteration: ʻĂzazyâhûw

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5812

Derivation: from H5810 (עָזַז) and H3050 (יָהּ); Jah has strengthened;

Definition

Azazjah, the name of three Israelites

KJV Translation

Azaziah.

Appears In (1 verse)

1 Chronicles 27:20

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

A man living at the time of Divided Monarchy, only mentioned at 2Ch.31.13
§ Azaziah = "Jehovah is mighty"
1) a Levite musician in the reign of David who played the harp in the procession which brought the ark from Obed-edom to Jerusalem
2) a Levite in the reign of king Hezekiah of Judah in charge of the tithes
3) father of Hoshea, prince of the tribe of Ephraim when David took the census
