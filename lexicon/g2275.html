ἥττημα

hḗttēma — G2275

Transliteration: hḗttēma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2275

Derivation: from G2274 (ἡττάω);

Definition

a deterioration, i.e. (objectively) failure or (subjectively) loss

KJV Translation

diminishing, fault
