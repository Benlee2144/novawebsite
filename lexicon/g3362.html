ἐὰν μή

eàn mḗ — G3362

Transliteration: eàn mḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3362

Derivation: i.e. G1437 (ἐάν) and G3361 (μή);

Definition

if not, i.e. unless

KJV Translation

X before, but, except, if, no, (if, + whosoever) not
