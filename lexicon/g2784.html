Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2784

κηρύσσω

kērýssō — G2784

Transliteration: kērýssō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2784

Derivation: of uncertain affinity;

Definition

to herald (as a public crier), especially divine truth (the gospel)

KJV Translation

preacher(-er), proclaim, publish

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
