φρόνησις

phrónēsis — G5428

Transliteration: phrónēsis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5428

Derivation: from G5426 (φρονέω);

Definition

mental action or activity, i.e. intellectual or moral insight

KJV Translation

prudence, wisdom

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

φρόνησις, -εως, ἡ
(φρονέω), [in LXX for בִּינָה, תָּבוּן, חׇכְמָה, etc. ;]
understanding, practical wisdom, prudence: Luk.1:17, Eph.1:8.†
SYN.: see: σοφία, and cf. Lft., Notes, 317 (AS)
