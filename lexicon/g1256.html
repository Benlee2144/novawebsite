διαλέγομαι

dialégomai — G1256

Transliteration: dialégomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1256

Derivation: middle voice from G1223 (διά) and G3004 (λέγω);

Definition

to say thoroughly, i.e. discuss (in argument or exhortation)

KJV Translation

dispute, preach (unto), reason (with), speak
