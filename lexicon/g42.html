ἁγιωσύνη

hagiōsýnē — G42

Transliteration: hagiōsýnē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G42

Derivation: from G40 (ἅγιος);

Definition

sacredness (i.e. properly, the quality)

KJV Translation
