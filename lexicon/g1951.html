ἐπιλέγομαι

epilégomai — G1951

Transliteration: epilégomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1951

Derivation: middle voice from G1909 (ἐπί) and G3004 (λέγω);

Definition

to surname, select

KJV Translation

call, choose
