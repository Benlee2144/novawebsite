יַעַן

yaʻan — H3282

Transliteration: yaʻan

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3282

Derivation: from an unused root meaning to pay attention;

Definition

properly, heed; by implication, purpose (sake or account); used adverbially to indicate the reason or cause

KJV Translation

because (that), forasmuch ([phrase] as), seeing then, [phrase] that, [phrase] wheras, [phrase] why.

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

conj

prep

3) why (with interrogative pron)
