ἄκαρπος

ákarpos — G175

Transliteration: ákarpos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G175

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and G2590 (καρπός);

Definition

barren (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

without fruit, unfruitful
