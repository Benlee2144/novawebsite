מִסְפָּר

miçpâr — H4557

Transliteration: miçpâr

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4557

Derivation: from H5608 (סָפַר);

Definition

a number, definite (arithmetical) or indefinite (large, innumerable; small, a few); also (abstractly) narration

KJV Translation

[phrase] abundance, account, [idiom] all, [idiom] few, (in-) finite, (certain) number(-ed), tale, telling, [phrase] time.
