ἀπεκδύομαι

apekdýomai — G554

Transliteration: apekdýomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G554

Derivation: middle voice from G575 (ἀπό) and G1562 (ἐκδύω);

Definition

to divest wholly oneself, or (for oneself) despoil

KJV Translation

put off, spoil

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀπεκ-δύομαι,
future -δύσομαι [ῡ]: aorist 1 -εδῡσάμην:— strip off oneself: metaphorically, put off, τὸν παλαιὸν ἄνθρωπον[NT]
