keçeph — H3701

Transliteration: keçeph

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3701

Derivation: from H3700 (כָּסַף);

Definition

silver (from its pale color); by implication, money

KJV Translation

money, price, silver(-ling).
