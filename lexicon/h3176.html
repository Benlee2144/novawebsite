יָחַל

yâchal — H3176

Transliteration: yâchal

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3176

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to wait; by implication, to be patient, hope

KJV Translation

(cause to, have, make to) hope, be pained, stay, tarry, trust, wait.
