Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H7727

שׁוֹבָב

Shôwbâb — H7727

Transliteration: Shôwbâb

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7727

Derivation: the same as H7726 (שׁוֹבָב); rebellious;

Definition

Shobab, the name of two Israelites

KJV Translation

Shobab.

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

A man of the tribe of Judah living at the time of Egypt and Wilderness, only mentioned at 1Ch.2.18;
son of: Caleb (H3612H) and Jerioth(?) (H3408);
brother of: Hur (H2354), Jesher (H3475), Ardon (H0715), Mareshah@1Ch.2.42 (H4337), Haran (H2771B), Moza (H4162), Gazez (H1495), Jahdai (H3056), Sheber (H7669), Tirhanah (H8647), Shaaph (H8174H), Sheva (H7724) and Achsah (H5915H)
§ Shobab = "rebellious"
1) son of David by Bathsheba
2) a Judaite, son of Caleb by his wife Azubah and grandson of Hezron
