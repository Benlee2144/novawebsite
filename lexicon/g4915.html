συνηλικιώτης

synēlikiṓtēs — G4915

Transliteration: synēlikiṓtēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4915

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and a derivative of G2244 (ἡλικία);

Definition

a co-aged person, i.e. alike in years

KJV Translation

equal
