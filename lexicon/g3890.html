παραμύθιον

paramýthion — G3890

Transliteration: paramýthion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3890

Derivation: neuter of G3889 (παραμυθία);

Definition

consolation (properly, concretely)

KJV Translation

comfort

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

παραμῡθ-ιον, τό,
address, exhortation, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; encouragement, τοῦ μὴ φοβεῖσθαι [Refs]
__2 assuagement, abatement of, καμάτων [Refs 5th c.BC+]; πυρσῶν of the fires of love, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; τοῖς γὰρ πλουσίοις πολλὰ π. φασιν εἶναι many consolations, [Refs 1st c.BC+]
__3 παραμύθια πλησμονῆς stimulants of a sated appetite, [Refs 5th c.BC+]

Philippians 2:1
