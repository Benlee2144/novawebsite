κραυγάζω

kraugázō — G2905

Transliteration: kraugázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2905

Derivation: from G2906 (κραυγή);

Definition

to clamor

KJV Translation

cry out

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

κραυγ-άζω,
={κράζω}, of dogs, bay, [Refs]; of ravens, croak, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]; of men, cry aloud, shout, [NT+4th c.BC+]
