ὕβρις

hýbris — G5196

Transliteration: hýbris

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5196

Derivation: from G5228 (ὑπέρ);

Definition

insolence (as over-bearing), i.e. insult, injury

KJV Translation

harm, hurt, reproach
