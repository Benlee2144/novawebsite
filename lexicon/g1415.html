δυνατός

dynatós — G1415

Transliteration: dynatós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1415

Derivation: from G1410 (δύναμαι);

Definition

powerful or capable (literally or figuratively); neuter possible

KJV Translation

able, could, (that is) mighty (man), possible, power, strong

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
