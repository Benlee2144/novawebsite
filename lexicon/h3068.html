יְהֹוָה

Transliteration: Yᵉhôvâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3068

Derivation: from H1961 (הָיָה); (the) self-Existent or Eternal;

Definition

Jehovah, Jewish national name of God

KJV Translation

Jehovah, the Lord. Compare H3050 (יָהּ), H3069 (יְהֹוִה).
