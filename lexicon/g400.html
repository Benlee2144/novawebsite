ἀναφωνέω

anaphōnéō — G400

Transliteration: anaphōnéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G400

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and G5455 (φωνέω);

Definition

to exclaim

KJV Translation

speak out

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

__2 proclaim, βασιλέα[Refs]: with dative, decree, τοῦ ἀναπεφωνημένου Νουμηνίῳ στεφάνου[Refs]
__3 ἀ. τὴν ἐλευθερίαν claim liberty, [Refs 1st c.AD+]
__4 of poetic utterance, [Refs 4th c.BC+]; of any utterance, with accusative, [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__5 invoke, in passive, [Refs 5th c.AD+]
