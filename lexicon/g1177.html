δεκαδύο

dekadýo — G1177

Transliteration: dekadýo

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1177

Derivation: from G1176 (δέκα) and G1417 (δύο);

Definition

two and ten, i.e. twelve

KJV Translation

twelve
