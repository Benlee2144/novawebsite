פַּחַד

pachad — H6343

Transliteration: pachad

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6343

Derivation: from H6342 (פָּחַד);

Definition

a (sudden) alarm (properly, the object feared, by implication, the feeling)

KJV Translation

dread(-ful), fear, (thing) great (fear, -ly feared), terror.
