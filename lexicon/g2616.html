καταδυναστεύω

katadynasteúō — G2616

Transliteration: katadynasteúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2616

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and a derivative of G1413 (δυνάστης);

Definition

to exercise dominion against, i.e. oppress

KJV Translation

oppress
