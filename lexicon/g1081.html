γέννημα

génnēma — G1081

Transliteration: génnēma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1081

Derivation: from G1080 (γεννάω);

Definition

offspring; by analogy, produce (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

fruit, generation
