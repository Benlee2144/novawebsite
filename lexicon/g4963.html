συστροφή

systrophḗ — G4963

Transliteration: systrophḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4963

Derivation: from G4962 (συστρέφω);

Definition

a twisting together, i.e. (figuratively) a secret coalition, riotous crowd

KJV Translation

+ band together, concourse
