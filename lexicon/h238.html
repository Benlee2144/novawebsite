Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H238

אָזַן

ʼâzan — H238

Transliteration: ʼâzan

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H238

Derivation: a primitive root; probably to expand; but used only as a denominative from H241 (אֹזֶן);

Definition

to broaden out the ear (with the hand), i.e. (by implication) to listen

KJV Translation

give (perceive by the) ear, hear(-ken). See H239 (אָזַן).
