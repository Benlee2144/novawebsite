μαθητής

mathētḗs — G3101

Transliteration: mathētḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3101

Derivation: from G3129 (μανθάνω);

Definition

a learner, i.e. pupil

KJV Translation

disciple

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

μᾰθητ-ής, οῦ, ὁ,
learner, pupil, τῆς Ἑλλάδος [Refs 5th c.BC+]; of dancing, [Refs 4th c.BC+]: frequently in Attic dialect of the pupils of philosophers and rhetoricians, οὐ θέμις πλὴν τοῖς μ. λέγειν [Refs 5th c.BC+] with genitive _of things_, τούτου τοῦ μαθήματος μ. a studentofit, [Refs]; μ. ἰατρικῆς a student of medicine,[Refs]; apprentice, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
