Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4573

σεβάζομαι

sebázomai — G4573

Transliteration: sebázomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4573

Derivation: middle voice from a derivative of G4576 (σέβομαι);

Definition

to venerate, i.e. adore

KJV Translation

worship

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

σεβ-άζομαι,
Epic dialect aorist σεβάσσατο [NT+8th c.BC+]:—to be afraid of, with accusative, σεβάσσατο γὰρ τό γε θυμῷ [Refs 8th c.BC+]
__2 later, ={σέβομαι}, ξεῖνον θανόντα [Refs 4th c.AD+]

Appears In (1 verse)

Romans 1:25
