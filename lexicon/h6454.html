פָּסֵחַ

Pâçêach — H6454

Transliteration: Pâçêach

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6454

Derivation: from H6452 (פָּסַח); limping;

Definition

Paseach, the name of two Israelites

KJV Translation

Paseah, Phaseah.

Appears In (4 verses)

1 Chronicles 4:12Ezra 2:49Nehemiah 3:6Nehemiah 7:51

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

A man living at the time of Exile and Return, only mentioned at Neh.3.6;
father of: Joiada (H3111)
§ Paseah or Phaseah = "limper"
1) son of Eshton of the tribe of Judah
2) ancestor of a family of temple slaves who returned from exile with Zerubbabel
3) father of Jehoiada, one who helped repair the wall of Jerusalem in the time of Nehemiah
