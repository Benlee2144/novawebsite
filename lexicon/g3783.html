ὀφείλημα

opheílēma — G3783

Transliteration: opheílēma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3783

Derivation: from (the alternate of) G3784 (ὀφείλω);

Definition

something owed, i.e. (figuratively) a due; morally, a fault

KJV Translation

debt

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ὀφείλ-ημα, ατος, τό,
