διαστέλλομαι

diastéllomai — G1291

Transliteration: diastéllomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1291

Derivation: middle voice from G1223 (διά) and G4724 (στέλλω);

Definition

to set (oneself) apart (figuratively, distinguish), i.e. (by implication) to enjoin

KJV Translation

charge, that which was (give) commanded(-ment)
