Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1750

ἐνειλέω

eneiléō — G1750

Transliteration: eneiléō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1750

Derivation: from G1772 (ἔννομος) and the base of G1507 (εἱλίσσω);

Definition

to enwrap

KJV Translation

wrap in

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐνειλ-έω,
wrap in, τι ὀθονίῳ [Refs 1st c.AD+]:—middle, τινὰ κακοῖσι [Refs 4th c.AD+]:—passive, to be enwrapped, ἐν [τῇ γῇ] [LXX+4th c.BC+]
__II metaphorically, engage, ἐνίων αὑτοὺς ἐνειληκότων οἰκονομίαις [Refs 2nd c.BC+]:—passive, to be engaged, entangled in or with, τοῖς πολεμίοις [Refs 1st c.AD+]; ὥσπερ ἄρκυσιν ἐνειλημένους probably to be read instead of -λημμ-, [Refs 1st c.AD+]; βρέφη-ημένα τὰς χεῖρας Artem.[same place]; come to blows with, [Refs 1st c.AD+]
