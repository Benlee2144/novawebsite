συνοχή

synochḗ — G4928

Transliteration: synochḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4928

Derivation: from G4912 (συνέχω);

Definition

restraint, i.e. (figuratively) anxiety

KJV Translation

anguish, distress

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
