κατακυριεύω

katakyrieúō — G2634

Transliteration: katakyrieúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2634

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G2961 (κυριεύω);

Definition

to lord against, i.e. control, subjugate

KJV Translation

exercise dominion over (lordship), be lord over, overcome
