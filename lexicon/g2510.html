καθάπτω

katháptō — G2510

Transliteration: katháptō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2510

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G680 (ἅπτομαι);

Definition

to seize upon

KJV Translation

fasten on
