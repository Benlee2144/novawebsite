διαφυλάσσω

diaphylássō — G1314

Transliteration: diaphylássō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1314

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G5442 (φυλάσσω);

Definition

to guard thoroughly, i.e. protect

KJV Translation

keep
