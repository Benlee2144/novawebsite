חֲמָתִי

Chămâthîy — H2577

Transliteration: Chămâthîy

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2577

Derivation: patrial from H2574 (חֲמָת);

Definition

a Chamathite or native of Chamath

KJV Translation

Hamathite.

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)
