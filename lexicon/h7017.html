Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7017

Derivation: or קִינִי; (1 Chronicles 2:55), patronymic from H7014 (קַיִן);

Definition

a Kenite or member of the tribe of Kajin

KJV Translation

Kenite.
