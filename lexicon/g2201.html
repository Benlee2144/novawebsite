ζεῦγος

zeûgos — G2201

Transliteration: zeûgos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2201

Derivation: from the same as G2218 (ζυγός);

Definition

a couple, i.e. a team (of oxen yoked together) or brace (of birds tied together)

KJV Translation

yoke, pair

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
