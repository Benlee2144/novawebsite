παραμένω

paraménō — G3887

Transliteration: paraménō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3887

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and G3306 (μένω);

Definition

to stay near, i.e. remain (literally, tarry; or figuratively, be permanent, persevere)

KJV Translation

abide, continue
