εὐαγγελίζω

euangelízō — G2097

Transliteration: euangelízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2097

Derivation: from G2095 (εὖ) and G32 (ἄγγελος);

Definition

to announce good news (\

KJV Translation

declare, bring (declare, show) glad (good) tidings, preach (the gospel)
