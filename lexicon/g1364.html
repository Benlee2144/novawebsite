δίς

dís — G1364

Transliteration: dís

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1364

Derivation: adverb from G1417 (δύο);

Definition

twice

KJV Translation

again, twice

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

δίς [ῐ],
adverb twice, doubly, with Nouns, δ. τόσσον twice as much, [Refs 8th c.BC+]twice over, [Refs 5th c.BC+] = son and grandson of N., [Refs]; Αὐρήλιος Αὐξάνων δ. [Refs]; Αὐρ. Δοῦ[ρ]λος δ. [Refs] —In compounds δι-, but δισ- in δισμύριοι, δισχίλιοι, δισθανής, δίσαβος, δισάρπαγος, δίσευνος, etc. (Cf. Sanskrit dvis 'twice', Latin bis.)
