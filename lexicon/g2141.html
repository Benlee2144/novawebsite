εὐπορέω

euporéō — G2141

Transliteration: euporéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2141

Derivation: from a compound of G2090 (ἑτοιμάζω) and the base of G4197 (πορεία);

Definition

(intransitively) to be good for passing through, i.e. (figuratively) have pecuniary means

KJV Translation

ability
