בָּחַר

bâchar — H977

Transliteration: bâchar

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H977

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

properly, to try, i.e. (by implication) select

KJV Translation

acceptable, appoint, choose (choice), excellent, join, be rather, require.
