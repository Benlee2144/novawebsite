σύζυγος

sýzygos — G4805

Transliteration: sýzygos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4805

Derivation: from G4801 (συζεύγνυμι);

Definition

co-yoked, i.e. (figuratively) as noun, a colleague; probably rather as a proper name; Syzygus, a Christian

KJV Translation

yokefellow

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
