πτῶμα

ptōma — G4430

Transliteration: ptōma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4430

Derivation: from the alternate of G4098 (πίπτω);

Definition

a ruin, i.e. (specially), lifeless body (corpse, carrion)

KJV Translation

dead body, carcase, corpse
