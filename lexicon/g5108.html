τοιοῦτος

toioûtos — G5108

Transliteration: toioûtos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5108

Derivation: (including the other inflections); from G5104 (τοί) and G3778 (οὗτος);

Definition

truly this, i.e. of this sort (to denote character or individuality)

KJV Translation

like, such (an one)
