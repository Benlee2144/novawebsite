Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H1826

דָּמַם

dâmam — H1826

Transliteration: dâmam

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1826

Derivation: a primitive root (compare H1724 (דָּהַם), H1820 (דָּמָה));

Definition

to be dumb; by implication, to be astonished, to stop; also to perish

KJV Translation

cease, be cut down (off), forbear, hold peace, quiet self, rest, be silent, keep (put to) silence, be (stand) still, tarry, wait.
