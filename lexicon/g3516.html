νήπιος

nḗpios — G3516

Transliteration: nḗpios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3516

Derivation: from an obsolete particle (implying negation) and G2031 (ἔπος);

Definition

not speaking, i.e. an infant (minor); figuratively, a simple-minded person, an immature Christian

KJV Translation

babe, child (+ -ish)
