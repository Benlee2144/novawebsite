ἐνεργής

energḗs — G1756

Transliteration: energḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1756

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and G2041 (ἔργον);

Definition

active, operative

KJV Translation

effectual, powerful

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐνεργ-ής, ές,
later form of ἐνεργός, active, effective, μηχανὰς ἐνεργεῖς ποιοῦντες [Refs 1st c.BC+]; of medicines, strong, [Refs 1st c.AD+] more effective, πρός τινα [Refs 4th c.BC+]: superlative -έστατος, πρός τι [Refs 1st c.BC+]
