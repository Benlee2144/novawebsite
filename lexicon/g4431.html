πτῶσις

ptōsis — G4431

Transliteration: ptōsis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4431

Derivation: from the alternate of G4098 (πίπτω);

Definition

a crash, i.e. downfall (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

fall
