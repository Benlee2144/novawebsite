נָתַק

nâthaq — H5423

Transliteration: nâthaq

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5423

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to tear off

KJV Translation

break (off), burst, draw (away), lift up, pluck (away, off), pull (out), root out.
