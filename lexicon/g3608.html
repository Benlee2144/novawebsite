ὀθόνιον

othónion — G3608

Transliteration: othónion

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3608

Derivation: neuter of a presumed derivative of G3607 (ὀθόνη);

Definition

a linen bandage

KJV Translation

linen clothes
