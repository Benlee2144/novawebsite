δειπνέω

deipnéō — G1172

Transliteration: deipnéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1172

Derivation: from G1173 (δεῖπνον);

Definition

to dine, i.e. take the principle (or evening) meal

KJV Translation

sup (X -er)
