Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2711

καταψύχω

katapsýchō — G2711

Transliteration: katapsýchō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2711

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G5594 (ψύχω);

Definition

to cool down (off), i.e. refresh

KJV Translation

cool

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

κατα-ψύχω [ῡ],
cool, chill, ὕδωρ κ. τὴν ξηρὰν ἀναθυμίασιν [Refs 4th c.BC+]; αἱ ἄτομοι.. κατέψυξαν [τὸ σῶμα] [Refs 4th c.BC+]:—passive, future - ψῠγήσομαι [Refs 2nd c.AD+]: perfect -έψυγμαι: aorist -εψύχθην, also -εψύγην [ῠ] [Refs 4th c.BC+]:—to be chilled, become cold, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; of persons, κατεψυγμένοι, opposed to θερμοί, [Refs]
__2 metaphorically, οὐ -έψυξαν τὴν ὁρμήν did not allow their ardour to cool, [Refs 1st c.AD+]:— passive, κατέψυκτο τὸ πρακτικόν [Refs 1st c.AD+]
__3 cool, refresh, καταψύχει πνοή [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__II dry land after irrigation, [Refs 3rd c.BC+] — passive, of a country, χώρα κατεψυγμένη dried or parched up, [Refs 1st c.BC+]
__III intransitive, cool down, of persons, [LXX]
