ἐπιμένω

epiménō — G1961

Transliteration: epiménō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1961

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G3306 (μένω);

Definition

to stay over, i.e. remain (figuratively, persevere)

KJV Translation

abide (in), continue (in), tarry
