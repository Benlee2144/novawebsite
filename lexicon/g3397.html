Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3397

μικρόν

mikrón — G3397

Transliteration: mikrón

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3397

Derivation: masculine or neuter singular of G3398 (μικρός) (as noun);

Definition

a small space of time or degree

KJV Translation

a (little) (while)

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

little, small, short, lesser
little, small, in size quantity, etc. \2; \2; \2 small, little in age, young, not adult, \2; little, short in time, \2; μικρόν, i.e. χρόνον, a little while, a short time, \2; μετὰ μικρόν, after a little while, a little while afterwards, \2; little in number, \2; small, little in dignity, low, humble, \2; μικρόν, as an adverb, little, a little, \2
