μικρόν

mikrón — G3397

Transliteration: mikrón

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3397

Derivation: masculine or neuter singular of G3398 (μικρός) (as noun);

Definition

a small space of time or degree

KJV Translation

a (little) (while)

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

little, small, short, lesser
little, small, in size quantity, etc. Mt. 13:32; Lk. 12:32; Rev. 3:8; small, little in age, young, not adult, Mk. 15:40; little, short in time, Jn. 7:33; μικρόν, i.e. χρόνον, a little while, a short time, Jn. 13:33; μετὰ μικρόν, after a little while, a little while afterwards, Mt. 26:73; little in number, Lk. 12:32; small, little in dignity, low, humble, Mt. 10:42; 11:11; μικρόν, as an adverb, little, a little, Mt. 26:39
