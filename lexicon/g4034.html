Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4034

περιλάμπω

perilámpō — G4034

Transliteration: perilámpō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4034

Derivation: from G4012 (περί) and G2989 (λάμπω);

Definition

to illuminate all around, i.e. invest with a halo

KJV Translation

shine round about
