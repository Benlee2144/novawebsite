Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4481

Ῥεμφάν

Rhemphán — G4481

Transliteration: Rhemphán

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4481

Derivation: by incorrect transliteration for a word of Hebrew origin (H03594);

Definition

Remphan (i.e. Kijun), an Egyptian idol

KJV Translation

Remphan
