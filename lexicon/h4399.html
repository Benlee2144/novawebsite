מְלָאכָה

mᵉlâʼkâh — H4399

Transliteration: mᵉlâʼkâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4399

Derivation: from the same as H4397 (מֲלְאָךְ);

Definition

properly, deputyship, i.e. ministry; generally, employment (never servile) or work (abstractly or concretely); also property (as the result of labor)

KJV Translation

business, [phrase] cattle, [phrase] industrious, occupation, ([phrase] -pied), [phrase] officer, thing (made), use, (manner of) work((-man), -manship).
