βραδύνω

bradýnō — G1019

Transliteration: bradýnō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1019

Derivation: from G1021 (βραδύς);

Definition

to delay

KJV Translation

be slack, tarry

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

βρᾰδύνω,
future -ῠνῶ[LXX]: aorist ἐβράδῡνα [Refs 2nd c.AD+]: pluperfect ἐβεβραδύκειν [Refs 2nd c.AD+]: (βραδύς):
__I transitive, make slow, delay, [LXX]:—passive, to be delayed, τἀπὸ σοῦ βραδύνεται [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II intransitive, loiter, delay, εἰ βραδύνοιμεν βοῇ [Refs 4th c.BC+] (so in middle, χεῖρα δ᾽ οὐ βραδύνεται.. ἁρπάσαι δόρυ [Refs 5th c.BC+]: with infinitive, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]; βραδύνει σοι τοῦτ; are you slow, slack in this ? [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
