τεῖχος

teîchos — G5038

Transliteration: teîchos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5038

Derivation: akin to the base of G5088 (τίκτω);

Definition

a wall (as formative of a house)

KJV Translation

wall
