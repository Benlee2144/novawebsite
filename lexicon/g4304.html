προμελετάω

promeletáō — G4304

Transliteration: promeletáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4304

Derivation: from G4253 (πρό) and G3191 (μελετάω);

Definition

to premeditate

KJV Translation

meditate before
