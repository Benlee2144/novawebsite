אַחְזַי

ʼAchzay — H273

Transliteration: ʼAchzay

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H273

Derivation: from H270 (אָחַז); seizer;

Definition

Achzai, an Israelite

KJV Translation

Ahasai.

Appears In (1 verse)

Nehemiah 11:13

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

A man of the tribe of Levi living at the time of Exile and Return, first mentioned at 1Ch.9.12;
son of: Meshillemith (H4921);
father of: Jahzerah (H3170); also called Ahzai (KJV: Ahasai) at Neh.11.13;
Another name of me.shul.lam (מְשֻׁלָּם "Meshullam" H4918M)
