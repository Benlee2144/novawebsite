γεύομαι

geúomai — G1089

Transliteration: geúomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1089

Derivation: a primary verb;

Definition

to taste; by implication, to eat; figuratively, to experience (good or ill)

KJV Translation

eat, taste
