שְׁנַיִם

shᵉnayim — H8147

Transliteration: shᵉnayim

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H8147

Derivation: dual of H8145 (שֵׁנִי); feminine שְׁתַּיִם;

Definition

two; also (as ordinal) twofold

KJV Translation

both, couple, double, second, twain, [phrase] twelfth, [phrase] twelve, [phrase] twenty (sixscore) thousand, twice, two.
