Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G5048

τελειόω

teleióō — G5048

Transliteration: teleióō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5048

Derivation: from G5046 (τέλειος);

Definition

to complete, i.e. (literally) accomplish, or (figuratively) consummate (in character)

KJV Translation

consecrate, finish, fulfil, make) perfect
