νηστεία

nēsteía — G3521

Transliteration: nēsteía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3521

Derivation: from G3522 (νηστεύω);

Definition

abstinence (from lack of food, or voluntary and religious); specially, the fast of the Day of Atonement

KJV Translation

fast(-ing)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

νηστ-εία, ἡ,
fast, νηστεῖαι καὶ ὁρταί [NT+5th c.BC+]; νηστείας ὄζειν [Refs 4th c.BC+]; at Athens, name for the third day of the Thesmophoria, [Refs 3rd c.BC+]
