תִּפְאָרָה

tiphʼârâh — H8597

Transliteration: tiphʼârâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H8597

Derivation: or תִּפְאֶרֶת; from H6286 (פָּאַר);

Definition
