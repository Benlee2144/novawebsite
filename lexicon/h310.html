אַחַר

ʼachar — H310

Transliteration: ʼachar

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H310

Derivation: from H309 (אָחַר);

Definition

properly, the hind part; generally used as an adverb or conjunction, after (in various senses)

KJV Translation

after (that, -ward), again, at, away from, back (from, -side), behind, beside, by, follow (after, -ing), forasmuch, from, hereafter, hinder end, [phrase] out (over) live, [phrase] persecute, posterity, pursuing, remnant, seeing, since, thence(-forth), when, with.
