κόσμιος

kósmios — G2887

Transliteration: kósmios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2887

Derivation: from G2889 (κόσμος) (in its primary sense);

Definition

orderly, i.e. decorous

KJV Translation

of good behaviour, modest

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
