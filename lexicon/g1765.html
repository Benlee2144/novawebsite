ἐνισχύω

enischýō — G1765

Transliteration: enischýō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1765

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and G2480 (ἰσχύω);

Definition

to invigorate (transitively or reflexively)

KJV Translation

strengthen
