προακούω

proakoúō — G4257

Transliteration: proakoúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4257

Derivation: from G4253 (πρό) and G191 (ἀκούω);

Definition

to hear already,i.e. anticipate

KJV Translation

hear before
