συμμέτοχος

symmétochos — G4830

Transliteration: symmétochos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4830

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G3353 (μέτοχος);

Definition

a co-participant

KJV Translation

partaker
