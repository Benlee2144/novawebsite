ὅραμα

hórama — G3705

Transliteration: hórama

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3705

Derivation: from G3708 (ὁράω);

Definition

something gazed at, i.e. a spectacle (especially supernatural)

KJV Translation

sight, vision

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ὅρᾱμ-α, ατος, τό,
