Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2530

καθότι

kathóti — G2530

Transliteration: kathóti

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2530

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά);

Definition

and G3739 (ὅς) and G5100 (τὶς); according to which certain thing, i.e. as far (or inasmuch) as

KJV Translation

(according, forasmuch) as, because (that)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

καθότι,
Ionic dialect κατ-, for καθ᾽ ὅ τι (which should perhaps be written) in what manner, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; κ. γέγραπται as is written, [Refs 3rd c.BC+], etc.; so far as, inasmuch as, [Refs 2nd c.BC+]
