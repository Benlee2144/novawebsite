ἐπιβουλή

epiboulḗ — G1917

Transliteration: epiboulḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1917

Derivation: from a presumed compound of G1909 (ἐπί) and G1014 (βούλομαι);

Definition

a plan against someone, i.e. a plot

KJV Translation

laying (lying) in wait

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐπιβουλ-ή, ἡ,
