τραχηλίζω

trachēlízō — G5136

Transliteration: trachēlízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5136

Derivation: from G5137 (τράχηλος);

Definition

to seize by the throat or neck, i.e. to expose the gullet of a victim for killing (generally, to lay bare)

KJV Translation

opened
