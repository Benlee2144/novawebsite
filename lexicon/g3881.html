Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3881

παραλέγομαι

paralégomai — G3881

Transliteration: paralégomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3881

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and the middle voice of G3004 (λέγω) (in its original sense);

Definition

(specially), to lay one's course near, i.e. sail past

KJV Translation

pass, sail by
