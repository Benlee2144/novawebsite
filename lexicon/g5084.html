τήρησις

tḗrēsis — G5084

Transliteration: tḗrēsis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5084

Derivation: from G5083 (τηρέω);

Definition

a watching, i.e. (figuratively) observance, or (concretely) a prison

KJV Translation

hold
