ἀκαιρέομαι

akairéomai — G170

Transliteration: akairéomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G170

Derivation: from a compound of G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and G2540 (καιρός) (meaning unseasonable);

Definition

to be inopportune (for oneself), i.e. to fail of a proper occasion

KJV Translation

lack opportunity
