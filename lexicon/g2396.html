Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2396

ἴδε

íde — G2396

Transliteration: íde

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2396

Derivation: second person singular imperative active of G1492 (εἴδω);

Definition

used as an interjection to denote surprise; lo!

KJV Translation

behold, lo, see

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἴδε

__A (Attic dialect ἰδέ Hdn.Gr.1.431), aorist 2 imperative of εἶδον, behold, [Refs 8th c.BC+]; used by Trag. in Lyric poetry, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
