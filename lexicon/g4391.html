προϋπάρχω

proÿpárchō — G4391

Transliteration: proÿpárchō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4391

Derivation: from G4253 (πρό) and G5225 (ὑπάρχω);

Definition

to exist before, i.e. (adverbially) to be or do something previously

KJV Translation

+ be before(-time)
