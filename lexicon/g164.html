αἰχμάλωτος

aichmálōtos — G164

Transliteration: aichmálōtos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G164

Derivation: from (a spear) and a derivative of the same as G259 (ἅλωσις);

Definition

properly, a prisoner of war, i.e. (genitive case) a captive

KJV Translation

captive
