ἐνταφιάζω

entaphiázō — G1779

Transliteration: entaphiázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1779

Derivation: from a compound of G1722 (ἐν) and G5028 (τάφος);

Definition

to inswathe with cerements for interment

KJV Translation

bury
