ἐπαρκέω

eparkéō — G1884

Transliteration: eparkéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1884

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G714 (ἀρκέω);

Definition

to avail for, i.e. help

KJV Translation

relieve

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
