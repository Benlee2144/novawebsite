Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3483

ναί

naí — G3483

Transliteration: naí

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3483

Derivation: a primary particle of strong affirmation;

Definition

yes

KJV Translation

even so, surely, truth, verily, yea, yes

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ναί,
adverb, used to express strong affirmation, yea, verily, in [Refs 8th c.BC+] yea thee, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 frequently in oaths, ν. μά yea by.., see at {μ; ν}. alone, ν. τὰν Κόραν [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ν. πρὸς θεῶν ἀρήξατ᾽ [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II in answers, yes, τοῦτ᾽ ἐτήτυμο; Answ. ν. [NT+5th c.BC+]
__II.2 ν. followed by ἀλλά, etc., to mark a qualified assent, yes, but.., [Refs 4th c.BC+]; ν., κατὰ σχολήν γε ἴσως· οὐ μήν.. [Refs 5th c.BC+]
