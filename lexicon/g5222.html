ὑπάντησις

hypántēsis — G5222

Transliteration: hypántēsis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5222

Derivation: from G5221 (ὑπαντάω);

Definition

an encounter or concurrence (with G1519 (εἰς) for infinitive, in order to fall in with)

KJV Translation

meeting
