ἐπιλείπω

epileípō — G1952

Transliteration: epileípō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1952

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G3007 (λείπω);

Definition

to leave upon, i.e. (figuratively) to be insufficient for

KJV Translation

fail
