אֲשֵׁרָה

ʼăshêrâh — H842

Transliteration: ʼăshêrâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H842

Derivation: or אֲשֵׁירָה; from H833 (אָשַׁר); happy;

Definition

Asherah (or Astarte) a Phoenician goddess; also an image of the same

KJV Translation

grove. Compare H6253 (עַשְׁתֹּרֶת).
