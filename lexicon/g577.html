ἀποβάλλω

apobállō — G577

Transliteration: apobállō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G577

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G906 (βάλλω);

Definition

to throw off; figuratively, to lose

KJV Translation

cast away
