Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4994

σωφρονίζω

sōphronízō — G4994

Transliteration: sōphronízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4994

Derivation: from G4998 (σώφρων);

Definition

to make of sound mind, i.e. (figuratively) to discipline or correct

KJV Translation

teach to be sober

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

σωφρον-ίζω,
