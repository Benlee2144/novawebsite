ἀνοικοδομέω

anoikodoméō — G456

Transliteration: anoikodoméō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G456

Derivation: from G303 (ἀνά) and G3618 (οἰκοδομέω);

Definition

to rebuild

KJV Translation

build again

__II build again, rebuild, πόλιν καὶ τείχη[Refs 5th c.BC+]occupy again with buildings, [Refs 1st c.BC+]:—passive, metaphorically, to be exalted, [LXX]
