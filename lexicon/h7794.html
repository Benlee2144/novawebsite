שׁוֹר

shôwr — H7794

Transliteration: shôwr

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7794

Derivation: from H7788 (שׁוּר);

Definition

KJV Translation

bull(-ock), cow, ox, wall (by mistake for H7791 (שׁוּר)).
