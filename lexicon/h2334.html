חַוּוֹת יָעִיר

Chavvôwth Yâʻîyr — H2334

Transliteration: Chavvôwth Yâʻîyr

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2334

Derivation: from the plural of H2333 (חַוָּה) and a modification of H3265 (יָעוּר);

Definition

hamlets of Jair, a region of Palestine

KJV Translation

(Bashan-) Havoth-jair.

Appears In (4 verses)

1 Chronicles 2:23Deuteronomy 3:14Judges 10:4Numbers 32:41

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)


§ Havoth-jair = "villages of Jair"
certain villages on the east of Jordan, in Gilead or Bashan, which were taken by Jair, the son of Manasseh, and called after his name; 60 or 23 or 30 cities in total
