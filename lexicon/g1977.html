ἐπιῤῥίπτω

epirrhíptō — G1977

Transliteration: epirrhíptō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1977

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G4496 (ῥίπτω);

Definition

to throw upon (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

cast upon
