δικαιόω

dikaióō — G1344

Transliteration: dikaióō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1344

Derivation: from G1342 (δίκαιος);

Definition

to render (i.e. show or regard as) just or innocent

KJV Translation

free, justify(-ier), be righteous
