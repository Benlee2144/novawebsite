ἐπαισχύνομαι

epaischýnomai — G1870

Transliteration: epaischýnomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1870

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G153 (αἰσχύνομαι);

Definition

to feel shame for something

KJV Translation

be ashamed

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐπαισχ-ύνομαι,
future -αισχυνθήσομαι, to be ashamed at or of, τῷ οὐνόματι [Refs 5th c.BC+]; τινά or τι, [Refs 5th c.BC+]: with infinitive, to be ashamed to do, [Refs 4th c.BC+]: with participle, to be ashamed of doing or having done a thing, [Refs 5th c.BC+]: absolutely, feel shame, show a sense of shame, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II late in active, make ugly, mar, [Refs 4th c.AD+]
