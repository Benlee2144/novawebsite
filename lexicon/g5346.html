φημί

phēmí — G5346

Transliteration: phēmí

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5346

Derivation: properly, the same as the base of G5457 (φῶς) and G5316 (φαίνω);

Definition

to show or make known one's thoughts, i.e. speak or say

KJV Translation

affirm, say
