ὑγιής

hygiḗs — G5199

Transliteration: hygiḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5199

Derivation: from the base of G837 (αὐξάνω);

Definition

healthy, i.e. well (in body); figuratively, true (in doctrine)

KJV Translation

sound, whole
