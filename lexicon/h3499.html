יֶתֶר

yether — H3499

Transliteration: yether

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3499

Derivation: from H3498 (יָתַר);

Definition

properly, an overhanging, i.e. (by implication) an excess, superiority, remainder; also a small rope (as hanging free)

KJV Translation

[phrase] abundant, cord, exceeding, excellancy(-ent), what they leave, that hath left, plentifully, remnant, residue, rest, string, with.
