Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G5574

ψεύδομαι

pseúdomai — G5574

Transliteration: pseúdomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5574

Derivation: middle voice of an apparently primary verb;

Definition

to utter an untruth or attempt to deceive by falsehood

KJV Translation

falsely, lie
