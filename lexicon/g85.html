ἀδημονέω

adēmonéō — G85

Transliteration: adēmonéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G85

Derivation: from a derivative of (to be sated to loathing);

Definition

to be in distress (of mind)

KJV Translation

be full of heaviness, be very heavy

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀδημονέω,
to be sorely troubled or dismayed, be in anguish, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ὑπό τινος to be puzzled by.., [Refs 4th c.BC+], which is found only as variant in [Refs 5th c.BC+]
