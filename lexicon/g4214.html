πόσος

pósos — G4214

Transliteration: pósos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4214

Derivation: from an absolute (who, what) and G3739 (ὅς);

Definition

interrogative pronoun (of amount) how much (large, long or (plural) many)

KJV Translation

how great (long, many), what
