κεφαλίς

kephalís — G2777

Transliteration: kephalís

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2777

Derivation: from G2776 (κεφαλή);

Definition

properly, a knob, i.e. (by implication) a roll (by extension from the end of a stick on which the manuscript was rolled)

KJV Translation
