יוֹאָחָז

Yôwʼâchâz — H3099

Transliteration: Yôwʼâchâz

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3099

Derivation: a form of H3059 (יְהוֹאָחָז);

Definition

Joachaz, the name of two Israelites

KJV Translation

Jehoahaz, Joahaz.

Appears In (4 verses)

2 Chronicles 34:82 Chronicles 36:22 Chronicles 36:42 Kings 14:1

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

A king of the tribe of Judah living at the time of Divided Monarchy, first mentioned at 2Ki.23.30;
son of: Josiah (H2977) and Hamutal (H2537);
brother of: Zedekiah (H6667H);
half-brother of: Jehoiakim (H3079) and Shallum (H7967J); also called Johanan at 1Ch.3.15(?);
Another spelling of ye.ho.a.chaz (יְהוֹאָחָז "Jehoahaz" H3059H)
§ Joahaz or Jehoahaz = "Jehovah has grasped"
the father of Joah, Josiah's chronicler
