δεκάτη

dekátē — G1181

Transliteration: dekátē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1181

Derivation: feminine of G1182 (δέκατος);

Definition

a tenth, i.e. as a percentage or (technically) tithe

KJV Translation

tenth (part), tithe

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

δεκάτη, -ης, ἡ prop. fem. (sc. μέρος) of δέκατος, -η, -ον,
[in LXX for מַעֲשֵׂר: Gen.14:20, Neh.12:44, and al. ;]
a tenth part, a tithe: Heb.7:2, 4; 8, 9 (for a curious inversion of the Biblical use, see MM, see word).†
(AS)
