ἀμώμητος

amṓmētos — G298

Transliteration: amṓmētos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G298

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G3469 (μωμάομαι);

Definition

unblamable

KJV Translation

blameless
