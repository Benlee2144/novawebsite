Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3670

ὁμολογέω

homologéō — G3670

Transliteration: homologéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3670

Derivation: from a compound of the base of G3674 (ὁμοῦ) and G3056 (λόγος);

Definition

to assent, i.e. covenant, acknowledge

KJV Translation

con- (pro-)fess, confession is made, give thanks, promise
