אַף

ʼaph — H639

Transliteration: ʼaph

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H639

Derivation: from H599 (אָנַף);

Definition

properly, the nose or nostril; hence, the face, and occasionally a person; also (from the rapid breathing in passion) ire

KJV Translation

anger(-gry), [phrase] before, countenance, face, [phrase] forebearing, forehead, [phrase] (long-) suffering, nose, nostril, snout, [idiom] worthy, wrath.
