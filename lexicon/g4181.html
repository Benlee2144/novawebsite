πολυμερῶς

polymerōs — G4181

Transliteration: polymerōs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4181

Derivation: adverb from a compound of G4183 (πολύς) and G3313 (μέρος);

Definition

in many portions, i.e. variously as to time and agency (piecemeal)

KJV Translation

at sundry times

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
