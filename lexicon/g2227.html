ζωοποιέω

zōopoiéō — G2227

Transliteration: zōopoiéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2227

Derivation: from the same as G2226 (ζῶον) and G4160 (ποιέω);

Definition

to (re-)vitalize (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

make alive, give life, quicken

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
