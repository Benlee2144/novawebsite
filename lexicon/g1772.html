ἔννομος

énnomos — G1772

Transliteration: énnomos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1772

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and G3551 (νόμος);

Definition

(subjectively) legal, or (objectively) subject to

KJV Translation

lawful, under law

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἔννομος, ον,

__2 of persons, keeping within the law, upright, just, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; also, subject to the law, μὴ ὢν ἄνομος Θεοῦ, ἀλλ᾽ ἔ. Χριστοῦ [NT]
__II (νέμομαι) feeding in, i.e. inhabiting, οἳ γᾶς τότ᾽ ἦσαν ἔννομοι [Refs 4th c.BC+]
