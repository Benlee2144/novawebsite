παίω

paíō — G3817

Transliteration: paíō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3817

Derivation: a primary verb;

Definition

to hit (as if by a single blow and less violently than G5180 (τύπτω)); specially, to sting (as a scorpion)

KJV Translation

smite, strike
