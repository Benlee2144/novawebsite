ἐπισκέπτομαι

episképtomai — G1980

Transliteration: episképtomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1980

Derivation: middle voice from G1909 (ἐπί) and the base of G4649 (σκοπός);

Definition

to inspect, i.e. (by implication) to select; by extension, to go to see, relieve

KJV Translation

look out, visit

ἐπι-σκέπτομαι
(late form of ἐπισκοπέω, which see), [in LXX very freq., chiefly for פָּקַד ;]
__1. to inspect, examine.
__2. (a) to visit: with accusative, Act.7:23 15:36 (cf. Jdg.15:1); especially, the sick and afflicted (as in MGr. and sometimes in cl.), Mat.25:36, 43, Jas.1:27 (cf. \2);
__(b) in LXX and NT (as פָּקַד in Gen.21:1, Exo.4:31, Psa.8:5, al.), to visit with help, to care for: Luk.1:68, 78 7:16, Act.15:14, Heb.2:6;
__(with) to visit with punishment (Jer.9:25, Psa.89:33, al.; cf. MM, Exp., xiv; Cremer, 863).†
