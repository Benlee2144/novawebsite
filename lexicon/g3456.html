Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3456

μυκτηρίζω

myktērízō — G3456

Transliteration: myktērízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3456

Derivation: from a derivative of the base of G3455 (μυκάομαι) (meaning snout, as that whence lowing proceeds);

Definition

to make mouths at, i.e. ridicule

KJV Translation

mock
