Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H3426

יֵשׁ

yêsh — H3426

Transliteration: yêsh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3426

Derivation: perhaps from an unused root meaning to stand out, or exist; entity; used adverbially or as a copula for the substantive verb (H1961 (הָיָה));

Definition

there is or are (or any other form of the verb to be, as may suit the connection)

KJV Translation

(there) are, (he, it, shall, there, there may, there shall, there should) be, thou do, had, hast, (which) hath, (I, shalt, that) have, (he, it, there) is, substance, it (there) was, (there) were, ye will, thou wilt, wouldest.
