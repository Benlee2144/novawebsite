ἄνυδρος

ánydros — G504

Transliteration: ánydros

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G504

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and G5204 (ὕδωρ);

Definition

waterless, i.e. dry

KJV Translation

dry, without water

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἄνυδρ-ος, ον,
(ὕδωρ) waterless, of arid countries, [Refs 8th c.BC+] Trag. cited in Phot.p.151 R.; especially without spring-water, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ἡ ἄνυδρος (i.e. γῆ) [LXX+4th c.BC+]; of seasons, [Refs 5th c.BC+], of a corpse, deprived of funeral lustrations; unwatered, σμύρνα[Refs]
__II ἄνυδρον, τό, ={στρύχνον μανικόν}, [Refs 1st c.AD+]
