ἔνεδρον

énedron — G1749

Transliteration: énedron

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1749

Derivation: neuter of the same as G1747 (ἐνέδρα);

Definition

an ambush, i.e. (figuratively) murderous design

KJV Translation

lying in wait
