τιμωρέω

timōréō — G5097

Transliteration: timōréō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5097

Derivation: from a comparative of G5092 (τιμή) and ouros (a guard);

Definition

properly, to protect one's honor, i.e. to avenge (inflict a penalty)

KJV Translation

punish
