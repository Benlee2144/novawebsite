μέτωπον

métōpon — G3359

Transliteration: métōpon

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3359

Derivation: from G3326 (μετά) and ops (the face);

Definition

the forehead (as opposite the countenance)

KJV Translation

forehead
