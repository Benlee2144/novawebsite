יְהוּדָה

Yᵉhûwdâh — H3063

Transliteration: Yᵉhûwdâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3063

Derivation: from H3034 (יָדָה); celebrated;

Definition

Jehudah (or Judah), the name of five Israelites; also of the tribe descended from the first, and of its territory

KJV Translation

Judah.
