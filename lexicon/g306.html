ἀναβάλλομαι

anabállomai — G306

Transliteration: anabállomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G306

Derivation: middle voice from G303 (ἀνά) and G906 (βάλλω);

Definition

to put off (for oneself)

KJV Translation

defer
