φρόνημα

phrónēma — G5427

Transliteration: phrónēma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5427

Derivation: from G5426 (φρονέω);

Definition

(mental) inclination or purpose

KJV Translation

(be, + be carnally, + be spiritually) mind(-ed)
