μακροχρόνιος

makrochrónios — G3118

Transliteration: makrochrónios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3118

Derivation: from G3117 (μακρός) and G5550 (χρόνος);

Definition

long-timed, i.e. long-lived

KJV Translation

live long

__2 dwelling a long time, [LXX]
