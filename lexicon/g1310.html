διαφημίζω

diaphēmízō — G1310

Transliteration: diaphēmízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1310

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and a derivative of G5345 (φήμη);

Definition

to report thoroughly, i.e. divulgate

KJV Translation

blaze abroad, commonly report, spread abroad, fame
