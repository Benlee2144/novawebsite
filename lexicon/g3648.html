ὁλόκληρος

holóklēros — G3648

Transliteration: holóklēros

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3648

Derivation: from G3650 (ὅλος) and G2819 (κλῆρος);

Definition

complete in every part, i.e. perfectly sound (in body)

KJV Translation

entire, whole

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
