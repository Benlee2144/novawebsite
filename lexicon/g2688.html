κατάστημα

katástēma — G2688

Transliteration: katástēma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2688

Derivation: from G2525 (καθίστημι);

Definition

properly, a position or condition, i.e. (subjectively) demeanor

KJV Translation

behaviour
