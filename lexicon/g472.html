ἀντέχομαι

antéchomai — G472

Transliteration: antéchomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G472

Derivation: from G473 (ἀντί) and the middle voice of G2192 (ἔχω);

Definition

to hold oneself opposite to, i.e. (by implication) adhere to; by extension to care for

KJV Translation

hold fast, hold to, support
