προέρχομαι

proérchomai — G4281

Transliteration: proérchomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4281

Derivation: from G4253 (πρό) and G2064 (ἔρχομαι) (including its alternate);

Definition

to go onward, precede (in place or time)

KJV Translation

go before (farther, forward), outgo, pass on
