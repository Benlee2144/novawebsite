Σαμοθρᾴκη

Samothráikē — G4543

Transliteration: Samothráikē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4543

Derivation: from G4544 (Σάμος) and (Thrace);

Definition

Samo-thrace (Samos of Thrace), an island in the Mediterranean

KJV Translation

Samothracia
