ἱερατεία

hierateía — G2405

Transliteration: hierateía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2405

Derivation: from G2407 (ἱερατεύω);

Definition

priestliness, i.e. the sacerdotal function

KJV Translation

office of the priesthood, priest's office

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἱερᾱτ-εία, ἡ,
priesthood, [NT+4th c.BC+], etc.: Ionic dialect ἱρητήη [Refs 5th c.BC+]; later ἱερητείη and ἱέρᾱτ-α [Refs 4th c.BC+]
