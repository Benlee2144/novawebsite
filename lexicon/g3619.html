οἰκοδομή

oikodomḗ — G3619

Transliteration: oikodomḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3619

Derivation: feminine (abstract) of a compound of G3624 (οἶκος) and the base of G1430 (δῶμα);

Definition

architecture, i.e. (concretely) a structure; figuratively, confirmation

KJV Translation

building, edify(-ication, -ing)
