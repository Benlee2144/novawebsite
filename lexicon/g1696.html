ἐμμένω

emménō — G1696

Transliteration: emménō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1696

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and G3306 (μένω);

Definition

to stay in the same place, i.e. (figuratively) persevere

KJV Translation

continue
