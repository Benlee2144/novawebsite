Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4853

συμφυλέτης

symphylétēs — G4853

Transliteration: symphylétēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4853

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and a derivative of G5443 (φυλή);

Definition

a co-tribesman, i.e. native of the same country

KJV Translation

countryman

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

συμφῡλ-έτης, ου, ὁ,
of the same φυλή, [Refs]: generally, fellow-countryman, [NT]

Appears In (1 verse)

1 Thessalonians 2:14
