Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H7121

קָרָא

qârâʼ — H7121

Transliteration: qârâʼ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7121

Derivation: a primitive root (rather identical with H7122 (קָרָא) through the idea of accosting a person met);

Definition

to call out to (i.e. properly, address by name, but used in a wide variety of applications)

KJV Translation

bewray (self), that are bidden, call (for, forth, self, upon), cry (unto), (be) famous, guest, invite, mention, (give) name, preach, (make) proclaim(-ation), pronounce, publish, read, renowned, say.
