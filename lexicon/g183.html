ἀκατάσχετος

akatáschetos — G183

Transliteration: akatáschetos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G183

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G2722 (κατέχω);

Definition

unrestrainable

KJV Translation

unruly

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

ἀκατάσχετος, -ον
(κατέχω), [in LXX: Job.31:11, \2 * ;]
that cannot be restrained: Jas.3:8 Rec. (for -στατος, which see).†
