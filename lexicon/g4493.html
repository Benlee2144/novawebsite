ῥιπή

rhipḗ — G4493

Transliteration: rhipḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4493

Derivation: from G4496 (ῥίπτω);

Definition

a jerk (of the eye, i.e. (by analogy) an instant)

KJV Translation

twinkling
