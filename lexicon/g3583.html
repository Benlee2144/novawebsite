Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G3583

ξηραίνω

xēraínō — G3583

Transliteration: xēraínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3583

Derivation: from G3584 (ξηρός);

Definition

to desiccate; by implication, to shrivel, to mature

KJV Translation

dry up, pine away, be ripe, wither (away)
