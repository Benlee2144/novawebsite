Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G524

ἀπαλγέω

apalgéō — G524

Transliteration: apalgéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G524

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and (to smart);

Definition

to grieve out, i.e. become apathetic

KJV Translation

be past feeling

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀπαλγ-έω,
put away sorrow for, τὰ ἴδια[Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II generally, to be despondent, ἀ. ταῖς ἐλπίσιν[NT+2nd c.BC+]

Appears In (1 verse)

Ephesians 4:19
