ἀλοάω

aloáō — G248

Transliteration: aloáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G248

Derivation: from the same as G257 (ἅλων);

Definition

to tread out grain

KJV Translation

thresh, tread out the corn
