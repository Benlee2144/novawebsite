ἐπακολουθέω

epakolouthéō — G1872

Transliteration: epakolouthéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1872

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and G190 (ἀκολουθέω);

Definition

to accompany

KJV Translation

follow (after)
