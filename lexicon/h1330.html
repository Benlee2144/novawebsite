בְּתוּלָה

bᵉthûwlâh — H1330

Transliteration: bᵉthûwlâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H1330

Derivation: feminine passive participle of an unused root meaning to separate;

Definition

a virgin (from her privacy); sometimes (by continuation) a bride; also (figuratively) a city or state

KJV Translation

maid, virgin.
