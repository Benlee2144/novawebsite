δαπανάω

dapanáō — G1159

Transliteration: dapanáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1159

Derivation: from G1160 (δαπάνη);

Definition

to expend, i.e. (in a good sense) to incur cost, or (in a bad one) to waste

KJV Translation

be at charges, consume, spend
