Σύρος

Sýros — G4948

Transliteration: Sýros

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4948

Derivation: from the same as G4947 (Συρία);

Definition

a Syran (i.e. probably Tyrian), a native of Syria

KJV Translation
