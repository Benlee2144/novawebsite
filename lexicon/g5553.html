χρυσίον

chrysíon — G5553

Transliteration: chrysíon

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5553

Derivation: diminutive of G5557 (χρυσός);

Definition

a golden article, i.e. gold plating, ornament, or coin

KJV Translation

gold
