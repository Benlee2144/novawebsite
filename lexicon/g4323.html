προσανατίθημι

prosanatíthēmi — G4323

Transliteration: prosanatíthēmi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4323

Derivation: from G4314 (πρός) and G394 (ἀνατίθεμαι);

Definition

to lay up in addition, i.e. (middle voice and figuratively) to impart or (by implication) to consult

KJV Translation

in conference add, confer
