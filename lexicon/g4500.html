ῥοιζηδόν

rhoizēdón — G4500

Transliteration: rhoizēdón

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4500

Derivation: adverb from a derivative of (a whir);

Definition

whizzingly, i.e. with a crash

KJV Translation

with a great noise
