αἰδώς

aidṓs — G127

Transliteration: aidṓs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G127

Derivation: perhaps from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and G1492 (εἴδω) (through the idea of downcast eyes);

Definition

bashfulness, i.e. (towards men), modesty or (towards God) awe

KJV Translation

reverence, shamefacedness

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

__2 regard for others, respect, reverence, αἰδοῦς οὐδεμιῆς ἔτυχον[Refs 6th c.BC+]; αἰ. τοκέων respect for them, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; τὴν ἐμὴν αἰδῶ respect for me, [Refs 4th c.BC+]; regard for friends, αἰδοῦς ἀχαλκεύτοισιν ἔζευκται πέδαις[Refs 5th c.BC+]; especially regard for the helpless, compassion, αἰδοῦς κῦρσαι[Refs 5th c.BC+]; forgiveness, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
