ἀρκέω

arkéō — G714

Transliteration: arkéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G714

Derivation: apparently a primary verb (but probably akin to G142 (αἴρω) through the idea of raising a barrier);

Definition

properly, to ward off, i.e. (by implication) to avail (figuratively, be satisfactory)

KJV Translation

be content, be enough, suffice, be sufficient
