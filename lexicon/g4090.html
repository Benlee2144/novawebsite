πικρῶς

pikrōs — G4090

Transliteration: pikrōs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4090

Derivation: adverb from G4089 (πικρός);

Definition

bitterly, i.e. (figuratively) violently

KJV Translation

bitterly
