אָבָה

ʼâbâh — H14

Transliteration: ʼâbâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H14

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to breathe after, i.e. (figuratively) to be acquiescent

KJV Translation

consent, rest content will, be willing.

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

1) to be willing, consent
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to be willing
1a2) to consent, yield to, accept
1a3) to desire
