צָמַח

Transliteration: tsâmach

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6779

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

bear, bring forth, (cause to, make to) bud (forth), (cause to, make to) grow (again, up), (cause to) spring (forth, up).
