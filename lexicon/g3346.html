μετατίθημι

metatíthēmi — G3346

Transliteration: metatíthēmi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3346

Derivation: from G3326 (μετά) and G5087 (τίθημι);

Definition

to transfer, i.e. (literally) transport, (by implication) exchange, (reflexively) change sides, or (figuratively) pervert

KJV Translation

carry over, change, remove, translate, turn
