ἀποθήκη

apothḗkē — G596

Transliteration: apothḗkē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G596

Derivation: from G659 (ἀποτίθημι);

Definition

a repository, i.e. granary

KJV Translation

barn, garner

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀπο-θήκη, ἡ,
any place wherein to lay up a thing, magazine, storehouse, [Refs 5th c.BC+]burial-place, [Refs]
__2 refuge, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II anything laid by, store, ἀποθήκην ποιεῖσθαι ἐς τὸν Πέρσην lay up store of favour with him, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
