προτείνω

proteínō — G4385

Transliteration: proteínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4385

Derivation: from G4253 (πρό) and (to stretch);

Definition

to protend, i.e. tie prostrate (for scourging)

KJV Translation

bind
