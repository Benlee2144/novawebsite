ὄρνις

órnis — G3733

Transliteration: órnis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3733

Derivation: probably from a prolonged form of the base of G3735 (ὄρος);

Definition

a bird (as rising in the air), i.e. (specially), a hen (or female domestic fowl)

KJV Translation

hen
