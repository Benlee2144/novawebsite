μανθάνω

manthánō — G3129

Transliteration: manthánō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3129

Derivation: prolongation from a primary verb, another form of which, , is used as an alternate in certain tenses;

Definition

to learn (in any way)

KJV Translation

learn, understand
