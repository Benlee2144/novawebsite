δοῦλον

doûlon — G1400

Transliteration: doûlon

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1400

Derivation: neuter of G1401 (δοῦλος);

Definition

subservient

KJV Translation

servant

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

servant, slave; in the NT a person owned as a possession for various lengths of times (Hebrew slaves no more than seven years, Gentile slaves without time limit), of lower social status than free persons or masters; slaves could earn or purchase their freedom
a male slave, or servant, of various degrees, \2, and frequently ; a servitor, person of mean condition, \2 feminine δούλη, a female slave; a handmaiden, \2; Acts 2:18; δοῦλος, used figuratively, in a bad sense, one involved in moral or spiritual thraldom, \2; \2; \2 \2 in a good sense, a devoted servant or minister, Acts 16:17; \2 one pledged or bound to serve, \2 \2
