ἀκριβόω

akribóō — G198

Transliteration: akribóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G198

Derivation: from the same as G196 (ἀκριβέστατος);

Definition

to be exact, i.e. ascertain

KJV Translation

enquire diligently
