εὐθέως

euthéōs — G2112

Transliteration: euthéōs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2112

Derivation: adverb from G2117 (εὐθύς);

Definition

directly, i.e. at once or soon

KJV Translation

anon, as soon as, forthwith, immediately, shortly, straightway

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
