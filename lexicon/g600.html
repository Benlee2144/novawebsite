ἀποκαθίστημι

apokathístēmi — G600

Transliteration: apokathístēmi

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G600

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G2525 (καθίστημι);

Definition

to reconstitute (in health, home or organization)

KJV Translation

restore (again)
