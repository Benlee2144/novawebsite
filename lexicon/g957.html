βελτίον

beltíon — G957

Transliteration: beltíon

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G957

Derivation: neuter of a compound of a derivative of G906 (βάλλω) (used for the comparative of G18 (ἀγαθός));

Definition

better

KJV Translation

very well

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

βελτ-ίων, ον,
