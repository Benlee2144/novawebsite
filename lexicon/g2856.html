Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2856

κολοβόω

kolobóō — G2856

Transliteration: kolobóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2856

Derivation: from a derivative of the base of G2849 (κολάζω);

Definition

to dock, i.e. (figuratively) abridge

KJV Translation

shorten

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

κολοβ-όω,

__II of Time, curtail, shorten, τὰς ἡμέρας [NT]
