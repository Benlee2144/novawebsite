ἐπιφανής

epiphanḗs — G2016

Transliteration: epiphanḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2016

Derivation: from G2014 (ἐπιφαίνω);

Definition

conspicuous, i.e. (figuratively) memorable

KJV Translation

notable
