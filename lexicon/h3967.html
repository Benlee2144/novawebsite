מֵאָה

mêʼâh — H3967

Transliteration: mêʼâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3967

Derivation: or מֵאיָה; properly, a primitive numeral

Definition

a hundred; also as a multiplicative and a fraction

KJV Translation

hundred((-fold), -th), [phrase] sixscore.
