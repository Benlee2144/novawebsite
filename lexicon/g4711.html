σπυρίς

spyrís — G4711

Transliteration: spyrís

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4711

Derivation: from G4687 (σπείρω) (as woven);

Definition

a hamper or lunch-receptacle

KJV Translation

basket
