κατιόω

katióō — G2728

Transliteration: katióō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2728

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and a derivative of G2447 (ἰός);

Definition

to rust down, i.e. corrode

KJV Translation

canker

passive, become rusty, tarnished, [LXX+1st c.BC+]
