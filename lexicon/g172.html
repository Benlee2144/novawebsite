ἄκακος

ákakos — G172

Transliteration: ákakos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G172

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and G2556 (κακός);

Definition

not bad, i.e. (objectively) innocent or (subjectively) unsuspecting

KJV Translation

harmless, simple

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἄκᾰκ-ος, ον,
unknowing of ill, guileless, [NT+5th c.BC+]
__2 innocent, simple (compare εὐήθης), [Refs 4th c.BC+]. adverb -κως[Refs 4th c.BC+]
__II unharmed, [Refs 7th c.BC+]
__II.2 unadulterated, [Refs 6th c.AD+]
