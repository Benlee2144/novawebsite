συνεργός

synergós — G4904

Transliteration: synergós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4904

Derivation: from a presumed compound of G4862 (σύν) and the base of G2041 (ἔργον);

Definition

a co-laborer, i.e. coadjutor

KJV Translation

companion in labour, (fellow-)helper(-labourer, -worker), labourer together with, workfellow
