ταλαιπωρέω

talaipōréō — G5003

Transliteration: talaipōréō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5003

Derivation: from G5005 (ταλαίπωρος);

Definition

to be wretched, i.e. realize one's own misery

KJV Translation

be afflicted
