Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H3526

כָּבַס

kâbaç — H3526

Transliteration: kâbaç

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3526

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to trample; hence, to wash (properly, by stamping with the feet), whether literal (including the fulling process) or figurative

KJV Translation

fuller, wash(-ing).
