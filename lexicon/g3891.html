παρανομέω

paranoméō — G3891

Transliteration: paranoméō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3891

Derivation: from a compound of G3844 (παρά) and G3551 (νόμος);

Definition

to be opposed to law, i.e. to transgress

KJV Translation

contrary to law
