σύγχυσις

sýnchysis — G4799

Transliteration: sýnchysis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4799

Derivation: from G4797 (συγχέω);

Definition

commixture, i.e. (figuratively) riotous disturbance

KJV Translation

confusion
