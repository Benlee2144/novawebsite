παραχρῆμα

parachrēma — G3916

Transliteration: parachrēma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3916

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and G5536 (χρῆμα) (in its original sense);

Definition

at the thing itself, i.e. instantly

KJV Translation

forthwith, immediately, presently, straightway, soon
