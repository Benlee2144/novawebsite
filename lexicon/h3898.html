לָחַם

lâcham — H3898

Transliteration: lâcham

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3898

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to feed on; figuratively, to consume; by implication, to battle (as destruction)

KJV Translation

devour, eat, [idiom] ever, fight(-ing), overcome, prevail, (make) war(-ring).
