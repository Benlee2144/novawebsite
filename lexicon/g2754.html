κενοδοξία

kenodoxía — G2754

Transliteration: kenodoxía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2754

Derivation: from G2755 (κενόδοξος);

Definition

empty glorying, i.e. self-conceit

KJV Translation

vain-glory

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

κενοδοξ-ία, ἡ,
liability to vain imagination, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II vanity, conceit, [NT+2nd c.BC+]; especially of false prudery, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]

Appears In (1 verse)

Philippians 2:3
