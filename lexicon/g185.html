ἀκέραιος

akéraios — G185

Transliteration: akéraios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G185

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G2767 (κεράννυμι);

Definition

unmixed, i.e. (figuratively) innocent

KJV Translation

harmless, simple
