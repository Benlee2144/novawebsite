אִשָּׁה

ʼishshâh — H802

Transliteration: ʼishshâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H802

Derivation: feminine of H376 (אִישׁ) or H582 (אֱנוֹשׁ); irregular plural, נָשִׁים;(used in the same wide sense as H582 (אֱנוֹשׁ))

Definition

a woman

KJV Translation

(adulter) ess, each, every, female, [idiom] many, [phrase] none, one, [phrase] together, wife, woman. Often unexpressed in English.

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

: another
1) woman, wife, female
1a) woman (opposite of man)
1b) wife (woman married to a man)
1c) female (of animals)
1d) each, every (pronoun)
