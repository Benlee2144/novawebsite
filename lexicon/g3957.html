πάσχα

páscha — G3957

Transliteration: páscha

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3957

Derivation: of Chaldee origin (compare H06453);

Definition

the Passover (the meal, the day, the festival or the special sacrifices connected with it)

KJV Translation

Easter, Passover

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

πάσχα, τό,
indeclinable, the Hebrew “Passover” (from pāsa[hudot] 'pass over') or Paschal feast, [LXX]
__2 paschal supper, [NT]
__3 paschal lamb, θύειν τὸ π. [LXX+NT]
