זָרַע

zâraʻ — H2232

Transliteration: zâraʻ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2232

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to sow; figuratively, to disseminate, plant, fructify

KJV Translation

bear, conceive seed, set with sow(-er), yield.
