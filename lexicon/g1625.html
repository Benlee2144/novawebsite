ἐκτρέφω

ektréphō — G1625

Transliteration: ektréphō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1625

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G5142 (τρέφω);

Definition

to rear up to maturity, i.e. (genitive case) to cherish or train

KJV Translation

bring up, nourish

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐκτρέφω,
