διαγίνομαι

diagínomai — G1230

Transliteration: diagínomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1230

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G1096 (γίνομαι);

Definition

to elapse meanwhile

KJV Translation

X after, be past, be spent

(Ion. and late Gk. for διαγίγν-), [in LXX, \2 * ;]
__1. to go through, to pass, e.g. τ. νύκτα; absol., to live.
