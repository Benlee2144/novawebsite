Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1500

εἰκῆ

eikē — G1500

Transliteration: eikē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1500

Derivation: probably from G1502 (εἴκω) (through the idea of failure);

Definition

idly, i.e. without reason (or effect)

KJV Translation

without a cause, (in) vain(-ly)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

εἰκῇ,
without plan or purpose, at random, at a venture, [Refs 6th c.BC+]
__II in vain, [NT+1st c.BC+]
__III slightly, moderately, ἀγγεῖα εἰ. πεπυρωμένα [Refs 2nd c.BC+]
