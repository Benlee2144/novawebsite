ἀνέκλειπτος

anékleiptos — G413

Transliteration: anékleiptos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G413

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G1587 (ἐκλείπω);

Definition

not left out, i.e. (by implication) inexhaustible

KJV Translation

that faileth not

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
