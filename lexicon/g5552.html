χρύσεος

chrýseos — G5552

Transliteration: chrýseos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5552

Derivation: from G5557 (χρυσός);

Definition

made of gold

KJV Translation

of gold, golden

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

Χρύσεος, -εα, -εον (-οῦς, -ῆ, -οῦν; on accusative sing. fem., -ᾶν, Rev.1:13 and genitive pl. uncontr., -εων, Rev.2:1, LTr., see M, Pr., 48 and cf. Thackeray, Gr., x, 172 f.)
(χρυσός), [in LXX chiefly for זָהָב ;]
golden, i.e. made or overlaid with gold: 2Ti.2:20, Heb.9:4, Rev.1:12-13, 20 2:1 4:4 5:8 8:3 9:13, 20 14:14 15:6-7 17:4 21:15.†
