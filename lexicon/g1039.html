βύσσινος

býssinos — G1039

Transliteration: býssinos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1039

Derivation: from G1040 (βύσσος);

Definition

made of linen (neuter a linen cloth)

KJV Translation

fine linen
