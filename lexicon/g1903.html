ἐπενδύτης

ependýtēs — G1903

Transliteration: ependýtēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1903

Derivation: from G1902 (ἐπενδύομαι);

Definition

a wrapper, i.e. outer garment

KJV Translation
