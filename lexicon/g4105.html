πλανάω

planáō — G4105

Transliteration: planáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4105

Derivation: from G4106 (πλάνη);

Definition

to (properly, cause to) roam (from safety, truth, or virtue)

KJV Translation

go astray, deceive, err, seduce, wander, be out of the way
