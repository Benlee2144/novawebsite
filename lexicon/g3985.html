πειράζω

peirázō — G3985

Transliteration: peirázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3985

Derivation: from G3984 (πεῖρα);

Definition

to test (objectively), i.e. endeavor, scrutinize, entice, discipline

KJV Translation

assay, examine, go about, prove, tempt(-er), try
