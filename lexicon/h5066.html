Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H5066

נָגַשׁ

nâgash — H5066

Transliteration: nâgash

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5066

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to be or come (causatively, bring) near (for any purpose); euphemistically, to lie with a woman; as an enemy, to attack; religious to worship; causatively, to present; figuratively, to adduce an argument; by reversal, to stand back

KJV Translation

(make to) approach (nigh), bring (forth, hither, near), (cause to) come (hither, near, nigh), give place, go hard (up), (be, draw, go) near (nigh), offer, overtake, present, put, stand.
