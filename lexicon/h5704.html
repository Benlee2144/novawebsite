Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H5704

עַד

ʻad — H5704

Transliteration: ʻad

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5704

Derivation: properly, the same as H5703 (עַד) (used as a preposition, adverb or conjunction; especially with a preposition);

Definition

as far (or long, or much) as, whether of space (even unto) or time (during, while, until) or degree (equally with)

KJV Translation

against, and, as, at, before, by (that), even (to), for(-asmuch as), (hither-) to, [phrase] how long, into, as long (much) as, (so) that, till, toward, until, when, while, ([phrase] as) yet.
