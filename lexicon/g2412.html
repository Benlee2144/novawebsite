ἱεροπρεπής

hieroprepḗs — G2412

Transliteration: hieroprepḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2412

Derivation: from G2413 (ἱερός) and the same as G4241 (πρέπω);

Definition
