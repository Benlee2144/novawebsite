συγγενής

syngenḗs — G4773

Transliteration: syngenḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4773

Derivation: from G4862 (σύν) and G1085 (γένος);

Definition

a relative (by blood); by extension, a fellow countryman

KJV Translation

cousin, kin(-sfolk, -sman)
