καταλλαγή

katallagḗ — G2643

Transliteration: katallagḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2643

Derivation: from G2644 (καταλλάσσω);

Definition

exchange (figuratively, adjustment), i.e. restoration to (the divine) favor

KJV Translation

atonement, reconciliation(-ing)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

καταλλ-ᾰγή, ἡ,
exchange, especially of money, [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__2 money-changer's profit, agio, [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__3 freight, merchandise, metaphorically, [ἀραὶ] βαρεῖαι κ.[Refs 4th c.BC+]
__4 change, difference, [Refs 1st c.BC+]
__II change from enmity to friendship, reconciliation, καταλλαγὰς ποιεῖσθαι πρός τινας [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II.2 reconciliation of sinners with God, [NT]
