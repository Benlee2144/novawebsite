ἐξετάζω

exetázō — G1833

Transliteration: exetázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1833

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and (to examine);

Definition

to test thoroughly (by questions), i.e. ascertain or interrogate

KJV Translation

ask, enquire, search
