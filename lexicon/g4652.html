σκοτεινός

skoteinós — G4652

Transliteration: skoteinós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4652

Derivation: from G4655 (σκότος);

Definition

opaque, i.e. (figuratively) benighted

KJV Translation

dark, full of darkness

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
