στρεβλόω

streblóō — G4761

Transliteration: streblóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4761

Derivation: from a derivative of G4762 (στρέφω);

Definition

to wrench, i.e. (specially), to torture (by the rack), but only figuratively, to pervert

KJV Translation

wrest

στρεβλ-όω,

__II twist or wrench a dislocated limb, with a view to setting it, σ. τὸν πόδα [Refs 5th c.BC+]; also of wrestlers, [Refs 2nd c.AD+] (passive):—middle, ἐς τοὐπίσω τὰς χεῖρας σ. [Refs 4th c.AD+]:—passive, στρεβλωθῆναι acquire a squint, [Refs 1st c.AD+]

__II.3 metaphorically, pervert or distort words, [NT]:—passive, [LXX]
