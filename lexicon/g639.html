ἀπορέω

aporéō — G639

Transliteration: aporéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G639

Derivation: from a compound of G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and the base of G4198 (πορεύομαι);

Definition

to have no way out, i.e. be at a loss (mentally)

KJV Translation

(stand in) doubt, be perplexed
