Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G2343

θησαυρίζω

thēsaurízō — G2343

Transliteration: thēsaurízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2343

Derivation: from G2344 (θησαυρός);

Definition

to amass or reserve (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

lay up (treasure), (keep) in store, (heap) treasure (together, up)
