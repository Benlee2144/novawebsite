προστάτις

prostátis — G4368

Transliteration: prostátis

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4368

Derivation: feminine of a derivative of G4291 (προΐστημι);

Definition

a patroness, i.e. assistant

KJV Translation
