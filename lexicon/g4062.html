περιτρέπω

peritrépō — G4062

Transliteration: peritrépō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4062

Derivation: from G4012 (περί) and the base of G5157 (τροπή);

Definition

to turn around, i.e. (mentally) to craze

KJV Translation

+ make mad
