μαρτύρομαι

martýromai — G3143

Transliteration: martýromai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3143

Derivation: middle voice from G3144 (μάρτυς);

Definition

to be adduced as a witness, i.e. (figuratively) to obtest (in affirmation or exhortation)

KJV Translation

take to record, testify
