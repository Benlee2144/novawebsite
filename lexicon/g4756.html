στρατιά

stratiá — G4756

Transliteration: stratiá

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4756

Derivation: feminine of a derivative of (an army;

Definition

from the base of G4766 (στρώννυμι), as encamped); camp-likeness, i.e. an army, i.e. (figuratively) the angels, the celestial luminaries

KJV Translation

host
