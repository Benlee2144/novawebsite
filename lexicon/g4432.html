πτωχεία

ptōcheía — G4432

Transliteration: ptōcheía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4432

Derivation: from G4433 (πτωχεύω);

Definition

beggary, i.e. indigence (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

poverty
