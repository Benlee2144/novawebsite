ἐκπίπτω

ekpíptō — G1601

Transliteration: ekpíptō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1601

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G4098 (πίπτω);

Definition

to drop away; specially, be driven out of one's course; figuratively, to lose, become inefficient

KJV Translation

be cast, fail, fall (away, off), take none effect
