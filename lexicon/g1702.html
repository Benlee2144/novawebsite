Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1702

ἐμπαίζω

empaízō — G1702

Transliteration: empaízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1702

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and G3815 (παίζω);

Definition

to jeer at, i.e. deride

KJV Translation

mock
