βαθύς

bathýs — G901

Transliteration: bathýs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G901

Derivation: from the base of G939 (βάσις);

Definition

profound (as going down), literally or figuratively

KJV Translation

deep, very early
