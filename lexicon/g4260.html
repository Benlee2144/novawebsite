Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4260

προβαίνω

probaínō — G4260

Transliteration: probaínō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4260

Derivation: from G4253 (πρό) and the base of G939 (βάσις);

Definition

to walk forward, i.e. advance (literally, or in years)

KJV Translation

+ be of a great age, go farther (on), be well stricken
