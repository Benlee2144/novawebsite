κατεσθίω

katesthíō — G2719

Transliteration: katesthíō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2719

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G2068 (ἐσθίω) (including its alternate);

Definition

to eat down, i.e. devour (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

devour
