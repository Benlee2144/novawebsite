Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1402

δουλόω

doulóō — G1402

Transliteration: doulóō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1402

Derivation: from G1401 (δοῦλος);

Definition

to enslave (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

bring into (be under) bondage, X given, become (make) servant

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

δουλ-όω,
enslave, [Refs 5th c.BC+]:—mostly in passive, to be enslaved, ὑπὸ Πέρσῃσι, ὑπὸ Ἁρπάγου, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; αἱ ψυχαὶ δεδούλωνται [Refs 5th c.BC+]:—middle (with perfect δεδουλῶσθαι [Refs 5th c.BC+], make subject to oneself, enslave, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
