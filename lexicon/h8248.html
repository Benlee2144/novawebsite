שָׁקָה

shâqâh — H8248

Transliteration: shâqâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H8248

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to quaff, i.e. (causatively) to irrigate or furnish apotion to

KJV Translation

cause to (give, give to, let, make to) drink, drown, moisten, water. See H7937 (שָׁכַר), H8354 (שָׁתָה).
