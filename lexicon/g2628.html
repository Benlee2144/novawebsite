κατακολουθέω

katakolouthéō — G2628

Transliteration: katakolouthéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2628

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and G190 (ἀκολουθέω);

Definition

to accompany closely

KJV Translation

follow (after)
