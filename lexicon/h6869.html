צָרָה

tsârâh — H6869

Transliteration: tsârâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6869

Derivation: feminine of H6862 (צַר);

Definition

tightness (i.e. figuratively, trouble); transitively, a female rival:

KJV Translation

adversary, adversity, affliction, anguish, distress, tribulation, trouble.
