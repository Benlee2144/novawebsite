δράκων

drákōn — G1404

Transliteration: drákōn

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1404

Derivation: probably from an alternate form of (to look);

Definition

a fabulous kind of serpent (perhaps as supposed to fascinate)

KJV Translation

dragon

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
