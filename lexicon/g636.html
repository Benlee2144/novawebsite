ἀποπλέω

apopléō — G636

Transliteration: apopléō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G636

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G4126 (πλέω);

Definition

to set sail

KJV Translation

sail away
