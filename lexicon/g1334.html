διηγέομαι

diēgéomai — G1334

Transliteration: diēgéomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1334

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G2233 (ἡγέομαι);

Definition

to relate fully

KJV Translation

declare, shew, tell

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

διηγ-έομαι,
