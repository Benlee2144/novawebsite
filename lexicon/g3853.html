παραγγέλλω

parangéllō — G3853

Transliteration: parangéllō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3853

Derivation: from G3844 (παρά) and the base of G32 (ἄγγελος);

Definition

to transmit a message, i.e. (by implication) to enjoin

KJV Translation

(give in) charge, (give) command(-ment), declare
