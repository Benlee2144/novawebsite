ὕπνος

hýpnos — G5258

Transliteration: hýpnos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5258

Derivation: from an obsolete primary (perhaps akin to G5259 (ὑπό) through the idea of subsilience);

Definition

sleep, i.e. (figuratively) spiritual torpor

KJV Translation

sleep
