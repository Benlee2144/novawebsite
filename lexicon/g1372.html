διψάω

dipsáō — G1372

Transliteration: dipsáō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1372

Derivation: from a variation of G1373 (δίψος);

Definition

to thirst for (literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

(be, be a-)thirst(-y)
