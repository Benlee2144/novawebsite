ἐπαρχία

eparchía — G1885

Transliteration: eparchía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1885

Derivation: from a compound of G1909 (ἐπί) and G757 (ἄρχω) (meaning a governor of a district, \

Definition

a special region of government, i.e. a Roman præfecture

KJV Translation

province

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἐπαρχ-ία, ἡ,
the government of an ἔπαρχος, or the district governed by him, = Latin provincia, [NT+2nd c.BC+]; of Carthage, empire, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
__II military 'command', force occupying a district, [Refs 3rd c.BC+]
