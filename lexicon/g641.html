ἀποῤῥίπτω

aporrhíptō — G641

Transliteration: aporrhíptō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G641

Derivation: from G575 (ἀπό) and G4496 (ῥίπτω);

Definition

to hurl off, i.e. precipitate (oneself)

KJV Translation

cast
