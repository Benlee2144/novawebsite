βλαστάνω

blastánō — G985

Transliteration: blastánō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G985

Derivation: from (a sprout);

Definition

to germinate; by implication, to yield fruit

KJV Translation

bring forth, bud, spring (up)
