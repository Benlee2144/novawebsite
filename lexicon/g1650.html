ἔλεγχος

élenchos — G1650

Transliteration: élenchos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1650

Derivation: from G1651 (ἐλέγχω);

Definition

proof, conviction

KJV Translation

evidence, reproof

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
