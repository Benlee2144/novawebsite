צְרִי

Tsᵉrîy — H6874

Transliteration: Tsᵉrîy

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H6874

Derivation: the same as H6875 (צְרִי);

Definition

Tseri, an Israelite

KJV Translation

Zeri. Compare H3340 (יִצְרִי).

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

A man of the tribe of Levi living at the time of United Monarchy, first mentioned at 1Ch.25.3;
son of: Jeduthun (H3038);
brother of: Obed-edom (H5654), Mattithiah (H4993H), Gedaliah (H1436I), Jeshaiah (H3470H) and Hashabiah (H2811I); also called Izri at 1Ch.25.11;
Also named: yits.ri (יִצְרִי "Izri" H3339)
§ Zeri = "built"
son of Jeduthun and a musician in the court of David
