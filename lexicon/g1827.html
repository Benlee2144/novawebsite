ἐξελέγχω

exelénchō — G1827

Transliteration: exelénchō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1827

Derivation: from G1537 (ἐκ) and G1651 (ἐλέγχω);

Definition

to convict fully, i.e. (by implication) to punish

KJV Translation

convince
