ῥητῶς

rhētōs — G4490

Transliteration: rhētōs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4490

Derivation: adverb from a derivative of G4483 (ῥέω);

Definition

out-spokenly, i.e. distinctly

KJV Translation
