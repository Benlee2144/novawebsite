ἄνομος

ánomos — G459

Transliteration: ánomos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G459

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and G3551 (νόμος);

Definition

lawless, i.e. (negatively) not subject to (the Jewish) law; (by implication, a Gentile), or (positively) wicked

KJV Translation

without law, lawless, transgressor, unlawful, wicked
