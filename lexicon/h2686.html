Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H2686

חָצַץ

châtsats — H2686

Transliteration: châtsats

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2686

Derivation: a primitive root (compare H2673 (חָצָה)lemma חָעָה ayin, corrected to חָצָה); also as denominative from H2671 (חֵץ)

Definition

properly, to chop into, pierce or sever; hence, to curtail, to distribute (into ranks); to shoot an arrow

KJV Translation

archer, [idiom] bands, cut off in the midst.

Judges 5:11Job 21:21Proverbs 30:27
