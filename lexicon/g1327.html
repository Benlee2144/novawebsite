διέξοδος

diéxodos — G1327

Transliteration: diéxodos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1327

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G1841 (ἔξοδος);

Definition

an outlet through, i.e. probably an open square (from which roads diverge)

KJV Translation

highway
