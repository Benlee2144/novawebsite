ταπεινοφροσύνη

tapeinophrosýnē — G5012

Transliteration: tapeinophrosýnē

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5012

Derivation: from a compound of G5011 (ταπεινός) and the base of G5424 (φρήν);

Definition

humiliation of mind, i.e. modesty

KJV Translation

humbleness of mind, humility (of mind, loneliness (of mind)
