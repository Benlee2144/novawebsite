ἐπίσημος

epísēmos — G1978

Transliteration: epísēmos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1978

Derivation: from G1909 (ἐπί) and some form of the base of G4591 (σημαίνω);

Definition

remarkable, i.e. (figuratively) eminent

KJV Translation

notable, of note
