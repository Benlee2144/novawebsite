μεθόριος

methórios — G3181

Transliteration: methórios

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3181

Derivation: from G3326 (μετά) and G3725 (ὅριον);

Definition

bounded alongside, i.e. contiguous (neuter plural as noun, frontier)

KJV Translation

border
