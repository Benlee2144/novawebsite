εἰδωλολάτρης

eidōlolátrēs — G1496

Transliteration: eidōlolátrēs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1496

Derivation: from G1497 (εἴδωλον) and the base of G3000 (λατρεύω);

Definition

an image- (servant or) worshipper (literally or figuratively)
