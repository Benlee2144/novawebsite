δάκρυ

dákry — G1144

Transliteration: dákry

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1144

Derivation: of uncertain affinity;

Definition

a tear

KJV Translation

tear

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

δάκρυ, τό,
used by Poets for the sake of meter for δάκρυον in singular δάκρυ and dative plural δάκρυσι [Refs 8th c.BC+]:—tear, [Refs 8th c.BC+]
__II generally, drop, λιβάνου [Refs 5th c.BC+]. (Cf.Latin lacruma, Gothic tagr, O[Refs 5th c.BC+] tear.)
