קֶלַע

qelaʻ — H7050

Transliteration: qelaʻ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7050

Derivation: from H7049 (קָלַע);

Definition

a sling; also a (door) screen (as if slung across), or the valve (of the door) itself

KJV Translation
