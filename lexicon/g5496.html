Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G5496

χειραγωγέω

cheiragōgéō — G5496

Transliteration: cheiragōgéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5496

Derivation: from G5497 (χειραγωγός);

Definition

to be a hand-leader, i.e. to guide (a blind person)

KJV Translation

lead by the hand
