ἀστραπή

astrapḗ — G796

Transliteration: astrapḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G796

Derivation: from G797 (ἀστράπτω);

Definition

lightning; by analogy, glare

KJV Translation

lightning, bright shining

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
