מִטָּה

miṭṭâh — H4296

Transliteration: miṭṭâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4296

Derivation: from H5186 (נָטָה);

Definition

a bed (as extended) forsleeping or eating; by analogy, a sofa, litter or bier

KJV Translation

bed(-chamber), bier.
