חָזָה

châzâh — H2372

Transliteration: châzâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H2372

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to gaze at; mentally to perceive, contemplate (with pleasure); specifically, to have avision of

KJV Translation

behold, look, prophesy, provide, see.
