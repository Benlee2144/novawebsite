ἁλιεύω

halieúō — G232

Transliteration: halieúō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G232

Derivation: from G231 (ἁλιεύς);

Definition

to be a fisher, i.e. (by implication) to fish

KJV Translation

go a-fishing
