Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G4438

πυκτέω

pyktéō — G4438

Transliteration: pyktéō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4438

Derivation: from a derivative of the same as G4435 (πυγμή);

Definition

to box (with the fist), i.e. contend (as a boxer) at the games (figuratively)

KJV Translation

fight

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

πυκτ-εύω,
Boeotian dialect πουκτ-, box, spar, [NT+6th c.BC+]; ἐς σὸν κρᾶτα π. strike with the fist on the head, [Refs 5th c.BC+]: generally, fight, ξιφήρη π. [Refs 3rd c.AD+]; of gladiators, [Refs]:—middle, πεπύκτευται αὐτόν [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
