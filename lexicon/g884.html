ἀχάριστος

acháristos — G884

Transliteration: acháristos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G884

Derivation: from G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a presumed derivative of G5483 (χαρίζομαι);

Definition

thankless, i.e. ungrateful

KJV Translation

unthankful
