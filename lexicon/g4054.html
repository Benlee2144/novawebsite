περισσότερον

perissóteron — G4054

Transliteration: perissóteron

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4054

Derivation: neuter of G4055 (περισσότερος) (as adverb);

Definition

in a more superabundant way

KJV Translation

more abundantly, a great deal, far more
