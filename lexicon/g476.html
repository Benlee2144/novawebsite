ἀντίδικος

antídikos — G476

Transliteration: antídikos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G476

Derivation: from G473 (ἀντί) and G1349 (δίκη);

Definition

an opponent (in a lawsuit); specially, Satan (as the arch-enemy)

KJV Translation

adversary

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

ἀντίδῐκ-ος, ον,
