ἐλαιών

elaiṓn — G1638

Transliteration: elaiṓn

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1638

Derivation: from G1636 (ἐλαία);

Definition

an olive-orchard, i.e. (specially) the Mount of Olives

KJV Translation

Olivet
