αὐλίζομαι

aulízomai — G835

Transliteration: aulízomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G835

Derivation: middle voice from G833 (αὐλή);

Definition

to pass the night (properly, in the open air)

KJV Translation

abide, lodge
