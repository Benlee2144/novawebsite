ἀμύνομαι

amýnomai — G292

Transliteration: amýnomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G292

Derivation: middle voice of a primary verb;

Definition

to ward off (for oneself), i.e. protect

KJV Translation

defend
