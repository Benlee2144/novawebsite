Ζηλωτής

Zēlōtḗs — G2208

Transliteration: Zēlōtḗs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2208

Derivation: the same as G2207 (ζηλωτής);

Definition

a Zealot, i.e. (specially) partisan for Jewish political independence

KJV Translation

Zelotes
