מַכָּה

makkâh — H4347

Transliteration: makkâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4347

Derivation: or (masculine) מַכֶּה; (plural only) from H5221 (נָכָה); a blow (in 2 Chronicles 2:10, of the flail); by implication,

Definition

a wound; figuratively, carnage, also pestilence

KJV Translation

beaten, blow, plague, slaughter, smote, [idiom] sore, stripe, stroke, wound(-ed).
