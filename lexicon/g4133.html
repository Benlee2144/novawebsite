πλήν

plḗn — G4133

Transliteration: plḗn

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4133

Derivation: from G4119 (πλείων);

Definition

moreover (besides), i.e. albeit, save that, rather, yet

KJV Translation

but (rather), except, nevertheless, notwithstanding, save, than

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
