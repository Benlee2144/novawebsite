Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H5061

נֶגַע

negaʻ — H5061

Transliteration: negaʻ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5061

Derivation: from H5060 (נָגַע);

Definition

a blow (figuratively, infliction); also (by implication) a spot (concretely, a leprous person or dress)

KJV Translation

plague, sore, stricken, stripe, stroke, wound.
