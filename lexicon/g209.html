ἀκωλύτως

akōlýtōs — G209

Transliteration: akōlýtōs

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G209

Derivation: adverb from a compound of G1 (Α) (as a negative particle) and a derivative of G2967 (κωλύω);

Definition

in an unhindered manner, i.e. freely

KJV Translation

no man forbidding him

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
