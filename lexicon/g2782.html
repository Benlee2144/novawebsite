κήρυγμα

kḗrygma — G2782

Transliteration: kḗrygma

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2782

Derivation: from G2784 (κηρύσσω);

Definition

a proclamation (especially of the gospel; by implication, the gospel itself)

KJV Translation

preaching

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

κήρυγ-μα, ατος, τό,
(κηρύσσω) that which is cried by a herald, proclamation, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; ἐκ τοῦ κ. by proclamation, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; announcement of victory in games, [Refs 2nd c.AD+]; mandate, summons, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; reward offered by proclamation, [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__II preaching, [NT]
