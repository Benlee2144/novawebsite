שְׁאוֹל

shᵉʼôwl — H7585

Transliteration: shᵉʼôwl

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H7585

Derivation: or שְׁאֹל; from H7592 (שָׁאַל);

Definition

Hades or the world of the dead (as if a subterranean retreat), including its accessories and inmates

KJV Translation

grave, hell, pit.

⚠ Our Analysis Differs

Sheol is the Hebrew realm of the dead — not “hell” as commonly taught. It is where ALL the dead went, righteous and wicked alike. English Bibles translating this as “hell” collapsed four distinct concepts into one. See our study.
