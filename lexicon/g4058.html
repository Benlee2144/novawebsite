περιστερ-ά, ἡ,
common pigeon or dove, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; specifically, Columba livia domestica, and so distinguished from φάψ, φάττα, οἰνάς, τρυγών, [Refs 4th c.BC+]: περιστερός, ὁ, cock-pigeon, [Refs 5th c.BC+] (of a carrier-pigeon), [Refs 2nd c.AD+]
