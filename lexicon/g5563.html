χωρίζω

chōrízō — G5563

Transliteration: chōrízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5563

Derivation: from G5561 (χώρα);

Definition

to place room between, i.e. part; reflexively, to go away

KJV Translation

depart, put asunder, separate
