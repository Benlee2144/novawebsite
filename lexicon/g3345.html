μετασχηματίζω

metaschēmatízō — G3345

Transliteration: metaschēmatízō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3345

Derivation: from G3326 (μετά) and a derivative of G4976 (σχῆμα);

Definition

to transfigure or disguise; figuratively, to apply (by accommodation)

KJV Translation

transfer, transform (self)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
