σπαράσσω

sparássō — G4682

Transliteration: sparássō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G4682

Derivation: prolongation from (to grasp;

Definition

apparently strengthened from G4685 (σπάω) through the idea of spasmodic contraction); to mangle, i.e. convluse with epilepsy

KJV Translation

rend, tear
