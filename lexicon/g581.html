ἀπογενόμενος

apogenómenos — G581

Transliteration: apogenómenos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G581

Derivation: past participle of a compound of G575 (ἀπό) and G1096 (γίνομαι);

Definition

absent, i.e. deceased (figuratively, renounced)

KJV Translation

being dead

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
