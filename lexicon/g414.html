ἀνεκτότερος

anektóteros — G414

Transliteration: anektóteros

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G414

Derivation: comparative of a derivative of G430 (ἀνέχομαι);

Definition

more endurable

KJV Translation

more tolerable
