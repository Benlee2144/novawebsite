θανατήφορος

thanatḗphoros — G2287

Transliteration: thanatḗphoros

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2287

Derivation: from (the feminine form of) G2288 (θάνατος) and G5342 (φέρω);

Definition

death-bearing, i.e. fatal

KJV Translation

deadly
