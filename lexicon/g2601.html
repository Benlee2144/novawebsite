καταβιβάζω

katabibázō — G2601

Transliteration: katabibázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G2601

Derivation: from G2596 (κατά) and a derivative of the base of G939 (βάσις);

Definition

to cause to go down, i.e. precipitate

KJV Translation

bring (thrust) down

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

κατα-βιβάζω
[in LXX for יָרַד (hi., etc.): Deu.21:4, Eze.31:16, al. ;]
to cause to go down, cast down: pass., ἕως ᾅδου, Mat.11:23 (WH, R, mg.), Luk.10:15 (WH, mg.).†
