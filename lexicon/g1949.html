ἐπιλαμβάνομαι

epilambánomai — G1949

Transliteration: epilambánomai

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1949

Derivation: middle voice from G1909 (ἐπί) and G2983 (λαμβάνω);

Definition

to seize (for help, injury, attainment, or any other purpose; literally or figuratively)

KJV Translation

catch, lay hold (up-)on, take (by, hold of, on)

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)
