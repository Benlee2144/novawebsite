נָבָא

nâbâʼ — H5012

Transliteration: nâbâʼ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H5012

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

to prophesy, i.e. speak (or sing) by inspiration (in prediction or simple discourse)

KJV Translation

prophesy(-ing), make self a prophet.
