מַצֵּבָה

matstsêbâh — H4676

Transliteration: matstsêbâh

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H4676

Derivation: feminine (causatively) participle of H5324 (נָצַב);

Definition

something stationed, i.e. a column or (memorial stone); by analogy, an idol

KJV Translation

garrison, (standing) image, pillar.
