λογισμός

logismós — G3053

Transliteration: logismós

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G3053

Derivation: from G3049 (λογίζομαι);

Definition

computation, i.e. (figuratively) reasoning (conscience, conceit)

KJV Translation

imagination, thought

🏛#x1f3db; Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

λογ-ισμός, ὁ,
counting, calculation, τῶν ἡμερῶν [Refs 5th c.BC+]: in plural, numbers, arithmetic, λογισμοὺς μανθάνειν [Refs 5th c.BC+]
__2 account, reckoning, [Refs 5th c.BC+]; λ. λαμβάνειν hold an audit, [Refs 4th c.BC+]
__II without reference to number, calculation, reasoning, τοῦ ξυμφέροντος λογισμῷ [Refs 5th c.BC+]; λ. αὐτοκράτορι (see. αὐτοκράτωρ [Refs 5th c.BC+]; personified, opposed to Θυμός, [Refs 4th c.BC+]
