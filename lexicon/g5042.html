τεκνογονία

teknogonía — G5042

Transliteration: teknogonía

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5042

Derivation: from the same as G5041 (τεκνογονέω);

Definition

childbirth (parentage), i.e. (by implication) maternity (the performance of maternal duties)

KJV Translation

childbearing
