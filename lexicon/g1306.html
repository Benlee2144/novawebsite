Lexicon • Greek • Strong’s G1306

διαυγάζω

diaugázō — G1306

Transliteration: diaugázō

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1306

Derivation: from G1223 (διά) and G826 (αὐγάζω);

Definition

to glimmer through, i.e. break (as day)

KJV Translation

dawn

📜 Extended Lexicon (Abbott-Smith / STEPBible)

δι-αυγάζω
[in Aq.: Job.25:5 * ;]
to shine through, to dawn: 2Pe.1:19 (cf. ἕως οὗ διαπνεύσῃ ἡ ἡμέρα, Gal.2:17).†
(AS)

glance, shine through, τῷ σχισμῷ [NT+2nd c.BC+]; to be transparent, Mnesith. cited in [Refs 4th c.AD+]
