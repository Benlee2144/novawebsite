δεῦτε

deûte — G1205

Transliteration: deûte

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1205

Derivation: from G1204 (δεῦρο) and an imperative form of (to go);

Definition

come hither!

KJV Translation

come, X follow

🏛 Classical & Secular Greek Usage (Liddell-Scott-Jones)

δεῦτε,
adverb, as plural of δεῦρο, come hither! in [Refs 8th c.BC+]: rarely in Lyric poetry, [Refs 7th c.BC+]: in later Prose, δ. οἰκοδομήσωμεν [LXX+NT+1st c.AD+]
