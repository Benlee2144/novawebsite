Lexicon • Hebrew • Strong’s H3021

יָגַע

yâgaʻ — H3021

Transliteration: yâgaʻ

Pronunciation:

Language: Hebrew

Strong’s: H3021

Derivation: a primitive root;

Definition

properly, to gasp; hence, to be exhausted, to tire, to toil

KJV Translation

faint, (make to) labour, (be) weary.
