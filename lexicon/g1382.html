δοκιμή

dokimḗ — G1382

Transliteration: dokimḗ

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1382

Derivation: from the same as G1384 (δόκιμος);

Definition

test (abstractly or concretely); by implication, trustiness

KJV Translation

experience(-riment), proof, trial
