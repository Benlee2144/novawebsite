φθόγγος

phthóngos — G5353

Transliteration: phthóngos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G5353

Derivation: from G5350 (φθέγγομαι);

Definition

utterance, i.e. a musical note (vocal or instrumental)

KJV Translation

sound
