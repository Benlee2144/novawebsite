ἔμφυτος

émphytos — G1721

Transliteration: émphytos

Pronunciation:

Language: Greek

Strong’s: G1721

Derivation: from G1722 (ἐν) and a derivative of G5453 (φύω);

Definition

implanted (figuratively)

KJV Translation

engrafted
