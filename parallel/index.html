Parallel Bible

Compare Four Translations Side by Side — KJV (1611) • YLT Young’s Literal (1898) • ASV American Standard (1901) • WEB World English Bible

See how different translators handled the same Greek and Hebrew text. The KJV gives you tradition. Young’s Literal gives you the raw structure of the original. The ASV gives you the most literal major translation. The WEB gives you modern readable English. When they all agree, you can trust the English. When they diverge, that’s where the original language matters most.
